OTHER MATTERS
Commissioner Coleman asked if equalization credits were provided for Detroit-Based
Business (DBB) verses Detroit-Headquartered Business (DHB). Attorney Pospiech
replied yes. The department uses the same formula offered in the City's ordinance. He
also said that at one time it was an advantage for vendors that lived in the city. Director
Brown added that the department could revisit the procurement policy and recommend
a different structure.

2. Review of Implementation Issues Relating to the Health Benefits Tiered Subsidy Schedule as
Reviewed by the Retirement Board on September 5, 2013
Trustee Fitzgerald opened the meeting with a general discussion on implementation issues relating to
the tiered subsidy schedule. In discussion the Committee suggested multiple areas for consideration
to support implementation of the subsidy schedule and to ensure that it is both equitable and efficient
to administer.
Trustee Samstein requested that staff prepare a project plan with milestone dates and completed
impact analysis in the following areas: legal review, cost and liability projections, operational impact

The Trustees also discussed revising the target date to allow adequate implementation planning and

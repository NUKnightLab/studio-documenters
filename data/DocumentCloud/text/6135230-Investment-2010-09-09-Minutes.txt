MUNICIPAL EMPLOYEES’ ANNUITY AND BENEFIT FUND OF CHICAGO
SPECIAL MEETING NO. 2010-09-01
SEPTEMBER 9, 2010

A special meeting of the Retirement Board of the Municipal Employees’ Annuity and
Benefit Fund of Chicago was held in the Offices of the Fund on September 9, 2010
regarding the state of the markets. The meeting was called to order at 9:40 a.m.

Investment Manager Panel
Mr. Wesner, of Marquette Associates introduced a panel of our investment managers
who are experts in their niche who were invited to speak on behalf of their specific
investment philosophy and their current market outlook. The following individuals
presented market overviews and forecasts:
Michael Dow – UBS Fixed Income
David Saunders – K2 Advisors – Hedged Equity
Stephanie Braming – William Blair – Emerging Markets Equity
After each presentation there was short discussion regarding potential investment
strategies for the Fund as well as market forecasts for the next 3-5 years.

The Board thanked Messrs. Dow, Saunders and Ms. Braming for their presentations.
Real Estate Discussion
Mr. Wesner introduced the panelists of the second panel who are experts in real estate
investments to present their views on the outlook for investments in real estate. The
following individuals presented market overviews and forecasts:
Jay Butterfield – American Realty
Stephan Murphy – JP Morgan
There was a short discussion regarding the information presented. The Board thanked
Messrs. Butterfield and Murphy for their presentations.

Fiduciary Insurance
Following the investment presentations, the Board authorized the Executive Director to
bind fiduciary insurance coverage through Kaercher Campbell & Associates with terms
as presented. Motion by Mr. Gibson. Second by Mr. Lux.
Carried: Ayes – Messrs. Malatesta, Lux, Gibson and Mr. Walsh (Proxy to the
Treasurer). Noes – None.
With no further presentations, the meeting ended at 11:30 a.m.

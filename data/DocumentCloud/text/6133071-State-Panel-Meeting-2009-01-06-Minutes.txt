III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD DECEMBER 9, 2008

Chairman Gallagher called for approval of the minutes of the December 9, 2008 meeting. Member Piper

by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gallagher asked Executive Director Brosnan to present the State Panel administrative actions for
the period of time from December 1 through December 31, 2008. He distributed the report to the Board and
summarized its highlights. The Board examined the report and discussed it.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.
Case No. S-UC-08-062
American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31/State of Illinois,
Department of Central Management Services

Chairman Gallagher called the case and summarized its background. Acting General Counsel Brosnan
reviewed the issues presented by the exceptions to Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) Harvey's Recommended
Decision and Order (RDO), and recommended that the Board find no merit to the exclusions sought by the
Employer and decline to take up on its own motion the ALJ's resolution of the "direct" and "adjust
grievances" indicia, as no party filed exceptions thereto. After discussion, Member Piper moved to accept
the acting general counsel's recommendation. Member Washington seconded the motion, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

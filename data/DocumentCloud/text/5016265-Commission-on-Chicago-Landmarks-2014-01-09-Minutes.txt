The following project was reviewed by the PRC:
1.

100 N. Central
(Garfield Park Fieldhouse – 29th Ward)
Proposal:

Proposed new concrete and metal accessible ramp at the main (east)
entrance, removal of a 1960s concrete ramp at the southeast terrace and
recreation of stairs at the southeast terrace to match the historic
configuration in this location.

Action:

Approved unanimously with the following conditions:
1. The concrete ramp and base shall match the color and texture of the
existing historic concrete foundation; and,
2. The metal railings shall have a light finish to more closely resemble
the color of the historic masonry; and,
3. The permit plans shall include proposed details of the southeast stairs,
which shall match the size, configuration, color and texture of the
historic stairs and balusters seen elsewhere on the building.

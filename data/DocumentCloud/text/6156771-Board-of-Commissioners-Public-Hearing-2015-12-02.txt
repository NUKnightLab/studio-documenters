NOTICE OF PUBLIC HEARING
In accordance with the provisions of “An Act in the relation of the creation, maintenance, operation,
and improvement of the Chicago Park District”, 70 ILCS 1505 et seq., PUBLIC NOTICE is hereby
given that A PUBLIC HEARING on the YEAR 2016 PROPOSED OPERATING BUDGET REPORT
and the TENTATIVE APPROPRIATION ORDINANCE will be held on Wednesday, December 2,
2015 at 3:30 p.m., in the 8th Floor Board Room of the Chicago Park District Administration Building
at 541 North Fairbanks, Chicago, Illinois, 60611.
The normal rules for addressing the Board of Commissioners during the Public (Budget) Hearing
will be modified for this hearing only. Speakers will be allowed to sign-up to speak in person until
3:30 P.M on December 2, 2015. The rules for on-line and telephone sign-up remain the same
(speaker requests close by 4:00 PM the Tuesday before the meeting).

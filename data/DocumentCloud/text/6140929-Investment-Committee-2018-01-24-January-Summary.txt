News from your Retirement Board
Pre-Retirement Seminar
The FABF pre-retirement seminars for 2018 will be held on Saturday, March 10th (2C EMS-4) and
Saturday, September 8th (1C EMS-2) at the Quinn Fire Academy on 558 W. DeKoven from 7:30 A.M.1:30 P.M. Some of the topics that will be discussed include:
•
•
•

Retiree Healthcare
Social Security and Medicare
Deferred Compensation

An RSVP is required, so please call (312) 726-5823 or email seminar@fabf.org and specify which date
you wish to attend. The seminars are provided for those members within three years of actual
retirement.

FABF Updated Website
I welcome you to view our revamped website at www.fabf.org. The website now contains updated
information and has been remodeled with the ease of navigation in mind. Should you have any
comments or suggestions, please direct them to our IT systems analyst, Marc Torres, at mtorres@fabf.org.

Brass Bill Payment
The Fund received the City’s Brass Bill contribution in the amount of $1,628,155 for fiscal year 2016
on December 28, 2017 pursuant to section 5/6-211(g) of the pension code. The City’s fiscal year 2017
Brass Bill contribution is expected to be received by the Fund no later than March 31, 2018.
The Retirement Board will continue to act prudently to ensure that all monies due and owing to the
Fund are collected.
Fund Secretary

Captain Anthony Martin

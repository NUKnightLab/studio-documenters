Notice of Illinois Gaming Board Meeting
Regular Board Meeting - Riverboat
The Illinois Gaming Board will convene a regular meeting of the Board in Open Session on
Tuesday, June 12, 2018 at 11:00 A.M. in the Board’s Conference Room on the 3rd floor of the Michael
A. Bilandic Building, and immediately retire to Closed Executive Session. The Board will reconvene its
regular meeting in Open Session on Wednesday, June 13, 2018 at 9:00 A.M. in the 5th floor of the
Michael Bilandic Building. The subject matters to be discussed are included on the attached proposed
Riverboat agenda.
Please note that the regular Riverboat meeting agenda is typically posted 48 hours prior to the
scheduled meeting date.

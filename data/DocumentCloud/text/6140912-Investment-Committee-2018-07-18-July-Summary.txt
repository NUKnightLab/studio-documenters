MONTHLY NEWS AND INFORMATION FROM YOUR RETIREMENT BOARD
Active Member Trustee Election
An election will be held to fill the office of Active Firefighter Member of The Retirement Board which
becomes vacant December 1, 2018, by reason of the expiration of the term of a present incumbent. The
Trustee position is an unpaid position and does require a fairly significant commitment of time. Any active
member who has an interest and applicable background should consider becoming a candidate as the welfare
of all FABF members is impacted by the governance of the Board of Trustees.
Persons who desire to run as a candidate for the office to be filled in this election may register in person at the
office of the Fund between the hours of 8:00 a.m. and 4:30 p.m., September 4, 2018 through September 7,
2018 or by delivering a properly completed original statement of candidacy to the office of the Fund.
Ballots will be mailed to all persons eligible to vote no later than September 19, 2018. Persons voting must
return their ballots to the Retirement Board of the Fireman's Annuity and Benefit Fund of Chicago c/o The
Firemen’s Annuity and Benefit Fund of Chicago by mail.
Please refer to www.FABF.org for additional information.
EMWQ Gift Fund
The Ende Menzer Walsh and Quinn, Retirees’ Widows’ and Childrens’ Assistance Fund is pleased to report
that it has received a donation in the amount of $5,000 from Chive Charities, a large philanthropic charity
dedicated to those diagnosed with rare medical disorders, veterans and first responders. The EMWQ Fund is
both pleased and proud to be recognized by this tremendous national organization. The EMWQ Fund has
consistently operated by the highest principals of commitment and integrity. It is because of this diligence that
Chive has deemed our organization to be worthy of their support. Please visit their website at
www.chivecharities.org.

Fund Secretary,

Captain Anthony Martin

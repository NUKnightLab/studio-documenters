8. Old Business
A.

2018-629
Attachments:

Continued Discussion of Proposed GLWA Settlement Policy
Settlement Policy
GLWA Proposed Settlement Policy.docx

Director Daddow made a motion, supported by Vice Chairman Baker, to amend
the proposed policy from $250,000 for GLWA (Great Lakes Water Authority) action
down to $100,000, and DWSD (Detroit Water and Sewerage Department) action
down to $50,000, and that legal invoices be brought to the Legal Committee for
review.
Secretary Munfakh made a motion, supported by Director Daddow, to amend the
threshold for all settlements, GLWA and DWSD, to $125,000, and all external
legal invoices reviewed by the Legal Committee.
Director Brown made a motion, supported by Director Daddow, to amend the
Committee Consideration under DWSD Actions to add two additional bullets:
The amount of the alleged liability in existence on or before December 31, 2015;
and Whether the alleged liability was transferred to and/or assumed by GLWA,
pursuant to Article IV of Regional Sewage Disposal System Lease and/or the
Regional Water Supply System Lease.
Chairman Hendrix requested a motion to adopt the Proposed GLWA Settlement
Policy as Amended.
Motion By: Robert Daddow
Support By: Brian Baker
Action: Adopt as Amended
The motion carried by a unanimous vote.

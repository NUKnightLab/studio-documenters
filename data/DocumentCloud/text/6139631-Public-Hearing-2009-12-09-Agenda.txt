Pursuant to 70 ILCS 2605/5.6 of the “Metropolitan Water Reclamation Act”,
public notice is hereby given that a Public Hearing will be held at 10:00
a.m. on Wednesday, December 9, 2009 in the Board Room, 100 East Erie
Street, Chicago, Illinois, to consider the proposed Budget and proposed
tax levy of the Metropolitan Water Reclamation District of Greater Chicago
for the fiscal year ending December 31, 2010.
The Tentative Budget is available for review on the District’s website,
www.MWRD.org and also on file and available for inspection in the Office
of the Clerk of the District, Room 243,100 East Erie Street, Chicago,
Illinois.

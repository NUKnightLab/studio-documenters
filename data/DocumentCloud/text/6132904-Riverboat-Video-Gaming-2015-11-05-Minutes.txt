MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING - RIVERBOAT
OPEN SESSION
November 6, 2015
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
Springfield, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
On Friday, November 6, 2015 a Regular Meeting - Riverboat of the Illinois Gaming Board
(“Board”) was held on the 5th floor at 160 N. LaSalle at the Springfield Hilton, Springfield,
Illinois at 9:00 A.M. Present were the following: Chairman Don Tracy, Members Lee Gould,
Hector Alejandre, Dee Robinson and Thomas Dunn.
Member Alejandre moved that the Board approve the open session minutes from the Illinois
Gaming Board Regular Meeting held on September 22, 2015. Member Gould seconded the
motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
CHAIRMAN’S COMMENTS –
Chairman Tracy introduced the newest Board Member, Dee Robinson. Chairman Tracy also
stated that the Diversity Business Fair will be held at Par-A-Dice Casino on November 17, 2015.

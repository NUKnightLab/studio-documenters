Mr. Walsh presented the proposed Board meeting dates and office holiday schedule for 2020. The Board
gave direction to implement the proposed office holiday schedule for 2020, but deferred approval of the
proposed 2020 Board Meeting dates.
Retiree Healthcare Mailing
Mr. Walsh conveyed a request from Blue Cross Blue Shield of Illinois (BCBSIL) and Aetna that LABF
assist in facilitating an insurance mailing to City of Chicago employee annuitants. Mr. Walsh explained
that the insurance information would be mailed in an LABF envelope and include a cover letter from LABF
explaining that LABF, as a matter of law, cannot and does not endorse insurance plans. The Board gave
direction to proceed with the mailing so long as BCBSIL and Aetna cover the cost of the mailing.

Pension Investments will hold hearings on October 23-24, 2019 and that the LABF was scheduled to testify
on October 24th at 9:30a.m.

Carmichael Litigation
The Board discussed the plaintiffs’ motion to lift the escrow order which would allow the contributions
associated with those affected by this matter to be made to the LABF instead of to the escrow account.
Mr. Walsh noted the ambiguities with respect to the appropriate contribution amount were discussed in
court.

Underwood Litigation
Mr. Walsh updated the Board with respect to the two appeals pending in this case. Additionally, Mr.
Walsh reported that there was a hearing scheduled in the Circuit Court on Tuesday, October 22, 2019 to
consider the plaintiffs’ motion to order the defendants to pay interest on health insurance subsidies and to
consider plaintiffs’ motion to enter a “final judgment”. The LABF and other defendants have filed briefs
opposing each of these motions. Mr. Carroll reported that Mr. Krislov has requested that the judge
presiding in this case be recused.

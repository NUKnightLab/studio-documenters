Committee on Public Works - Second Reading

10493-18

An Ordinance authorizing the County of Allegheny to acquire by
condemnation certain right-of-way and temporary construction easement
areas from a parcel required to stabilize the slope supporting Tarentum
Culmerville Road south of Allegheny Valley Expressway (SR 28) in
Tarentum Borough, and to take such further action as may be necessary
under applicable law, including the Eminent Domain Code.

Sponsors:

Chief Executive
This ordinance enables the County to proceed with acquiring the property needed for
stabilizing Tarentum Culmerville Road; some of the acquisitions will last only though
construction, while others will be permanent, and the property is being acquired from an
entity that appears to be a defunct coal mining company.

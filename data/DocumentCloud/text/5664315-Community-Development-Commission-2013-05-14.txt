B.

NEAR WEST TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 27)
Request authority for the Department of Housing and Economic Development to
negotiate a redevelopment agreement with Lake Street Studios, LP for
redevelopment of the property located at 727 W. Lake Street in the Near West Tax
Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and to recommend to the City
Council of the City of Chicago the designation of Lake Street Studios, LP as
Developer.
Project Manager: Camille Loggins
Motioned by Martin, seconded by Olander. Approved 9-0. Yeas: Buford, Garner,
Martin, Mooney, Newsome, Olander, Perez, Sahli and Ward. Stein not present.
13-CDC-14

 C.

ROSELAND/MICHIGAN REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 9)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Housing and Economic
Development’s intention to enter into a negotiated sale with The Blessed Child for
the disposition of the property located at 10736 S. Michigan Avenue in the
Roseland/Michigan Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, to
request alternative proposals, and to approve the sale of the property to The
Blessed Child if no responsive alternative proposals are received.
Project Manager: Michelle Nolan
Motioned by Martin, seconded by Ward. Approved 9-0. Yeas: Buford, Garner,
Martin, Mooney, Newsome, Olander, Perez, Sahli and Ward. Stein not present.
13-CDC-15

D.

OGDEN/PULASKI TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA

the Odgen/Pulaski Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area.
Project Manager: Michele Rhymes
Motioned by Sahli, seconded by Buford. Approved 10-0. Yeas: Buford, Garner,
Martin, Mooney, Newsome, Olander, Perez, Sahli, Stein and Ward.

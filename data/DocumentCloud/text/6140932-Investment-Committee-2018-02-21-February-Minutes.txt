EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR’S REPORT:
A. MONTHLY REPORT:

The Executive Director presented his monthly report to the Board.
The Executive Director reported that the Fund was in the process of responding to 34 requests to
transfer downstate service credits as allowed under P.A. 96-0727 and the Fund is beginning the
process to also members to reestablish service credits in the downstate funds and then transfer
those credits to this fund.
The Fund is in the process of drafting an RFP or RFI for a Risk Assessment System Review which
will be presented to the Board for approval at the March meeting.
The Popular Report had been posted on the Fund’s website and mailed to all participants.
The Trustees requested that the Fund provide information to members transitioning to Medicare.
The Trustees were invited to participate in a Board meeting software demonstration at the
conclusion of the Board meeting.
The Fund’s updated Website has been launched.

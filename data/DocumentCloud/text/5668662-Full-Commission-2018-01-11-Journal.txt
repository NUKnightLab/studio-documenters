 PLEDGE OF ALLEGIANCE TO THE FLAG OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA
Chairman Woronchak advised all to stand to recite the Pledge of Allegiance.

INVOCATION
Chairman Woronchak called upon Commissioner Scott for the introduction of Darcel
Brown, Legislative Research Assistant, who gave the following invocation:
“God, we thank you for a new year. Thank you for leading us through this past year and
we ask for your continued guidance during 2018. Lord, we ask that you continue to help the
Commissioners to lead with integrity and a heart to decree decisions that are best for those
who reside in Wayne County. Continue to renew their strength as they face challenges and
overcome the obstacles ahead. I pray that you would bless their efforts with success. We say
a special prayer for Commissioner Al Haidous; God we pray that you would comfort him and
his family during this time of loss. These blessings we ask in your name. AMEN.”

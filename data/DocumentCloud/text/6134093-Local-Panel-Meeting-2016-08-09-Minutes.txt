STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on August 9, 2016
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 N LaSalle Street, 4th Floor, Room S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Board; Richard Lewis, Member of the Board; Kathryn
Zeledon Nelson, General Counsel; Melissa Mlynski, Executive Director; Anna Hamburg-Gal, Associate General Counsel;
and staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD ON July 12, 2016

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the July 12, 2016 meeting. Member Anderson moved to approve
the minutes. Member Lewis seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR’S REPORT

Executive Director Mlynski presented the Executive Director report. The Chair and Members discussed the report.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.

Case No. L-RC-15-003
County, Municipal Employees’, Supervisors’, and Foremens’ Union, Local 1001; Water Pipe
Extension, Bureau of Engineering Laborers Local 1092; Cement Workers Local 76; Laborers Int’l
Union of North America/City of Chicago

General Counsel Nelson called the case, noted that no party filed exceptions to Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) Heery’s
Recommended Decision and Order (RDO) and asked whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own motion.
The Board declined to do so.
2.

Case No. L-CB-16-036
Al Wagner/Automated Mechanics Union, Local #701

General Counsel Nelson called the case and stated that the Executive Director issued a dismissal on a charge alleging that
Respondent violated Section 10(b) of the Act. She then discussed the Charging Party’s appeal and Respondent’s response
that was filed. After her presentation, she recommended that the board remand the case for further investigation. After
discussion, Member Lewis moved to remand the case for further investigation. Member Anderson seconded the motion,
and the Board passed it unanimously by a voice vote.
VI.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded it was
unnecessary.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board, in
Chicago, Illinois, this 8th day of September, 2016.

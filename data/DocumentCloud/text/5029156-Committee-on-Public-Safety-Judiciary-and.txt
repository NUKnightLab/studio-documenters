Communication dated October 4, 2018 (received October 24th) requesting
Commission approval of a two-year grant agreement between the Charter
County of Wayne and the U.S. Department of Justice, Office of Justice Programs
in the amount of $360,000 to accept the FY 18 Innovative Prosecution Solution to
Combat Violent Crime & Illegal Opioids grant award, which targets illegal opioid
suppliers for the purpose of reducing opioid overdose deaths through targeted
enforcement in crime “hot spots” within Detroit, increasing prosecutorial staff and
using the grand jury process; the County Executive advises that funding will be
used to hire two full-time project consultant attorneys and one full-time legal
intern and there is no match requirement; the term of the agreement is from
October 1, 2018 through September 30, 2020; the revenue from the agreement
will be deposited into Account No. 101 22912 529000 (Innovative Pros Solution).
(2019-93-001)

4.

Communication dated October 16, 2018 (received October 24th) requesting
Commission approval of a three-year grant agreement between the Charter
County of Wayne and the U.S. Department of Justice, Office of Justice Programs
in the amount of $1,500,000 to bolster the resources of the Wayne County
Sexual Assault Kit Task Force (WCSAKTF); the County Executive advises that
funding will cover WCSAKTF personnel, including a site coordinator, one
paralegal and one executive assistant in the first year; in the second and third
year of the grant, being FY 20 and FY 21, the grant will fund the above-named
positions and four assistant prosecutors; the term of the grant is from October 1,
2018 through September 30, 2021; the revenue from the grant will be deposited
into Account No. 101 22910 529000 (SAKI-2018). (2019-93-002)

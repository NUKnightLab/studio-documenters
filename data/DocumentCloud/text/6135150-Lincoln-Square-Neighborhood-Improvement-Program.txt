Perfunctory Business
o Commissioners Present: Melaina Prest, Ben Munro, Anthony Qaiyum, Zach Maiorca
o Others Present: Ryan Warsing (LSRCC), Rudy Flores (LSRCC)
o Commissioners Absent: Chris Dallas, Shelley Young, Pat Bader
Meeting Called to order at 9:11 am – Z. Maiorca (motion), A. Qaiyum (second)
Minutes from 5/25/17 meeting approved – C. Dallas (motion), M. Prest (second)
2017 Financials YTD – A. Qaiyum (motion), Z. Maiorca (second)
o The SSA has received $0 out of $242,994 in expected levies and loss collection
o The SSA has received $0 out of $3,400 in TIF Rebates
o The SSA has spent $46,305 of its $246,394 budget
 All expenses to date have been made via LSRCC Line of Credit
o Profit and Loss – R. Warsing presented detailed YTD profit/loss and balance sheet
New Business
o Review of proposed Landscaping Projects
 Rose Garden Plantings APPROVED – A. Qaiyum (motion), M. Prest (second)
o Final 2018 Budget approved – Z. Maiorca (motion), B. Munro (second)
o Discussion of 2018 Contracts
 Commissioners expressed satisfaction with current contracts, and preferred to
explore multi-year contracts as opposed to one-year contracts
 Commissioners stated their preference for comprehensive snow plowing limited
to Giddings Plaza, where services are most in need
 Commissioners agreed it would be a good idea to remind property owners of
their snow plowing obligations as stated in the City code
o PaintWorks Update
 PaintWorks’ first mural is being moved from Easy Breezy Laundry (2807 W
Lawrence) to 2803 W Lawrence, a more visible site next to the original
 Artist Franklin Riley will be submitting initial renderings of his piece very soon
 An ioby fundraising page for PaintWorks’ first Mural is currently live
 The PaintWorks website now has a form for local artists to submit themselves
for future consideration
Announcements
o Greg Petersen is no longer on the Commission, bringing total vacancies to two
o The next SSA Meeting will be Thursday, July 27, 9:00am at the LSRCC office
Meeting adjourned at 10:00am

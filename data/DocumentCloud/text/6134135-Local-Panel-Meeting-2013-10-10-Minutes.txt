STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on October 10, 2013
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, Suite S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Board; Richard Lewis, Member of the Board; Melissa
Mlynski, Executive Director; Jerald S. Post, General Counsel; and staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD September 10, 2013

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the September 10, 2013 meeting. Member Anderson moved to
approve the minutes. Member Lewis seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote. The Board
welcomed Investigator Yumnah Tayyab to its staff.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gierut asked Executive Director Mlynski to present the Local Panel administrative actions report for the period
of time from September 1 through September 30, 2013. She presented the report and summarized its highlights. The
Board examined the report and discussed it. Executive Director Mlynski then presented the fiscal and mediation reports.
The Board discussed and questioned various aspects of both reports.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.
Case No. L-CA-13-052
Fraternal Order of Police, Lodge 7/ City of Chicago

Chairman Gierut moved to immediately hear the oral argument scheduled in Case No. L-CA-13-052, Fraternal Order of
Police, Lodge 7 and City of Chicago. The Board agreed. The parties to the case presented oral argument to the Board. At
the conclusion thereof, Chairman Gierut moved that the Board hold this case for decision until the November meeting.
The Board agreed.
2.
Case No. L-CB-13-037
Basharath Ali Khan/ American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31
Chairman Gierut asked General Counsel Post to call the case. General Counsel Post stated that on July 25, 2013 the
Executive Director issued a dismissal of the charge alleging that Respondent violated Section 10(b) of the Illinois Public
Labor Relations Act. General Counsel Post then asked Counsel Kazanjian to review the issues raised by the Charging
Party’s appeal. After his presentation, Counsel Kazanjian recommended that the Executive Director's dismissal be
affirmed. After discussion, Member Anderson moved to accept the Executive Director’s dismissal. Member Lewis
seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VI.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded that it was not
necessary at this time.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Lewis made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board, in
Chicago, Illinois, this 5th day of November, 2013.

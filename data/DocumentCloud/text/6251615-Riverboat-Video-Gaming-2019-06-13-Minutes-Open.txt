Regular Meeting, Open Session Minutes – Riverboat and Video Gaming
Illinois Gaming Board
Chicago, Illinois
June 13, 2019
Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”) convened for a Regular Meeting – Riverboat and Video Gaming – on
Thursday June 13, 2019 in the Conference Room on the 5th Floor of the Michael Bilandic Building
Center, in Chicago, Illinois pursuant to the Illinois Open Meetings Act, 5 ILCS 120/1 et seq.
A roll call was taken. The following Board members were physically present: Chairman Don Tracy,
Member Steve Dolins, Member Ruben Ramirez and Member Dionne Hayden. Four members of the
Board being physically present, a quorum was satisfied.
At approximately 9:02 A.M., Member Dolins moved that the Board go into closed session pursuant to
Section 2(c), paragraphs (1), (11), (14), and (21) of the Open Meetings Act and Section 6(d) of the
Riverboat Gambling Act to discuss items on the closed session agenda relating to specific personnel, the
closed session minutes, investigations, and pending litigation. Member Ramirez seconded the motion.
The Board approved the motion unanimously by voice vote. Thereafter, the Board moved from the
Conference Room on the 5th Floor of the Michael Bilandic Building to the Conference Room on the 3rd
Floor of the building for a closed session meeting; the minutes of which are separately recorded.
At approximately 10:05 A.M., the Board returned to the Conference Room on the 5th Floor of the
Michael Bilandic Building Center, in Chicago, Illinois to reconvene its Regular Meeting – Riverboat and
Video Gaming – of Thursday June 13, 2019 pursuant to the Illinois Open Meetings Act, 5 ILCS 120/1 et
seq.
The following Board members were physically present: Chairman Don Tracy, Member Steve Dolins,
Member Ruben Ramirez and Member Dionne Hayden. Four members of the Board being physically
present, a quorum was satisfied.

CHAIRMAN COMMENTS

 June 13, 2019 Riverboat/Video Gaming Regular Board Meeting
The Chairman welcomed the new IGB Administrator, Marcus D. Fruchter. The Chairman also
welcomed the Board’s newest member, Dionne Hayden. Chairman Tracy thanked former Member Dee
Robinson for her years of passionate and energetic service to the Board. The Chairman announced that
the next Board meeting is scheduled for August 1, 2019. The Chairman offered some comments about
the Board’s March 1, 2019 approval of Churchill Downs Inc.’s acquisition of a controlling interest in
Rivers Casino and in particular, the concerns regarding the provision that Churchill Downs makes a
good faith effort to sell up to 10% of its equity in Rivers Casino to statutory investors within 90 days of
the March 1st approval.
BOARD MEMBER COMMENTS
Member Dolins also thanked former Member Robinson for her years of great service to the Board.

ADMINISTRATOR REPORT
Administrator Marcus D. Fruchter stated that he is honored to be serving as the IGB Administrator and
thanked the many talented and hard-working staff members that he has worked with in the past few
weeks. Administrator Fruchter also commented that during his time as Administrator, the IGB will
continue to be independent, ethical, fair, transparent and accountable to the people of the State of
Illinois. Administrator Fruchter reported that there are currently 7,004 licensed locations operating
approximately 32,055 video gaming terminals. He also reported that the IGB has converted
approximately 79% of the video gaming terminals to the G2S protocol which translates to approximately
24,987 machines. Administrator Fruchter estimated that the G2S conversion project may be completed
sometime in the Fall of 2019. The Administrator also announced that the IGB has developed a standard
form Use Agreement that is now available on the IGB website and that the IGB has opened up a 45 day
comment period so any interested parties can review and offer comments on the agreement via email.

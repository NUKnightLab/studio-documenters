Notice of Illinois Gaming Board Meeting
Regular Board Meeting – Riverboat/Video Gaming
The Illinois Gaming Board will convene a regular meeting of the Board in Open Session on
Friday, November 2, 2018 at 8:00 A.M. in the Conference Room of the Wyndham Hotel in Springfield,
and immediately retire to Closed Executive Session. The Board will reconvene its regular meeting in
Open Session on Friday, November 2, 2018 at 9:00 A.M. in the Conference Room of the Wyndham
Hotel in Springfield. The subject matters to be discussed are included on the attached proposed
Riverboat/Video Gaming agenda.
Please note that the regular Riverboat/Video Gaming meeting agenda is typically posted 48
hours prior to the scheduled meeting date.

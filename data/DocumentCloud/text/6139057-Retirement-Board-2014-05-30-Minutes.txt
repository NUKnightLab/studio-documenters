FELONY CONVICTION
Lewellen, Glenn
The Board's attorney advised the Board of a Court Order entered in March 2014, from the US Attorney's
Office which indicates the U.S. Department of Treasury has entered an Order, on February 7, 2014,
against Lewellen directing the satisfaction of the Forfeiture Judgment with Substitute Assets, citing to
monies in the amount of $59,639.56, which represent the total sum of Mr. Lewellen’s pension
contributions. The Board will comply with the Court Order and such funds will be disbursed
immediately.

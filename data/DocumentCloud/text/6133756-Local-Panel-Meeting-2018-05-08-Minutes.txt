STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on May 8, 2018
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 9:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman of the Local Panel; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Local Panel; Angela C. Thomas,
Member of the Local Panel; Kimberly Stevens, Executive Director; Helen J. Kim, General Counsel; Anna Hamburg-Gal,
Associate General Counsel; and staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD April 17, 2018

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the April 17, 2018 meeting. Member Anderson moved to approve
the minutes. Member Thomas seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gierut asked Executive Director Stevens to present the Executive Director’s reports. She presented the reports
and summarized the highlights.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1. Case No. L-CA-17-034
Fraternal Order of Police, Lodge #7/City of Chicago (Police Department)

General Counsel Kim called the case. She stated that the Board had heard oral arguments on this matter at the Board’s April
meeting, which was recorded by a court reporter. After brief discussion, Member Thomas moved to hold the case in
abeyance. Member Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
2. Case No. L-CB-16-051
Reginald J. Dean/American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31
General Counsel Kim called and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member Anderson moved
to accept the Executive Director dismissal. Member Thomas seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by
a voice vote.
VI.

PUBLIC COMMENT

No members of the public were present.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board, in
Chicago, Illinois, this 5th day of June 2018.

PORTAGE PARK TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 45)
Request authority for the Department of Planning and Development to negotiate a
redevelopment agreement with Irving Park Property Holdings, LLC for
redevelopment of the property located at 4901 W. Irving Park Road in the Portage

Holdings, LLC as Developer.

Motioned by Butler, seconded by Ward. Approved 11-0. Yeas: Alphonse, Buford,
Butler, Griggs, Moreno, Newsome, Olander, Perez, Reifman, Ward and Carrott.

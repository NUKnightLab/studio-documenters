Review of UniCare HMO Plan.
The committee met to discuss the notification received by the Fund on October 29, 2009 from
UniCare stating their intention to exit the commercial health insurance markets of Illinois. As a
result, UniCare would no longer be in a position to provide group health benefits to the Fund.
The committee reviewed options and next steps as identified by Ms. Finch, Director of Health
Benefits. The committee also discussed the offer to move the UniCare HMO group to BCBS for
2010 at the existing agreed rate effective January 1, 2010. The committee discussed the fact that
the Board should respond to the offer letter presented by BCBS stating the parameters of the
accepting the agreement including the premium agreement along with additional terms regarding
the payment for and administration of the plan.

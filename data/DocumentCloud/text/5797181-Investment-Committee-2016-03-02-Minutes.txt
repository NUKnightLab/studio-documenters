Re: Grand Personalty, Inc.: 2014 State Tax Return
Resolution Moved By: Trustee Nickleberry – Supported By: Trustee Szymanski
WHEREAS, the General Retirement System is the sole shareholder of General Resorts
Services, Inc., and
WHEREAS, General Resorts Services, Inc. is the sole shareholder of the corporation,
and
WHEREAS, in 2015 the corporation filed all of its required 2014 Federal and State
income taxes, and
WHEREAS, the corporation owed the State of Michigan $24,000 in income taxes for
2014 but did not pay the State of Michigan by the required due date, and
WHEREAS, the corporation is no longer active and was dissolved in 2015, and as part
of the dissolution, the State of Michigan, Department of Treasury, conducted a tax audit
of the corporation, and
WHEREAS, the State of Michigan Department of Treasury sent a letter dated February
26, 2016 informing the corporation that the corporation owed the State of Michigan the
$24,000 that the corporation had declared on its 2014 income tax return, plus interest
and penalties in the amount of $3,338.00, for a total taxes due of $27,338.00; NOW
THEREFORE BE IT
RESOLVED, that the Board authorizes the payment of $27,338.00 to the State of
Michigan by check, and be it further
RESOLVED, that upon the issuance of the foregoing check, the Board’s staff remit the
check, together with a copy of the Department of Treasury’s February 2, 2016 letter to
the State of Michigan in payment of the corporation’s 2014 Michigan income taxes.
Yeas: Cetlinski, Cowan, Hatty, Nickleberry, Sheehan and Szymanski – 6
Nays: None

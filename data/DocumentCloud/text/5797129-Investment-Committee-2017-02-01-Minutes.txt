GENERAL COUNSEL’S REPORT (Cont’d)
RE: Robert D. Standifer v. Lytell Standifer
Wayne County Circuit Court; Case No. 11-106156-DO
Resolution Moved By: Trustee Nickleberry Supported By: Trustee Cetlinski
WHEREAS, the Board of Trustees is in receipt of a Domestic Relations Order (the
“Order”), dated January 19, 2017, wherein Lytell Standifer, the Alternate Payee, is
awarded certain rights to the benefits of Robert D. Standifer, the Participant, and
WHEREAS, Mr. Standifer retired on December 11, 2008, elected the Plan’s Option D
form of benefit, and annuitized his accumulated contributions from the Annuity Savings
Fund, and
WHEREAS, pursuant to the terms of the Order, the Alternate Payee is entitled to a
portion of the Participant’s retirement allowance payable from the Retirement System,
and
WHEREAS, the Order provides that for the first 18 months, the Alternate Payee will
receive $1,082.76 per month of the Participant’s retirement benefit payable from the
Retirement System, and after the first 18 months, the Alternate Payee will receive
$462.00 per month of the Participant’s retirement benefit payable from the Retirement
System, and
WHEREAS, said matter had been discussed with legal counsel who has opined that the
applicable terms of said court order are consistent with the provisions of the Retirement
System and applicable law, therefore be it
RESOLVED, that the Board acknowledges receipt of said court order and the Board will
pay pension benefits consistent with the order as soon as administratively feasible, and
further
RESOLVED, that a copy of this resolution be immediately attached as the top sheet of
the pension file and other appropriate records be kept for the Retirement System
relative to this matter, and further
RESOLVED, that copies of this resolution be sent to Robert D. Standifer, the
Participant; Robert S. Vanderford, Esq., attorney for the Participant; Lytell Standifer, the
Alternate Payee; and Paul S. Clark, Esq., attorney for the Alternate Payee.
Yeas: Anthony, Benson, Cetlinski, Cowan, Johnson, Naglick, Nickleberry, Oxendine and
Sheehan – 8
Nays: None
Abstentions: Johnson -1

18-0102

The Board of Water Commissioners for the City of Detroit, Water and Sewerage
Department authorizes the Director to enter into Contract # 6001423 with Jack
Doheny Companies, Inc., to provide parts and/or repair services for
Industrial Combination Loaders, Jet Rodders, and other Sewer Cleaning
Equipment for Vactor Equipment owned by the Detroit Water & Sewerage
Department (DWSD). The contract approval is requested for a period of
four-years at a cost not to exceed $1,400,000.00 and also authorizes the Director
to take such other action as may be necessary to accomplish the intent of this vote.
Sponsors: Naughton

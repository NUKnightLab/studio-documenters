to consider various documents, which have been supplied to the Board. Counsel for O’Donnell appeared
before the Board, and was informed that the Board will review the new documents and reports; thereafter
will enter a decision, and send a copy to O’Donnell’s attorney and a copy to the Judge, to complete the
record in this matter.

Executive Director reviewed with the Board the following matters.

•

Reporting to the Board that Trustee Avila will be resigning from his position as Trustee effective
June 1, 2017.

A letter from Senator John Mulroe was distributed to the Trustees with respect to Public Act 990905, which affects’ the Cost Of Living Adjustment (COLA) for certain fund members. Previous
discussion directed that absent legislative change, some interpretive guidance by sponsors of the
legislation would be welcomed. Senator Mulroe, a sponsor of the Bill, sent a letter to the Executive
Director that clarifies the legislative intent of the bill. This is in addition to another letter received
from Representative Dan Burke, which also discussed the legislative intent of the bill. After
lengthy discussion It was moved by Trustee Wright, seconded by Trustee Maloney that based upon
the letters received from Senator Mulroe and Representative Burke, indicating legislative intent of
Public Act 99-0905, the Board apply the ‘catch-up’ provision of the 3% cost of living adjustment to
those retired members who were born in 1955 and 1956, but had already received an incremental
increase in 2015 and/or 2016.

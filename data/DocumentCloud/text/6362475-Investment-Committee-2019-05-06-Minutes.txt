Fiduciary Liability Coverage

The Committee discussed the renewal of the various coverages under the Fiduciary
Liability Policies. The Committee discussed the appropriate level of liability coverage.
The Chief Investment Officer and General Counsel were requested to research the level
of coverage carried by other comparable systems and report back to the Investment
Committee. The following motion was the result of said discussion:

Motion #194006: June Nickleberry moved to revise the Fiduciary Liability coverages .
as discussed; taking away the last layer of $10 million in coverage and reviewing this
matter further after a report from staff. Thomas Sheehan supported. The motion passed
unanimously.

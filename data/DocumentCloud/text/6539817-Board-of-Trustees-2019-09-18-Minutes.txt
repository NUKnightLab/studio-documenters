Anthony Adams v. Deborah Ross Adams
Oakland County Circuit Court Case No. 10SC009-SD-DO
Resolution Moved By: Trustee Sheehan - Supported By: Trustee Hall-Wagner
WHEREAS, the Board of Trustees is in receipt of a Eligible Domestic Relations Order
(“EDRO”) dated December 9, 2011, wherein Deborah Ross Adams, the Alternate
Payee, is awarded certain rights to the retirement allowance of Anthony Adams, the
Participant, and
WHEREAS, the Retirement System consists of both a defined benefit plan and a
defined contribution (a/k/a the Annuity Savings Fund) plan, and
WHEREAS, pursuant to the EDRO, the Alternate Payee is entitled only to a portion of
the Participant’s defined benefit plan payable from the Retirement System, and

-9-

 GENERAL RETIREMENT SYSTEM
CITY OF DETROIT

MEETING NO. 4242
SEPTEMBER 18, 2019

WHEREAS, the payments from the plan to the Alternate Payee shall begin when the
Participant commences benefits from the Retirement System, or upon the Alternate
Payee’s election, but no time before the Participant’s earliest normal retirement
eligibility, and
WHEREAS, the Board’s policy is to require that the cost of the actuary’s calculations
are to be borne by the parties to the domestic relations proceedings and the parties’
EDRO provides that the parties are to equally share responsibility for any and all
additional costs for actuarial services, and
WHEREAS, said matter had been discussed with legal counsel who has opined that the
applicable terms of said court order are consistent with the provisions of the Retirement
System and applicable law including Public Act 46 of 1991 (MCLA 38.1701) as
amended, therefore be it
RESOLVED, that the Board acknowledges receipt of said court order, will pay pension
benefits consistent with said order upon application by either the Participant or the
Alternate Payee, and further
RESOLVED, that a copy of this resolution be immediately attached as the top sheet of
the pension file and other appropriate records be kept for the Retirement System
relative to this matter, and further
RESOLVED, that copies of this resolution be sent to Anthony Adams, the Participant;
Deborah Ross Adams, the Alternate Payee; and the Board’s Actuary.

Yeas: Anthony, Benson, Brown, Cowan, Hall-Wagner, Naglick, Perkins, Sheehan and
Chairperson Nickleberry– 9
Nays: None

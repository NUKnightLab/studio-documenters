STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
STATE PANEL
Minutes of the State Panel Meeting
Held on April 8, 2008
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 11:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, Suite S-400, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Jackie Gallagher, Chairman (participated by telephone-conference); Michael Coli, Member of the Board;
Charles Hernandez, Member of the Board; Michael Hade, Member of the Board; John F. Brosnan, Executive
Director; and staff members. John F. Brosnan, Sharon B. Wells, Sylvia Rios, and Philip M. Kazanjian acted
as counsel to the Board at this meeting. Rex Piper was absent due to illness. Member Hade chaired the
meeting.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD MARCH 11, 2008

Member Hade called for approval of the minutes of the March 11, 2008 meeting. Member Coli moved to
approve the minutes. Member Hernandez seconded. The Board passed the motion—three in favor, none
against—Chairman Gallagher abstained.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Member Hade asked Executive Director Brosnan to present the State Panel administrative actions for the
period of time from March 1 through March 31, 2008. He distributed the report to the Board and
summarized its highlights. The Board examined the report and discussed it.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS

1.

Case No. S-CA-06-255

VI.

OTHER BOARD MATTERS

Member Hade noted that there was no fiscal report and the only legislative matter of interest was the
agency's April 17 Senate budget hearing, which was routine. Member Coli made a motion to leave open
session. Member Hade seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Member Coli made a motion to go into executive session to discuss personnel matters. Chairman Gallagher
seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote. Subsequently, at the end of the
discussion, Member Coli made a motion to leave executive session. Member Hernandez seconded the
motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote. Member Coli then made a motion to return to
open session. Member Hernandez seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice
vote.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Member Hade called for a motion to adjourn. Member Coli made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the
Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the State Panel of the Illinois Labor
Relations Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 13th day of May, 2008.

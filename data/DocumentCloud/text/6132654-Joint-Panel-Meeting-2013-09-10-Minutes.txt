ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
JOINT PANEL
Minutes of the Joint Panel Meeting
Held on September 10, 2013

II.

PRESENT

John Hartnett, Joint Panel Chairman; Paul S. Besson, Member of the State Panel; James Q. Brennwald,
Member of the State Panel; Michael Coli, Member of the State Panel; Albert Washington, Member of the State
Panel; Robert M. Gierut, Local Panel Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Local Panel; Richard
Lewis, Member of the Local Panel; Melissa Mlynski, Executive Director; Jerald S. Post, General Counsel; and
staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES

Chairman Hartnett called for approval of the minutes of the July 9, 2013 meeting. Member Washington
moved to approve the minutes. Local Chairman Gierut seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously,
by a voice vote.
IV.

LEGISLATIVE/FISCAL/MEDIATION REPORTS

Executive Director Mlynski presented the legislative report updating the Board as to the status of legislation
that could affect the agency. Executive Director Mlynski then presented the mediation report. Fiscal Officer
Hildebrand presented the fiscal report. The Chair and Members discussed the fiscal report at length.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
NONE

VI.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

Member Besson made a motion to leave open session. Member Brennwald seconded the motion, and the
Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote. Member Besson made a motion to go into executive session to
discuss personnel matters pursuant to 5 ILCS 120/2(c)(1). Local Chairman Gierut seconded the motion, and
the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote. Subsequently, at the end of the discussion, Member Coli
made a motion to leave executive session. Member Besson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it
unanimously, by a voice vote. Member Anderson then made a motion to return to open session. Member
Washington seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Hartnett called for a motion to adjourn. Member Coli made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and
the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Joint Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations
Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 5th day of November, 2013.

Report of Monthly Meeting No. 2012-07 held on Thursday, July 19, 2012
.
President Durkan called the meeting to order. After greeting the Board and staff, President Durkan
introduced Anthony Clancy, newly appointed by the elected trustees as trustee of the Municipal Employees’
Annuity and Benefit Fund of Chicago. Mr. Clancy has worked for the Board of Education since 2005. He
assumes the unexpired portion of the term vacated by John K. Gibson after his retirement on June 13,
2012. His position as trustee is up for election in September.
PUBLIC COMMENT
There were no requests to address the Board.

HEARINGS
The Matter of Ronald Youngblood (Annuity Overpayment)
Mary Patricia Burns, attorney for the Fund, gave an overview of the matter related to the overpayment of
benefits to both Ronald Youngblood and, upon his death, his widow, Rose Youngblood.
Ms. Burns discussed the terms of the settlement the Board had reached with Mrs. Youngblood as a result
of the overpayment.
On the advice of counsel, Mr. Guest made a motion to approve the Agreed Order and Stipulation regarding
the settlement of the matter. Second by Mr. Ahmad.
Carried: Ayes – Messrs. Ahmad, Clancy, Durkan, Guest, Ms. Neely. Nays – None.

LEGISLATION
Legislative Update
Ms. Schanding gave an update on legislation that may affect the Fund. No actions taken.

INVESTMENTS
Watchlist
This matter was taken out of order. Mr. Walsh reviewed the Watchlist. No actions taken.
Flash Report
Mr. Wesner reviewed the current portfolio and specifically discussed issues pertaining to certain current
managers. Based on this discussion, Ms. Neely made a motion to place RCP on watch due to
organizational changes. Second by Mr. Ahmad.
Carried: Ayes – Messrs. Ahmad, Clancy, Durkan, Guest, Ms. Neely. Nays – None.
Ms. Neely made a motion to place UBS on watch due to a change in their management teams. Second by
Mr. Ahmad.
Carried: Ayes – Messrs. Ahmad, Clancy, Durkan, Guest, Ms. Neely. Nays – None.

ROSELAND/MICHIGAN TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 9)
Request authority to approve the sale of the property located at the southwest and
southeast corners of 115th Street and Michigan Avenue in the Roseland/Michigan
Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area to Crown Commercial
Real Estate & Development, Inc., the designated successful respondent to the RFP
issued by the Department of Planning and Development on August 18, 2004 for
the purchase and redevelopment of the property, and to recommend to the City
Council of the City of Chicago the designation of Crown Commercial Real Estate
& Development, Inc. as Developer.
Project Manager: Karen J. Forté
Motioned by Martin, seconded by Sahli. Approved 5-0. Yeas: Hassan, Kostiner,
Martin, Sahli, and Lowry. Leon recused. Bland and Stein absent.

STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
STATE PANEL
Minutes of the State Panel Meeting
Held on April 9, 2019
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 11:00 A.M.— by video conference at 160 N. LaSalle Street, 13th Floor,
Chicago, IL and 801 S. 7th Street, Room 5A, Springfield, IL

II.

PRESENT

Kendra Cunningham, Member of the Board; Kimberly Stevens, Executive Director; and staff members participated
via video conference from the Board’s Springfield office. John Cronin, Member of the Board; Jose Gudino, Member
of the Board; William Lowry, Member of the Board; Helen Kim, General Counsel; Anna Hamburg-Gal, Associate
General Counsel; and staff members participated via video conference from the Chicago location. At the outset of the
meeting, Member Lowry nominated Member Gudino to chair the meeting. Member Cronin seconded. The Board
passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD March 12, 2019

Acting Chairman Gudino called for approval of the minutes of the March 12, 2019 meeting. Member Lowry moved
to approve the minutes. Member Cronin seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Acting Chairman Gudino asked Executive Director Stevens to present the reports. She presented the reports and
summarized their highlights.
V.

REVIEW OF PROPOSED WRITTEN DECISIONS AND ORDERS
1.

Case No. S-CA-19-020
The Heath Care, Professional, Technical, Office, Warehouse, and Mail Order Employees
Union, Local 743, Int’l Brotherhood of Teamsters and Village of Riverdale

General Counsel Kim called the case. Member Lowry made a motion to approve the proposed written decision.
Member Cronin seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
2.

Case No. S-CB-19-009
Brandon Santiago and Villa Park Professional Firefighters Association, Int’l Assoc. of Fire
Fighters, Local 2392

General Counsel Kim called the case. Member Lowry made a motion to approve the proposed written decision.
Member Cronin seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VI.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.

Case No. S-CA-19-034
Harold B. Thompson and State of Illinois, Department of Central Management Services
(Employment Security)

General Counsel Kim called and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member Lowry moved
to affirm the Executive Director dismissal. Member Cronin seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously,
by a voice vote.

 2.

Case No. S-CB-19-010
Harold B. Thompson and American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees,
Council 31

General Counsel Kim called and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member Lowry moved
to affirm the Executive Director dismissal. Member Cronin seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously,
by a voice vote.
VII.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded it was
unnecessary.
VIII.

PUBLIC COMMENT

No members of the public sought to make comments.
IX.

ADJOURNMENT

Acting Chairman Gudino called for a motion to adjourn. Member Lowry moved to adjourn the meeting, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the State Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board,
in Chicago, Illinois, this 7th day of May 2019.

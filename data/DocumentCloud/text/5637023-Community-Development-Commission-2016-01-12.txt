 III.

NEW BUSINESS
A.

HUMBOLDT PARK COMMERCIAL TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT
AREA (WARD 26)
Request authority for the Department of Planning and Development to negotiate a
redevelopment agreement with La Casa Norte for redevelopment of the property
located at 3527-31 W. North Avenue in the Humboldt Park Commercial Tax
Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and to recommend to the City
Council of the City of Chicago the designation of La Casa Norte as Developer.
Presenter: Dinah Wayne
Motioned by Newsome, seconded by Cepeda. Approved 7-0. Yeas: Buford,
Cepeda, Moreno, Newsome, Perez, Reifman and Carrott.
16-CDC-1

B.

MONTROSE/CLARENDON TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 46)
Request authority for the Department of Planning and Development to negotiate a
redevelopment agreement with Montrose Clarendon Partners, LLC for
redevelopment of the property located at 4400-4424 and 4401-4415 N. Clarendon
Avenue in the Montrose/Clarendon Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment
Project Area, and to recommend to the City Council of the City of Chicago the
designation of Montrose Clarendon Partners, LLC as Developer.
Presenter: Mary Bonome
Motioned by Perez, seconded by Buford. Approved 7-0. Yeas: Buford, Cepeda,
Moreno, Newsome, Perez, Reifman and Carrott.
16-CDC-2

C.

CENTRAL WEST TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 27)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Planning and Development’s
intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Rush University Medical Center for
the disposition of the property generally located at 301-339 S. Damen Avenue in
the Central West Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, to
request alternative proposals, and to approve the sale of the property to Rush
University Medical Center if no responsive alternative proposals are received.
Presenter: Mary Bonome
Motioned by Newsome, seconded by Cepeda. Approved 7-0. Yeas: Buford,
Cepeda, Moreno, Newsome, Perez, Reifman and Carrott.
16-CDC-3

 D.

CENTRAL WEST REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 27)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Planning and Development’s
intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Blackhawk Community Ice Rink,
LLC for the disposition of the property generally located at 1801 W. Jackson
Boulevard in the Central West Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project
Area, to request alternative proposals, and to approve the sale of the property to
Blackhawk Community Ice Rink, LLC if no responsive alternative proposals are
received.

Motioned by Moreno, seconded by Perez. Approved 6-0. Yeas: Buford, Cepeda,
Moreno, Newsome, Perez, Reifman and Carrott. Cepeda not present.

29.
Submitting report relative to Economic Hardship Exemption in Special Assessment
Districts. (Your Honorable Body, through the Budget, Finance, and Audit
Standing Committee, has forwarded a memorandum from the Legislative
Policy Division (LPD) dated June 5, 2019, regarding the above-referenced
topic with a proposed ordinance attached for Corporation Counsel’s review
and approval as to form. Taxes and special assessments are distinct under the
law. While it is clear that special assessments on the basic of benefits received
are not general property taxes, they are collected in the same manner as
property taxes. There are no instances of relief from special assessments
absent specific statutory exemptions (such as a Business Improvement Zone
pursuant to MCL 125.990h).

MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING - RIVERBOAT
OPEN SESSION
April 18, 2013
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
On Wednesday, April 18, 2013 a Regular Meeting - Riverboat of the Illinois Gaming Board
(“Board”) was held on the 5th floor at 160 N. LaSalle at the Michael J. Bilandic Building,
Chicago, Illinois at 10:20 A.M. Present were the following: Members Maribeth Vander Weele,
Michael Holewinski and Chairman Aaron Jaffe.
APPROVAL OF MINUTES
Member Holewinski moved that the Board approve the open session minutes of its
Regular Meeting of March 20, 2013. Member Vander Weele seconded the motion. The
Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
Dissemination of Closed Session Minutes: July 2011-December 2012
Member Vander Weele moved that the Board approved the Dissemination of the
Closed Session Minutes of Meetings from July 2011 through December 2012. The
Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
ADMINISTRATOR’S REPORT –
Administrator Ostrowski referenced the General Manager’s meeting that was held in the Fall of
2012 and the follow up regarding issues discussed.
OWNER LICENSEE ITEMS
•

Par-A-Dice Gaming Corporation d/b/a Par-A-Dice Hotel & Casino – Kevin
R. Kraft, Director of Operations - Level One
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Holewinski moved that the Board
approve Kevin R. Kraft as an Occupational Licensee Level One in his
position as Director of Operations of Par-A-Dice Gaming Corporation d/b/a
Par-A-Dice Hotel & Casino. Member Vander Weele seconded the motion. The
Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.

•

Elgin Riverboat Resort d/b/a Grand Victoria Casino – David E. Landgraf,
Internal Auditor – Level One

 Based on staff’s investigation, Member Vander Weele moved that the Board
approve David E. Landgraf as an Occupational Licensee Level One in his
position as Internal Auditor of Elgin Riverboat Resort d/b/a Grand Victoria
Casino. Member Holewinski seconded the motion. The Board approved the
motion unanimously by roll call vote.
•

The Alton Gaming Company d/b/a Argosy Casino Alton – Gregory S.
Saunders, General Manager & Vice President – Level One
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Holewinski moved that the Board
approve Gregory S. Saunders as an Occupational Licensee Level One in his
position as General Manager and Vice President of The Alton Gaming
Company d/b/a Argosy Casino Alton. Member Vander Weele seconded the
motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.

OCCUPATIONAL LICENSE APPROVAL AND DENIALS –
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Vander Weele moved that the Board find
suitable and approve 22 applications for Level 2 Occupational Licenses and 105
applications for Level 3 Occupational Licenses and that the Administrator issue
Notices of Denial to Alvaro Escobar. Member Holewinski seconded the motion. The
Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
At 10:25 A.M. Member Holewinski motioned to adjourn and Member Vander Weele seconded
the motion. All Members voted in favor of adjournment.
Respectfully submitted,
Mary C. Boruta
Secretary to the Administrator

ROLL CALL
The Clerk of the Board called the roll. All four Board Members answered present.

ANNOUNCEMENTS
The Chair, Barbara Flynn Currie, welcomed members of the public and staff.

PUBLIC REMARKS
None.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES
The minutes of the Board’s October 3, 2019 regular meeting were approved by a vote of 4-0.

 RULEMAKINGS
R18-32

Amendments to General Use Water Quality Standards for
Chloride (Water) – The Board granted the rulemaking
proponent’s motion to dismiss.

4-0

ADJUSTED STANDARDS
ADMINISTRATIVE CITATIONS
ADJUDICATORY CASES
PCB 18-83

John D. Warsaw v. IEPA (UST Appeal) – The Board affirmed
the Illinois Environmental Protection Agency’s June 12, 2018
determination to deny Fund reimbursement.

4-0

PCB 20-18

Landfill 33 v. IEPA (Land – Permit Appeal) – The Board
accepted for hearing this permit appeal involving a site in
Effingham County.

4-0

PCB 20-19

Joe Stead v. IEPA (Water – Tax Certification) – The Board
found and certified that specified facilities of Joe Stead located
in Mercer County are pollution control facilities for preferential
tax treatment under the Property Tax Code.

4-0

PCB 20-20

Dixie Travel Plaza v. IEPA (UST Appeal) – The Board
accepted for hearing this underground storage tank appeal
involving a site in McLean County.

4-0

OTHER ITEMS

The Chair noted the pending retirement of Nancy Hoepfner, the
Board’s Rulemaking Coordinator. The Chair, on behalf of the Board,
presented a proclamation honoring, congratulating, and thanking
Nancy Hoepfner for 18 years of service to the Board and the people
of the State of Illinois. The Board adopted the proclamation by a
vote of 4-0.

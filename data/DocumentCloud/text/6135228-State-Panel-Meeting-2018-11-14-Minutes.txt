STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
STATE PANEL
Minutes of the State Panel Meeting
Held on November 14, 2018
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 11:00 A.M.— by video conference at 160 N. LaSalle Street, Room S-1316,
Chicago, IL and EPA, 1021 North Grand Ave East, 2nd Floor, Springfield, IL

II.

PRESENT

John Hartnett, Chairman; Keith Snyder, Member of the Board; Kimberly Stevens, Executive Director; Helen Kim,
General Counsel; and staff members participated via video conference from the Board’s Springfield office. John
Samolis, Member of the Board; Christopher Glynn, Member of the Board; Anna Hamburg-Gal, Associate General
Counsel; and staff members participated via video conference from the Chicago location.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD October 16, 2018

Chairman Hartnett called for approval of the minutes of the October 16, 2018 meeting. Member Samolis moved to
approve the minutes. Member Glynn seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

REVIEW OF PROPOSED WRITTEN DECISIONS AND ORDERS
1. Case No. S-RC-17-003
Int’l Association of Firefighters, Local 2391, AFL-CIO, CLC and Village of River Forest

General Counsel Kim called the case. Member Samolis made a motion to approve the proposed written decision.
Member Snyder seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
2. Case No. S-UC-17-067
State of Illinois, Department of Central Management Services and American Federation of State,
County and Municipal Employees, Council 31
General Counsel Kim called the case. Member Samolis made a motion to approve the proposed written decision.
Member Samolis seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.

Case No. S-CA-15-028
Nassor Zarif and Chief Judge of the Circuit Court of Cook County (Cook County Juvenile
Temporary Detention Center)

General Counsel Kim called the case and noted that no party filed exceptions to the Administrative Law Judge’s
Recommended Decision and Order. The Board declined to review the decision on its own motion.
2. Case No. S-CA-18-056
American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31 and Chief Judge of
the Circuit Court of Cook County
General Counsel Kim called the case and noted that no party filed exceptions to the Administrative Law Judge’s
Recommended Decision and Order. The Board declined to review the decision on its own motion.

 3. Case No. S-RC-18-026
Village of Mundelein and Int’l Association of Firefighters, Local 4786, Mundelein Professional
Fire Fighters
General Counsel Kim called the case and noted that no party filed exceptions to the Administrative Law Judge’s
Recommended Decision and Order. The Board declined to review the decision on its own motion.
4. Case No. S-UC-17-083
State of Illinois, Department of Central Management Services and American Federation of State,
County and Municipal Employees, Council 31
General Counsel Kim called the case to the Board noting that the Union filed a Motion to Stay. After discussion,
Member Snyder moved to deny the Motion. Member Samolis seconded the motion, and the Board passed it
unanimously, by a voice vote.
5. Case No. S-CA-18-085
Policemen’s Benevolent Labor Committee and City of Sparta
General Counsel Kim called the case to the Board noting that the Respondent filed a Motion for Sanctions. After
discussion, Member Snyder moved to deny the Motion. Member Samolis seconded the motion, and the Board passed
it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VI.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded it was
unnecessary.
VII.

PUBLIC COMMENT

No members of the public sought to make comments.
VIII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Hartnett called for a motion to adjourn. Member Samolis moved to adjourn the meeting, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the State Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board,
in Chicago, Illinois, this 11th day of December 2018.

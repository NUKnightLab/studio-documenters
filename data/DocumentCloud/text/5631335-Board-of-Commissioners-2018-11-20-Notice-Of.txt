The Business Session and Public Hearing portion of the Board Meeting will commence immediately following Committee
Meetings, but not before the specified time in the Notice.
Commissioners will then vote on whether to go into closed meeting pursuant to the Open Meetings Act, 15 ILCS 120/2 to

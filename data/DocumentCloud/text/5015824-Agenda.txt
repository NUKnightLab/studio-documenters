3.

Communication dated July 24, 2018 (received July 30th) requesting Commission
approval of a transition agreement between the Charter County of Wayne and
AFSCME Council 25, Local 1659, AFL-CIO to ensure that employees at the
Wyandotte Wastewater Treatment Facility (WWTF) are able to exercise their
preference in making a career choice between electing to accept employment
with the future operator of the facility, Veolia, or continue employment with the
County; the County Executive advises that the Local 1659 employees at the
facility may instead elect to continue employment with the County and exercise
rights and opportunities within the County in accordance with the collective
bargaining agreement; the term of the agreement will commence upon the
County entering into an agreement for the sale of assets related to the Downriver
Sewage Disposal System and the WWTF (the System) to the Downriver Utility
Wastewater Authority (DUWA), and the contracting out of the operation of the
System by DUWA to a third-party management company. (2018-43-022)

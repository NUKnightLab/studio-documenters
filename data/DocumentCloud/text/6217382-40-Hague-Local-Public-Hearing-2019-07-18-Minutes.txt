Mr. Vosburg called the meeting to order at 5:35 PM.
Mr. Vosburg and Ms. Capler informed the hearing of the tax increment financing request per the Brownfield
Plan, provided an overview of the project, and answered questions regarding the activities to be reimbursed
through tax increment financing.
Mr. Check and Ms. Masserang provided details regarding the redevelopment plan for the project location
and answered questions regarding parking, anticipated rental rates, environmental cleanup, affordable
housing, the plans for employment of Detroit residents and contractors, and engagement with community
groups including Vanguard CDC and other block clubs.
Ms. Smith suggested the Developer contact Jenkins Construction regarding the project.
Paper copies of the Brownfield Plan and renderings for the project were provided.
Mr. Claxton spoke in favor of the project stating that the project was a good thing and will be a high quality
development, but expressed concern over visitor parking and access to the park by neighbors.
Ms. Judkins expressed support for the project and development in the area.
Ms. Davis expressed support for the project and development and encouraged the Developer to work with
community groups to provide more information on the project and assist with more development in the
neighborhood.
There were no comments received in opposition of the project.

CHICAGO TRANSIT AUTHORITY
ADA ADVISORY COMMITTEE
NOTICE OF REGULAR COMMITTEE MEETING

March 14, 2016

A Regular Meeting of the Chicago Transit Authority’s ADA Advisory Committee
will be held on Monday, April 10, 2017, at 1:30 o’clock in the afternoon, at the offices
of the Chicago Transit Authority, 567 West Lake Street, Second Floor, Boardroom,
Chicago, Illinois, 60661 for the purpose of considering:
1. ADA Compliance
2. Customer Service
and to transact such other business that may properly come before the Committee at that
time.
Prior to the Meeting, from 1:00 o’clock to 1:30 o’clock in the afternoon, CTA’s
Manager of ADA Compliance Programs, Amy J. Serpe, and a representative from CTA’s
Customer Service Department will be available in the Boardroom to take individual
service complaints/comments from the public.

GREGORY P. LONGHINI

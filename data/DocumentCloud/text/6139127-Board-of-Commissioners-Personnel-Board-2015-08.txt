PUBLIC NOTICE
IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE PROVISION OF AN ACT OF THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY, OF
THE STATE OF ILLINOIS ENTITLED “AN ACT IN RELATION TO MEETING”, PUBLIC NOTICE
IS HEREBY GIVEN THAT THE PERSONNEL BOARD OF THE CHICAGO PARK DISTRICT WILL
CONVENE A REGULAR MEETING ON TUESDAY, AUGUST 18, 2015 AT 2:00 PM AT 541 N.
FAIRBANKS CT. IN THE LOWER LEVEL ROOM IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE PROVISIONS
OF THE ILLINOIS OPEN MEETING ACT, THOSE PORTIONS OF THIS MEETING DEALING
WITH INFORMATION REGARDING THE APPOINTMENT, OR DISMISSAL OF OFFICERS OF
EMPLOYEES WILL BE CLOSED TO THE PUBLIC DATED AT CHICAGO, ILLINOIS ON THE 18
DAY OF AUGUST , 2015 BY THE ORDER OF THE PERSONNEL BOARD OF THE CHICAGO
PARK DISTRICT.

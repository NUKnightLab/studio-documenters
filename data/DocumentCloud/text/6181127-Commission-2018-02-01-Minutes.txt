Marketing / Beautification / Safety / Other:
• Street Cleaning: Request to have trash cans cleaned and power washed. $40 per
can, estimated max job cost is $4,000.
*Chris Hunt motioned to approve the $4,000 to clean the SSA trash cans. Steven Tobiason
seconded this motion and it was approved unanimously
• Banners & Holiday Decorations: Holiday decorations have been removed. One
Chicago banners coming down, working on replacing SSA banners with CEG.
• Landscaping: Tree Trimming scheduled for February 22 & 23rd.
• Discover West Town Mobile App: Glitch in the auto upload finally fixed.
Finalizing map glitch updates.
• Master Plan: Meeting with CDOT went well. Discussed how Chicago Ave was a
high crash corridor in their Vision Zero plan and would like to do something to
improve the safety of the street. CTA is conducting a study of the #66 bus that will
be released soon. CDOT will talk to Alderman for approval to study and potentially
update the street. After discussion with Alderman, and CTA study is released –
CDOT will move forward with studying potential street updates and we will
partner with them to start the community outreach portion of the project. Hopefully
street updates will happen in the beginning of 2019.
• People Spot: Discussed putting the project on hold due to needing a stripped
parking lane which doesn’t make since if the street will be updated very shortly.
Going to postpone project but keep planning for a potential release in 2019 to
happen after Chicago avenue updates are finished through CDOT.
*Adam Williams motioned to approve the postponement of the People Spot project. Sara
Dulkin seconded this motion and it was approved unanimously.
• Bike Racks: Discussed CDOT partnership options. Commissioners want to see
the racks available and prices through partnership with CDOT.
• Bike Friendly Business District: Program will re-start in the Spring of 2018
working with new hire Sara Bemer on program.
• Security: Discussed adding anti-smash glass as a eligible cost to cover with the
Security Rebate Grant. Will add to application and submit for approval at March
SSA Commission meeting.

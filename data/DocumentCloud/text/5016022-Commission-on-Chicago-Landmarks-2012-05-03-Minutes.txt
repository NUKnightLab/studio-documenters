MINUTES OF THE MEETING

COMMISSION ON CHICAGO LANDMARKS

MAY 3, 2012

The Commission on Chicago Landmarks held a regular meeting on May 3, 2012. The meeting was
held at City Hall, 121 N. LaSalle St., City Hall Room 201-A, Chicago, Illinois. The meeting began at
12:45 p.m.

Approval of the Minutes of the April 5. 2012, Regular Meeting
Motioned by Wong, seconded by Reed. Approved unanimously. (7-0)

Commission member Tony Hu arrives.
2.

Report from Public Hearing on Final Landmark Recommendation to City Council
CONTINENTAL CENTER
55 E. Jackson Boulevard

WARD 2

Public Hearing Held: April 17, 2012
Location:
Historic Preservation Office, 33 N. LaSalle St., Room 1600
Hearing Officer:
Anita Blanchard
Anita Blanchard presented the hearing officer’s report from the public hearing. Resolution to
adopt the Final Landmark Recommendation to City Council that the CONTINENTAL
CENTER be designated as a Chicago Landmark.
Motioned by Reed, seconded by Baird. Approved unanimously. (8-0)

 3. 

Final Report on Approved Dismantlement, Demolition, Repair and Reconstruction Plan
FARWELL BUILDING
664 N. Michigan Av.

WARD 42

Dijana Cuvalo presented an informational report to the Commission on the completion of the
project. Ms. Cuvalo reported that the reinstallation of the historic elements on the new structure
is now complete and a final reassembly report has been prepared by the owner. This report is
the third and final report to update the Commission on the status of the Farwell Project.
4. 

Other Business
Report on Permit Decisions for the Month of April 2012
Dijana Cuvalo presented the report of the staff to the Commission (see attached).

5. 

Announcement
Program Committee Meeting for Suggestions Received from the Public for Possible
Chicago Landmark Designation
ANNOUNCED: The Program Committee will meet on Thursday, June 7, prior to the regular
Commission meeting in the Historic Preservation Conference Room, 33 N. LaSalle St.,
Room 1600, to hear suggestions from the public for possible Chicago Landmark designations.
The meeting time will be posted as part of the meeting notice. Please note that suggestions
must be submitted to the Commission on the suggestion form by Tuesday, May 15th, to be
considered at the meeting. The form is available online and from the Historic Preservation
office.

6. 

Adjournment
There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned at 1:13 p.m.
Motioned by Wong, seconded by Reed. Approved unanimously. (8-0)

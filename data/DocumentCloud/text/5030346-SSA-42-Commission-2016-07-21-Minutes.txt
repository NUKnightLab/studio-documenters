Commissioner Jackson called the meeting to order at 10:21am.
The agenda was accepted with no change.
The minutes for July were accepted with no changes.
Ava St. Claire of The Moonrose Agency presented the New Banners
Campaign to the commissioners. She designed 3 campaigns, “WESOULFULLY-ACTIVE”. Commissioner Jackson recommended we
include diversity in the banners. Ava agreed to send over new images.
Commissioner Jackson moved to approve Bannerville to create the
banners if they agreed to reduce the price below $5,000. The motion
was approved.
Hank Zemola provided a list of talents for the August 21st event. He’s
holding a slot in the lineup for 106.3 identified talent. Sponsorship
dollars pledged are approximately $12,000. Entertainment should be
final the week of July 25th. CSEM is waiting to confirm the surprise
guest. Allpoints Security will provide all security detail for the event.
They will place emphasis on the drop off areas to avoid bottleneck
traffic jams, checking bags for glass and weapons. Commissioner
Karim suggested a walkthrough at the venue with Allpoints and CSEM.
Commissioner Jackson recommended dialogue with 003rd district
commander to discuss the loitering and violence at 71st & Jeffery and

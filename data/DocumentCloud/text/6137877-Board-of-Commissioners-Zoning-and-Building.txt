DOCKET #8445 – R. ENCISO, Owner, Application (No. V-08-63): Variation to
increase height of fence in front yard from 3 feet to 4 feet; increase pole height
in side yard from 6 feet to 12 feet 6 inches for rolling gate in the R-5 single
Family Residence District. The subject property consists of approximately 0.19
of an acre, located on the southwest corner of Grand Avenue and Roberta
Avenue Leyden Township, County Board District #16. Recommendation:
That the increase in height of fence in front yard from 3 feet to 4 feet be
granted by a vote of 5-0. That the increase in pole height in side yard from
6 feet to 12 feet 6 inches for a rolling gate for a commercial truck be
denied by a vote of 3-2.
Conditions:

None

Objectors:

Several neighbors appeared at the hearing and objected to the
commercial rolling gate in the single family residence district
(applicant built gate without permit).

Commissioner Goslin, seconded by Vice Chairman Murphy moved approval of
Communication Nos. 267262, 289034 and 296212. The motion carried unanimously.

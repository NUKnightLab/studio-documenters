RESOLUTION REGARDING THE MEETING OF THE CONTRACT

(PROPOSED RESOLUTION). Submitting a Proposed Resolution sponsored by
Roberto Maldonado, County Commissioner.

PROPOSED RESOLUTION

WHEREAS, in 2000, the US. Court of Appeals for the Seventh Circuit struck
down the County?s Minority and Women?s Business Enterprise (MBENVBE)

Ordinance as applied to construction contracts because it was unconstitutional;
and

WHEREAS, in the absence of this ordinance, minority (African American,
Hispanic, and Asian) and women owned firms each received only 1 percent of

Cook County construction contracts compared to 44 percent prior to the ruling;
and

WHEREAS, in 2006, the Board of Commissioners implemented an "Interim
Construction Contract Program Ordinance? to ensure fair

representation for minority contractors who have suffered discrimination in
County construction projects; and

WHEREAS, since adoption of the Interim Ordinance, attorney Colette Holt, a
nationally renowned expert who specializes in the field of minority contract
participation and who drafted the County?s Interim Ordinance, has been working

on a Disparity Study that substantiates the need for the Interim Ordinance; and

WHEREAS, the Interim Ordinance sunsets on December 31, 2008 and if the
Board intends to re-authorize it, a Disparity Study must be completed.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that the Contract Compliance
Committee of the Cook County Board will convene for the purpose of holding an
informational meeting to hear a presentation from Colette Holt on the status of
the Disparity Study prior to the expiration of the Interim Ordinance.

Chairman Maldonado introduced Colette Holt, Attorney at Law and MBENVBE Consultant. Ms.
Holt presented an overview of the Cook County Availability Study currently underway and stated
that after the original construction piece of the Cook County MBENVBE Ordinance was struck
down as unconstitutional, MBENVBE participation dropped from 40% to Citing numerous
difficulties in obtaining accurate data from the County due to its over-reliance on an outdated
paper system, Ms. Holt stated she will not be able to complete the Availability Study by the
December 31, 2008 sunset date of the Interim MBENVBE Construction Contract Program
Ordinance and requested an extension through June 30, 2009. Chairman Maldonado stated he
would amend the Interim Ordinance to sunset on the requested date.

Commissioner Sims, seconded by Commissioner Peraica, moved the approval of
Communication Number 296495. The motion carried.

Commissioner Sims moved to adjourn the meeting, Seconded by Commissioner Peraica.
The motion carried and the meeting was adjourned.

CHAIR'S COMMENT
Chairman Einheuser began the evening by expressing, on behalf of the entire DWSD
family, our sincerest condolences to the family of Tyrone Williams who so tragically
died on September 12. Both DWSD and the Detroit Fire Department take very
seriously our shared responsibility to ensure that fire hydrants are operational an
accessible across the city.
The Chair added, "It’s pointless to engage in speculation tonight about the underlying
facts that led to this very tragic event. But we want to assure the people of Detroit of
two things: First, we are seriously committed to a full and complete investigation of all
the facts and circumstances that led to this tragic event. Second, our investigation will
be transparent and the results will be made public".

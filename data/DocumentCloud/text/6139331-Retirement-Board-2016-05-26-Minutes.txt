• The Director requested approval and legal review by the Board’s attorney, of a ‘letter of
understanding’ between the Fund and Morneau Shepell, which is the administrator of enrollment and
healthcare changes for the retiree healthcare plans sponsored by the City of Chicago. Currently the
Fund’s staff provides, on a weekly basis, to the Benefit’s Management Office of the City of Chicago
(BMO) various documents relevant to retired members’ health insurance applications, copies of
Medicare cards, birth certificates, death certificates, etc. The City then forwards these documents to
Morneau Shepell, for enrollment changes. The Director wishes to provide the required
documentation identified directly to Morneau Shepell, in order to assist in timely processing of
enrollments and changes to coverage. BMO would continue to receive the same documents. The
Board agreed to the proposed change in procedures and proposed agreement.

 III.

OLD BUSINESS
A.

LITTLE VILLAGE INDUSTRIAL TIF REDEVELOPMENT AREA
(WARD 22)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Planning and Development’s
intention to modify the terms of a negotiated sale with Chicago Southwest
Development Corporation, which resolution was approved by the commission on
January 15, 2013, for the disposition of the property located at 3201-3345 W. 31st
Street and 3100 S. Kedzie Avenue in the Little Village Industrial Tax Increment
Financing Redevelopment Project Area, to request alternative proposals, and to
approve the modification to the terms of the sale if no responsive alternative
proposals are received.
Project Manager: Mary Bonome
Motioned by Duque, seconded by Logan. Approved 11-0. Yeas: Buford, Cepeda,
Davis, Duque, Logan, Mooney, Newsome, Sercye, Stein, Ward and Perez.

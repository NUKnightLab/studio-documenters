Re: Certification of Trustee Election Results
Resolution Moved By: Trustee Benson – Supported By: Trustee Cetlinski
WHEREAS, the Board of Trustees of the General Retirement System of the City of
Detroit is vested with the authority and fiduciary responsibility for the administration,
management, and operation of the Retirement System, and
WHEREAS, the provisions of the plan are subject to the applicable provisions of the
Internal Revenue Code, applicable state law, the applicable charter and ordinance
provisions of the City of Detroit, the Combined Plan for the General Retirement System,
and applicable collective bargaining agreements, and
WHEREAS, the Board of Trustees pursuant to the Plan provisions and the Board’s
Election Procedures began the election process for an open Trustee position with the
Notices being posted requesting all nominating petitions for candidates tobe submitted
by March 11, 2016 at 4:00 p.m., and
WHEREAS, as of the Board’s March 16, 2016 meeting, only incumbent Trustee June
Nickleberry has submitted a nominating petition, and
WHEREAS, there being no other candidates so nominated for the position of Trustee;
THEREFORE BE IT
RESOLVED, that the Board hereby acknowledges that the deadline for nominating
petitions for candidates has closed; and be it further
RESOLVED, that the Board declares June Nickleberry the winning candidate for the
open position of Trustee; and be it further
RESOLVED, that a copy be maintained in the Retirement System records.
Yeas:

Anthony, Benson, Cetlinski, Cowan, Hatty, Higgs, Johnson, Nickleberry,
Sheehan and Szymanski – 10

Nays: None

Regular Meeting, Open Session Minutes - Video
Illinois Gaming Board
Chicago, Illinois
February 9, 2018
The Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”) convened for a Regular Meeting – Video – on February 8,
2018. The following Board members were present in the Illinois Gaming Board conference
room: Chairman Don Tracy, Members Steve Dolins, Hector Alejandre, Dee Robinson and
Michael Hansen.
The following Staff members were also in attendance: Administrator Mark Ostrowski, General
Counsel Agostino Lorenzini, Deputy General Counsel James Pellum, Deputy General Counsel
Sheera Hobfoll, Deputy Administrator-Investigations Scott Deubel, Deputy Administrator-Audit
and Financial Analysis Jim Lopinski, Finance Manager Tammy Compton, License Coordinator
Robert Burke, Office Manager and Acting Secretary to the Board Lucy Bautista, Senior Project
Manager Mark Stevens, and Deputy Administrator-Enforcement Frank Spizzirri. Legislative
Liaison Caleb Melamed attended via telephone.
At 11:44 AM, the meeting was called to order. Member Dolins moved that the Board retire to
closed session pursuant to Section 2(c), paragraphs (1), (11), (14), (15) and (21) of the Open
Meetings Act and Section 6(d) of the Riverboat Gambling Act to discuss the items listed on the
closed session agenda relating to personnel, staff recommendations, closed session minutes,
investigations concerning applicants and licensees, and probable or imminent disciplinary
complaints and litigation. Member Robinson seconded the motion. The Board approved the
motion unanimously by voice vote.
At 2:22 PM, the Board moved to adjourn the video closed session meeting. Member moved to
recess to closed session the next day. Member Alejandre seconded the motion. The Board
approved the motion unanimously by voice vote.
On February 9, 2018, at 8:45 AM, the closed session was called back into order. At 9:10 AM,
Member Dolins moved to retire into close session to discuss pending litigation. Member Hansen
seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by voice vote.
At 9:10 AM the Board moved to adjourn the video meeting. Member Dolins moved to recess to
open session that morning, Friday February 9, 2018 after the Riverboat meeting. Member
Robinson seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by voice vote.
On Friday, February 9, 2018, the Board reconvened the Regular Meeting – Video – of the
Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”) on the 5th floor of the Michael Bilandic Building in Chicago,
Illinois immediately following its Regular Meeting - Riverboat. Present were the following:
Chairman Don Tracy, Members Steve Dolins, Hector Alejandre, Dee Robinson and Michael
Hansen.

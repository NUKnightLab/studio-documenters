There were no requests to address the Board.

Mr. Molaro, the Fund’s Legislative Liaison, was unable to attend the Fund’s meeting because he was

available managers.
Watchlist
Mr. Yoon reported no changes to the current Watchlist.
Liquidity Needs

liquidate any assets to pay benefits.

March 26, 2019.

Investment Fund to assist with April, May and June liquidity needs. Seconded by Trustee Thompson.

Investment Officer to attend the Pension Bridge Conference in April and for the Fund to reimburse or pay all
expenses in accordance with Fund policies. Seconded by Trustee Thompson.

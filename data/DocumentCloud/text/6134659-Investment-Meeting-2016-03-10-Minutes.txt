MUNICIPAL EMPLOYEES’ ANNUITY AND BENEFIT FUND OF CHICAGO
INVESTMENT MEETING 2016-03-01
MARCH 10, 2016

An Investment Meeting of the Retirement Board of the Municipal Employees’ Annuity and
Benefit Fund of Chicago was held in the Offices of the Fund on March 10, 2016 at 9:06 a.m. to
hear economic updates and market perspectives and an update on the current status of the
Newport Capital Real Estate investment.

A quorum was present.
The following staff and consultants were present: James Mohler, Executive Director, Martin
Burns, Burke, Burns and Pinelli, Fund Counsel, Shawn Evans, Investment Analyst, Brian
Wrubel, Jamie Wesner, Neil Capps and David Hernandez of Marquette Associates and Stacey
Ruffolo, Project Coordinator.
Rock Creek
Afsanen Beschloss, CEO, Alifia Doriwala, Senior Partner
Ms. Beschloss and Ms. Doriwala updated the Board on the current status of its investment with
Rock Creek. Following their update, they offered their opinion of the state of the market and
potential investment opportunities. Following this discussion, the Board thanked them for their
presentation.
MacKay Shields
Therese Hernandez, Erik Gold
Mr. Gold updated the Board on the current status of its investment with MacKay Shields.
Following the update, Mr. Gold offered his opinion on the state of the market and potential
investment opportunities. Following the discussion, the Board thanked them for their
presentation.
Newport Capital
Derrick McGavick, Ms. Jana Langston, Ms. Lynette Mays
On a motion by Trustee Johnson, the Board moved into closed session at 10:58 a.m. in
accordance with 5 ILCS 120/2(c)(11) to discuss topics involving current litigation against the
Fund. Second by Trustee Guest.

International Small Cap Equity RFP Response Review
The Investment Consultant reported on the responses to the Fund’s RFP for an International
Small Cap Equity Manager. It was suggested that finalist presentations will be during the
Regular April meeting of the Retirement Board.

With no further presentations, the meeting was adjourned at 11:40 a.m. on a motion by Trustee
Guest. Seconded by Trustee Johnson. Carried by a voice vote.

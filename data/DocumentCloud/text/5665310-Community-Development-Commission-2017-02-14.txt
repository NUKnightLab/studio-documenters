A.

43RD/COTTAGE GROVE TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 4)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Planning and Development’s
intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Keaney Construction, Inc., or a
related entity, for the disposition of the property located at 4619-21 S. Champlain
Avenue in the 43rd/Cottage Grove Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment
Project Area, to request alternative proposals, and to approve the sale of the
property to Keaney Construction, Inc., or a related entity, if no responsive
alternative proposals are received.
Project Manager: Brian O’Donnell
Motioned by Delgado, seconded by Newsome. Approved 8-0. Yeas: Buford,
Davis, Delgado, Griggs, Newsome, Reifman, Rhodes and Perez. Cepeda not
present.

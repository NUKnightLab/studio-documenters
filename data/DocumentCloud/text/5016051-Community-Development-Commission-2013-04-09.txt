 III.

NEW BUSINESS
A.

NORTH BRANCH NORTH TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 32)
Request authority to acquire property needed for the Fullerton-Damen-Elston road
realignment project of the 1800 to 2000 blocks of West Fullerton Avenue, the
2300 to 2500 blocks of North Damen Avenue and the 2300 to 2400 blocks of
North Elston Avenue in the North Branch North Tax Increment Financing
Redevelopment Project Area.
Project Manager: Edward Lewis
Motioned by Martin, seconded by Duque. Approved 7-0. Yeas: Carrott, Duque,
Martin, Mooney, Newsome, Perez and Sahli.
13-CDC-9

B.

119TH/HALSTED TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 34)
Request authority to enter into a negotiated sale with Mr. Bruce Hardy for the
disposition of the property located at 621 W. 119th Street in the 119th/Halsted Tax
Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, to request alternative proposals
and to approve the sale of the property to Mr. Hardy if no responsive alternative
proposals are received.
Project Manager: Edward Lewis
Motioned by Perez, seconded by Sahli. Approved 7-0. Yeas: Carrott, Duque,
Martin, Mooney, Newsome, Perez and Sahli.
13-CDC-10

C.

KINZIE INDUSTRIAL TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 27)
Request authority to enter into a negotiated sale with NeighborSpace for the
disposition of the property located at 407, 413-15 and 419 N. Kedzie Avenue, 405
N. Albany Avenue and 416-28 N. Whipple Street in the Kinzie Industrial Tax
Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, to request alternative proposals
and to approve the sale of the property to NeighborSpace if no responsive
alternative proposals are received.
Project Manager: Michele Rhymes
Motioned by Perez, seconded by Duque. Approved 7-0. Yeas: Carrott, Duque,
Martin, Mooney, Newsome, Perez and Sahli.
13-CDC-11

SSA #2 Commissioners’ Meeting
Central Gyros Restaurant
January 10, 2017, 2pm
Commissioners Present: Kurt Koziol, Mike Valenti, John Toumplis
Others Present: Larry Lynch, David Potete, Kevin Lamm, Andrew Tomlin, George Frei,
Michael Caldarulo, Reid Mackin.
Quorum & Meeting Minutes
With three commissioners present, a quorum was determined to exist. Mike motioned
to accept the October minutes; Kurt seconded the motion, and the motion was approved
unanimously.
Administration
Bob Garza has rescinded his application for SSA Commissioner. The county ethics
statement is due on May 1, and the city statement is due on May 31.
Mike motioned to accept Eilts’ proposal for the 2016, 2017 & 2018 audits at the cost of
$2000 per year. John seconded the motion, and the motion was approved
unanimously.
Finances
Three financial statements were presented: (1) Year-end Profit & Loss vs. 2015; (2)
Year-end vs. 2016 budget; (3) Year-end Balance Sheet vs. 2015.
Garage Maintenance & Repair
Superior Surfaces completed the resurfacing project. A new banner was created and
will be hung above the garage entrance soon. Imperial Landscaping has plowed snow
on three separate occasions in December.
The next scheduled meeting is April 25 to approve the 2016 audit.

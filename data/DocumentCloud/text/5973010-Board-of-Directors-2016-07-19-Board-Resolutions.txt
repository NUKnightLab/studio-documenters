RESOLUTIONS ADOPTED BY THE
CHICAGO INFRASTRUCTURE TRUST
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
ON JULY 19, 2016

MEETING MINUTES
RESOLVED, that the minutes of the meeting of the Board of Directors
held on April 20, 2016, in the form previously provided to the members of the
Board of Directors, be and hereby are approved.

2016 ANNUAL BUDGET
RESOLVED, that the Chicago Infrastructure Trust’s (the “Trust”) budget
for its 2016 fiscal year attached hereto as Exhibit A be and hereby is adopted and
approved as the Trust’s 2016 Annual Budget.

SMART LIGHTING PROJECT
WHEREAS, the Board of Directors previously authorized the staff of the
Trust to prepare procurement documents in furtherance of the certain energy
efficiency initiatives relating to street lights in Chicago (the “Smart Lighting
Project”); and
WHEREAS, the staff has reviewed the responses to such request for
qualifications and recommends to the Board of Directors that the list of
respondents set forth on Exhibit B attached hereto be approved as the finalists
from which the winning contractor shall be selected;
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Trust’s officers be, and
hereby are, authorized to request from the candidates listed on Exhibit B attached
hereto their final proposals for the Smart Lighting Project.

 GENERAL
RESOLVED, that in order to fully carry out the intent and effectuate the
purposes of the foregoing resolutions, any of the Trust’s officers be, and each
hereby is, authorized to take all such further actions, and to execute and deliver all
such further agreements, instruments, documents or certificates in the name and
on behalf of the Trust, and under its corporate seal or otherwise, and to pay all
such fees and expenses, which shall in their judgment be necessary, proper or
advisable and to perform all of the obligations of the Trust in connection with the
foregoing resolutions.

STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on July 9, 2013
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, Suite S-400, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Board; Richard Lewis, Member of the Board;
Melissa Mlynski, Executive Director; Jerald S. Post, General Counsel; and staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD June 13, 2013

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the June 13, 2013 meeting. Member Anderson moved to
approve the minutes. Member Lewis seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gierut asked Executive Director Mlynski to present the Local Panel administrative actions report for the
period of time from June 1 through June 30, 2013. She presented the report and summarized its highlights. The
Board examined the report and discussed it.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.
Case No. L-CB-13-020
Salvatore Ziccarelli/ International Brotherhood of Teamsters, Local 700

Chairman Gierut asked General Counsel Post to call the case. General Counsel Post stated that on April 29, 2013
the Executive Director issued a dismissal of the charge alleging that Respondent violated Section 10(b) of the
Illinois Public Labor Relations Act. General Counsel Post then asked Counsel Hamburg-Gal to review the issues
raised by the Charging Party’s appeal and Respondent’s response. After her presentation, Counsel Hamburg-Gal
recommended that the Executive Director's dismissal be reversed and remanded for further investigation. After
discussion, Member Lewis moved to reverse and remand the dismissal for further investigation. Member Anderson
seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
2.
Case No. L-CB-13-029
Richard Sanabria/ Illinois Fraternal Order of Police, Lodge 7
Chairman Gierut asked General Counsel Post to call and present the case. General Counsel Post stated that the
Executive Director issued a dismissal on March 21, 2013 and that the Charging Party filed an appeal alleging that
Respondent violated Section 10(b) of the Illinois Public Labor Relations Act. After General Counsel Post presented
and discussed the issues of the case and Charging Party’s appeal, he recommended that the Board affirm the
Executive Director’s dismissal. After discussion, Member Anderson made a motion to affirm the dismissal.
Member Lewis seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VI.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded that it was
not necessary at this time.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the
Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

 Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations
Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 6th day of August, 2013.

3.

Communication dated October 12, 2018 (received November 16th) requesting
Commission approval of a modification to an intergovernmental agreement
between the Charter County of Wayne and the U.S. Marshals Service to continue
to provide housing and care of U.S. Marshals detainees; the modification notates
that U.S. Marshals detainees should only be housed in Wayne County Jail
Division 3 (William Dickerson Detention Facility), with detainees under certain
exceptions being housed in Wayne County Jail Division 1 (Andrew C. Baird
Detention Facility); the County Executive advises that the per-diem rate remains
$145 per day, plus $41 per hour for guard/transportation services; anticipated
annual revenue under this agreement is estimated to be $975,000; the term of
the modified IGA is from September 26, 2018 until terminated by either party with
written notice; the revenue from the agreement will be deposited to Account Nos.
101 35100 603030 ($75,000 County Jail) and 101 35110 603030 ($900,000
WCJ-Hamtramck). (2008-71-017M1)

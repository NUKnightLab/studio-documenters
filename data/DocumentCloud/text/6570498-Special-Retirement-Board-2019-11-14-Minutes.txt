Public Comment
The Executive Director stated that Trustee Nevius had requested that he be allowed to participate in the meeting
by telephone. It was noted that a quorum of the Board was physically present at the meeting.
It was moved by Trustee McFadden and seconded by Trustee Goode pursuant to the Open Meetings Act, 5 ILCS
120/7, that Trustee Nevius be allowed to participate by telephone.
Vote Result:

MOTION ADOPTED BY VOICE VOTE

Trustee Wilson, President of the Board, then opened the meeting for public comment and no one having requested
to address the Board, the next item of business on the Agenda was considered.
1. Interview of Candidates for Forest Preserve Vacancy
Regina Tuczak, Executive Director, reported that all the candidates who had expressed interest in serving as a
Forest Preserve District Employee Member on the Board had been notified of the Special Meeting and would
appear before the Board at specific times designated to them. One candidate had contacted the Executive
Director that morning and stated that her appearance might be delayed due to transportation problems. The
candidate asked if another date could be scheduled for her appearance, if necessary. The Board considered the

It was moved by Trustee Fitzgerald and seconded by Trustee McFadden that the Board adjourn the Executive
Session called pursuant to Section 2(c)(3) of the Open Meetings Act and return to open session.
2. Review and Possible Appointment of Candidate
After the return to open session, it was moved by Trustee McFadden and seconded by Trustee Fitzgerald,
pursuant to Section 9-187 of the Illinois Pension Code, that Stephen Hughes be appointed as the Forest
Preserve District Employee Member on the Board to fill the vacancy created by the recent resignation and that
he should serve in that position during the remainder of the unexpired term of the withdrawn member.
Trustee Ochalla stated that the record should reflect that the proposed motion was different from the straw
poll taken by the trustees during the Executive Session.
Vote Result:

A voice vote having been called, five members having voted ‘yea’
and two members having voted ‘nay’;
MOTION ADOPTED BY VOICE VOTE

The Executive Director was directed to notify all the candidates of the Board’s decision as soon as might be
practicable.
3. Old Business/New Business
There was no old business or new business discussed.
4. Adjournment
There being no further business before the Board, it was moved by Trustee Fitzgerald and seconded by
Trustee Goode that the meeting be adjourned.
Vote Result:

MOTION ADOPTED BY VOICE VOTE

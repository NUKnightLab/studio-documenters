Minutes for October 26, 2010 Executive Session
Trustees Present: Robert Benjamin, John E. Fitzgerald, Mark Kilgallon, Constance Kravitz, Patrick J.
McFadden
Staff Present: Daniel R. Degnan, Executive Director; Nickol Hackett, Chief Investment Officer;
Fernando Vinzons, Investment Analyst.
It was moved by Trustee Kravitz and seconded by Trustee Benjamin that the Board convene an
Executive Session pursuant to Section 2(c)(7) of the Open Meetings Act, 5 ILCS 120, to discuss the
preliminary results of the investment consultant search.
Roll Call Vote:
AYES: Benjamin, Fitzgerald, Kilgallon, Kravitz, McFadden
NAYS: None
Vote Result: MOTION ADOPTED.
The Trustees discussed the selection process and the level of direct contact that would be appropriate
between Trustees and consultants. They also discussed the reasons they entered into the search.
Ms. Hackett discussed the results of the further due diligence including each firm’s strengths and
weaknesses and each firm’s philosophy with regard to asset classes and asset allocation. The
Trustee’s again discussed the asset allocation and the resources needed to support the various asset
classes.
It was moved by Trustee Fitzgerald and seconded by Trustee Kravitz that, no action being taken, the
Executive Session be adjourned and that the Board return to open session.
Vote Result: MOTION ADOPTED UNANIMOUSLY BY VOICE VOTE.

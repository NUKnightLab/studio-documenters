 III.

NEW BUSINESS
A.

PROPOSED DIVERSEY/CHICAGO RIVER TIF REDEVELOPMENT
PROJECT AREA (WARD 1)
Request authority to prepare a feasibility study and housing impact study for the
proposed Diversey/Chicago River Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment
Project Area.
Project Manager: Terrance Johnson
Motioned by Cepeda, seconded by Newsome. Approved 11-0. Yeas: Alphonse,
Buford, Butler, Cepeda, Davis, Griggs, Newsome, Perez, Reifman, Ward and
Carrott. Moreno recused.
16-CDC-6

B.

CANAL/CONGRESS TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 25)
Request authority to acquire the property bounded by Van Buren and Harrison
streets, Canal Street and the Chicago River in the Canal/Congress Tax Increment
Financing Redevelopment Project Area.
Project Manager: Mary Bonome
Motioned by Perez, seconded by Newsome. Approved 11-1. Yeas: Alphonse,
Butler, Cepeda, Davis, Griggs, Moreno, Newsome, Perez, Reifman, Ward and
Carrott. Nay: Buford.
16-CDC-7

C.

CANAL/CONGRESS TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 25)
Request authority to advertise and issue a Request For Proposals for the purchase
and redevelopment of the property bounded by Van Buren and Harrison streets,
Canal Street and the Chicago River in the Canal/Congress Tax Increment
Financing Redevelopment Project Area.
Project Manager: Mary Bonome
Motioned by Perez, seconded by Newsome. Approved 6-3. Yeas: Butler, Moreno,
Newsome, Perez, Reifman and Carrott. Nays: Buford, Davis and Griggs.
Alphonse, Cepeda and Ward not present.
16-CDC-8

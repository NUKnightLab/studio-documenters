Notice of Illinois Gaming Board Meeting
Special Board Meeting
The Illinois Gaming Board will convene a special meeting of the Board in Open Session on
Monday, May 6, 2019 at 11:00 A.M. on the 2nd floor of the James R. Thompson Center at 100 W.
Randolph, Chicago, Illinois, in room 2-025. The Board anticipates it will immediately retire to Closed
Executive Session, with additional Open Session to follow. The subject matters to be discussed are
included on the attached proposed Riverboat/Video Gaming agenda.
Please note that the regular Riverboat/Video Gaming meeting agenda is typically posted 48
hours prior to the scheduled meeting date.

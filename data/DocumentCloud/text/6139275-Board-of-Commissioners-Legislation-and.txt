RESOLUTION IN SUPPORT OF HR 3179, THE MARKETPLACE AND EQUITY ACT
(PROPOSED RESOLUTION). Submitting a Proposed Resolution sponsored by Peter N.
Silvestri, County Commissioner; Co-sponsored by Larry Suffredin, County Commissioner.

PROPOSED 

 

IN SUPPORT OF HR 3179, THE MARKETPLACE AND EQUITY ACT
WHEREAS, our nation is founded upon small, local businesses; and

WHEREAS, local businesses add to the integrity and quality of life in our communities and
country; and

WHEREAS, local businesses support local organizations, charities, churches, schools,
libraries and athletic and ?ne arts programs; and

WHEREAS, local businesses collect sales taxes that help fund various levels of
government; and

WHEREAS, local businesses are at a disadvantage with internet based businesses, as they
collect sales taxes while internet businesses are not generally so compelled; and

WHEREAS, this. disparity hurts local businesses and local communities throughout the
country; and

WHEREAS, it is estimated that $23 billion in sales taxes go uncollected nationally.

NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the President and Board of
Commissioners support the passage of HR 3179 and urge the members of Congress and the
United States Senate from the state of Illinois to support this initiative.

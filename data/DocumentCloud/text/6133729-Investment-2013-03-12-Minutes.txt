MUNICIPAL EMPLOYEES’ ANNUITY AND BENEFIT FUND OF CHICAGO

Benefit Fund was held in the Fund offices on March 12, 2013. The meeting was called to
order at 9:00 a.m.

The following issues were discussed and reviewed for the Board:
Economic Update
Mr. Laurenti, Deputy Chief Economist for Mesirow Financial, presented an overview of
the global economic climate.
Fixed Income Environment
Mr. Philo, Senior Managing Director and Head of High Yield for MacKay
Shields,presented an overview on the current Fixed Income investment environment.
Portfolio Stress Test Results
Mr. Leonberger reviewed the results of a portfolio stress test that the Fund’s Investment
Consultant, Marquette Associates, conducted to gauge the overall health of the Fund’s
investments.

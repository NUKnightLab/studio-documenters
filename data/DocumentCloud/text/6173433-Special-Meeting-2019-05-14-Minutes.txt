MUNICIPAL EMPLOYEES’ ANNUITY AND BENEFIT FUND OF CHICAGO
SPECIAL MEETING NO. 2019-05-01
MAY 14, 2019

A special meeting of the Retirement Board of the Municipal Employees’ Annuity and Benefit
Fund was held in the Fund offices on May 14, 2019 regarding personnel matters. The
meeting was called to order at 9:02 a.m.
The following Board members were present: Trustee Thomas McMahon, President, Trustee
Erin Keane, Vice President, Trustee Verna Thompson, Recording Secretary and Trustee
Jeffrey Johnson.
Additionally, the following individuals were present: Mr. Jim Mohler, Executive Director,
Ms. Stacey Ruffolo, Deputy Executive Director, Ms. Donna Hansen, Office Manager and Ms.
Mary Pat Burns, Attorney, Burke, Burns & Pinelli.
On a motion by Trustee Johnson, the Board moved into closed session at 9:03 a.m. in
accordance with ILCS 120/2(c)(1) to discuss personnel matters. Seconded by Trustee
Thompson.

February 4, 2019

NOTICE OF SPECIAL MEETING OF THE DETROIT
CHARTER REVISION COMMISSION
In accordance with Section 5(4) of the Michigan Open Meetings Act, MCL 15.265(4), the
Detroit Charter Revision Commission will hold a Sub-Committee Charter meeting on Thursday,
February 7, 2019, at 12:00 noon (Economic Growth Committee). The meeting will be held at
the Detroit Association of Black Organizations located at 12048 Grand River Ave, Detroit, MI
48204. The Public is invited and encouraged to attend.
Additional information regarding this meeting may be obtained from the Office of the City
Clerk at (313) 224-3266.

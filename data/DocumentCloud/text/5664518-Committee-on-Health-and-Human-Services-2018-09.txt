3.

Communication dated July 26, 2018 (received August 6th) requesting Commission
approval of an intergovernmental agreement between the Charter County of Wayne
and HealthChoice of Michigan not to exceed $747,182 to provide administrative and
management support to HealthChoice of Michigan to fulfill its duties as a municipal
health facilities corporation, through Wayne County Access to Care (WCATC), to
ensure that the needs of the low-income, elderly, or other vulnerable groups within
Wayne County have improved access to health care services; the County Executive
advises that HealthChoice of Michigan advances and promotes the health and
welfare of residents of Wayne County through a variety of programs and attempts to
ensure that access to reasonably priced medical, dental and pharmacy care are
available and this contract is necessary for the operation of the HealthChoice
programs which currently serve approximately 7,500 members; the term of the
agreement is from October 1, 2018 through September 30, 2019; the revenue from
the agreement will be deposited into Account No. 101 60202 626000 (PCMS-ABW
Program). (2018-16-001)

4.

County Executive advises the JAIS system is the case management and

term of the contract is from October 1, 2018 through September 30, 2023; the cost

THE FOLLOWING PROCEDURES WILL GOVERN THE MEETING PROCESS:
1. Board Members who vote "Nay, Present, or Abstain" or have a question on any item
may request the item be removed from the Consent Agenda.
2. Citizens in the audience who address the Board on any item may request the item be
removed from the Consent Agenda.
3. Items removed from the Consent Agenda are considered separately.
4. One roll call vote is taken to cover all Consent Agenda Items.

Meeting Summary
Speakers
Jeff Esser, CEO of Government Finance Officer’s Association presented the
Distinguished Budget Presentation Award to the District for the 2008 Budget and
Certificates of Achievement for Excellence in Financial Reporting to the District, the
Retirement Fund, and to the Retiree Health Care Trust for the Fiscal Year 2007. (Re:
Finance Item 1)
George Blakemore, a private citizen, inquired about law firm of Barnes & Thornburg’s
racial demographics. (Re: Purchasing Item 35)
Jerry Gulley, Executive Director and Meredith Murry, Volunteer Board President of
Shore Community Services, Inc. requested that the services the center provides be
taken into consideration when renewing their lease. (Re: Real Estate Item 46)
George Blakemore, a private citizen, inquired about Shore Community Services, Inc.
demographics of staff and the individuals being assisted. (Re: Real Estate Item 46)
Joe Ramirez, son of Manuel Ramirez, accepted the memorial resolution on behalf of
his family. (Re: Miscellaneous and New Business Item 47)
Patricia Young accepted the resolution recognizing her service as a commissioner of
the District. (Re: Miscellaneous and New Business Item 48)
George Blakemore, a private citizen, is looking forward to the District’s display and
resolutions honoring Black History Month. Also inquired whether District vehicles are
equipped with tracking devices.

Announcements
Jill Horist announced that the 2009 African American History Celebration Honoring
Black Engineers and the Underground Railroad will be held on Friday, February 27,
2009 at 4:45 p.m. - 6:45 p.m. in the Main Office Building Board Room.

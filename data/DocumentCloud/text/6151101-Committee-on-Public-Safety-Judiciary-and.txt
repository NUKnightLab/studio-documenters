New Business

1.

Ratification of the Chair’s action and exigent approval of a grant application
between the Charter County of Wayne and the Michigan Department of
Corrections in the amount of $2,990,785 for the Community Corrections Plan and
Application for Fiscal Year 2020; the County Executive advises that this grant will
provide for the Comprehensive Plan and Services and Drunk Driver Jail
Reduction Community Treatment Services, and the Community Corrections plan
includes screening and assessments of eligible offenders for PA 511 programs,
and affords offenders access to jail- and community-based treatment and
supervision services; the term of the agreement is from October 1, 2019 through
September 30, 2020; the revenue from the agreement will be deposited into
Account No. 281 32000 543000 (Community Correction). (2019-19-003)
Note: On April 30, 2019, Chair Bell authorized exigent approval of the grant
application in the amount of $2,990,785 under the provisions of the
Procurement Ordinance and has advised that this is for a period not to
exceed 60 days at which time the appropriate Committee(s) of the
Commission will complete the review of this matter.

3.

Communication dated May 2, 2019 (received May 6th) requesting Commission
approval of a grant agreement between the Charter County of Wayne and the
State of Michigan, Department of State Police-Emergency Management and
Homeland Security Division in the amount of $4,500 for the 2018-19 Hazardous
Material Emergency Preparedness Planning Program (HMEP-PP) to assist
Wayne County in the review of new Superfund Amendment and Reauthorization
Act (SARA) Title III hazardous materials emergency response plans, as well as
for the updating of previously submitted plans for facilities subject to SARA Title
III in Wayne County; the County Executive advises that this grant requires a 25%
match of any federal funds received and matching funds will be supplied by the
County in the form of an “in kind” contribution credited from the salary and fringe
benefits of personnel working on this grant; the term of the grant is from October
1, 2018 through September 30, 2019; the revenue from the grant will be
deposited into Account No. 101 42617 529000 (HMEP-PP). (2019-02-009)

4.

Communications dated April 4, 2019 (received May 27th) requesting Commission
approval of an agreement between the Charter County of Wayne and Wayne
County Community College District (WCCCD) in the amount of $1,400,000 to
provide security detail and outreach efforts at various WCCCD campuses,
including patrol and other law enforcement services such as investigation and
arrest, from time to time as deemed appropriate; the Sheriff advises that the
Sheriff’s Office will continue a unit known as WCCCD Detail, consisting of sworn
Sheriff’s law enforcement personnel and other approved staff and services
agreed upon by the Sheriff and Chancellor and the activities of the detail will be
dedicated exclusively to the security of the WCCCD Eastern Campus,
Northwestern Campus and Downtown Campus; the term of the agreement is
from July 1, 2019 through June 30, 2021; the revenue from the agreement will be
deposited into Account No. 266 32193 626000 (Sheriff-WCCCD Unit). (2019-71037)

In this case 32 feet are required and 14 feet 6 inches.

of the main building is required; in this case 15 feet is required and a portion of the building is
8 feet 3 inches. A 3,018 square foot side yard area at the north side of the building is required
where approximately 1,400 square feet are proposed.

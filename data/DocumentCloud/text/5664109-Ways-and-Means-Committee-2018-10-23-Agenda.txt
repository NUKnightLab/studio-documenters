3.

Communication dated September 26, 2018 (received September 28th)
requesting Commission approval of a proposed amendment to the 2017-2018
Appropriations Ordinance (Enrolled Ordinance No. 2017-635) in accordance with
Budget Adjustment No. 2018-35-271 certifying revenue in the amount of
$160,050,000 in the Downriver Sewage System Fund (590), $6,741,007 in the
Northeast Sewage Disposal System Fund (599), as well as certifying and
decertifying revenue in the amount of $5,000,000 in the Debt Service-Sewage
Fund (303); this budget adjustment submitted by Management and Budget
serves to recognize revenue from fund balance from Downriver Sewage System
and Northeast Sewage Disposal System funds in order to make contributions to
the Wayne County Employee Retirement System (WCERS) and to Michigan
Employee Retirement System (MERS) regarding Other Post Employee Benefits
(OPEB); also, from the Downriver Sewage Fund, funds will be transferred to the
newly created Debt Service-Sewage Fund in order to make payment for the
Downriver Community Judgment in the future. (2018-35-271)

4.

Communication dated October 1, 2018 (received October 3rd) requesting
Commission approval of a proposed amendment to the 2018-2019
Appropriations Ordinance (Enrolled Ordinance No. 2018-683) in accordance with
Budget Adjustment No. 2019-35-001 certifying revenue in the amount of
$226,523 in the Community Corrections Fund (281); the County Executive
advises that the Department of Health, Veterans and Community Wellness has
received the PA 511 grant from the State of Michigan for FY 2018-19, which
funds adult corrections programs designated to improve jail utilization and reduce
the prison commitment rate and this budget adjustment will certify funds in order
to bring the grant award in line with the amended budget. (2019-35-001)

5.

Communication dated October 4, 2018 (received October 9th) requesting
Commission approval of a proposed amendment to the 2017-2018
Appropriations Ordinance (Enrolled Ordinance No. 2017-635) in accordance with
Budget Adjustment No. 2018-35-277 certifying revenue in the amount of $9,102
in the HCS-Nutrition Fund (223); the County Executive advises that the budget
for Senior Services serves to add funds provided by The Senior Alliance in the
amended budget for Nutrition Services in order to provide sufficient funding to
address financial operational deficiencies in assorted accounts. (2018-35-277)

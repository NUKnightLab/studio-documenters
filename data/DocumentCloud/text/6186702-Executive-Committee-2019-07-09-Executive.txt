Executive Committee Minutes

Wicker Park Bucktown Chamber of Commerce – 1414 N. Ashland Ave.

Staff: Alice Howe (Program Specialist), Garrett Karp (Program Manager) Pamela Maass (WPBCC

from line item 2.11 (Graffiti Abatement) for 32nd Ward’s graffiti removal supplies. (19-0702)

from line item 1.10 (Community Grants) for “The Wicker of Oz” event to take place in Wicker

DG recommends. All in favor RECOMMENDATION PASSES.

for the Commission meeting.

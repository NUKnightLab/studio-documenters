 

EXECUTIVE SESSION ITEM NO. 4
RESOLUTION NO. 2010-CHA-159
WHEREAS, the Board of Commissioners has reviewed the Board Letter dated December 21, 2010
requesting authorization to 1) sell the fee interest in the land at 300 W. 23rd Street to
Neighborhood Redevelopment Associates, L.P.; and 2) execute such other documents as
necessary to implement the forgoing.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE CHICAGO HOUSING AUTHORITY
THAT

the Board of Commissioners authorizes the Chief Executive Officer or his designee to: 1)
sell the fee interest in the land at 300 W. 23rd Street to Neighborhood Redevelopment
Associates, L.P. for an amount of $315,000; and 2) execute such other documents as
necessary to implement the forgoing.

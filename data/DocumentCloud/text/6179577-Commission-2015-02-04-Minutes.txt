Commissioner Meeting
Wednesday, February 4, 2015 9 a.m.
RPBA’s Office – 1448 W. Morse Ave.

Commission Meeting Minutes
Attending: Jim McHale, Chairperson; Chris Johnson, Vice Chairperson; Kevin Owings,
Commissioner; Dorothy Milne, Secretary; Ana Bermudez (SSA #24 Manager), Carolina Juarez
(RPBA Staff), Sandi Price (RPBA Staff).
Absent: Al Goldberg, Treasurer
1. Welcome
Meeting was called to order at 9:07 a.m.
2. Community and Public Input
None.
3. Review and approval of December 2014 minutes
The minutes were reviewed. No changes, corrections nor additional information were
requested. Motion to approve the December 2014 minutes (Johnson / Owings).
Unanimously approved.
4. Action Items: 2015 Events
SSA 24 has received a sponsorship request from Touhy Park for a total of $1,200.00 to
support their 2015 programming. Specific events include: Movie in the Park and
Shakespeare in the Park. Motion to sponsor Touhy Park of the Chicago Park Districts
with a total of $1,200.00 for 2015 programming with the understanding that the park
will provide the Commission a schedule of events (Johnson/ Owings). Unanimously
approved.
Ana proposed the review of sponsorship levels for Glenwood Sunday Market and Clark
Street Festival for 2015. Commission discusses pros and cons. Motion to sponsor
Glenwood Sunday Market at $16,000 level and Clark Street Festival at a $10,000.00 level
(Owings / Johnson). Unanimously approved.
Commission requested that in the future all events submit a type of proposal that
include the value the event provides the SSA and the attendance as a reference when
working on budgets.
● Mile of Murals

STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on July 12, 2011
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, Suite S-400, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Jacalyn J. Zimmerman, Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Board; Edward Sadlowski, Member
of the Board; John F. Brosnan, Executive Director; Jerald S. Post, General Counsel; and staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD June 7, 2011

Chairman Zimmerman called for the approval of the minutes from the June 7, 2011 meeting. Member
Anderson moved to approve the minutes of the June 7, 2011 meeting. Chairman Zimmerman seconded. The
Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Zimmerman asked Executive Director Brosnan to present the Local Panel administrative actions
report for the period of time from June 1 to June 30, 2011. He presented the report and summarized its
highlights.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.
Case No. L-CA-09-044
National Nurses Organizing Committee/County of Cook

Chairman Zimmerman called the case, noting that no party filed exceptions to Administrative Law Judge’s
(ALJ) Hamburg-Gal's Recommended Decision and Order (RDO), and asked whether the Board wished to
review the decision on its own motion. The Board declined to do so.
2.
Case No. L-CA-10-069
Chicago Joint Board, Local 200, RWDSU, UFCW International Union/County of Cook
Chairman Zimmerman called the case, noting that no party filed exceptions to ALJ Deanna Rosenbaum's
RDO, and asked whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own motion. The Board declined to
do so.
3.
Case No. L-CB-11-014
Violar Murry/American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31
Chairman Zimmerman called the case, noting that Executive Director Brosnan issued a dismissal on April
28, 2011, with an appeal filed. General Counsel Post, with the assistance of the Board's extern, Kelly Coyle,
reviewed the issues presented by the appeal and recommended that the Board affirm the Executive Director's
dismissal finding charging party’s charge was untimely filed, further noting charging party had not sufficiently
alleged the Union’s intentional misconduct, nor alleged a valid basis for challenging an arbitration award. After
discussion, Member Sadlowski moved to accept the dismissal. Member Anderson seconded the motion, and
the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

 VI.

OTHER BOARD MATTERS

The Board opted to review the fiscal and legislative reports in the joint panel meeting scheduled for 10:30
a.m. on this date. After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the
Board concluded that it was unnecessary at this time.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Zimmerman called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the
meeting, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor
Relations Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 9th day of August, 2011.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD JULY 13, 2010

Chairman Zimmerman called for approval of the minutes of the July 13, 2010 meeting. Member Coli

unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Zimmerman asked Executive Director Brosnan to distribute the State Panel administrative
actions report for the period of time from July 1 through July 31, 2010. He distributed the report to the
Board, and the Board examined it.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.
Case No. S-RC-09-144
American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31/State of
Illinois, Department of Central Management Services

Chairman Zimmerman called the case. General Counsel Post reviewed the issues presented by the
exceptions to Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) Bell's Recommended Decision and Order (RDO), and
recommended that the Board adopt the result determined by the ALJ. Member Washington moved to
accept General Counsel Post's recommendation. Member Hade seconded the motion, and after
discussion, the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
2.
Case No. S-CA-09-007
Oak Lawn Professional Fire Fighters Association, International Association of Fire
Fighters, Local 3405/Village of Oak Lawn
Chairman Zimmerman called the case. General Counsel Post reviewed the issues presented by the
exceptions to ALJ Rios' RDO, and recommended that the Board affirm the ALJ's decision. Member
Washington moved to adopt General Counsel Post's recommendation. Member Kimbrough seconded
the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

 3.

Case No. S-RC-09-060

Illinois, Department of Central Management Services
Chairman Zimmerman called the case. General Counsel Post reviewed the issues presented by the
exceptions to ALJ Waechter's RDO, on remand from the Illinois Appellate Court, and recommended
that the Board uphold the ALJ's decision. Member Coli moved to accept the ALJ's decision. Member
Washington seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

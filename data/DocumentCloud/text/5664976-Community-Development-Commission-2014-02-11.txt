PROPOSED 107TH/HALSTED TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 34)

Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, recommend designation of
the area as a Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and

Ward. Perez not present.

A.

PILSEN INDUSTRIAL CORRIDOR TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT
AREA (WARD 25)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Planning and Development’s
intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Pilsen Wellness Center, Inc. for the
disposition of the property located at 2259 S. Damen Avenue in the Pilsen
Industrial Corridor Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and the
disposition of the property located at 1925 W. 23rd Street, which is not located in a
TIF district, to request alternative proposals, and to approve the sale of the
property to Pilsen Wellness Center, Inc. if no responsive alternative proposals are
received.
Project Manager: Christopher Jang
Motioned by Perez, seconded by Garner. Approved 13-0. Yeas: Buford, Cepeda,
Carrott, Davis, Duque, Garner, Kostiner, Logan, Mooney, Newsome, Perez, Stein
and Ward.

WHEREAS, the Administration and County Board of Cook County understand

initiative; and

WHEREAS, the Cook County Suburban Economic Development Commission
(CCSEDC) was incorporated as of November 1, 2007 as a not?for-pro?t, public-
private, multi-jurisdictional economic development vehicle comprised of four
suburban councils of municipal government and a Chairperson from suburban

WHEREAS, the Cook County Suburban Economic Development Commission
supplants Enterpriz Cook County which was voluntarily dissolved on October
31,2007;and

WHEREAS, the mission of the Cook County Suburban Economic Development

and new revenues throughout suburban Cook County; and

WHEREAS, the Cook County Suburban Economic Development Commission is
funded by non-governmental corporate donations, contracts and contributions.

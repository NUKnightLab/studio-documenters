Cuyahoga County Personnel Review Commission
Wednesday, April 3, 2019
2429 Superior Viaduct, 3rd Floor

4:00 p.m.
1) CALL TO ORDER
Chairman Boseman called the meeting to order at 4:05 p.m.
2) ROLL CALL
Chairman Boseman asked AA2 Phil Tomko to call the roll. Chairman Boseman, Commissioner
Southerington, and Commissioner Colaluca were present. No one was absent; a quorum was
determined.
3) APPROVAL OF MINUTES from March 6, 2019 and Special Meeting on March 8, 2019
Commissioner Colaluca made a motion to approve the minutes from March 6, 2019 and Special
Meeting on March 8, 2019; Commissioner Southerington seconded the motion. All were in favor; no
objections.
4) PUBLIC COMMENT—Nothing submitted
5) UNFINISHED BUSINESS—Nothing submitted
6) NEW BUSINESS
a) Appeals
i) Lisa Taylor – Report & Recommendation
Appellant Lisa Taylor informed the Commissioners that she agreed with the Report &
Recommendation. Discussion ensued.
Commissioner Colaluca made a motion to accept the Hearing Officer’s Report &
Recommendation; Commissioner Southerington seconded the motion. All were in favor; no
objections. Commissioner Colaluca confirmed with the Director that the staff will conduct an
audit per the Hearing Officer’s recommendation and report back at the next PRC meeting.
b) Report on Ethics Compliance 2018
Staff Attorney Sara DeCaro presented the 2018 Ethics Report; discussion ensued.

d) Request to Extend an Eligibility List
Public Works requests extension of Sewer Maintenance Worker eligibility list
Manager of Employment Testing George Vaughan presented a request from Public Works to
extend the life of the Eligibility List for Sewer Maintenance Worker to July 28, 2019 to
accommodate anticipated additional staffing needs.
Chairman Boseman moved to accept the request to extend the Eligibility List established for
Sewer Maintenance Worker to July 28, 2019; Commissioner Colaluca seconded. All were in
favor; no objections.
e) Class Plan Revision Requests
Classification and Compensation Manager Albert Bouchahine presented the Class Plan Revision
Requests. Albert Bouchahine asked that the Commissioners not consider the two Business
Intelligence Analyst positions at the request of Human Resources and IT. Discussion ensued.
Chairman Boseman made a motion to approve the Class Plan Revision Requests with the
exception of the two Business Intelligence Analyst positions; Commissioner Southerington
seconded the motion. All were in favor; no objections.
7) PUBLIC COMMENT—Nothing submitted
6) EXECUTIVE SESSION – Discussion of personnel matter
Director Rebecca Kopcienski presented a hiring recommendation for Alexandra Hamame for the
part-time position of Intern.
Chairman Boseman made a motion to approve the hiring recommendation of Ms. Hamame for the
part-time position of Intern; Commissioner Southerington seconded the motion. All were in favor;
no objections.
7) OTHER BUSINESS—Nothing submitted
8) ADJOURNMENT
Chairman Boseman made a motion to adjourn the meeting at 4:21 p.m.; Commissioner Colaluca
seconded. All were in favor; no objections.

Mr. Lopez updated the Board on the monthly report of the Fund investments.
Liquidity Needs
Mr. Lopez reviewed the liquidity needs of the Fund. Based on Marquette and staff recommendation,
Trustee Guest made a motion to liquidate up to $45 million for February benefit payments from the sources
identified by the Fund's Investment Officer. Second by Trustee Johnson.
Carried: Ayes – Trustee Guest, Trustee Johnson, Trustee McMahon and Trustee Widawsky. Nays – None.
Watchlist
Mr. Lopez reviewed the Watchlist and recommended no changes. No actions taken.

Old Business

Exchange Agreement and authorize the Executive Director to execute the document on behalf of the Fund.
Seconded by Trustee Johnson.
Carried: Ayes – Trustee Guest, Trustee Johnson, , Trustee McMahon and Trustee Widawsky. Nays – None.

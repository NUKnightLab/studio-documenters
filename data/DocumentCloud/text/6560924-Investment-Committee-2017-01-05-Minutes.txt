CONFIRMATION OF THE RECOMMENDATION OF THE PFRS INVESTMENT COMMITTEE TO

By Trustee Diaz – Supported by Trustee Trozak
WHEREAS, at its regular meeting on January 5, 2017, the Board of Trustees of the Police and Fire
Retirement System of the City of Detroit (“PFRS”), received a report from General Counsel
regarding the re-appointment of Rebecca S. Sorensen to continue to serve as an Independent
Member of the PFRS Investment Committee (“IC”), and
WHEREAS, Exhibit B of the October 17, 2014 Contribution Agreement (“PFRS Governance
Terms”) provides that upon the expiration of the term of an Independent Member of the IC, the
remaining Independent Member shall make a recommendation to the PRFS Board as to a
Successor Independent Member seeking confirmation by the Board of such recommendation, and
WHEREAS, the IC recommended the re-appointment of Rebecca S. Sorensen to serve as an
Independent Member at its December 12, 2016 IC Meeting. The recommendation was presented
to the Board for confirmation at its December 15, 2016 Board Meeting, at which time the Board
sought clarification as to the length of the new term for Ms. Sorensen, and
WHEREAS, the IC addressed this issue as requested by the Board at its December 22, 2016
Special IC Meeting and determined the appropriate length of the term shall be six years (the length
of the longest term provided in the PFRS Governance Terms), and
WHEREAS, the requested clarification was provided to the Board at its January 5, 2017 by
General Counsel and this matter was discussed by the Board and the Board confirmed the
recommendation of the IC regarding the re-appointment of Ms. Sorensen, and
WHEREAS, the Board has considered this matter in open session, therefore be it
RESOLVED, that the Board confirms the recommendation of the IC for the re-appoint of Ms.
Sorensen to serve a six year term as an Independent Member of the PFRS IC, and be it further
RESOLVED, that a copy of this resolution shal be forwarded to the PFRS Investment Committee:

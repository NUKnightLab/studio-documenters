STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on April 9, 2019
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman of the Local Panel; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Local Panel; Angela C. Thomas,
Member of the Local Panel; Helen J. Kim, General Counsel; Anna Hamburg-Gal, Associate General Counsel; and staff
members. Kimberly F. Stevens, Executive Director participated by telephone. Chairman Gierut acknowledged the newly
appointed state panel members in the audience.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD March 12, 2019

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the March 12, 2019 meeting. Member Anderson moved to approve
the minutes. Member Thomas seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gierut asked Executive Director Stevens to present the reports. She presented the reports and summarized their
highlights.
V.

REVIEW OF PROPOSED WRITTEN DECISIONS AND ORDERS
A.
Case No. L-CA-16-080
Reginald Dean and City of Chicago

General Counsel Kim called the case. Member Thomas made a motion to approve the proposed written decision. Member
Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
B.
Case No. L-CA-19-071
Stella Okwu and County of Cook, Health and Hospital System (Stroger Hospital)
General Counsel Kim called the case. Member Thomas made a motion to approve the proposed written decision. Member
Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VI.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.
Case No. L-CA-16-011
Int’l Brotherhood of Teamsters, Local 700 and City of Chicago (Department of Streets and Sanitation)

General Counsel Kim called the case, noted that Administrative Law Judge Donald W. Anderson issued a Recommended
Decision and Order with no exceptions filed and asked whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own motion.
The Board declined to do so.
2.
Case No. L-CA-18-041
Illinois Fraternal Order of Police and County of Cook and Sheriff od Cook County
General Counsel Kim called and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member Thomas moved to
reverse the Executive Director’s dismissal and remand to the Executive Director to issue a complaint for hearing. Member
Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

 3.
Case No. L-CB-19-013
Fahad Nazir and Cook County Pharmacy Assoc., Chicago Joint Board, Retail, Wholesale and Department
Store Union, Local 200
General Counsel Kim called and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member Anderson moved
to affirm the Executive Director’s dismissal. Member Thomas seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously,
by a voice vote.
4.
Case No. L-CB-19-014
Fahad Nazir and Cook County Pharmacy Assoc., Chicago Joint Board, Retail, Wholesale and Department
Store Union, Local 200
General Counsel Kim called and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member Thomas moved to
affirm the Executive Director’s dismissal. Member Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously,
by a voice vote.
VII.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded it was unnecessary.
VIII.

PUBLIC COMMENT

No members of the public sought to make comments.
IX.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board, in
Chicago, Illinois, this 7th day of May 2019.

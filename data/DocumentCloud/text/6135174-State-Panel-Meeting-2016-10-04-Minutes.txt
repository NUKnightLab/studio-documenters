STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
STATE PANEL
Minutes of the State Panel Meeting
Held on October 4, 2016
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 12:30 P.M.—via videoconference at 100 N Randolph Street, Room 9-035,
Chicago, Illinois and 131 West Mason, Room 104 Training Center, Springfield, Illinois.

II.

PRESENT

John Hartnett, Chairman; Keith Snyder, Member of the Board; Melissa Mlynski, Executive Director; Sarah Kerley,
Deputy General Counsel; and staff members present in Springfield. Michael Coli, Member of the Board; John
Samolis, Member of the Board; Albert Washington, Member of the Board; Anna Hamburg-Gal, Associate General
Counsel; and staff members present in Chicago.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD SEPTEMBER 7, 2016

Chairman Hartnett called for approval of the minutes of the September 7, 2016, meeting. Member Washington
moved to approve the minutes. Member Samolis seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice
vote.
V.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Hartnett asked Executive Director Mlynski to present the Executive Director’s report. She presented the
report and summarized the highlights.
VI.

BOARD ACTIONS

There were no cases for Board review.
VII.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded it was unnecessary.

VIII.

PUBLIC COMMENT

No members of the public were in attendance.
IX.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Hartnett called for a motion to adjourn. Member Washington moved to adjourn the meeting, and the
Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the State Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations
Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 15th day of November, 2016.

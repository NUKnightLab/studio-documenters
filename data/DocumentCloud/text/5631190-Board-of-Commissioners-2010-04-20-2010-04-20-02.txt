RESOLUTION NO. 2010-CHA-36
WHEREAS, the Board of Commissioners has reviewed the Board letter dated
April 14, 2010, requesting authorization to: (1) enter into a Land Exchange
Agreement between the Chicago Housing Authority and St. Luke Church of God
in Christ for land within the Cabrini Extension South redevelopment site; (2)
submit acquisition and disposition applications to the U.S. Department of Housing
and Urban Development (“HUD”); and (3) execute and deliver documents
necessary or appropriate to implement the foregoing;
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE CHICAGO HOUSING AUTHORITY
THAT,

the Board of Commissioners authorizes the Chief Executive Officer or his
designee to: (1) enter into a Land Exchange Agreement between the Chicago
Housing Authority and St. Luke Church of God in Christ for land within the
Cabrini Extension South redevelopment site; (2) submit any necessary acquisition
and disposition applications to HUD; and (3) execute and deliver any documents
necessary or appropriate to implement the foregoing. It is anticipated that the
CHA land to be conveyed, upon receipt of HUD disposition approval, will be
approximately .20519 acres. The land received from St. Luke Church of God in
Christ, upon receipt of HUD acquisition approval, shall be acceptable to CHA and
of equal or greater value than the CHA land.

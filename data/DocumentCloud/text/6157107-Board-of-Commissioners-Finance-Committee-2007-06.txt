On behalf of the County Cable Negotiation T earn, approval is requested for the
franchise renewal agreement with Comcast Cable Communications, Inc. to
provide cable television services for Unincorporated Cook County.
Lengthy negotiations secured the following significant terms:
Five (5) years, with two successive two- year renewal
options.

Term:

Comcast has agreed to a minimum three-year term if

5% of Comcast Cable's gross revenues, the statutory

County Channel:

Comcast will provide the County with access to a cable

Capital Grant:

Governmental (P.E.G .) Capital Fee of $0.35 per month per

agreement.

auditing provisions of gross revenues and the strengthening of the County's
remedies in the event of defaults.

PROPOSED ORDINANCE AMENDMENT
Title 2 Chapter 4 Section 2 - Events Requiring Permits
BE IT ORDAINED, by the Forest Preserve District of Cook County Board of Commissioners, that Title
2 - Forest Preserve Land and Property, Chapter 4 - Recreation in Forest Preserves, Section 2 - Events
Requiring Permits of the Forest Preserve District of Cook County Code is hereby amended as Follows:
Sec. 2-4-2 Events Requiring Permits.
*****
B. Permit Required. No person shall use any portion of the Forest Preserve District property nor
any of the buildings within said District, for any of the following uses, unless the person has first
paid the appropriate permit application fee and obtained, and has physical possession of, and has
available for inspection, a permit allowing
such use in writing from the District, with proper
authorization of the General Superintendent or his or her designee, or when required, the Board of
Commissioners, in accordance with Section 7-1-1 Fee Schedule:
-1-1 Fee Schedule:
*****
C. Permit Rules and Application Review.
*****
2. Duration of Permit: The District may, in its discretion, make the permit valid for a period of
less than one (1) year, or any portion thereof, or on an annual basis; provided, however, that the
fees charged are consistent with Title 7, Chapter 1 (Fee Schedule) of the District's Code of
Ordinances. Permits are not transferable and permit fees paid to the District are not refundable.
The actual permit (not an electronic copy) Permits must be available for inspection at and

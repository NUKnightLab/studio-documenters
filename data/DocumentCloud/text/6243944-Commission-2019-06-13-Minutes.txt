SSA 54 Commission Meeting Thurs., June 13, 2019, 8am, Royal Coffee

Minutes
In Attendance: Heather Hill, Commissioner; Jennifer Clark, Commissioner; Chris Bell, Commissioner;
Tony Fox, Commissioner; Sara Lukens, Potential Commissioner; Cynthia Ryan, SSA #54 Manager
Guest: Cecilia Rodriguez, Loyola University
1.Welcome
2. Community & Public Input
There was no community & public input.
3. Approval of April 11, 2019 Minutes
Tony Fox moved to approve the minutes. Jennifer Clark seconded it. Motion passed.
4. Summer on the Plaza
Cecilia Rodriguez of Loyola University presented “Summer on the Plaza” results and asked for this year’s
sponsorship of $5500. “Summer on the Plaza” is live music bands playing on different plazas on Sheridan
Road from June through October. Chris Bell moved to approve the sponsorship for $5500. Jennifer Clark
seconded it. Motion passed.
5. 2020 Draft Budget
Cynthia Ryan presented the 2020 draft budget for the Commissioners to review. Sheridan Road will
need to be reconstituted and funds have been put into the budget to accommodate this. Tony Fox
moved to approve the 2020 draft budget. Chris Bell seconded it. Motion passed.
6. New Commissioners
There are two seats available to be a commissioner. Chris Bell suggested Allen Smith who is a property
owner and was the Ex-President of Rogers Park Builders Group. Cynthia Ryan will also reach out to Nick
Andersen who is the new General Manager of Hampton Inn.
7. Electrical Boxes Update
Cynthia working on the application.
8. Loyola Contribution Financials
There were no questions regarding the Loyola Contribution Financials.
9. Program Tracker 2019
There were no questions regarding the Program Tracker.

 10. Old Business
Chris Bell brought up sponsorship for the music festival that will take place on Sheridan Road &
Columbia on Sat., July 13, 2019. Jennifer Clark moved to approve $2500 sponsorship. Tony Fox seconded
it. Motion passed.

11. New Business
Meeting adjourned at 9:30 am.

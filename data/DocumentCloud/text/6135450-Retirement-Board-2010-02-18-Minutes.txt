HEARINGS
The Matter of Emanuel Purvis (Spousal Annuity Refund)
In attendance at this hearing were Mr. Everett A. Braden, Attorney for Mr. Purvis and Mr. Emanuel Purvis.
In the Matter of Emanuel Purvis, the Board considered the issue of Emanuel Purvis, an annuitant who was
married at the time he applied for annuity. Thereafter, Mr. Purvis applied for and was granted a Judgment
of Declaration of Invalidity of Marriage in the Circuit Court of Cook County, Illinois.
Mr. Heiss presented evidence and testimony on behalf of the Fund citing the case of Paul Powers v.
Retirement Board of the Policeman’s Annuity and Benefit Fund of Chicago (copy attached for reference
and incorporation into the record).
Mr. Braden presented evidence and testimony on behalf of Mr. Purvis citing the case of Josephine A.
Byrnes v. the Firemen’s Annuity and Benefit Fund of Chicago . (copy attached for reference and
incorporation into the record).
Following the oral evidence in this Hearing, the Board moved into Executive Session at 9:31 a.m. in
accordance with ILCS 120/2 (c)(4) to discuss evidence and testimony presented in the Matter of Emanuel
Purvis. Motion by Mr. Lux. Second by Ms. Neely.
Carried: Ayes – Messrs. Malatesta, Lux, Brejnak and Ms. Neely. Noes – None.
The Board moved out of Executive Session at 9:50 a.m. Motion by Ms. Neely. Second by Mr. Brejnak.
Carried: Ayes – Messrs. Malatesta, Lux, Brejnak and Ms. Neely. Noes – None.
No actions taken.
In regular session, the Board recognized the Declaration of Invalidity of Marriage granted to Mr. Purvis on
October 29, 2009 which declared the marriage between Emanuel and Anita Purvis invalid as of the date of
the marriage. Therefore, Mr. Purvis was granted a refund of the contributions for a spouse’s annuity in the
amount of $23,707.16. Motion by Mr. Brejnak. Second by Ms. Neely.
Carried: Ayes – Messrs. Malatesta, Lux, Brejnak and Ms. Neely. Noes – None.
(There is a court transcript of the oral evidence.)

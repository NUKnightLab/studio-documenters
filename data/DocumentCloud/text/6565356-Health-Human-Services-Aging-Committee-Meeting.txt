Youth Opportunities Unlimited

Unlimited and Ms. Emily Campbell, Associate Director and Senior Fellow for the
Center for Community Solutions, provided the Committee with an overview of their

youth and the means for opportunity and connection in Cuyahoga County for
disengaged youth. Discussion ensued.
Committee members asked questions of Mr. Dorn and Ms. Campbell pertaining to the
item, which they answered accordingly.

INVOCATION
Chairman Woronchak called upon Vice-Chair Bell for the introduction of Darcel Brown,
Legislative Research Assistant, who gave the following invocation:
“Lord, we thank you for this day. Thank you for hearing and answering the prayers of
those in this room. We ask that your grace be present during today’s deliberations. Provide
your wisdom to each Commissioner as they decree decisions that are best for those who live
in Wayne County. Renew their strength and reward them for their dedication as they continue
to serve the people of this County. These things we ask in Jesus’ name. AMEN.”

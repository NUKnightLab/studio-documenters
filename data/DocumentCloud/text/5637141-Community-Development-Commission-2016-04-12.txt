 III.

NEW BUSINESS
A.

CENTRAL WEST TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 27)
Request authority for the Department of Planning and Development to enter into a
negotiated sale with Western Adams, LLC for the disposition of the property
located at 147 S. Western Avenue in the Central West Tax Increment Financing
Redevelopment Project Area, to request alternative proposals and to approve the
sale of the property to Western Adams, LLC if no responsive alternative proposals
are received.
Project Manager: Lisa Washington
Motioned by Ward, seconded by Newsome. Approved 8-0. Yeas: Butler, Davis,
Newsome, Perez, Reifman, Rhodes, Ward and Carrott. Buford and Moreno not
present.
16-CDC-9

47th/KING DRIVE TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 3)

4148-56 S. Calumet Avenue in the 47th/King Drive Tax Increment Financing
Redevelopment Project Area, to request alternative proposals, and to approve the
sale of the property to NeighborSpace if no responsive alternative proposals are
received.
Project Manager: Michele A. Rhymes

Davis, Newsome, Perez, Reifman, Rhodes, Ward and Carrott. Moreno abstained.

(WARD 9)

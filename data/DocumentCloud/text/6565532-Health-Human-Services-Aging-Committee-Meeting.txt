1. CALL TO ORDER
Chairwoman Conwell called the meeting to order at 1:32 p.m.

Ms. Conwell asked Assistant Deputy Clerk Johnson to call the roll. Committee

There were no public comments given.

A motion was made by Mr. Miller, seconded by Mr. Jones and approved by unanimous
vote to approve the minutes from the June 19, 2019 meeting.

 exceed $630,826.00; authorizing the County Executive to execute the

declaring the necessity that this Resolution become immediately effective.

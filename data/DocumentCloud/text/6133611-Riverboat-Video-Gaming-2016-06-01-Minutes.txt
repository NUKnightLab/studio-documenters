MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING - RIVERBOAT
OPEN SESSION
June 2, 2016
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
Chicago, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
On Thursday, June 2, 2016 a Regular Meeting - Riverboat of the Illinois Gaming Board
(“Board”) was held on the 5th floor at 160 N. LaSalle at the Michael J. Bilandic Building,
Chicago, Illinois at 9:05 A.M. Present were the following: Chairman Don Tracy, Members
Thomas Dunn, Hector Alejandre, Member Dee Robinson, and Member Steve Dolins.
APPROVAL OF MINUTES – Open Session
Member Alejandre moved to approve the open session minutes from its Regular Meeting of
April 21, 2016. Member Dolins seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion
unanimously by roll call vote.
CHAIRMAN'S COMMENTS –
The Chairman commented on Minority spend items.
ADMINISTRATOR'S COMMENTS –
Administrator Ostrowski welcomed new Board Member Steve Dolins and thanked Lee Gould for
his service to the Board and the State of Illinois.
BOARD POLICY ITEM:
Proposed Amended to Riverboat Rules
Member Robinson moved to amend Section 245 of the Board's Rules by authorizing Staff to
submit a proposal for First Notice Filing with the Secretary of State upon final review and
approval by the Administrator. Member Dunn seconded the motion. The Board approved the
motion unanimously by roll call vote.

(Board Action / Vote Required)
Hollywood Casino Aurora, LLC d/b/a Hollywood Casino Aurora, and Hollywood

 both Hollywood Casino Aurora, LLC d/b/a Hollywood Casino Aurora, and Hollywood

Des Plaines Development Limited Partnership d/ba Harrah's Joliet Hotel & Casino
– Michele Wolff – Staff Internal Auditor – Level 1
Member Robinson moved that the Board approve Michele Wolff as a Level One
Occupational Licensee in her position as Staff Internal Auditor of Des Plaines
Development Limited Partnership d/b/a Harrah's Joliet Hotel & Casino. Member Dolins
seconded the motion. The motion passed unanimously by roll call vote.

 REQUEST FOR HEARING:
Pamela Grant
Member Dunn moved to rescind the Board's April 2016 denial of the occupational license
application of Pamela Grant and grant her a Level 2 Occupational License. Member
Alejandre seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call
vote.
DISCIPLINARY ACTIONS:
Danny Weatherby
Jolena Baker
Mildred Lynn
Member Alejandre moved to revoke the occupational license of Danny Weatherby,
Jolena Baker and Mildred Lynn due to their conduct of overcharging patrons, said
action to take effect 21 days from the date of service of the Complaint, unless an Answer
to the Complaint is filed within that time frame pursuant to Board Rule 1125. Member
Dunn seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call
vote.
Ricardo Lopez (Level 2 occupational licensee)
Member Robinson moved to revoke the occupational license of Ricardo Lopez due to his
conduct of abandoning his security post in the middle of shift without approval, said action to
take effect 21 days from the date of service of the Complaint, unless an Answer to the Complaint
is filed within that time frame pursuant to Board Rule 1125. Member Dolins seconded the
motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
At 9:18 A.M. Member Dunn motioned to adjourn and Member Dolins seconded the motion. All
Members voted in favor of adjournment.

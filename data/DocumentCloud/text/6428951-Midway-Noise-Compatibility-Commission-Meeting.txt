REPORT OF THE CHICAGO DEPARTMENT OF AVIATION (“CDA”)
A. 2nd Quarter 2017 Airport Noise Management System (ANMS) Report
Mr. Frame asked Mr. Jackson of Landrum & Brown to present the 2nd Quarter 2017
Airport Noise Management System Report.
Mr. Jackson provided a brief overview of the information contained in the report
and proceeded with the details.
Runway Use Report
In the 2nd Quarter the majority of arrivals and departures were on Runway 04 L/R.
April – The most used departure runway was 04 L/R going northeast with 43% of
all departures and 22 R/L with 32%, followed by 31C with 23% and last 13C with
2%.
The most used arrival runway coming in from the northeast was 04 L/R with 48%,
then 22 R/L with 31%, followed by 31C with 19% and last 13C with 2%.
May – The most used departure runway was 04 L/R with 449 followed by 22 R/L
with 27%, next 31C with 23% and last 13C with 1%.
The most used arrival runway was 04 L/R with 52% of all arrivals, followed by 22
R/L with 25%, then 31C with 22% and 13C with 1%.
June – The most used departure runway was 22 R/L with 38% followed by 31C
with 32%, then 04 L/R with 27% and last 13C with 3%.
The most used arrival runway was 22 R/L with 37%, followed by 31C with 30%,
and 04 L/R with 30% and last 13C with 3%.
Airline and Aircraft Fleet Mix Report
There was an average of 720 operations per day with 96 of those occurring at
night. Southwest Airlines had the highest operations with an average of 482
operations per day with 79 of those occurring at night. The commission members
identified an error on the bottom half of the page and Mr. Frame stated that the
CDA would make the corrections.

Noise Hotline Report
In the 2nd Quarter there was a total of 43,736 complaints. The highest number of
complaints came from Burbank with 29,096 and Justice with 6,233. This
significant number of complaints were from 1 address and 81% of all complaints
came from 2 addresses. There was a substantial increase in the number of
complaints in the 2nd Quarter of 2017 compared to previous the 1st Quarter of
2017. The number of communities that submitted complaints in the 2nd Quarter
was greater than previous months.

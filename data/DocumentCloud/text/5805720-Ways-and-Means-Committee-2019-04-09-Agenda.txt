2.

Communication dated December 13, 2018 (received December 19th) from
Warren Evans, Wayne County Executive, forwarding Executive Order 2018-02
(Executive Order to Control Expenditures) to take action necessary to ensure the
solvency and fiscal stability of Wayne County; the County Executive advises that

3.

Communication dated March 11, 2019 (received March 29th) requesting
Commission approval of a proposed amendment to the 2018-2019
Appropriations Ordinance (Enrolled Ordinance No. 2018-683) in accordance with
Budget Adjustment No. 2019-35-125 certifying revenue in the amount of $45,000
in the County Health Fund (221); the County Executive advises that the budget
adjustment for the Department of Health, Veterans and Community Wellness
serves to certify additional revenue awarded through the Comprehensive
Planning and Budgeting Contract (CPBC) for the special project titled “Surge
Capacity for Targeted Intervention of Hepatitis A Local Health Department
Workplan”; the funds will be allocated to Personnel and Services and Contractual
Services. (2019-35-125)

4.

Communication dated March 7, 2019 (received March 29th) requesting
Commission approval of a proposed amendment to the 2018-2019
Appropriations Ordinance (Enrolled Ordinance No. 2018-683) in accordance with
Budget Adjustment No. 2019-35-130 certifying revenue in the amount of $4,100
in the County Health Fund (221); the County Executive advises that the budget
adjustment for the Department of Health, Veterans and Community Wellness
serves to certify revenue received for the Michigan Department of Environmental
Quality (MDEQ) Clean Michigan Initiative-Clean Water Fund grant used for
beach monitoring; the funds are allocated to Personnel, Fringes, Pension and
Operating Expenses. (2019-35-130)

5.

Communication dated March 22, 2019 (received March 29th) requesting
Commission approval of a proposed amendment to the 2018-2019
Appropriations Ordinance (Enrolled Ordinance No. 2018-683) in accordance with
Budget Adjustment No. 2019-35-135 certifying revenue in the amount of $10,000
in the General Fund (101); the County Executive advises that the budget
adjustment serves to certify a grant to the Wayne County Sheriff’s Office from
AAA Michigan to be used for training focused on impaired driving; this grant was
received after the FY 18/19 budget was adopted. (2019-35-135)

6.

Communication dated March 6, 2019 (received March 22nd) requesting
Commission approval of a proposed amendment to the 2018-2019
Appropriations Ordinance (Enrolled Ordinance No. 2018-683) in accordance with
Budget Adjustment No. 2019-35-129 reallocating expenditures in the amount of
$65,472: $16,686 in the General Fund (101), $24,393 in the Drug Enforcement
Program Fund (265) and $24,393 in the Jail Commissary Fund (575); the County
Executive advises that the budget adjustment for the Sheriff’s Office serves to
reallocate funds in the amended budget in order to provide sufficient funding for 3
temporary employees, one each in the Sheriff’s Administration, Drug
Enforcement and Jail Commissary units. (2019-35-129)

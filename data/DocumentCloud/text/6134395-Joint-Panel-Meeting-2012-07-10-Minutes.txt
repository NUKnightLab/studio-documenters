III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES

Chairman Zimmerman called for approval of the minutes of the June 12, 2012 meeting. Member Washington
moved to approve the minutes. Member Besson seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a
voice vote.
IV.

LEGISLATIVE/FISCAL REPORTS

Executive Director Mlynski reported various legislative issues. She then presented the fiscal report, reviewing
in detail the general ledger sheet and the disbursement record. The Board then discussed and questioned
various aspects of the fiscal report.
VI.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Zimmerman called for a motion to adjourn. Member Besson made a motion to adjourn the meeting,
and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Joint Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations
Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 14th day of August, 2012.

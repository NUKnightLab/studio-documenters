RETIREMENT SYSTEM CIVIL SERVANT TITLES
BY TRUSTEE NEARY – SUPPORTED BY TRUSTEE NAGLICK
WHEREAS, on February 25, 2016, the Executive Director presented a request to the Board
of Trustees (the “Board”) to change the Retirement System’s civil servant employee titles to
the newly-created titles utilized by the Finance Department, Office of the Chief Financial
Officer, and
WHEREAS, the Retirement System is technically a division of the Finance Department of
the City of Detroit, and
WHEREAS, by virtue of a 2009 ruling by Judge Giovan of the Wayne County Circuit Court,
the Retirement System's Executive Director has full authority over the employees of the
System, and
WHEREAS, the change in titles will result in rate increases for non-appointed employees
but will not increase the Retirement System's administration budget, and
WHEREAS, this matter was discussed in open session, therefore be it
RESOLVED, the Board accepts the recommendation of the Executive Director and adopts
the titles and rate increases as presented by the Executive Director:

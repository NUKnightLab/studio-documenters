Walter L. Miller, Jr. v. Sheila M. Miller
Wayne County Circuit Court Case No. 14-105676-DM
Resolution Moved By: Trustee Cowan - Supported By: Trustee Perkins
WHEREAS, the Board of Trustees is in receipt of a Domestic Relations Order for the
General Retirement System of the City of Detroit (the “Order”) dated September 16,
2019, wherein Sheila M. Miller, the Alternate Payee, is awarded certain rights to the
benefits of Walter L. Miller, Jr., the Participant, and
WHEREAS, pursuant to the terms of the Order, the Alternate Payee is entitled to a
portion of both the Participant’s defined benefit portion and defined contribution portion,
if applicable, of his retirement allowance payable from the Retirement System, and
WHEREAS, said matter had been discussed with legal counsel who has opined that the
applicable terms of said court order are consistent with the provisions of the Retirement
System and applicable law, therefore be it
RESOLVED, that the Board acknowledges receipt of said court order, and the Board
will pay pension benefits consistent with the order as soon as administratively feasible,
and further
RESOLVED, that a copy of this resolution be immediately attached as the top sheet of
the pension file and other appropriate records be kept for the Retirement System
relative to this matter, and further
RESOLVED, that copies of this resolution be sent to Walther L. Miller, Jr., the
Participant; and Sheila M. Miller, the Alternate Payee.

Yeas: Anthony, Benson, Brown, Cowan, Hall-Wagner, Naglick, Perkins, Sheehan and
Chairperson Nickleberry– 9
Nays: None

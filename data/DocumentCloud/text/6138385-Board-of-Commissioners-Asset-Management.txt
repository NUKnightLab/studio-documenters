Presented by: ANNA ASHCRAFT, Director, Real Estate Management Division
PROPOSED MISCELLANEOUS ITEM OF BUSINESS
Department:

Real Estate Management

Summary: Requesting permission to refer a real estate transaction involving a lease at 28 N. Clark, for the
Clerk of the Circuit Court to the Asset Management Committee of the Board, for discussion in executive session,
and possible action in open session.
This request for a closed meeting is made pursuant to an exception to the Illinois Open Meetings Act, 5 ILCS
120/2(c)(6).
Legislative History: 4/1/15 Board of Commissioners referred to the Asset Management Committee.
A motion was made by Commissioner Silvestri, seconded by Commissioner Tobolski, that this
Miscellaneous Item of Business and the following four action recommendations be recommended for
approval: (1) Approval of the Termination Option Agreement, granting the Landlord the option to
terminate the Lease in accordance with the terms of the agreement; (2) Authorize the President or the
Real Estate Director to execute and deliver the Termination Option Agreement and any other documents
necessary to effectuate the option; (3) Authorize the Comptroller to pay the termination fee of $807,650

 to the Landlord; (4) Approval to add LL-12, consisting of 21,554 square feet, to the County’s occupancy at
the Daley Center, and relocating the Clerk of the Circuit Court Child Support function to LL-12. The
motion carried by the following vote:
Ayes:

Chairman Murphy, Vice-Chairman Butler, Schneider, Silvestri and Tobolski (5)

Absent:

García, Gorman, Sims and Steele (4)

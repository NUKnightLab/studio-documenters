STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on May 19, 2015
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, N703, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Board; Richard Lewis, Member of the Board; Kathryn
Zeledon Nelson, General Counsel; Sarah Kerley, Deputy General Counsel; Anna Hamburg-Gal, Associate General
Counsel; and staff. Melissa Mlynski, Executive Director; and staff members participated via video conference from
Capitol City Training Center, 130 Mason Street, Room 104 in Springfield. Chairman Gierut welcomed Kathryn Zeledon
Nelson as the board’s new General Counsel.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD APRIL 16, 2015

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the April 16, 2015 meeting. Member Anderson moved to approve
the minutes. Member Lewis seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gierut asked Executive Director Mlynski to present the Local Panel administrative actions report. She
presented the report and summarized the highlights. The Board examined the report and discussed.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.
Case No. L-CA-10-045
International Brotherhood of Teamsters, Local 700/City of Chicago

General Counsel Nelson called the case, noted that no party filed exceptions to Administrative Law Judge Katherine
Vanek’s Recommended Decision and Order and asked whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own
motion. The Board declined to do so.
2.
Case No. L-CA-14-063
International Brotherhood of Teamsters, Local 700/County of Cook and Sheriff of Cook County
General Counsel Nelson called the case, noted that no party filed exceptions to Administrative Law Judge Anna
Hamburg-Gal’s Recommended Decision and Order and asked whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own
motion. The Board declined to do so.
VI.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded it was
unnecessary.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Lewis moved to adjourn the meeting, and the Board passed it
unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board, in
Chicago, Illinois, this 9th day of June, 2015.

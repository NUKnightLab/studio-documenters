EMERGENCY CONTRACT

Call Meeting to Order
President O' Brien presiding, called the meeting to order at 3:00 p.m.

Roll Call
Present:

7 - Frank Avila; Patricia Horton; Gloria Alitto Majewski; Kathleen Therese
Meany; Cynthia M. Santos; Debra Shore; and Terrence J. O'Brien

Absent:

1 - Barbara J. McGowan

Clerk to Read Call of the Meeting
At the direction of President Terrence J. O’Brien, there will be a special
Meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the Metropolitan Water
Reclamation District of Greater Chicago (District) on Wednesday, March 11,
2009 at 3:00 p.m. in the Board Room, 100 East Erie Street, Chicago, Illinois.
The purpose of the meeting is to authorize emergency funding relating to the
recommission of the Mainstream Pumping Station.
/s/Jacqueline Torres
Clerk

A motion was made by Gloria Alitto Majewski, seconded by Kathleen Therese
Meany, that the Call of the Meeting be Published and Filed. The motion
carried by acclamation. Chorus of ayes - No nays

Authority to increase the purchase order and amend the agreement for
Emergency Contract 09-900-01, Dewater, Inspect, Repair and Otherwise
Recommission the Mainstream Pumping Station, to Jay Dee Contractors,
Inc. in an amount of $900,000.00 from an amount of $100,000.00 to an
amount of $1,000,000.00, Account 901-30000-667220, Purchase Order
5000982
A motion was made by Gloria Alitto Majewski, seconded by Kathleen Therese
Meany, that this Agenda Item be Adopted. The motion carried by the following
roll call vote:

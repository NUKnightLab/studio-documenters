Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on September 14, 2010

II.

PRESENT

Jacalyn J. Zimmerman, Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Board; Edward E. Sadlowski,
Member of the Board; John F. Brosnan, Executive Director; Jerald S. Post, General Counsel; and staff
members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD AUGUST 10, 2010

Chairman Zimmerman called for approval of the minutes of the August 10, 2010 meeting. Member
Anderson moved to approve the minutes. Member Sadlowski seconded. The Board passed the motion
unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Zimmerman asked Executive Director Brosnan to distribute the Local Panel administrative
actions report for the period of time from August 1 through August 31, 2010. He distributed the report
to the Board and summarized its highlights. The Board examined the report and discussed it.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.
Case No. L-CA-09-009
Illinois Fraternal Order of Police, Lodge 7/City of Chicago

Chairman Zimmerman called the case. General Counsel Post reviewed the issues presented by the
exceptions to Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) Rios' Recommended Decision and Order (RDO), and
recommended that the Board reverse her determination and dismiss the underlying complaint. After
discussion, Member Anderson moved to accept General Counsel Post's recommendation. Member
Sadlowski seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
2.
Case No. L-CA-07-077
International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers, Local 134/Chicago Transit Authority
Chairman Zimmerman called the case, noting that no party filed exceptions to ALJ Tansino's RDO, and
asked whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own motion. The Board declined to do so.
3.
Case No. L-CA-10-042
Service Employees International Union, Local 73/City of Chicago
Chairman Zimmerman called the case, noting that Executive Director Brosnan issued a dismissal on
June 29, 2010, with an appeal filed. General Counsel Post reviewed the issues presented by the appeal,
and recommended that the Board uphold the executive director's order. After discussion, Member
Anderson moved to accept the executive director's order. Member Sadlowski seconded the motion, and
the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

 VI.

OTHER BOARD MATTERS

The Board heard and considered the fiscal and legislative reports. After a short discussion regarding
whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded that it was unnecessary at this time.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Zimmerman called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the
meeting, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor
Relations Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 5th day of October, 2010.

Notice of Illinois Gaming Board Meeting
Regular Board Meeting – Riverboat/Video Gaming
The Illinois Gaming Board will convene a regular meeting of the Board in Open Session on
Wednesday, January 30, 2019 at 9:00 A.M. in the Board’s Conference Room on the 3rd floor of the
Michael A. Bilandic Building, and immediately retire to Closed Executive Session. The Board will
reconvene its regular meeting in Open Session on Wednesday, January 30, 2019 at 10:00 A.M. on the 5th
floor of the Michael Bilandic Building. The subject matters to be discussed are included on the attached
proposed Riverboat/Video Gaming agenda.
Please note that the regular Riverboat/Video Gaming meeting agenda is typically posted 48
hours prior to the scheduled meeting date.

STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
STATE PANEL
Minutes of the State Panel Meeting
Held on April 12, 2016
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 1:00 P.M.— at One Natural Resources Way, Springfield, IL

II.

PRESENT

John Hartnett, Chairman; Michael Coli, Member of the Board; John Samolis, Member of the Board; Keith Snyder,
Member of the Board; Albert Washington, Member of the Board; Melissa Mlynski, Executive Director; and Sarah
Kerley, Deputy General Counsel. Kathryn Zeledon Nelson, General Counsel; Anna Hamburg-Gal, Associate General
Counsel; and staff members participated via phone from the Board’s Chicago office.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD MARCH 8, 2016

Chairman Hartnett called for approval of the minutes of the March 8, 2016, meeting. Member Washington moved to
approve the minutes. Member Samolis seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Hartnett asked Executive Director Mlynski to present the Executive Director’s reports. She presented the
reports and summarized the highlights.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.

Case Nos. S-UC-14-006 and S-UC-12-034
Service Employees International Union, Local 73, CTW/CLA/Secretary of State

Chairman Hartnett called the case. General Counsel Nelson stated that the Board had heard oral arguments on this
matter at the Board’s March meeting, which was recorded by a court reporter. Member Washington moved to accept
Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) Sarah Kerley’s Recommended Decision and Order (RDO). Member Samolis
seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
2.

Case No. S-RC-15-054
International Union of Operating Engineers, Local 150/DuPage County

Chairman Hartnett called the case. General Counsel Nelson noted that no party filed exceptions to ALJ Thomas
Allen’s RDO, and asked whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own motion. The Board declined to
do so.
VI.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Hartnett called for a motion to adjourn. Member Coli moved to adjourn the meeting, and the Board passed
it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the State Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations
Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 10th day of May, 2016.

MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING - RIVERBOAT
OPEN SESSION
July 18, 2013
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
On Thursday, July 18, 2013 a Regular Meeting - Riverboat of the Illinois Gaming Board
(“Board”) was held on the 5th floor at 160 N. LaSalle at the Michael J. Bilandic Building,
Chicago, Illinois at 10:45 A.M. Present were the following: Members Maribeth Vander Weele,
Lee Gould, Michael Holewinski and Chairman Aaron Jaffe.
APPROVAL OF MINUTES
Member Holewinski moved that the Board approve the open session minutes of its Regular
Meeting of June 20, 2013. Member Gould seconded the motion. The Board approved the
motion unanimously by roll call vote.
OWNER LICENSEE ITEMS
•

Boyd Gaming Corporation - Amended and Restated Existing Senior Credit
Facility
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Gould moved to approve Boyd Gaming
Corporation’s request for Board approval to waive the Two-Meeting Rule;
approve the request to revise the terms of the Amended and Restated
Existing Senior Credit Facility as delineated in its July 3, 2013
correspondence; and authorize the Administrator to approve the financing
commitments between the financing tranches prior to the execution of the
individual transactions. Member Vander Weele seconded the motion. The Board
approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.

•

Caesars Entertainment Corporation – Financing Transaction
Based on a review of staff’s investigation, Member Vander Weele moved to deny
Caesars Entertainment Corporation’s $300 million First Lien Notes due 2020
and the indenture terms therein that apply to Southern Illinois Riverboat
Casino Cruises, Inc. d/b/a Harrah’s Metropolis Casino and the general
partner ownership interests of Des Plaines Development Limited Partnership
d/b/a Harrah’s Joliet Casino and Hotel. Chairman Jaffe seconded the motion.
The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.

 •

Casino Queen, Inc. d/b/a Casino Queen – Owner’s License Renewal
Based on a review of staff's investigation, Member Vander Weele moved that the
Board approve the Owner's License of Casino Queen Inc. d/b/a Casino
Queen for a term of 4 years expiring in July, 2017 and designate the
following individuals and positions as Key Persons of Casino Queen, Inc.:
o
CQ Holding Company, Inc.
o
Jeffrey K. Watson
o
Robert R. Barrows
o
Charles Bidwell, III
o
James G. Koman
o
Timothy J. Rand
Member Gould seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion
unanimously by roll call vote.

SUPPLIER LICENSEE ITEMS –
•

SHFL Entertainment, Inc. – License Renewal
Based on Staff’s investigation, Member Holewinski moved that the Board renew
SHFL Entertainment, Inc.’s Supplier’s License for a period of four years,
expiring in July 2017, and authorize it to supply the following products to
licensed Illinois casinos: deck shufflers and the necessary software for those
shufflers; table game layouts; and progressive systems for table games.
Furthermore, I move to designate the following individual and position as Key
Persons of SHFL Entertainment, Inc.:
o Chief Executive Officer; and
o Michael Galvin Isaacs.
Member Gould seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously
by roll call vote.

OCCUPATIONAL LICENSE APPROVAL AND DENIALS –
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Gould moved that the Board find suitable and
approve 34 applications for Level 2 Occupational Licenses and 116 applications for
Level 3 Occupational Licenses. Member Vander Weele seconded the motion. The
Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
At 10:56 A.M. Member Vander Weele motioned to adjourn and Member Holewinski seconded
the motion. All Members voted in favor of adjournment.

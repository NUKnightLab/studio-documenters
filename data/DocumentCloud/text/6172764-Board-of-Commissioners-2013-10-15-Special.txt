MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL PERSONNEL COMMITTEE
OF THE
CHICAGO HOUSING AUTHORITY
September 5, 2013

The Special Personnel Committee of the Chicago Housing Authority held a Special Meeting on
Thursday, September 5, 2013, at approximately 12:00 noon at the CHA Corporate Offices,
located at 60 East Van Buren, Room 306, in Chicago, Illinois.
The meeting was called to order by Chairwoman Adela Cepeda, and upon roll call, those present
and absent were as follows:
Present:
Adela Cepeda
Harriet Johnson
Jack Markowski
Absent:

Bridget Reidy

Also present but not counted for the quorum was Chairwoman Z. Scott in addition to Ruth
Krugly, Board Counsel, Jennifer Hoyle, Executive Administrator, BOC; Lee Chuc-Gill,
Secretary to the Board of Commissioners and the general public.
There being a quorum present the meeting duly convened and business was transacted as
follows:
Pursuant to the Open Meetings Act, Chairwoman Cepeda announced that Committee members
were notified of the meeting via a Notice of Special Meeting, as indicated below. The Special
Personnel Committee Meeting Notice was also sent to interested parties on CHA’s Notice List
and posted on CHA’s website and the main lobby of 60 East Van Buren, Chicago, IL.

At the recommendation of Board Counsel, and pursuant to the Open Meetings Act, 5 ILCS 120,
proper notice was given so that Commissioners could retire into Closed Meeting to discuss
matters under the following exceptions: personnel related matters under (c)(1) and pending,
probable or imminent litigation under (c)(11). Upon Motion made by Commissioner
Markowski, properly seconded by Commissioner Johnson, the Commissioners voted
unanimously to adjourn to Closed Meeting.
The Commissioners subsequently reconvened in Open Session and upon roll call, those present
and absent were as follows:
Present:
Adela Cepeda
Harriet Johnson
Jack Markowski
Absent:

Bridget Reidy
2

 Also present but not counted for the quorum was Chairwoman Z. Scott in addition to Ruth
Krugly, Board Counsel, Jennifer Hoyle, Executive Administrator, BOC and Lee Chuc-Gill,
Secretary to the Board of Commissioners.
There being a quorum present, the meeting duly convened and business was transacted as
follows:
Based on CHA By-Laws, and in accordance with the Open Meetings Act, if a quorum of the
Commissioners is physically present at any meeting, a majority of the quorum may allow a
Commissioner to attend a meeting by electronic means if the Commissioner is prevented from
physically attending due to personal illness or employment purposes. Since Commissioner
Reidy was unable to physically attend the meeting, upon motion made and properly seconded,
the attending Commissioners voted unanimously to allow Commissioner Reidy to continue
participating in the meeting via telephone.
Per Chairwoman Cepeda, during closed meeting, commissioners discussed certain matters
pertaining to a personnel matter and pending, probable, imminent litigation. Since there was
no action required by the Special Personnel Committee the meeting of the Special Personnel
Committee was adjourned.

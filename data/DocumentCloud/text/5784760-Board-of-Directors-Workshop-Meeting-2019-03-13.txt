and the Economic Development Corporation of Wayne County, came before the

expansion project.

and Economy Team, came before the Board in support of the proposed sale of
land that GLWA currently leases from the City of Detroit, in support of the
Chrysler sale.

projects and the proposed sale of property.

and the City of Detroit Brownfield Redevelopment Authority, came before the

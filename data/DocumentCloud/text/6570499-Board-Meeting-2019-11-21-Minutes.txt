Midwest Generation, LLC v. IEPA (Water – Thermal

Layers, Inc. v. IEPA (Water – Tax Certification) – The Board

facilities for preferential tax treatment under the Property Tax
Code (35 ILCS 200/11-10 (2018)).

4-0

4-0

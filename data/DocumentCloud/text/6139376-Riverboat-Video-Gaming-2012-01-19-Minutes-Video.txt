On Thursday, January 19, 2012 a Regular Meeting - Video of the Illinois Gaming Board
(“Board”) was held at the Rivers Casino in Des Plaines, Illinois.
On Thursday, January 19, 2012 at approximately 9:30 A.M. the following Board members were
present: Chairman Jaffe, Members Z. Scott, Maribeth Vander Weele, Michael Holewinski and
Lee Gould. At 9:48 A.M. on Thursday, January 19, 2012, Chairman Jaffe called the meeting to
order. Pursuant to Section 2(c), paragraphs (1), (11), (14) and (21) of the Open Meetings Act
and Section 6(d) of the Riverboat gambling Act, Member Holewinski moved that the Board
retire to Closed Session to discuss the items listed under Closed Session on today’s agenda
and relating to the following subject matters:

Personnel matters;
Investigations concerning applicants and licensees; and
Closed session minutes.

Member Vander Weele seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by
voice vote. The Board recessed and went into open session at 11:10 A.M. Present were the
following: Chairman Jaffe, Member Gould, Member Vander Weele, Member Holewinski and
Member Scott.
APPROVAL OF MINUTES – Open Session
Member Scott moved that the Board approve the open session minutes of its Regular
Meeting of December 15, 2011. Member Vander Weele seconded the motion. The Board
approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote vote.
MANUFACTURER, DISTRIBUTOR AND SUPPLIER ITEMS
Items for Consideration –
●

AGS Illinois, LLLP – New Manufacturer’s, Distributor’s and Supplier’s License
Based on a review of staff’s investigation and recommendation, Member Vander Weele
moved that the Board approve AGS Illinois, LLLP for a Manufacturer’s,
Distributor’s and Supplier’s License effective January 19, 2012 for a period of one
(1) year, expiring in January 2013. Member Gould seconded the motion. The Board
approved the motion unanimously by roll call.

 TERMINAL OPERATOR ITEMS
●

Terminal Operators
Based on a review of staff’s investigation and recommendation, Member Gould moved
that the Board approve the following entities for Terminal Operator’s Licenses
effective January 19, 2012 for a period of one (1) year, expiring in January 2013:
AAA City Vendors Gaming LLC; Andy’s Video Gaming Co.; B & B Harris
Management, LLC; Cardinal Gaming, LLC; Diamond Gaming of Illinois, Inc.;
Heck Gaming, LLC; Illinois Gaming Systems, LLC; Illinois Video Slot
Management Corp.; Illinois Gold Rush, Inc.; J&J Ventures Gaming, LLC; M&A
Gaming, LLC; Metro East Video Gaming, Inc.; Paramount Gaming of Illinois, Inc.;
Quad Gaming, Inc.; TAV Gaming, Inc.; and Video Gaming Services, LLC
Member Scott seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll
call vote.

●

Chicago Touch Screen, LLC – Terminal Operator
Based on a review of staff’s investigation and recommendation, Member Holewinski
moved that the Board deny Chicago Touch Screen, LLC for a Terminal Operator’s
License. Member Scott seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion
unanimously by roll call vote.

At 11:30 A.M. Member Scott motioned to adjourn and Member Gould seconded the motion. All
Members voted in favor of adjournment.

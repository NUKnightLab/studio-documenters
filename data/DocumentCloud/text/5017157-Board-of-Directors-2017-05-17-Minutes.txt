CALL TO ORDER
Chairperson Mia Grillier called the meeting to order at 3:30 PM.
FINANCIAL YEAR 2017-2018 BUDGET REVIEW
Ms. Grillier encouraged the committee to review the proposed DBRA Budget for FY 20172018 as well as prior year DBRA budgets.
Ms. Kanalos explained that the Committee is meeting, per the Board’s request in 2016,
to review the budget in advance of the Board setting the public hearing for the budget.
Committee members discussed questions regarding the format of the budget as well as
the proposed changes in budget line items
Ms. Grillier, citing no further discussion, called for a motion recommending submission of
the proposed FY 2017-2018 DBRA Budget to the full Board and presenting the proposed
budget at a DBRA public hearing.
On a motion by Ms. McClain, seconded by Ms. Washington, submitting the
proposed FY 2017-2018 DBRA Budget to the full DBRA Board of Directors and
presenting the proposed budget at a DBRA public hearing was unanimously
approved.
PUBLIC COMMENT
None
ADJOURNMENT
Citing no further business, Ms. Grillier called for a motion to adjourn the meeting.
On a motion by Ms. Washington, seconded by Ms. McClain, the meeting was
unanimously adjourned at 3:43 PM.

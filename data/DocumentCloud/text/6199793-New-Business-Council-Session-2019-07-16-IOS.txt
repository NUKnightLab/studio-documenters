Section 2. The repeal provided for in Section 1 of this ordinance shall not affect: 1) any
offense or act committed or done before the enactment of this ordinance; 2) any penalty or
forfeiture incurred before the enactment of this ordinance; 3) any contract or right established or
accruing before the enactment of this ordinance; or 4) any pending prosecution, lawsuit, or
proceeding, of any judgment rendered before the enactment of this ordinance.

DOCKET #7047 - CHICAGO TITLE & TRUST AS TRUSTEE UTA #094089, c/o
Kanzler Farms, Owner, 810 Arlington Heights Road, Itasca, Illinois 60143, Application
(No. SU-20-15; Z00105). Submitted by same. Seeking a SPECIAL USE to continue
in the 1-2 General Industrial District for 6 more years rock crushing operation in
Section 32 of Hanover Township. Property consists of approximately 26 acres located
on the north side of West Bartlett Road approximately 515 feet east of Tameling Court
in Hanover Township. Intended use: A self-propelled, track mounted, portable rock
crushing machine. RECOMMENDATION: That the application be approved with
conditions as set forth.
The Village of Barlett by resolution objected to the Special Use and would consistently
object to the rock crushing operation as they had when the prior permit was issued.
The Zoning Board of Appeals also received a letter of objection from the Spring Lakes
Mobile Home Estates, located approximately 2,000 ft. West of the subject property.
The mobile home community feel that industrial zoning use no longer fits the
community.

MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING - VIDEO
OPEN SESSION
February 26, 2014
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
On Wednesday, February 26, 2014 a Regular Meeting - Video of the Illinois Gaming Board
(“Board”) was held on the 5th floor at 160 N. LaSalle at the Michael J. Bilandic Building,
Chicago, Illinois. The Open Session on Video Gaming started at approximately 10:27 A.M.
Present were Members Lee Gould, Maribeth Vander Weele and Michael Holewinski. Chairman
Aaron Jaffe participated via telephone.
APPROVAL OF MINUTES –
Member Gould moved that the Board approve the open session minutes of its Regular
Meeting of January 15, 2014. Member Vander Weele seconded the motion. The Board
approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
ADMINISTRATOR’S REPORT –
The Administrator stated that over 4,300 locations have been licensed, with 3,777 that are “live”.
There are over 15,137 machines in these locations. Staff had a conference call with the terminal
operators referencing illegal gambling pertaining to the Super Bowl. On the weekend in
question our investigators made spot checks at the licensed locations and found illegal gambling.
Disciplinary Complaints will be issued today along with a fine. Additional discipline up to and
including revocation will be considered in the future for illegal gambling.
PUBLIC COMMENTARY – Illinois Association of Licensed Terminal Operators
Scott Ferguson introduced himself to the Board and stated that the association’s membership
consists of licensed terminal operators.
SUPPLIER, DISTRIBUTOR AND MANUFACTURER LICNESEE ITEMS –

• Supplier Licensee Renewals: Ares Gaming Technologies LLC and Patriot
Gaming & Electronics, Inc.
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Holewinski moved that the Board renew the
Supplier Licenses of Ares Gaming Technologies LLC and Patriot Gaming &
Electronics, Inc., effective February 26, 2014, for a term of one year, expiring in
February 2015. Member Gould seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion
unanimously by roll call vote.

TECHNICIAN & TERMINAL HANDLER LICENSEE ITEMS –
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Holewinski moved that the Board approve 3
applications for Technician Licenses and 54 applications for Terminal Handler
Licenses and that the Administrator issue a Notice of Denial to the following
applicants: Kevin C. Dockham, Arron L. Schmitt, and William J. Shanklin.
Member Vander Weele seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion
unanimously by roll call vote.
VIDEO GAMING LOCATION LICENSEE ITEMS –
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Vander Weele moved that the Board approve
259 applications for Video Gaming Location Licenses, and that the Administrator
issue a Notice of Denial to the following applicants: Fat Cat’s, Inc., Blarney Castle,
Inc., and PAC Industries, Inc. Member Gould seconded the motion. The Board
approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.

DISCIPLINARY COMPLAINTS –
•

Based on staff’s investigation, Member Gould moved to issue Disciplinary
Complaints revoking the following licensees due to their conduct involving

the Complaints unless a $2,500.00 fine is paid or an Answer is properly filed
within that time period:
o Heroes West, LLC
o KMK Ventures, Inc.
o At The Post, LLC
o JJ’s Prime Time Pub, LLC
o Tommy’s on Waukegan, Inc.
o River Lee’s, Incorporated
o Crabby’s Pub, Incorporated
o Leslie Edgar Etchason Post #3999 VFW
o Shelbyville Lodge No. 1294 Loyal Order of Moose

Member Holewinski seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously
by roll call vote.
At 10:39 A.M. Member Vander Weele motioned to adjourn and Member Gould seconded the
motion. All Members voted in favor of adjournment.

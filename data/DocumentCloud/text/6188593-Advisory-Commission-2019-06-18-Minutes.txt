Community Events Grant Program
a. Weidl presented a $3,150 grant request from the Gill Park / Buena Circle Park Advisory
Council (GPBCPAC) for eighteen (18) events to be held from July - September of 2019.
i.
Weidl noted that some of the events occur on dates where other neighborhood
events are taking place. He also noted there there could be an opportunity to cut
costs by renting a portable toilet for the whole season, rather then for each event.
ii.
Shower recomended funding the five (5) major events that do not conflict with
other neighborhood events.
Motion to approve grant for GPBCPAC in the amount of $1,500.
Motion: Irpino
Second: Showers
VOTE: Approve – All
Motion Passes

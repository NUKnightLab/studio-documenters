STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on September 7, 2017
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman of the Local Panel; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Local Panel; Angela C. Thomas,
Member of the Local Panel; Helen J. Kim, General Counsel; Anna Hamburg-Gal, Associate General Counsel; and staff
members. Executive Director Kimberly Stevens participated by phone.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD August 8, 2017

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the August 8, 2017 meeting. Member Anderson moved to approve
the minutes. Member Thomas seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gierut asked Executive Director Stevens to present the Executive Director’s reports. She presented the reports
and summarized the highlights.
V.

REVIEW OF PROPOSED WRITTEN DECISIONS AND ORDERS
1.

Case Nos. L-CA-16-070 and L-CA-16-071
Megan E. Cook and Sheena Williamson/Cook County Recorder of Deeds

General Counsel Kim called the case. Member Thomas made a motion to approve the proposed written decision. Member
Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
2.

Case No. L-CB-17-021
Laura Wicik/American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31

General Counsel Kim called the case. Member Thomas made a motion to approve the proposed written decision. Member
Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VI.

BOARD ACTIONS
1. Case No. L-CA-15-042
Teamsters Local 700/County of Cook and Sheriff of Cook County

Associate General Counsel Hamburg-Gal called and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member
Anderson moved to reverse the Executive Director’s dismissal and issue a complaint for hearing. Member Thomas seconded
the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
2. Case No. L-CA-17-001
Michael J. Conroy/ City of Chicago (Fire Department)
Associate General Counsel Hamburg-Gal called and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member
Thomas moved to remand the Executive Director’s dismissal for further investigation. Member Anderson seconded the
motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

 3. Case No. L-CB-17-028
Dudlita Prewitt/American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31
General Counsel Kim called and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member Anderson moved
to accept the Executive Director’s dismissal and allow variance to accept Charging Party’s appeal. Member Thomas
seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VII.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded it was unnecessary.
VIII.

PUBLIC COMMENT

No members of the public were present.
IX.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board, in
Chicago, Illinois, this 17th day of October, 2017.

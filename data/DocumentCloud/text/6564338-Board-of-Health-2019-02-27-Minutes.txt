Thereupon, it was also moved by Mr. Wang, seconded by Dr. Williams, that the following
RESOLUTION (2019-27) be adopted:
BE IT RESOLVED to approve the application of an Across-the-Board 2019 salary increase of 2%, for
employees except ONA bargaining unit employees, contract employees, intermittent employees and
student appointments, and the Health Commissioner effective the first full pay period of January 2019.
The increases shall be applied as follows:
1) Employees whose salary is between the minimum and midpoint of their pay grade, shall
receive a 2% increase onto their base salary;
2) Employees whose salary is between the midpoint and maximum of their pay grade, shall
receive a 1% increase onto their base salary and a 1% increase as a lump sum payment that
does not alter their base pay;
3) Employees whose salary is above the maximum of their pay grade, shall receive a 2% increase
as a lump sum payment that does not alter their base pay.

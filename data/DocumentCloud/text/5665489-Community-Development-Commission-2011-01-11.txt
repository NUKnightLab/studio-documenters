III.

RECOGNITION OF ARNOLD RANDALL
Acting Chairman Jonathan Stein announced the resignation of Chairman Arnold Randall
and congratulated him on his appointment as General Superintendent of the Forest
Preserve District of Cook County. Arnold Randall was present to thank the commission
and remark on the important work that the CDC conducts.

 IV.

NEW BUSINESS
A.

WILSON YARD TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA AND
LAWRENCE/BROADWAY TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 46)
Request authority for the Department of Community Development to negotiate a
redevelopment agreement with Community Housing Partners XV L.P. for
redevelopment of the property located at 4509 N. Hazel Street, 4426 N. Magnolia
Avenue, 912-14 W. Montrose Avenue, and 4813 N. Winthrop Avenue in the
Wilson Yard and Lawrence/Broadway Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment
Project Areas, and to recommend to the City Council of the City of Chicago the
designation of Community Housing Partners XV L.P. as Developer.
Project Manager: Oneida Pate
Motioned by Carrott, seconded by White. Approved 9-0. Yeas: Carrott, Gomez,
Kostiner, Logan, Martin, Sahli, Todman, White and Stein.
11-CDC-01

B.

ENGLEWOOD NEIGHBORHOOD TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT
AREA (WARD 16)
Request authority to enter into a negotiated sale with AutoZone Development
Corp. for the disposition of the property located at 6630-38 S. Halsted Street in
the Englewood Neighborhood Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project
Area, to request alternative proposals, and to approve the sale of the property to
AutoZone Development Corp. if no responsive alternative proposals are received.
Project Manager: Michelle Nolan
Motioned by Martin, seconded by Sahli. Approved 9-0. Yeas: Carrott, Gomez,
Kostiner, Logan, Martin, Sahli, Todman, White and Stein.

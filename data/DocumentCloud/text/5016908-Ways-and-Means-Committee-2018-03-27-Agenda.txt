2.

Communication dated January 30, 2018 (received March 3rd) requesting
Commission approval of a proposed amendment to the 2017-2018
Appropriations Ordinance (Enrolled Ordinance No. 2017-635) in accordance with
Budget Adjustment No. 2018-35-080 certifying revenue in the amount of $6,500
in the Property Tax Forfeiture Fund (501) and certifying revenue in the amount of
$6,500 in the Delinquent Tax Revolving Fund-2015 (535); the County Executive
advises that the Treasurer’s Office proposes to increase administrative charges
by increasing Charges for Services revenue in the Property Tax Forfeiture Fund
(501) in order to cover additional costs associated with temporary personnel to
needed to complete a bank review, policy and procedure manual, and other
office updates and the Delinquent Tax Revolving Fund-2015 (535) will use
revenue from Use of Fund Balance in order to fund the additional expense from
Fund 501 relative to the additional temporary personnel. (2018-35-080)

3.

Communication dated February 6, 2018 (received March 3rd) requesting
Commission approval of a proposed amendment to the 2017-2018
Appropriations Ordinance (Enrolled Ordinance No. 2017-635) in accordance with
Budget Adjustment No. 2018-35-085 certifying revenue in the amount of $60,000
in the Property Tax Forfeiture Fund (501) and certifying revenue in the amount of
$60,000 in the Delinquent Tax Revolving Fund-2015 (535); the County Executive
advises that the Treasurer’s Office proposes to increase administrative charges
by increasing Charges for Services revenue in the Property Tax Forfeiture Fund
(501) in order to cover additional costs associated with temporary personnel to
complete various clerical and customer service duties during the busy season in
the Treasurer’s Office and the Delinquent Tax Revolving Fund-2015 (535) will
use revenue from Use of Fund Balance in order to fund the additional expense
from Fund 501 relative to the additional temporary personnel. (2018-35-085)

4.

Communication dated February 28, 2018 (received March 13th) requesting
Commission approval of a proposed amendment to the 2017-2018
Appropriations Ordinance (Enrolled Ordinance No. 2017-635) in accordance with
Budget Adjustment No. 2018-35-106 reallocating expenditures in the amount of
$15,000 in the General Fund (101); the County Executive advises that the
proposed budget adjustment for the Sheriff’s Office serves to reallocate funds in
the amended budget in order to provide sufficient funding for shredding services
at various locations. (2018-35-106)

5.

Communication dated February 7, 2018 (received February 21st) requesting

$11,094 in the General Fund (101); the County Executive advises that this
proposed budget adjustment for the Prosecuting Attorney serves to reallocate
funds in the amended budget for the Encourage Arrests program to the
Administration activity in order to provide sufficient funding for project consultant
costs. (2018-35-070)

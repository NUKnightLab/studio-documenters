There will be a meeting of the Legislation, Intergovernmental and Veterans Relations
Committee of the Board of Commissioners of Cook County on Wednesday, February 18,
2009 at the hour of 9:30 A.M. in the Board Room, Room 569, County Building, 118 North Clark
Street, Chicago, Illinois to consider the following:

transmitting a Communication, dated January 29, 2009:

Relations on February 4, 2009.

SECURITIES LENDING DISCUSSION
INVESTMENT OFFICER MIKE KRUSHENA AND MESSRS. J.M. RUSCETTI AND CALVIN
BORN, JR. (OF WILSHIRE) DISCUSSED SECURITIES LENDING AT LENGTH,
FOCUSING ON THE SYSTEM’S CURRENT SECURITIES LENDING PROGRAM
WITH BANK OF NEW YORK MELLON.
THE GENTLEMEN NOTED THAT CURRENTLY WITHIN THE SYTEM’S
SECURITIES LENDING PROGRAM WITH BONY, THE SYSTEM IS NOT
INDEMNIFIED FOR CASH COLLATERAL RISK AND INVESTS CASH
COLLATERAL IN U.S. GOVERNMENT AND CORPORATE BOND REPO; ASSETBACKED FLOATING INSTRUMENTS; FLOATING RATE NOTE INSTRUMENTS;
CERTIFICATES OF DEPOSITS; AND TIME DEPOSITS.
CURRENTLY THE
SYSTEM IS INDEMNIFIED AND ALLOWS U.S. GOVERNMENT INSTRUMENTS
ONLY AS IT RELATES TO NON-CASH COLLATERAL.
FOUR (4) BONY SCENARIOS WERE DISCUSSED AS WELL, DETAILING WHAT
THE SYSTEM WOULD EARN FOR THE FISCAL YEAR ENDING JUNE 30, 2017
IF NO CHANGES WERE MADE TO THE SYSTEM’S CURRENT PROGRAM;
EXPANDING NON-CASH COLLATERAL TYPES AND ALLOWING FOR EQUITY
REPO; WHAT THE SYSTEM WOULD EARN ANNUALLY IF THE SYSTEM’S
CURRENT PROGRAM WERE CHANGED TO ALLOW FOR U.S. GOVERNMENT
REPO ONLY; AND WHAT THE SYSTEM WOULD EARN ANNUALLY IF THE
SYSTEM’S CURRENT PROGRAM WERE CHANGED TO ALLOW FOR U.S.
GOVERNMENT AND EQUITY REPO ONLY.
THIS MATTER WILL BE DISCUSSED FURTHER AT A FUTURE MEETING
(FOCUSING ON INVESTMENT POLICY IMPLICATIONS AND PERMITTED BONY
INVESTMENTS). CHAIRMAN SMITH NOTED THAT SHOULD THE SYSTEM
ADOPT ONE OF THE FOUR BONY SENARIOS, THE SYSTEM’S INVESTMENT
POLICY MAY HAVE TO BE AMENDED.

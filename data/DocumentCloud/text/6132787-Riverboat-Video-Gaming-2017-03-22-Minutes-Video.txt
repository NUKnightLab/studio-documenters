Regular Meeting, Open Session Minutes - Video
Illinois Gaming Board
Chicago, Illinois
March 23, 2017
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
On Thursday, March 23, 2017, a Regular Meeting - Video of the Illinois Gaming Board
(“Board”) was held on the 5th floor at 160 N. LaSalle at the Michael J. Bilandic Building,
Chicago, Illinois immediately following its Regular Meeting - Riverboat. Present were the
following: Chairman Don Tracy, Members Hector Alejandre and Dee Robinson. Member
Thomas Dunn participated via telephone.
APPROVAL OF OPEN SESSION MINUTES:
Member Alejandre moved that the Board approve the open session minutes from its
Regular Meeting of February 22, 2017. Member Robinson seconded the motion. The motion
passed unanimously by roll call vote.

DISCIPLINARY ACTIONS
Untimely Submissions to the Board
Member Alejandre moved to issue a Disciplinary Complaint fining Gold Rush
Amusements, Inc. $5,000 for failing to timely submit its renewal application to the
Board, said action to take effect 21 days from the date of service of the Complaint,
unless an Answer is properly filed within that timeframe. Member Robinson
seconded the motion. The motion passed unanimously by roll call vote.
Member Alejandre moved to issue a Disciplinary Complaint fining Accel
Entertainment Gaming, LLC $10,000 for associating with an unsuitable individual,
said action to take effect 21 days from the date of service of the Complaint, unless an
Answer is properly filed within that timeframe. Member Robinson seconded the
motion. The motion passed unanimously by roll call vote.
Failure to Maintain Minimum Qualifications for Licensure
Member Robinson moved to issue a Disciplinary Complaint revoking the Terminal
Handler License of Rafael Santano due to a criminal conviction, said action to take
effect 21 days from the date of service of the Complaint, unless an Answer is
properly filed within that timeframe. Member Alejandre seconded the motion. The
motion passed unanimously by roll call vote.
Active Tax Liability and Failure to Meet the Minimum Qualifications for Licensure
Member Alejandre moved to issue a Disciplinary Complaint revoking the
establishment license of MI CHAPALA INC. d/b/a Mi Chapala for having an active
tax liability, and for failing to maintain the minimum qualifications for licensure,

4

 said action to take effect 21 days from the date of service of the Complaint, unless an
Answer is properly filed within that timeframe. Member Robinson seconded the
motion. The motion passed unanimously by roll call vote.
Failure to Maintain Valid Liquor License
Member Robinson moved to issue a Disciplinary Complaint revoking the
establishment license of PHALANX, INC. d/b/a Little Fort for operating video
gaming terminals without a valid liquor license, said action to take effect 21 days
from the date of service of the Complaint, unless a fine of $4,014.50 is paid or an
Answer is properly filed within that timeframe. Member Alejandre seconded the
motion. The motion passed unanimously by roll call vote.
Failure to Timely Disclose Changes in Criminal Record
Member Alejandre moved to issue a disciplinary complaint revoking the
establishment licenses of JCH, Inc. d/b/a j.j.'s 610 tap, and JCH, INC. d/b/a Big
Four, for failing to provide timely notice of the criminal arrest of the establishments'
owner, and for the conduct that resulted in the arrest; said action to take effect 21
days from the date of service unless an Answer is properly filed within that
timeframe. Member Robinson seconded the motion. The motion passed unanimously
by roll call vote.
Failure to Prevent Underage Access to VGTs
Member Alejandre moved that the Board issue a Disciplinary Complaint revoking
the establishment license of Yugo Grill, LLC d/b/a Yugo Grill for permitting
underage gambling and drinking at the location, said action to take effect 21 days
from the date of service of the Complaint, unless a $5,000 fine is paid, or an Answer
is properly filed within that timeframe. Member Robinson seconded the motion. The
motion passed unanimously by roll call vote.

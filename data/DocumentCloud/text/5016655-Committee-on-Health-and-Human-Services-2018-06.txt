D.

New Business

1.

Communication dated April 12, 2018 (received May 7th) requesting Commission
approval of a comparable-source, intergovernmental agreement between the
Charter County of Wayne and the Detroit Wayne Mental Health Authority in the
amount of $150,000 for grant projects; the County Executive advises that the
DWMHA is the Prepaid Inpatient Health Plan (PIHP) for Wayne County and
manages federal and state prevention treatment and recovery services in Wayne
County, in addition to mental health services and this IGA is entirely state-funded
and does not include federal dollars, nor match requirements; the term of the
agreement is from October 1, 2017 through September 30, 2018; the revenue from
the agreement will be deposited into Account No. 281 32000 817000 (Community
Correction). (2018-20-055)

2.

Communication dated April 11, 2018 (received May 10th) requesting Commission
approval of retroactive Modification No. 2 to a three-year, sole-source professional
services contract with two, one-year options to renew between the Charter County
of Wayne and Medical Center Emergency Services (MCES) (of Detroit) not to
exceed $492,384 exercising the final one-year option to renew to extend the term of
the contract and add funding to continue to provide emergency room services to
Wayne County jail inmates and Detroit Police detainees; the County Executive
advises that MCES is the sole provider of emergency medical physician services for
all hospitals in the Detroit Medical Center System, with the exception of Children’s
Hospital of Michigan; Modification No. 2 increases the total cost of the contract from
$1,892,601 to $2,384,985; the term of Modification No. 2 is from July 1, 2018
through June 30, 2019; the cost of Modification No. 2 will be charged to Account
No. 221 32600 834015 (Jail Medical). (2014-37-085M2)

II.

PRESENT

John Hartnett, Joint Panel Chairman; Paul S. Besson, Member of the State Panel; James Q. Brennwald, Member of the
State Panel; Michael G. Coli, Member of the State Panel; Albert Washington, Member of the State Panel; Robert M.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES

III.

Chairman Hartnett called for approval of the minutes of the September 11, 2012 meeting. Local Panel Chairman Gierut
moved to approve the minutes. Member Coli seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
LEGISLATIVE/FISCAL REPORTS

IV.

Executive Director Mlynski reported various legislative issues. She then presented the fiscal report, reviewing in detail
the general ledger sheet and the disbursement record. The Board then discussed and questioned various aspects of the
fiscal report.
BOARD ACTIONS

V.
A.

The Board considered the application of Mr. Daniel Nielson for addition to the agency's mediation/arbitration
roster. The Board discussed Mr. Daniel Nielson's overall experience and background. At the conclusion thereof,
Member Anderson made a motion to add Mr. Nielson to the Board's mediation/arbitration roster. Member
Washington seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.

B.

The Board finalized the following meeting dates for 2013:
1) In Chicago, Illinois, the Local Panel will meet at 10:00 a.m. and the State Panel will meet at 11:00
a.m. on January 8, March 12, April 16, May 16, July 9, August 13, September 10, November 5, and
December 10, 2013;
2) In Chicago, Illinois, the Local Panel will meet at 10:00 a.m. on February 7, June 13, and October 10,
2013; and
3) In Springfield, Illinois, the State Panel will meet at 11:00 a.m. on February 5, June 11, and October
18, 2013.
Member Besson made a motion to approve the 2013 meeting dates as listed above. Member Gierut seconded the
motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

VI.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

The Board questioned whether there was a need for executive session and after a short discussion, concluded it was not
necessary at this time.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Hartnett called for a motion to adjourn. Member Coli made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Joint Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board, in
Chicago, Illinois, this 11th day of December, 2012.

PUBLIC COMMENT
Member Ward Gerson opened the floor to public comment.
Joanne Warwick, Detroit resident expressed her concern with the process for the sale of side lots and
properties sold to a Community Partner and apparently still boarded to date.
John Lauve requested that presentations be clearer for attendees. Mr. Lauve also suggested that we
create a mechanism for a neighbor to neighbor platform.
Cindy Darrah, Detroit resident suggested that the Detroit Land Bank add a neighborhood help
component to the Community Partner policy and would like to see something that would prevent
residents in the community from bidding against outsiders on properties.

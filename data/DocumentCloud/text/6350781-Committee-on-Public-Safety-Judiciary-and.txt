2.

Communication dated May 31, 2019 (received July 4th) requesting Commission
approval of a subrecipient agreement between the Charter County of Wayne and
the Thomas M. Cooley Law School (of Lansing) in the amount of $252,601 for
the school’s Innocence Project to review and work to conclusion hundreds of cold
cases involving claims of innocence where biological materials may be available
for testing or retesting, through the Wayne County Prosecutor’s Office; the
County Executive advises that the Prosecutor’s Conviction Integrity Unit will
oversee the project and the grant funds will be used to perform DNA testing, hire
expert consultation and hire and dedicate one Cooley staff attorney; the term of
the agreement is from February 1, 2019 through December 31, 2020, and the
term may be extended for up to 18 months upon National Institute of Justice
approval; the cost of the agreement will be charged to Account No. 101 22987
817000 (DNA Evidence Testing). (2019-93-012)

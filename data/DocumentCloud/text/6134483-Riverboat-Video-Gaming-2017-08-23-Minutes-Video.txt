Regular Meeting, Open Session Minutes - Video
Illinois Gaming Board
Chicago, Illinois
August 24, 2017
On Thursday, August 24, 2017, a Regular Meeting – Video of the Illinois Gaming Board
(“Board”) was held on the 5th floor at the Michael Bilandic Building in Chicago, Illinois
immediately following its Regular Meeting - Riverboat. Present were the following: Chairman
Don Tracy, Members Hector Alejandre, Steve Dolins, Thomas Dunn and Dee Robinson.
APPROVAL OF OPEN SESSION MINUTES:
Member Dunn moved that the Board approve the open session minutes from its Regular Meeting
of June 28, 2017. Member Dolins seconded the motion. The motion passed unanimously by roll
call vote.
BOARD POLICY ITEM
•

Dissemination of Closed Session Minutes

Member Dunn moved, upon a review of the minutes of closed sessions held by the Board from
January 2017 through June 2017, that the Board approve the dissemination of those minutes,
since they no longer require confidential treatment. Member Robinson seconded the motion.
The motion passed unanimously by roll call vote.

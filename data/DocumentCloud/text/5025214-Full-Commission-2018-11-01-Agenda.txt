Dated September 20, 2018 (received September 26th) requesting
Commission approval of a contract between the Charter County of Wayne
and the Michigan Department of Transportation in the amount of $526,600
for storm water retention infrastructure construction work at the
intersections of Joy Road and Westwood Street, and Faust Avenue and
Artesian Street in Detroit; the County Executive advises that funding will

Commission approval through September 30, 2023; the revenue from the
contract will be deposited into Account No. 201 44905 511250 ($391,737
Federal TAP Funds); the cost of the contract will be charged to Account
No. 201 44908 818080 ($526,600 Construction). (2018-30-070)

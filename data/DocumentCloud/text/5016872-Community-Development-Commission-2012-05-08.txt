 III.

NEW BUSINESS
A.

LASALLE CENTRAL TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 2)
Request authority for the Department of Housing and Economic Development to
negotiate a redevelopment agreement with DeVry Inc. for redevelopment of
leased premises in the building located at 300 S. Riverside Plaza in the LaSalle
Central Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and to
recommend to the City Council of the City of Chicago the designation of DeVry
Inc. as Developer.
Project Manager: Christopher Jang
Motioned by Sahli, seconded by Newsome. Approved 9-0. Yeas: Buford, Duque,
Kostiner, Martin, Newsome, Olander, Perez, Sahli and Stein. Ward recused.

B.

BRONZEVILLE AND 35TH/STATE TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT
AREAS (WARD 3)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Housing and Economic
Development=s intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Park Boulevard IIB,
LLC for the disposition of the city property located at 3633-47 S. State Street and
9 E. 36th Place plus a to-be-vacated alley in the Bronzeville Tax Increment
Financing Redevelopment Project Area, to request alternative proposals and to
approve the sale of the property to Park Boulevard IIB, LLC if no responsive
alternative proposals are received; and request authority for the Department of
Housing and Economic Development to negotiate a redevelopment agreement
with Park Boulevard IIB, LLC for redevelopment of the city property plus
Chicago Housing Authority property to be leased to Park Boulevard IIB, LLC
located at 10 W. 37th Street, 15 W. 37th Street and 3700 S. Dearborn Street in the
35th/State Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and to
recommend to the City Council of the City of Chicago the designation of
Park Boulevard IIB, LLC as Developer if no responsive alternative proposals are
received.
Project Manager: Barbara Taylor
Motioned by Martin, seconded by Sahli. Approved 10-0. Yeas: Buford, Duque,
Kostiner, Martin, Newsome, Olander, Perez, Sahli, Stein and Ward.

FEBRUARY 3, 2010
NOTICE

There will be a meeting of the Legislation, Intergovernmental & Veterans Relations
Committee of the Board of Commissioners of Cook County on Tuesday, February 9, 2010 at
the hour of 9:30 A.M. in the Board Room, Room 569, County Building, 118 North Clark Street,
Chicago, Illinois to consider the following:
304802

COOK COUNTY SHERIFF’S MERIT BOARD (APPOINTMENT). Transmitting a
Communication, dated January 14, 2010 from Thomas J. Dart, Sheriff of Cook
County:
I hereby appoint Mr. KIM R. WIDUP to the Cook County Sheriff's Merit Board, to fill
the vacancy left by Mr. Michael D. Carey whose term expired March 2007. Mr.
Widup's term will begin immediately and expire on March 18, 2013.
Pursuant to Chapter 55 ILCS 5/3-7002, I hereby request the advice and consent of
the Cook County Board of Commissioners relative to the appointment of Mr. Widup.
*Referred to the Committee on Legislation, Intergovernmental & Veterans
Relations on 1/26/10.

304803

COOK COUNTY SHERIFF’S MERIT BOARD (APPOINTMENT). Transmitting a
Communication, dated January 14, 2010 from Thomas J. Dart, Sheriff of Cook
County:
I hereby appoint Mr. LANCE C. TYSON to the Cook County Sheriff's Merit Board,
to fill the vacancy left by Mr. Arthur R. Waddy whose term expired March 2009. Mr.
Tyson's term will begin immediately and expire on March 18, 2015.
Pursuant to Chapter 55 ILCS-5/3-7002, I hereby request the advice and consent of
the Board of Commissioners relative to the appointment of Mr. Tyson.
*Referred to the Committee on Legislation, Intergovernmental & Veterans
Relations on 1/26/10.

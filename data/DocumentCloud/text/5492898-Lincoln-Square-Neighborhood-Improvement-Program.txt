Lincoln Square Special Service Area #21

Commission Meeting Minutes
Minutes from Thursday, April 19, 2018 – 9:00am at Bistro Campagne
 Perfunctory Business
o Commissioners Present: Anthony Qaiyum, Melaina Prest, Todd Feinberg, Zach Maiorca
o Others Present: Ryan Warsing (LSRCC), Rudy Flores (LSRCC), Sean Cooper (LSRCC), Joy
Coombes (Eilts & Associates)
o Commissioners Absent: Shelley Young, Pat Bader, Ben Munro
 Meeting called to order at 9:16am – A. Qaiyum (motion), Z. Maiorca (second)
 Minutes from 2/15/18 meeting approved – M. Prest (motion), Z. Maiorca (second)
 2018 Financials approved – Z. Maiorca (motion), T. Feinberg (second)
o The SSA has received $162,181 out of $238,619 in expected taxes
o The SSA has spent $40,354 of its $277,070 budget
o Profit and Loss – R. Warsing presented detailed YTD profit/loss and balance sheet
 J. Coombes presented the SSA’s 2017 financial audit, which found no major deficiencies or
material weaknesses; Audit findings approved – Z. Maiorca (motion), T. Feinberg (second)
 Strategic Plan update – final recommendations from PLACE Consulting’s Plan were given to the
Commission by R. Warsing
 Revised SSA Bylaws, drafted by PLACE Consulting, were presented by R. Warsing. Proposals for
edits were made, and final Bylaws will be voted on at the next SSA meeting.
 Proposals for the SSA’s upcoming Master Plan were distributed and lightly reviewed
o Commissioners will study proposals and prepare questions by April 26
o When all questions have been answered, finalists will be invited to attend the next SSA
Commission meeting
 A site at 5011 N. Lincoln Ave (MINTS Medical) was approved to host the next PaintWorks public
mural – A. Qaiyum (motion), T. Feinberg (second)
 Announcements
 Chris Dallas has resigned from the SSA Commission, and Zach Maiorca will resign
after the next SSA meeting. This will bring the total number of vacancies to 4.
 Ryan Warsing plans to depart the LSRCC/SSA 21 at the end of July. His successor
will be hired in the summer, and will shadow him for a brief time.
o Next meeting – 9:00am Thursday, May 24 at Bistro Campagne
 This will be the 2019 Budget Meeting. Commissioners are encouraged to attend
with ideas for future spending, and/or contribute ideas in advance.
 Meeting adjourned at 10:49am

1. Board Members who vote "Nay, Present, or Abstain" or have a question on any item

removed from the Consent Agenda.
3. Items removed from the Consent Agenda are considered separately.

Study Session
A Study Session on Stormwater Management is scheduled for Thursday,
October 2, 2008, immediately following the Regular Board Meeting. The
purpose of the Study Session is to discuss detailed Watershed Planning status
including inundation mapping, Draft Watershed Management Ordinance status,
the future of drainage districts, the Stormwater Management Fund and
five-year forecast.

Announcements
The General Superintendent announced that the District will receive a
Recognition Award for it's Exemplary Biosloids Management Program. The
award will be presented at the WEFTEC awards ceremony on October 19, 2008
at McCormick Place.
The Illinois Pollution Control Board hearings will resume on September 8, 9,
and 10 and continue on the 23rd, 24th and 25th. All of these days will be used
for testimony and cross examination of the District's 22 witnesses.
The Executive Directors of NACWA and WEF will attend the September 18,
2008 Board Meeting to present the National Biosolids Partnership
Environmental Management System Certification. The Board of
Commissioners and Department Heads are invited to attend a luncheon at 1:30
p.m at the Allerton Hotel.

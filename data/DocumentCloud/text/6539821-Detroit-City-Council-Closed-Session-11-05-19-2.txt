Escrow (FIE) funds, to Housing and Revitalization Department (HRD)(the

funds to the NSP-1 account from both FIE and the General Fund. In addition,
there is outstanding program income in the NSP-1 bank account that must be
appropriated and spent for closeout of the program as required by HUD.)

NOTICE OF PUBLIC HEARING
In accordance with the provisions of "An Act in the relation of the creation, maintenance, operation,
and improvement of the Chicago Park District", 70 ILCS 1505 et. seq., PUBLIC NOTICE is hereby
given that a PUBLIC HEARING CONCERNING THE INTENT OF THE BOARD OF
COMMISSIONERS OF THE CHICAGO PARK DISTRICT TO SELL GENERAL OBLIGATION
LIMITED TAX BONDS OF THE DISTRICT, IN ONE OR MORE SERIES, IN THE AMOUNT OF NOT
TO EXCEED $50,000,000 will be held on Wednesday, June 10, 2015, at 3:30 P.M. in the 8th Floor
Board Room of the Administration Building at 541 N. Fairbanks Court, Chicago, Illinois 60611.

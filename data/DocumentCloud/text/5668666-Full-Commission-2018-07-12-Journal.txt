 PLEDGE OF ALLEGIANCE TO THE FLAG OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA
Chairman Woronchak advised all to stand to recite the Pledge of Allegiance.

INVOCATION
Chairman Woronchak called upon Commissioner Palamara for the introduction of
Darcel Brown, Legislative Research Assistant, who gave the following invocation:
“Lord, we thank you for this day. Thank you for hearing the heart’s prayer of each
person gathered here today. We ask that you would provide your knowledge, wisdom and
guidance to each Commissioner. During this meeting, we ask for a spirit of collaboration as
the deliberations take place. Let the decisions decreed here today cause the residents of
Wayne County to rejoice. I pray that as the Commissioners serve your people, you would
reward them, not only for the good that they do that is seen, but also for the good they do that
is unseen. These things we ask in Jesus name. AMEN.”

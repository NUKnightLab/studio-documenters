LEGAL
Legal Update
Mr. Heiss updated the Board on the case of Charles Romano v. The Municipal Employees’ Annuity and
Benefit Fund of Chicago. The Fund appealed the Appellate Court’s ruling that overturned the Board’s
decision to cancel Mr. Romano’s lifetime benefit based on his felony conviction.
The Illinois Supreme Court refused to hear the appeal. Therefore, the Board’s decision has been
overturned and Mr. Romano is allowed to receive an annuity after proper application.

BUDGET FORUM NOTICE AND AGENDA
Public Forum
The public budget forum for all parks throughout the city takes place on Wednesday, October 7,
2015 at Fosco Park, 1312 South Racine Avenue, Chicago, Illinois 60608. Registration to speak
will begin at 5:30 p.m., and the forum will begin promptly at 6 p.m
Those wishing to speak must sign-up in person the day of the meeting at the time and location
indicated above.
We encourage patrons to give input and feedback on the Chicago Park District 2016 budget by
speaking at the public forum OR submitting an idea now through the online web form located at:
http://www.chicagoparkdistrict.com/departments/finance/budget-and-management/
Comments from the in-person hearing and the web form will assist the Chicago Park District in its
2016 budget planning process.
The Chicago Park District relies on its patrons for input to provide quality programming and
amenities that cater to each unique Chicago neighborhood. We are asking taxpayers to take
advantage of this valuable opportunity to be a voice in the process and help shape their local
parks. Thanks in advance for your time and input.

Director of Budget and Management
General Superintendent
Juliet Azimi
Michael P. Kelly
and the Budget Team

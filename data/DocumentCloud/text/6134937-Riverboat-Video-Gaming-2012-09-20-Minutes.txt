MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING - RIVERBOAT
OPEN SESSION
September 20, 2012
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
On Thursday, September 20, 2012 a Regular Meeting - Riverboat of the Illinois Gaming Board
(“Board”) was held on the 2nd floor at 110 W. Randolph at the JRTC Building, Chicago, Illinois
at 9:59 A.M. Present were the following: Chairman Aaron Jaffe, Members Maribeth Vander
Weele, Michael Holewinski and Lee Gould.
APPROVAL OF MINUTES
Member Gould moved that the Board approve the open session minutes of its Regular
Meeting of August 16, 2012. Member Holewinski seconded the motion. The Board approved
the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
ADMINISTRATOR’S REPORT –
Administrator Ostrowski commented on the teleconference that he held with the General
Managers of the Riverboats and Tom Swoik, Executive Director, Illinois Casino Gaming
Association.
OWNER LICENSEE ITEMS
Items for Initial Consideration – Elgin Riverboat Resort d/b/a Grand Victoria Casino –
License Renewal
Items for Final Consideration •

Empress Casino Joliet Corporation d/b/a Hollywood Casino Joliet – Lowell
Louis Jacobsen, Jr., VP of Casino Operations - Level One
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Holewinski moved that the Board approve
Lowell Louis Jacobsen, Jr. as an Occupational Licensee Level One in his
position as Vice President of Casino Operations for Empress Casino Joliet
Corporation d/b/a Hollywood Casino – Joliet. Member Gould seconded the motion.
The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.

•

Elgin Riverboat Resort d/b/a Grand Victoria Casino – Randall C. Roberts,
Executive Committee Member – Key Person
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Vander Weele moved that the Board
approve Randall C. Roberts as Key Person in his position as Executive
Committee Member as a representative of RBG Illinois, LLC, a 50% owner of

 Elgin Riverboat Resort d/b/a Grand Victoria Casino. Member Holewinski
seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
•

Caesars Entertainment Corporation – Finance Transaction
Based on staff’s investigation, pursuant to Section 230(d) of the Board’s Adopted
Rules, Member Gould moved to approve Des Plaines Development Limited
Partnership d/b/a Harrah’s Joliet Casino and Southern Illinois Riverboat Casino
Cruises, Inc. d/b/a Harrah’s Metropolis Casino as Subsidiary Guarantors of the
principal amount of $750 Million of Caesars Entertainment Corporation’s
8.50% First Lien Notes due 2020, which were sold by Caesars Entertainment
Corporation in August, 2012 to qualified institutional buyers. Member Vander
Weele seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call
vote.

OCCUPATIONAL LICENSEE ITEMS
•

Licensure of Levels 2 and 3
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Holewinski moved that the Board find
suitable and approve 60 applications for Level 2 Occupational Licenses and 125
applications for Level 3 Occupational Licenses. Further, Member Holewinski
moved that the Administrator issue Notices of Denial to Robert A. Deere and
Shorron L. Moore. Member Vander Weele seconded the motion. The Board
approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.

At 10:30 A.M. Member Vander Weele motioned to adjourn and Member Holewinski seconded
the motion. All Members voted in favor of adjournment.
Respectfully submitted,

Mary C. Boruta

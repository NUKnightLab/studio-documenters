PUBLIC COMMENT
Jane Tessaro, Benefits Manager for the Fund publicly thanked the Board of Trustees, Jim Mohler,
Executive Director and the Fund staff for supporting her in her role as Benefits Manager. After she spoke,
Jim Mohler thanked her for her 44 years of dedicated service and recognized her skills and expertise as
“one-of-a-kind.” Ms. Tessaro will begin her well-earned retirement from the MEABF on December 1st,
2016.

The following topics were taken out of order.
LEGISLATION
Legislative Update
No issues were discussed.

INVESTMENTS
Monthly Performance Report
Mr. Wesner reviewed the Fund’s Monthly Performance Report. Based on his review, Trustee Guest made
a motion to place Keeley Asset Management on Watch for ownership changes. Seconded by Trustee
Johnson.
Carried: Ayes – Trustee Guest, Trustee Johnson, Trustee Keane, Trustee McMahon and Trustee
Summers. Nays – None.
Third Quarter Marquette Report
Mr. Wesner reviewed the Third Quarter Report. No actions taken.
Asset Allocation Presentation
Mr. Greg Leonberger, Marquette Associates, reviewed the current asset allocation of the Fund’s portfolio
and discussed the best options for a prudent rebalance of the assets. Based on his review and the
recommendation of Fund staff, Trustee Johnson made a motion to change the Fund’s asset allocation to
option A as presented. Seconded by Trustee Guest.
Carried: Ayes – Trustee Guest, Trustee Johnson, Trustee Keane, Trustee McMahon and Trustee
Summers. Nays – None.
Watchlist
Mr. Lopez reviewed the firms currently on watch. Based on Trustee Guest’s previous motion, Keeley Asset
Management was added to the Watchlist.
Liquidity Needs
Mr. Lopez reviewed the current liquidity needs of the Fund. Based on this review and recommendation of
Fund staff and the investment consultant, Trustee Guest made a motion to liquidate up to $50 million to
assist in paying December benefit payments. Seconded by Trustee Johnson.
Carried: Ayes – Trustee Guest, Trustee Johnson, Trustee Keane, Trustee McMahon and Trustee
Summers. Nays – None.

total account balances in paying future benefits.

MINUTES OF THE MEETING
COMMISSION ON CHICAGO LANDMARKS
February 2, 2017
The Commission on Chicago Landmarks held its regularly scheduled meeting on
February 2, 2017. The meeting was held at City Hall, 121 North LaSalle Street, Room 201-A,
Chicago, Illinois. The meeting began at 12:51 p.m.

meeting of the Commission on Chicago Landmarks.
Chairman Leon called the meeting to order.
1. Approval of the Minutes of Previous Meeting
Regular Meeting of January 5, 2017
Motioned by Tolliver, seconded by Reifman. Approved unanimously (6-0).

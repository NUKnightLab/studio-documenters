The Matter of John Bills (Pension Termination per 40 ILCS 5/8-251)
Mr. John Bills appeared at the meeting, without legal representation, although he was afforded the chance
to do so. Mr. Bills was provided with an opportunity to present any evidence that he felt would support his
claim that Section 8-251 should not apply to his felony conviction. Mr. Bills made a statement to the Board
and submitted a written statement, introduced into evidence as Annuitant Exhibit 1. Fund counsel then
presented documentary evidence, marked as Fund Exhibits 1-5, establishing the requirements of Section
8-251.

The Board moved into closed session at 11:16 a.m. in accordance with Section 2(c)(4) of the Open
Meetings Act in order to discuss testimony and evidence presented in the Matter of John Bills. Motion by

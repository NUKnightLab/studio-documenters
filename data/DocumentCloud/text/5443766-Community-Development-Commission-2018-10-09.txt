 III. NEW BUSINESS
A.

MIDWEST TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 28)
Request authority for the Department of Planning and Development to enter into a
negotiated sale with A Safe Haven Foundation for the disposition of the property
located at 2908-12 and 2916-20 W. Roosevelt Road and 1143 S. Richmond Street
in the Midwest Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, to request
alternative proposals, and to approve the sale of the property to A Safe Haven
Foundation if no responsive alternative proposals are received.
Project Manager: Barbara J. Taylor
Motioned by Butler, seconded by Newsome. Approved 6-0. Yeas: Buford, Butler,
Curtis, Newsome, Rhodes and Perez.
18-CDC-23

B.

116th/AVENUE O TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 10)
Request authority for the Department of Planning and Development to negotiate a
redevelopment agreement with NP Avenue O, LLC for redevelopment of the
property located west of Avenue O and south of 116th Street in the 116th/Avenue
O Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and to recommend to
the City Council of the City of Chicago the designation of NP Avenue O, LLC as
Developer.
Project Manager: Andrew Ranck
Motioned by Butler, seconded by Buford. Approved 5-0. Yeas: Buford, Butler,
Curtis, Newsome and Rhodes. Perez recused.
18-CDC-24

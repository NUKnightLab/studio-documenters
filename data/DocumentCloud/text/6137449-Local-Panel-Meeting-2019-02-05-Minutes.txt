Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on February 5, 2019

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman of the Local Panel; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Local Panel; Angela C. Thomas,
Member of the Local Panel; Helen J. Kim, General Counsel; Anna Hamburg-Gal, Associate General Counsel; and staff
members. Kimberly F. Stevens, Executive Director participated by telephone.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD January 8, 2019

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the January 8, 2019 meeting. Member Anderson moved to approve
the minutes. Member Thomas seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gierut asked Executive Director Stevens to present the reports. She presented the reports and summarized
their highlights.
V.

REVIEW OF PROPOSED WRITTEN DECISIONS AND ORDERS
1.
Case No. L-RC-16-034
American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31 and City of Chicago

General Counsel Kim called the case. Member Thomas made a motion to approve the proposed written decision. Member
Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VI.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.
Case No. L-CA-16-079
Fraternal Order of Police, Lodge #7 and City of Chicago (Department of Police)

General Counsel Kim called and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member Thomas moved to
hold the case in abeyance. Member Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VII.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded it was unnecessary.
VIII.

PUBLIC COMMENT

No members of the public sought to make comments.
IX.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board, in
Chicago, Illinois, this 12th day of March 2019.

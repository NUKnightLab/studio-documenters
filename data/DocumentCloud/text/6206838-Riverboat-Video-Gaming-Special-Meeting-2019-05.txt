Special Meeting, Open Session Minutes – Riverboat/Video Gaming
Illinois Gaming Board
Chicago, Illinois
May 6, 2019
The Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”) convened for a Special Meeting on Monday May 6, 2019
at approximately 11:00 A.M. on the 2nd floor of the James R. Thompson Center at 100 W.
Randolph Street, Chicago, IL, in Room 2-025 pursuant to the Illinois Open Meetings Act, 5
ILCS 120/1 et seq.
A roll call was taken. The following Board members were physically present: Chairman Don
Tracy, Member Steve Dolins and Member Ruben Ramirez. Due to a work conflict, Member Dee
Robinson participated telephonically. Three members of the Board being physically present, a
quorum was satisfied.
At approximately 11:02 A.M., Member Dolins moved that the Board go into closed session
pursuant to Section 2(c), paragraphs (1), (11), (14), and (21) of the Open Meetings Act and
Section 6(d) of the Riverboat Gambling Act to discuss items on the closed session agenda
relating to specific personnel. Member Ramirez seconded the motion. The Board approved the
motion unanimously by voice vote. Thereafter, the members of the public were asked to leave
the room and the Board remained in Room 2-025 to conduct its closed session meeting; the
minutes of which are separately recorded.
At approximately 11:10 A.M., the Board reconvened its open session Special Meeting –
Riverboat and Video Gaming – of May 6, 2019 in Room 2-025 pursuant to the Illinois Open
Meetings Act, 5 ILCS 120/1 et seq.
Chairman Tracy did not provide any reports or make any comments and no Board Member made
any comments.
Member Dolins moved that the Board appoint Marcus D. Fruchter as the Administrator of the
Illinois Gaming Board, effective May 16, 2019. Member Robinson seconded the motion. The
Board approved the motion unanimously by voice vote.
Member Dolins moved to adjourn the meeting. Member Ramirez seconded the motion. The
motion passed unanimously via voice vote.

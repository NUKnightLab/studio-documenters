(Historic Michigan Boulevard District – 42nd Ward)
Proposal:

Proposed reface of the existing non-historic illuminated rooftop “Santa Fe” sign
with new illuminated “Motorola” copy on existing structure, detailed in plans
dated 3/12/12.

Action:

Approved unanimously with the following condition:
1. As proposed, the “Motorola” copy shall be consistent with the existing
“Sante Fe” copy in terms of color and illumination levels at night. Should

to remain, the paint color shall be a dark, non-reflective color

3.

1936 S. Michigan
(Second Presbyterian Church – 3rd Ward)
Proposal:

Proposed construction of a new 60” x 102” free-standing changing image sign
with 42”-tall masonry base located along the east property line facing Michigan
Avenue. The total height proposed is 102”.

 Action:

Approved with the following conditions (vote was 2 in favor with
Commissioner Baird abstaining):
1. The changing image portion of the sign shall be no larger than 60” x 102”
and the stone-clad base of the sign shall be no taller than 16” so that the top
of the sign is aligned or below the window sill of the historic façade behind
the sign (76” total height). Alternatively, a taller, free standing sign would
be allowed at the southeast corner of the property or on the north side of the
property where there are no windows that would be obscured; and,
2. The proposed stone cladding for the base, with a smooth face and with a
slightly different color in comparison to the rusticated stone of the Church
facades, is approved as submitted. All electrical transformer boxes, conduit
and electrical connections shall be concealed under ground or within the sign
or base.

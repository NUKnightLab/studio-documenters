The Commission on Chicago Landmarks held a regular meeting on September 1, 2011. The meeting
was held at City Hall, 121 N. LaSalle St., City Hall Room 201-A, Chicago, Illinois. The meeting
began at 12:50 p.m.

meeting of the Commission on Chicago Landmarks.
Chairman Leon called the meeting to order. He announced that prior to the meeting the Commission
presented the 2011 Awards for Preservation Excellence. On behalf of the Commission, Chairman
Leon congratulated the award winners. He noted that the display boards for the award-winning
projects are available in PDF format on the Department of Housing and Economic Development
website and will be on display in City Hall.

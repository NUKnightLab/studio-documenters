 III.

OLD BUSINESS
A.

LITTLE VILLAGE INDUSTRIAL TIF REDEVELOPMENT AREA
(WARD 22)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Planning and Development’s
intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Chicago Southwest Development
Corporation for the disposition of the property located at 3200 S. Kedzie Avenue
and 3230 and 3354 W. 31st Street in the Little Village Tax Increment Financing
Redevelopment Project Area, to request alternative proposals, and to approve the
sale of the property to Chicago Southwest Development Corporation if no
responsive alternative proposals are received.
Project Manager: Mary Bonome
Motioned by Perez, seconded by Newsome. Approved 7-0. Yeas: Buford,
Mooney, Newsome, Olander, Perez, Sercye and Carrott.
14-CDC-44

IV.

NEW BUSINESS
A.

READ DUNNING TIF REDEVELOPMENT AREA (WARD 38)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Planning and Development’s
intention to enter into a negotiated sale with New Horizon Center for the
Developmentally Disabled for the disposition of a portion of the property located
at 4030 N. Oak Park Avenue in the Read Dunning Tax Increment Financing
Redevelopment Project Area, to request alternative proposals, and to approve the
sale of the property to New Horizon Center for the Developmentally Disabled if
no responsive alternative proposals are received.
Project Manager: Michelle Nolan
Motioned by Newsome, seconded by Olander. Approved 7-0. Yeas: Buford,
Mooney, Newsome, Olander, Perez, Sercye and Carrott.

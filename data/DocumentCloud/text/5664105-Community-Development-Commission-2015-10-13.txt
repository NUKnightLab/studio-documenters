NEW BUSINESS
A.

WOODLAWN TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 20)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Planning and Development’s
intention to enter into a negotiated sale with University of Chicago for the
disposition of the property located on the block bounded by 63rd and 64th streets,
Greenwood and University avenues in the Woodlawn Tax Increment Financing
Redevelopment Project Area, to request alternative proposals, and to approve the
sale of the property to University of Chicago if no responsive alternative proposals
are received.
Project Manager: Michelle Nolan
Motioned by Newsome, seconded by Davis. Approved 9-0. Yeas: Alphonse,
Butler, Davis, Griggs, Moreno, Newsome, Olander, Perez and Carrott.
15-CDC-28

B.

CHICAGO/CENTRAL PARK AND KINZIE INDUSTRIAL TIF
REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 27)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Planning and Development’s
intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Nelson Mandela Apartments, LP for
the disposition of the city property located at 549 N. Drake Avenue, 601-03 N.
Central Park Boulevard and 614 N. Spaulding Avenue in the Chicago/Central
Park Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and 605 and 607 N.
Sawyer Avenue, 524 and 530 N. Troy Avenue, and 3106, 3110, 3116, 3120, 3124
and 3145 W. Franklin Boulevard in the Kinzie Industrial Tax Increment Financing
Redevelopment Project Area, to request alternative proposals and to approve the
sale of the property to Nelson Mandela Apartments, LP if no responsive
alternative proposals are received; and request authority for the Department of
Planning and Development to negotiate a redevelopment agreement with Nelson
Mandela Apartments, LP for redevelopment of the city property plus the
developer property located at 600-02 N. St. Louis Avenue, 601 N. Sawyer Avenue
and 3112-14 W. Franklin Boulevard, and to recommend to the City Council of the
City of Chicago the designation of Nelson Mandela Apartments, LP as Developer
if no responsive alternative proposals are received.
Anna Booth

 Motioned by Perez, seconded by Moreno. Approved 8-0. Yeas: Butler, Davis,
Griggs, Moreno, Newsome, Olander, Perez and Carrott. Alphonse not present.
15-CDC-29

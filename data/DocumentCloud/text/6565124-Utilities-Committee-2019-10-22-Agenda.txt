1152-2019

AN EMERGENCY ORDINANCE Authorizing the general fund to receive
and use revenue from tax remittances collected by Cleveland Public
Power during the years 2020 and 2021 and transfer 100% of tax
receipts collected in both years back to Cleveland Public Power.

Sponsors:

Martin Keane, Kevin Kelley and By Departmental Request

Indexes:

CLEVELAND PUBLIC POWER; GENERAL FUND

1181-2019

AN EMERGENCY ORDINANCE Authorizing the Director of Public
Utilities to employ one or more professional consultants to produce a
Department of Utilities-specific new hire safety and security orientation
video and the production of an e-learning training module based off of the
Safety and Security Orientation video for existing employees that can be
added to Public Utilities existing web-based training platform.

Sponsors:

Brian Kazy, Kevin Kelley and By Departmental Request

Indexes:

PROFESSIONAL SERVICES CONTRACTS; UTILITIES DEPARTMENT

Attachments:

1181-2019 Prof Serv produce safety and security orientation video PU (002)

1249-2019

AN EMERGENCY ORDINANCE Authorizing the Director of Public
Utilities to enter into one or more requirement contracts without
competitive bidding with Northcoast Process Controls, Inc. for the
inspection and repair, including but not limited to labor and materials, of
existing valve actuator systems and appurtenances manufactured by
Rotork Manufacturing Company, for the Division of Water, Department
of Public Utilities, for a term of two years.

Sponsors:

Martin Keane, Kevin Kelley and By Departmental Request

Indexes:

CONTRACTS; UTILITIES DEPARTMENT; WATER DIVISION

Attachments:

Lakeview Avenue Row House District
Proposed renovation of existing 4-story masonry row house to two single-family
residences with third- and fourth-floor additions and new garage door openings on a

masonry samples prior to order and installation; and,

adjustment, and the Commission takes no position regarding any
requested variance/adjustment relative to the zoning code
requirements.

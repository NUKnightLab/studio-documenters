STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on June 8, 2017
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman of the Local Panel; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Local Panel; Kimberly Stevens,
Executive Director (participated by phone); Helen J. Kim, General Counsel; Anna Hamburg-Gal, Associate General
Counsel; and staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD MAY 16, 2017

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the May 16, 2017 meeting. Member Anderson moved to approve the
minutes. Chairman Gierut seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gierut asked Executive Director Stevens to present the Executive Director’s reports. She presented the reports
and summarized the highlights.
The Local Panel Members finalized the 2017 meeting dates. Member Anderson made a motion to approve the 2017 meeting
dates. Chairman Gierut seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.

Case No. L-CA-15-020
Service Employees International Union, Local 73/County of Cook

General Counsel Kim called the case, and noted that no party filed exceptions to Administrative Law Judge Sanceda’s
Recommended Decision and Order. The Board declined to review the decision on its own motion.
2.

Case Nos. L-CA-16-013 and L-CA-16-022
Kenneth Sawyer/City of Chicago (Streets and Sanitation)

General Counsel Kim called the case, and noted that no party filed exceptions to Administrative Law Judge Anna HamburgGal’s Recommended Decision and Order. The Board declined to review the decision on its own motion.

General Counsel Kim called the case, and noted that no party filed exceptions to Administrative Law Judge Anna HamburgGal’s Recommended Decision and Order. The Board declined to review the decision on its own motion.

 4.

American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31/City of Chicago (Unit

General Counsel Kim called the case, and noted that no party filed exceptions to Administrative Law Judge Sanceda’s

5.

Case No. L-CB-17-006
Karen Lindberg/Service Employees International Union, Local 73

General Counsel Kim called and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member Anderson moved
to accept the Executive Director dismissal with modifications. Chairman Gierut seconded the motion, and the Board passed
it unanimously, by a voice vote.
6.

Case No. L-CA-17-026
Shadonna Davis/County of Cook and Sheriff of Cook County

General Counsel Kim called and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member Anderson moved
to accept the Executive Director dismissal. Chairman Gierut seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by
a voice vote.

7.

Case No. L-CB-17-026
Geramy Webster/Amalgamated Transit Authority

General Counsel Kim called and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member Anderson moved
to accept the Executive Director dismissal with modifications. Chairman Gierut seconded the motion, and the Board passed
it unanimously, by a voice vote.

 VII.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded it was

VIII. PUBLIC COMMENT
No members of the public were present.

Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Chairman Anderson made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and

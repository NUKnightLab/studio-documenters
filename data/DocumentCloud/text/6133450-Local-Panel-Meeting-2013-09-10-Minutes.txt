STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on September 10, 2013
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, Suite S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Board; Richard Lewis, Member of the Board;
Melissa Mlynski, Executive Director; Jerald S. Post, General Counsel; and staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD August 6, 2013

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the August 6, 2013 meeting. Member Anderson moved to
approve the minutes. Member Lewis seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote. The
Board welcomed Investigator Yumnah Tayyab to its staff.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gierut asked Executive Director Mlynski to present the Local Panel administrative actions report for the
period of time from August 1 through August 31, 2013. She presented the report and summarized its highlights.
The Board examined the report and discussed it.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.
Case No. L-CA-10-070
Service Employees International Union, Local 73/ City of Chicago

Chairman Gierut asked General Counsel Post to call and present the case. General Counsel Post stated that on July
1, 2013, Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) Anna Hamburg-Gal issued a Recommended Decision and Order (RDO),
alleging the Respondent violated Sections 10(a)(4) and (1) of the Illinois Public Labor Relations Act. General
Counsel Post then presented and discussed the issues of the case, Charging Party’s appeal and Respondent’s crossexception and response. He then recommended that the board accept the ALJ’s recommendation with modification.
After discussion, Member Anderson made a motion to affirm General Counsel Post’s recommendation. Member
Lewis seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
2.
Case No. L-CB-13-007
Megan Curry/ Fraternal Order of Police, Lodge 7
Chairman Gierut called the case, noting that no party filed exceptions to ALJ Sidwell’s RDO, and asked whether
the Board wished to review the decision on its own motion. The Board declined to do so.
VI.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded that it was
not necessary at this time.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the
Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations
Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 10th day of October, 2013.

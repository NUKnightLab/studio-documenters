08-01-2017 RESOLUTION AUTHORIZING THE INTERIM EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR TO REMIT PAYMENT TO THE
CITY OF GENERAL SERVICES DEPARTMENT ON BEHALF OF THE DETROIT LAND BANK
AUTHORITY. Chairperson Ward Gerson provided background of the resolution. Motion by Member
Hosey to approve resolution. Supported by Member Raheem. MOTION APPROVED.

08-02-2017 RESOLUTION AUTHORIZING THE INTERIM EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR TO ENTER INTO A SERVICES
AGREEMENT ON BEHALF OF THE DETROIT LAND BANK AUTHORITY WITH RIGHTSIZE FACILITY
PERFORMANCE OF MICHIGAN, LLC. Heather Zygmontowicz, Assistant Director, Operations, provided
background on the resolution and walked the group through the plans for the 15th floor renovation.
Member Hosey inquired as to whether we are in line or below current market rates for renting per
square foot in the downtown footprint. He requested that Melissa Vela, Office Manager, and Gabriel
Guerrero, Staff Attorney II, research and put a process in place. Member Raheem inquired about any
change orders and how to ensure our current agreement is locked in. Heather stated that the cost for
renovation is currently $220,000 and will remain unless we request major changes. Chairperson Ward

Gerson requested that the resolution be amended to reflect that the price for the renovation not exceed
$220,000. Motion by Member Hosey to approve resolution. Supported by Member Raheem. MOTION
APPROVED.

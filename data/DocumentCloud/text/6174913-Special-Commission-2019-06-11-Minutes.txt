 MINUTES OF A SPECIAL MEETING
OF THE COMMISIONERS OF SSA 5

June 11, 2019

2. Items for Approval
Five Façade Rebate Applications for the following address were reviewed by the
commissioners:
8759 S. Commercial Ave.
3004 E. 88th St.
3006 E. 88th St.
3008 E. 88th St.
3010 E. 88th St.
Raul Gonzalez, property owner and Andrea Gonzalez where present at the
meeting to answer any and all questions raised by the commissioners regarding
the applications. After a lengthy discussion regarding the five rebate applications,
a motion to approve the maximum amount $15,000.00 for each of the five
applications with an understanding that the owner will maintain as many of the
architectural details as possible was made by Bonnie Dinell-Dimond, seconded
by Albert Garcia. Motion carried.

Adjournment

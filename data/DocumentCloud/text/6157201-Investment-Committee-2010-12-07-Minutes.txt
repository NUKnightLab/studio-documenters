6.

Evergreen IV Annex Term Extension Request;
Ms. Hackett presented a summary of the extension request by the Evergreen Fund IV
including an analysis of the fund’s performance and events to date.
Motion to Approve Evergreen IV Annex Term Extension Request. It was moved by
Trustee Fitzgerald and seconded by Trustee Agnes to recommend to the Board that
the County Fund consent to the proposed Evergreen IV Annex drawdown period
extension (through December 31, 2011) and that the Fund Investment Staff, in
consultation with Fund Legal Counsel and Investment Committee, be authorized to
take all actions reasonably necessary to effect and communicate the same.
Vote Result: MOTION ADOPTED UNANIMOUSLY BY VOICE VOTE

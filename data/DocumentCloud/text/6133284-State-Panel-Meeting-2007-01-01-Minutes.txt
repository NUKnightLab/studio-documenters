BOARD ACTIONS

Member Hade moved to go into executive session pursuant to 5 ILCS 120/2(c)(4) to consider the
evidence and testimony presented in the following matter:
Case No. S-RC-06-153
Clerk of the Circuit Court of Cook County/Int'l Brotherhood of Teamsters, Local
714/American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31
Member Coli seconded the motion and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote. Illinois Labor
Relations Board staff attorneys Philip Kazanjian and Sharon Wells were present for the closed session.
The Board returned to open session for adjournment, after having considered the evidence and testimony
presented therein.
IV.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gallagher called for a motion to adjourn. Member Hade made a motion to adjourn the meeting,
and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

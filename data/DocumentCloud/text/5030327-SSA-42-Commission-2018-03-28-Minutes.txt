1. Chairman Karim called the meeting to order at 6:03pm.
2. Minutes from the February 28th, 2018 meeting were approved.
3. The Commissioners decided not to accept the Radio Broadcast
proposal from IUBM. There were no other submissions for the
radio broadcast RFP. Tonya Trice will contact WBEZ and ask them
to attend our next SSA meeting to discuss a radio platform that
showcases SSA businesses/events.
4. The 2017 Year End Accomplishments PowerPoint will be posted on
the SSA#42 website.
5. The SSA #42 decal designs were reviewed. We will ask the
graphic designer to make modifications to the decal using the SSA
logo. Samples of the new designs will be resubmitted at the next
SSA Meeting.

 6. Due to an increase in the Chamber’s office rent, the 2018 Budget
line item #6.04 was increased from $7,200 to $7,500.

regarding prevailing wages for Cleanslate’s employees, to

Retained Parties document included in the 2018 service provider

meetings to evenings @ 6:30pm. The motion was seconded by
Gregory Smith. The motion was adopted.
10. There being no further business the meeting was adjourned at
9:20pm.

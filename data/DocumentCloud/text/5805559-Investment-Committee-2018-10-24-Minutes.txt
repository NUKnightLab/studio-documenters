ASSISTANT EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR’S REPORT
Assistant Executive Director, Gail A. Oxendine, addressed the following with the Board
of Trustees:
 Member updates
o William D. Sparks: Corrections are underway with respect to Mr. Sparks
request which will enable him to be eligible for early retirement per his
request.
o Andrew Daniels: Staff has thoroughly researched Mr. Daniels claim of not
being informed of the ASF recoupment which lessened his monthly
pension amount. Mr. Daniels was informed via letter that the estimates
that he received were very close to actual payment amount.
o Audrey Pogue: Ms. Pogue is requesting retirement based upon prior
estimates that she received, which were issued in error. Staff is looking
further into this concern.
 Housing and Revitalization Presentation
o Q&A session was provided to approximately 45 employees and was well
received.
 Website GO Live Newsletter
o The RSCD website is scheduled to go live in November. Date is
dependent on when system administration training is completed.
o The first quarterly newsletter draft is being reviewed and will be presented
to the Board for approval prior to the next Board meeting on November 7 th,
2018.
 Cessation of Membership; Re-employment by the Employer
o It has been determined that sixteen (16) current retirees were rehired by
the City of Detroit and their pension payments were not suspended as
required by the POA (Plan of Adjustment). A copy of the proposed letter
and POA language has been provided for Board review.
 Staff and Salary Information
o Staff salary information has been provided as requested by Trustee
Sheehan.

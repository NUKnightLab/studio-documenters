Date Issued: September 5, 2019
NOTICE OF PUBLIC HEARINGS
The Finance Committee of the Board of Directors of the Cook County Health and Hospitals System has
scheduled the public hearings listed below to receive public testimony on the proposed Cook County Health
(CCH) FY2020 Preliminary Budget and Three (3) Year Financial Forecast: FY2020-2022 for CCH’s Impact
2023 Strategic Plan (Impact 2023 Financial Forecast).
Public Hearing Dates/Times:
Tuesday, September 10, 2019 at 6:00 P.M.
Wednesday, September 11, 2019 at 9:00 A.M.
Location:
1950 West Polk Street, Room 5301
Chicago, Illinois
The public may testify at these hearings. Persons wishing to testify should give their name, and if applicable,
the name of the organization they are representing, to the Secretary to the Board, 1950 W. Polk, Room 9106,
Chicago, Illinois, Telephone: 312/864-0907, via email to dsantana@cookcountyhhs.org, or electronically, as
indicated below. Persons can also register at the hearing location at the time of the hearing. Speakers will be
called in the order in which they registered. Each speaker will be limited to three (3) minutes. If a speaker is
presenting a written statement, they are requested to submit fifteen (15) copies of any written statement to the
Secretary to the Board prior to speaking.
The purpose of these hearings is to receive information; no action will be taken at these hearings. The
materials related to the proposed CCH FY2020 Preliminary Budget and Impact 2023 Financial Forecast are
available on the CCH website (see link below). It will be considered by the CCH Board of Directors at a
Special Board Meeting scheduled for Friday, September 13, 2019 at 12:30 P.M.
Please visit the CCH website at https://cookcountyhealth.org/about/board-ofdirectors/budget-information/ for information and links to:
• View the proposed CCH FY2020 Preliminary Budget Presentation and Impact 2023 Financial
Forecast
• Register electronically as a public speaker for a hearing
• Submit electronically your remarks or testimony on the proposed CCH FY2020
Preliminary Budget and Impact 2023 Financial Forecast directly to the CCH
Finance Committee and Board (via Secretary’s Office)

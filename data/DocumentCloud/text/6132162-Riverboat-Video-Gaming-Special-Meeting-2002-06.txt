MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
June 3, 2002
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
A Special Meeting of the Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”) was held on June 3, 2002. The
Board met in the conference room of the Gaming Board Offices on the 3rd floor at 160 N.
LaSalle for closed session.
The following Board Members were present: Chairman Gregory Jones and Members Ira
Rogal, Elzie Higginbottom, and Tobias Barry.
The meeting convened at 9:20 A.M. Pursuant to Section 2(c), paragraph (11) of the Open
Meetings Act, Member Higginbottom moved that the Board retire to Closed Session to
discuss litigation matters. Member Barry seconded the motion. The Board adopted the
motion by unanimous consent, and retired to closed session.
The Board convened its Open Session at 10:07 A.M.
Administrative Hearing/ALJ Reports
IN RE THE DISCIPLINARY ACTION OF EMERALD CASINO, INC., DC-01-05 –
EMERALD CASINO, INC.’S MOTION TO DISINTERESTED MEMBERS OF THE
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD TO DISQUALIFY ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD MEMBER
Based upon a review of the parties’ written submission, Member Rogal moved that the
Board deny Emerald Casino, Inc.’s Motion to Disinterested Members of the Illinois
Gaming Board to Disqualify Illinois Gaming Board Member and issue a written order
consistent therewith. Member Barry seconded the motion. Chairman Jones abstained. The
Board approved the motion unanimously by voice vote.
At 10:10 A.M. Member Higginbottom moved that Pursuant to Section 2 (c), paragraph (4)
of the Open Meetings Act, the Board retire to Closed Session to discuss Emerald
Casino, Inc.’s Third Motion to Disqualify Administrative Law Judge. Member Barry
seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by voice vote.
The Board reconvened open session at 10:55 A.M.

 IN RE THE DISCIPLINARY ACTION OF EMERALD CASINO, INC., DC-01-05 –
EMERALD CASINO, INC.’S THIRD MOTION TO DISQUALIFY ADMINISTRATIVE
LAW JUDGE
Based upon a review of the parties’ written submissions, Member Rogal moved that the
Board deny Emerald Casino, Inc.’s Third Motion to Disqualify Administrative Law
Judge and issue a written order consistent therewith. Member Barry seconded the
motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by voice vote.
The Board adjourned at 10:56 A.M.

Respectfully submitted,
Monica Thomas

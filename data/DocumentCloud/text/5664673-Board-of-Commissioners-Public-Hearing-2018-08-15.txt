NOTICE OF PUBLIC HEARING
In accordance with the provisions of "An Act in the relation of the creation, maintenance, operation,
and improvement of the Chicago Park District", 70 ILCS 1505 et. seq., PUBLIC NOTICE is hereby
given that a PUBLIC HEARING CONCERNING THE INTENT OF THE BOARD OF
COMMISSIONERS OF THE CHICAGO PARK DISTRICT'S PROPOSAL TO SELL BONDS OF
THE DISTRICT, IN ONE OR MORE SERIES, IN THE AMOUNT OF NOT TO EXCEED
$95,000,000 FOR THE PURPOSE OF: (I) FINANCING CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROGRAMS
FOR THE PAYMENT OF LAND CONDEMNED OR PURCHASED FOR PARKS, FOR THE
BUILDING, MAINTAINING, IMPROVING AND PROTECTING OF THE SAME AND THE EXISTING
FACILITIES OF THE DISTRICT AND FOR THE REFINANCING OF BONDS PREVIOUSLY
ISSUED TO FINANCE THE SAME; (II) FINANCING HARBOR AND MARINA IMPROVEMENTS; AND
(III) FINANCING THE PAYMENT OF EXPENSES INCIDENT THERETO will be held on Wednesday,
August 15, 2018, at 11:30 A.M. in the 8th Floor Board Room of the Administration Building at 541 N.
Fairbanks Court, Chicago, Illinois 60611.

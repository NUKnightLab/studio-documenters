ZONING AND BUILDING AGENDA
OCTOBER 4, 2000

THE ZONING BOARD OF APPEALS RECOMMENDATION:
234833

DOCKET #6985 - FRANK A. RUFFOLO, Owner, 15225 South 71 st Avenue,
Orland Park, Illinois 60462, Application (No. SU-20-07; Z00038). Submitted
by John J. George, Daley & George, Ltd ., 20 South Clark Street, Suite 400 ,
Chicago, Illinois 60603. Seeking a SPECIAL USE, UNIQUE USE in the R-4
Single Family Residence District to park employee and commercial vehicles at
their residence in Section 18 of Bremen Township. Property consists of
approximately 5.8 acres located on the north side of 153rd Street
approximately 570 feet east of Harlem Avenue in Bremen Township. Intended
use: A Special Use for Unique Use in the R-4 Single Family Residence District
to continue to park employee and commercial vehicles at their residence for
seven years. If, after that period of time the applicant wishes to extend the
time, the enacting ordinance would delegate to the Zoning Board the ability to
extend the time, only after showing that there is a justification to continue the
use.
Recommendation: The Zoning Board of Appeals recommends that the
Special Use for Unique Use application be granted for seven years with
conditions as detailed in the Findings and Recommendations presented
herewith.

*The next regularly scheduled meeting is presently set for Tuesday, October 17, 2000.

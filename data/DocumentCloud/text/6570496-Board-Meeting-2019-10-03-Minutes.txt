ROLL CALL
The Clerk of the Board called the roll. All four Board Members answered present.

ANNOUNCEMENTS
Chairman Currie welcomed members of the public and staff.

PUBLIC REMARKS
None.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES
The minutes of the Board’s September 19, 2019 regular meeting were approved by a vote of 4-0.

 RULEMAKINGS
ADJUSTED STANDARDS
AS 19-1

Petition of Midwest Generation, LLC for an Adjusted Standard
from 35 Ill. Adm. Code 811 and 814 (Land) – The Board granted
petitioner’s motion and stayed this proceeding for 60 days to
Monday, December 2, 2019. The Board also directed petitioner
to file a status report at the close of the stay.

4-0

ADMINISTRATIVE CITATIONS
ADJUDICATORY CASES
PCB 19-78

Sierra Club v. Illinois Power Generating Company, Illinois
Power Resources Generating, LLC, Electric Energy, Inc., and
Vistra Energy Corporation (Water – Enforcement, Citizens) –
The Board granted respondents’ motion to bifurcate the
hearings and delay any expert discovery on remedy until after
the alleged violations phase of the case.

4-0

PCB 20-17

People of the State of Illinois v. Mor-Agra Grain Handling, Inc.
(Air – Enforcement) – Upon receiving a stipulation, proposed
settlement, and agreed motion for relief from the hearing
requirement in this enforcement action involving a facility
located in Boone County, the Board ordered publication of the
required newspaper notice.

4-0

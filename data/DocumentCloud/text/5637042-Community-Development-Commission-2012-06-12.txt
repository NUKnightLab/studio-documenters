 III.

NEW BUSINESS
A.

STOCKYARDS ANNEX TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 11)
Request authority for the Department of Housing and Economic Development to
negotiate a redevelopment agreement with Halsted Pershing Morgan, LLC for
redevelopment of the property located at 815 W. Pershing Road in the Stockyards
Annex Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and to recommend
to the City Council of the City of Chicago the designation of Halsted Pershing
Morgan, LLC as Developer.
Project Manager: John Molloy
Motioned by Stein, seconded by Sahli. Approved 9-0. Yeas: Carrott, Garner,
Kostiner, Martin, Mooney, Newsome, Olander, Sahli and Stein. Logan not
present.

B.

MIDWEST TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 28)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Housing and Economic
Development=s intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Twenty-Three
Corporation for the disposition of the property located at 2828-46 W. Taylor
Street and 2845-49 W. Arthington Street in the Midwest TIF Redevelopment
Project Area, to request alternative proposals, and to approve the sale of the
property to Twenty-Three Corporation if no responsive alternative proposals are
received.
Project Manager: Michelle Nolan
Motioned by Stein, seconded by Sahli. Approved 9-0. Yeas: Carrott, Garner,
Kostiner, Logan, Martin, Mooney, Newsome, Olander, Sahli and Stein.

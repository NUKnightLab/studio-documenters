The following projects were reviewed by the PRC:
1. 1638 N. Sedgwick
2nd Ward
Old Town Triangle District
Proposed new construction of a 3 ½-story, multi-family, masonry building with a roof
deck
Action:

Approved unanimously with the following conditions:
1. The proposed standard and utility-sized dark red brick (Oxford
Ironspot brick produced by Glen-Gery Brick) is approved. The
mortar color shall be changed to match the color of the face brick
rather than the proposed Pewter No.1319-06 grey mortar. Brick and
mortar samples shall be submitted with the permit application;
2. The cladding of the rooftop enclosure shall have a dark color;
3. The standard-sized brick wrap on the north elevation shall be extended
to at least 10’ behind the front façade; and,
4. The side walls of the concrete front entrance stairs shall be clad with
face brick to be more compatible with historic stairs within the district.

2. 2112 W. Haddon
2nd Ward
Ukrainian Village District
Proposed new construction of a 3-story, multi-family, masonry building with a roof
deck and garage
Action:

Approved unanimously with the following conditions:
1. The rooftop enclosure’s visibility be minimized by sloping its roof
down at the front and by reducing its width to the minimum required
to accommodate a stair;

 2. Front stair wing walls be reduced to the minimum height needed and
dimensioned drawings be submitted with the permit application;
3. Cladding samples be submitted with the permit application for staff
review and approval; and
4. Window details be submitted with the permit application for staff
review and approval.

3. 1327 N. Wicker Park
Wicker Park District
Proposed new construction of a 3-story, multi-family, masonry building
Action:

1st Ward

Approved unanimously with the following conditions:
1. Sample masonry be submitted with the permit application for staff
review and approval;
2. Window details be submitted with the permit application for staff
review and approval; and
3. The project as proposed would require a zoning change and other
relief, and the Commission takes no position regarding any requested
zoning change/variance relative to the zoning code requirements.

4. 2106 N. Hudson
43rd Ward
Mid-North District
Proposed rooftop addition, rear addition, and front façade alterations to an existing 2story single-family residence and a new detached rear garage
Action:

This item was not reviewed by PRC per request by the applicant.

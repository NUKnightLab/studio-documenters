STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on January 8, 2019
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman of the Local Panel; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Local Panel; Angela C. Thomas,
Member of the Local Panel; Helen J. Kim, General Counsel; Anna Hamburg-Gal, Associate General Counsel; and staff
members. Kimberly F. Stevens, Executive Director participated by telephone.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD December 11, 2018

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the December 11, 2018 meeting. Member Anderson moved to
approve the minutes. Member Thomas seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gierut asked Executive Director Stevens to present the reports. She presented the reports and summarized
their highlights.
V.

REVIEW OF PROPOSED WRITTEN DECISIONS AND ORDERS
1.
Case No. L-CB-18-020
Marqueal L. Williams and Amalgamated Transit Union, Local 241

General Counsel Kim called the case. Member Thomas made a motion to approve the proposed written decision. Member
Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
2.
Case No. L-CA-18-060
LaChelle Bowers and City of Chicago (Finance Department)
Case No. L-CB-18-038
LaChelle Bowers and American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31
General Counsel Kim called the case. Member Anderson made a motion to approve the proposed written decision.
Member Thomas seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VI.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.
Case No. L-RC-16-034
American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31 and City of Chicago

General Counsel Kim called and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member Thomas moved to
accept the Administrative Law Judge’s Recommended Decision and Order. Member Anderson seconded the motion, and
the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
2.
Case No. L-RC-19-004
American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31 and City of Chicago
General Counsel Kim called the case and noted that no party filed exceptions to Administrative Law Judge Owen’s
Recommended Decision and Order. The Board declined to review the decision on its own motion.
3.
Case Nos. L-RC-19-005 and L-RC-19-011
American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31 and City of Chicago
General Counsel Kim called the case and noted that no party filed exceptions to Administrative Law Judge Hamburg-Gal’s
Recommended Decision and Order. The Board declined to review the decision on its own motion.

 VII.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded it was unnecessary.
VIII.

PUBLIC COMMENT

No members of the public sought to make comments.
IX.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board, in
Chicago, Illinois, this 5th day of February 2019.

MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL MEETING
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
May 29, 2001
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
A Special Meeting of the Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”) was held on May 29, 2001 in
Room #S-304 of the State of Illinois Building, Chicago, Illinois, pursuant to the Illinois
Open Meetings Act, 5 ILCS 120/1 et seq.
The following Board Members were present: Chairman Gregory C. Jones and Members
Sterling M. Ryder, Ira Rogal, and Staci Yandle. Member Stuart P. Levine was not present.
Also in attendance were: Administrator Sergio E. Acosta, Deputy Administrators Allan S.
McDonald, Thomas Swoik, James W. Wagner, and Deputy Chief Legal Counsel Jeannette P.
Tamayo, and other members of the staff.

The meeting was convened at 3:15 p.m. Member Yandle moved that the Board retire
to Closed Session pursuant to Section 2 (c), paragraphs (1) and (11) of the Open
Meetings Act and Section 6(d) of the Riverboat Gambling Act to discuss the
following subject matters:
1. Pending litigation and matters involving probable litigation;and
2. Personnel matters.
Member Rogal seconded the motion. The Board adopted the motion by unanimous
consent.
The Board adjourned at 5:42 P.M.
Respectfully submitted,

Monica L. Thomas
Secretary to the Board

2.

Approval of Phone Poll to Invite Marquette Associates;
Motion for Approval and Adoption of Minutes. It was moved by Trustee McFadden
and seconded by Trustee Kravitz to spread upon the record the phone poll of Trustees
to invite Marquette Associates to present as a finalist in light of Mercer’s decision to
exit the public funds’ consulting business and, therefore, no longer participate in the
RFP process for Investment Consultant.
Vote Result: MOTION ADOPTED UNANIMOUSLY BY VOICE VOTE

3.

General Investment Consultant Search Finalist Presentations;
The following representatives of Callan Associates Inc. presented their Investment
Consultant qualifications and capabilities to the Investment Committee: Ronald
Peyton, Chairman and CEO, Kenneth E. Brunke Jr., Senior Vice-President, Kevin
Dolsen, Senior Vice-President, and Michael Joecken, Vice President.
From NEPC, LLC, the following representatives presented their Investment
Consultant qualifications and capabilities to the Investment Committee: Richard M.
Charlton, Chairman and CEO, John Krimmel, Senior Consultant, and Kristin R.
Finney-Cooke, Senior Consultant.
From Marquette Associates Inc., the following representatives presented their
Investment Consultant qualifications and capabilities to the Investment Committee:
Brian Wrubel, Managing Director, Pat Krolak, Managing Director, and Kelli Schrade,
Director of Manager Research.

Requests for Proposals for Service Interruption Contractor
Mamata Burgi, Procurement Manager reported that the department had solicited a
Request for Proposal (RFP) for contractual services to provide assistance to the
operations team for water service interruptions and/or restorations. Ms. Burgi said that
bids were due by Friday and that she was here to inform the committee that the
contract will come before the board at its February 21, 2018 meeting. Director Brown
added that the intent is to bring the project in house but until the department can staff
and train it cannot do without a contract.

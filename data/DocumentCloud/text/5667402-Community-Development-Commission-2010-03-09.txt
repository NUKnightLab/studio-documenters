RANDOLPH/WELLS REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 42)
Request authority for the Department of Community Development to negotiate a
redevelopment agreement with the following five entities regarding the
redevelopment of Randolph Tower located at 188 W. Randolph Street in the
Randolph/Wells Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and to
recommend to the City Council the designation of Randolph Tower City
Apartments LLC; Randolph Tower City Apartments, Inc.; Randolph Tower
Master Historic Tenant, LLC; Randolph Tower Affordable City Apartments,
LLC; and Randolph Tower Master Affordable Historic Tenant, LLC as
Developer.
Project Manager: Paul Zalmezak
Motioned by Sahli, seconded by Bland. Approved 5-0. Yeas: Bland, Kostiner,
Leon, Sahli and Stein. Logan recused.

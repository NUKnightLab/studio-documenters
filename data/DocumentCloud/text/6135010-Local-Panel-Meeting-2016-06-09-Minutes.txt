STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on June 9, 2016
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 N LaSalle Street, 4th Floor, Room S401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Board; Richard Lewis, Member of the Board; Kathryn
Zeledon Nelson, General Counsel; Anna Hamburg-Gal, Associate General Counsel; and staff members. Melissa Mlynski,
Executive Director; Sarah Kerley, Deputy General Counsel; and staff members participated via telephone conference from
the Board’s Springfield office.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD ON May 10, 2016

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the May 10, 2016 meeting. Member Anderson moved to approve
the minutes. Member Lewis seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR’S REPORTS

Executive Director Mlynski presented the Executive Director reports. The Chair and Members discussed the reports.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.

Case No. L-CA-16-007
Sherise M. Hogan/Chicago Transit Authority

Chairman Gierut called the case and asked General Counsel Nelson to present the issues to the Board. General Counsel
Nelson stated that the Executive Director issued an Order Holding Case in Abeyance and that Charging Party filed an
appeal. After her presentation, General Counsel Nelson recommended that the Board accept the Executive Director’s
Order. After discussion, Member Lewis moved to affirm the Executive Director’s Order Holding Case in Abeyance.
Member Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
2.

Case No. L-CB-16-015
James Kondilis/International Brotherhood of Teamsters, Local 700

Chairman Gierut called the case and asked General Counsel Nelson to present the issues to the Board. General Counsel
Nelson stated that the Executive Director issued a dismissal on a charge alleging that the Respondent violated Section
10(b) of the Act and that Charging Party filed an appeal and a supplemental appeal. After her presentation, General
Counsel Nelson recommended that the Board deny Charging Party’s supplemental appeal and to accept the Executive
Director’s dismissal. After discussion, Member Anderson moved to deny the supplemental appeal. Member Lewis
seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote. Then Member Lewis moved to affirm the
Executive Director’s dismissal. Member Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice
vote.
VI.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded it was
unnecessary.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board, in
Chicago, Illinois, this 12th day of July, 2016.

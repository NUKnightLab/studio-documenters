3.

Hedge Fund-of-Funds Search;
Callan reviewed the results of the hedge fund-of-funds search, including a brief
overview of the search objectives and summary due diligence for all the finalists.
Callan then presented optimization scenarios based on the portfolio characteristics of
each of the finalists for the Committee’s consideration. It was decided by the entire
Investment Committee to invite the four finalists to present to the Board at the
January 5, 2012 Board Meeting for further evaluation.

4.

Structure Implementation Update;
Ms. Hackett discussed the fee impact due to the restructuring of Fiduciary
Management Associates’ mandates.
Motion to Approve FMA’s Revised Fee for the Small Cap Value Portfolio. It was
moved by Trustee Fitzgerald and seconded by Trustee Janura to recommend to the
Board to approve FMA’s revised fee schedule for the management of the Small Cap
Value portfolio for the Cook County Fund. It is further moved that the Fund
Investment Staff and Executive Director be authorized to take all actions necessary to
effect the foregoing.

Discussion of Litigation Matters
Closed Session
Motion to recess the regular meeting and convene into closed session, pursuant to an exception
to the Open Meetings Act, 5 ILCS 120/2(c)(11), which states: “litigation, when an action
against, affecting or on behalf of the particular public body has been filed and is pending before
a court or administrative tribunal, or when the public body finds that an action is probable or
imminent, in which case the basis for the finding shall be recorded and entered into the minutes
of the closed meeting.”

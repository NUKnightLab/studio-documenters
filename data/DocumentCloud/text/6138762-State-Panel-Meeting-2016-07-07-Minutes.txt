BOARD ACTIONS
1.

Case Nos. S-CA-16-087 and S-CB-16-017
State of Illinois, Central Management Services/American Federation of State, County and
Municipal Employees, Council 31

Chairman Hartnett asked General Counsel Nelson to call and present the case. General Counsel Nelson stated that
Central Management Services (CMS) filed a Motion for the Board to Expedite its Final Decision and Order in this
impasse case. She then summarized the status of the case and made alternate recommendations for the Board to
consider. After detailed discussion by the Board, Member Washington moved to deny the motion and to schedule

the briefs and exceptions according to the Board rules and regulations. Member Samolis seconded the
motion, and the Board passed it unanimously by a voice vote.

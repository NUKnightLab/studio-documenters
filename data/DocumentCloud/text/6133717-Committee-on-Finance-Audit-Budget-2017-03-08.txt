ROLL CALL: Present: Chairman Silva, Peterson, Irvine, Alva Rosales, Youngblood, and Miller. There
quorum of six committee members present.

COMMITTEE ACTION: The committee reviewed the Finance report and approved the February 8th
2017 committee minutes.

Then, after extensive review by the committee, Chairman Silva asked for a motion to place the
recommended approved items on the omnibus for Board approval. The recommended items inclu
all agenda items.

Mr. Youngblood moved for approval of the omnibus, as stated, and the motion to approve the omn
was seconded by Mr. Irvine. The motion to approve the omnibus, and to recommend, to the Board
approval of the omnibus, passed with six yes votes. The recommended omnibus contained the
following items:

1 An ordinance regulating travel, meal and lodging expenses pursuant to the Illinois Local
Government Travel Expense Control Act.
2 An ordinance authorizing an agreement with Google, Inc. to access Authority property to co
images for Google’s Street View Program.
3 An ordinance authorizing a co-promotional trade agreement for 2017 with the City of Chicag
through its Department of Cultural Affairs and Special Events.
4 An ordinance approving the Authority’s 2013-2015 Title VI Program Triennial Report and
authorizing submittal to the Federal Transit Administration.

MOTION TO ADJOURN: Chairman Silva asked for a motion to adjourn the Finance, Audit, and Budg
committee meeting of March 8th, 2017. Mr. Youngblood moved to adjourn and the motion was
approved by unanimous voice vote.

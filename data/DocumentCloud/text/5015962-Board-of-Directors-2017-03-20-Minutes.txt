Mr. Vosburg called the public hearing to order at 5:40 PM.
Mr. Vosburg informed the hearing of the brownfield approval process and the tax increment
financing request per the Brownfield Plan. Mr. Castellanos, Ms. Balaban, and Mr. Stuntz provided
details regarding the redevelopment plan for the project location and answered questions
regarding: building rehabilitation, site remediation, future site users, project timeline and
Community Advisory Committee timing, green space use, employment of Detroit residents and
contractors, job training opportunities, residential home rehabilitation, plans for current
neighborhood residents to benefit from revitalization, boundaries of developer’s work, plans for

 wider neighborhood revitalization, consideration for the neighborhood history, affordable housing,
senior citizen residents, security, schools, and current neighborhood economic condition.
Five members of the public spoke in favor of the project, six additional members of the public
indicated written support of the project. There were no comments received in opposition to the
project.
Citing no further public comments, Mr. Vosburg closed the public hearing at 6:39 PM.

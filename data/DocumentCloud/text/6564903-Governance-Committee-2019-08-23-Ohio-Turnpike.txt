 BACKGROUND
Other facts to consider:
• In the NOACA region, the Ohio Turnpike has four exits in Lorain
County and one in Cuyahoga County.
• The Ohio Turnpike Commission is headquartered in Berea.
• There is currently no opportunity to receive funding from the Ohio
Turnpike Commission as bonding has been exhausted.
• Turnpike Commission Director Ferzan Ahmed has expressed interest
in participating in discussions on the Hyperloop.

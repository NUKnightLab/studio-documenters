MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING - VIDEO
OPEN SESSION
September 19, 2013
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
On Thursday, September 19, 2013 a Regular Meeting - Video of the Illinois Gaming Board
(“Board”) was held on the 5th floor at 160 N. LaSalle at the Michael J. Bilandic Building,
Chicago, Illinois. The Open Session on Video Gaming started at approximately 12:15 P.M.
Present were Members Lee Gould, Maribeth Vander Weele, Michael Holewinski and Chairman
Aaron Jaffe.
APPROVAL OF MINUTES –
Member Gould moved that the Board approve the open session minutes of its Regular
Meeting of August 22, 2013. Member Vander Weele seconded the motion. The Board
approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
ADMINISTRATOR’S REPORT –
Administrator Ostrowski stated that he was a guest speaker at the Illinois licensed Beverage
Association conference. The Administrator gave a brief video gaming summary indicating 2,402
locations are up and running and 9,794 VGT’s are operational. He also commented that when
someone comes in to inspect the premises or the VGT’s, location owners and representatives
should make sure that credentials are viewed stating that the agent is from the IGB.
SUPPLIER, DISTRIBUTOR AND MANUFACTURER LICENSEE ITEMS –
•

VGT Manufacturing, Inc. - Manufacturer & Distributor License Renewal
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Holewinski moved that the Board renew the
Manufacturer & Distributor Licenses of VGT Manufacturing, Inc., effective
September 19, 2013, for a term of one year, expiring in September 2014. Member
Gould seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call
vote.

•

PDS Gaming – Illinois, LLC – Distributor License Renewal
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Vander Weele moved that the Board renew
the Distributor License of PDS Gaming – Illinois, LLC, effective September 19,
2013, for a term of one year, expiring in September 2014. Member Gould
seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.

Action on Video Gaming Location Applicants Due to Legislative Changes
Pursuant to Public Acts 98-0077 and 98-0587, Member Holewinski moved that the
Board rescind the denials and/or application withdrawals of the Video Gaming
Location License applicants listed on the “September 19, 2013 Rescinded Denials
and Withdrawals” which will be posted on the IGB website and available after
the Board meeting. Member Vander Weele seconded the motion. The Board
approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.

PROPOSED COMPLAINTS AND DISCIPLINARY ACTIONS –
•

T’s Gaming, LLC – Terminal Operator Licensee

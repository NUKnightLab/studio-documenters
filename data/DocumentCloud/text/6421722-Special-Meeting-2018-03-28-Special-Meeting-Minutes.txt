Meeting of the Illinois Medical District Commission
2100 W. Harrison St., Chicago, IL 60612 Room 106
March 28, 2018, 2:30 p.m.
Minutes for March 28, 2018
Commissioners present: Blake Sercye, Neville Reid, John Cooke, Juan Morado, Jr. and Everett
Rand (via telephone)
Others present: Dr. Suzet McKinney, Kesner Bienvenu, Fabian Camarena, Jeanne Cotini, Chris
Fahey, Ryan Gage, Judith Salgado, Kate Schellinger, Nicole Vahl, Denielle Mathys (Court
Reporter), and Jennifer Van Wie (Foley & Lardner)
President Sercye opened the meeting welcoming all guests in the room. Mr. Bienvenu took the
roll call of Commissioners. President Sercye moved to allow Commissioner Rand to
participate via phone and Commissioner Reid seconded. The motion carried by voice vote.
President Sercye welcomed Mr. Juan Morado, Jr., who has been appointed by Mayor Emanuel as
new commissioner.
Old Business
Bond Redemption – Mr. Bienvenu provided an update on the bond redemption. Mr.
Bienvenu read the resolution. President Sercye moved that the resolution be approved, and
Commissioner Rand seconded, and the resolution was approved by voice vote.
Closed Session
Upon motion by President Sercye and seconded by Commissioner Reid, the Commission went
into closed session under the Open Meetings Act pursuant to 5 ILCS 120/2 (c) (1), (6) and (11).
Open Session
The Commission returned to open session. President Sercye mentioned that the Commission
will resume to approve the following:
Election of Officer – President Sercye moved that Commissioner Reid serve as Vice
President and Commissioner Cooke seconded, and was approved by voice vote.

Commissioner Rand moved that the meeting be adjourned. The motion was seconded
by Commissioner Cooke and approved by voice vote.
Respectfully submitted,
Cory Foster
Secretary

MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL MEETING

OF THE

COMMISSIONERS OF THE CHICAGO HOUSING AUTHORITY
April 9, 2008
9:00 a.m.
The Commissioners of the Chicago Housing Authority met in Special Session on Wednesday,
April 9, 2008 at approximately 3:10 p.m. at the 60 East Van Buren Corporate Offices, R oom
304 in Chicago, Illinois.
The meeting was called to order by Chairperson Nesbitt, and upon roll call, those present and
absent were as follows:
Present:

Hallie Amey
Michael Ivers
Martin Nesbitt
Carlos Ponce
Sandra Young
Mary Wiggins

Absent:

Bridget O’Keefe

Also present were Lorraine Hocker of the DuPage Housing Authority acting as the meeting
Facilitator; Lewis Jordan, Chief Executive Officer; Jorge Cazares, General Counsel; Pamela
Mitchell-Boyd, Managing Director of the Board Office and Lee Chuc-Gill, Custodian and
Keeper of Records.
There being a quorum present the meeting duly convened and business was transacted as
follows:
The Chairperson announced that pursuant to Section 2(c)(16) of the Open Meetings Act, 5ILCS
120 the Commissioners will vote on whether to go into Closed Executive Session and possibly
discuss personnel related and collective negotiating matters, purchase, sale and lease of real
estate property, pending, probably or imminent litigation and establishment of reserves and
settlement of claims.

 THE CERTIFICATE AS TO SERVICE OF NOTICE OF SPECIAL MEETING and the
WAIVER OF NOTICE AND CONSENT TO SPECIAL MEETING, as signed by the
Commissioners of said Authority, were read and ordered spread upon the minutes of this Special
Meeting and filed for record.

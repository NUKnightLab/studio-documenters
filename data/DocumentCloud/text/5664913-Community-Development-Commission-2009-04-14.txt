 III.

OLD BUSINESS
A.

CENTRAL WEST TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 27)
Request authority to designate Heartland Housing, Inc. and First Baptist
Congregational Church as the successful respondent and to reject all other
responses to the RFP issued by the Department of Planning and Development and
the Department of Housing on September 9, 2008 for the purchase and
redevelopment of the former Viceroy Hotel and adjacent lots located at 1519 W.
Warren Boulevard in the Central West Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment
Project Area. The hotel building has been found by the Chicago Historic
Resources Survey to possess architectural features that make it potentially
significant in the context of the surrounding community.
Project Manager: Mark Muenzer
Motioned by Bland, seconded by Martin. Approved 7-0. Yeas: Bland, Leon,
Logan, Martin, Sahli, Stein and Lowry.

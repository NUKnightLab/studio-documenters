MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL MEETING
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
June 7, 2000
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
A Special Meeting of the Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”) was held on June 7, 2000, in
room #S-304 of the State of Illinois Building, Chicago, Illinois, pursuant to the Illinois
Open Meetings Act, 5 ILCS 120.
The following Board members were present, Chairman Gregory C. Jones, members
Joseph Lamendella, Stuart Levine and Staci Yandle. Member Sterling M. Ryder was
present telephonically.
Also in attendance were: Administrator Sergio E. Acosta, Special Counsel to the Board
Robert F. Casey, Chief Legal Counsel Mareile’ Cusack, Deputy Administrators Joseph
Haughey, Allan McDonald, James Wagner, Thomas Swoik and other members of the
staff.
The meeting convened at 11:15 a.m.. Member Lamendella moved that the Board retire
to Closed Session pursuant to Section 2(c), paragraphs (4), and (14) of the Open
Meetings Act, to discuss the following subject matters:
1. Issues concerning owner licensee investigations;
2. Disciplinary Complaints
Member Levine seconded the motion. The Board adopted the motion by unanimous
consent.
The Board adjourned at 4:00.
Respectfully submitted,

Monica L. Thomas
Secretary to the Board

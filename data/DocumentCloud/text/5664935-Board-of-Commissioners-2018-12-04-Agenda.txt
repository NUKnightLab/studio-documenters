PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Pursuant to Cook County Code of Ordinances, public testimony will be permitted at regular and special
meetings of the Board. Duly authorized public speakers shall be called upon at this time to deliver
testimony germane to a specific item(s) on the meeting agenda, and the testimony must not exceed three
(3) minutes. The names of duly authorized speakers shall be published in the Post Board Action Agenda
and Journal of Proceedings as prepared by the Clerk of the Board.

PROPOSED RESOLUTION
TO FILL VACANCY IN THE OFFICE OF RECORDER OF DEEDS
WHEREAS, effective December 3, 2018, Cook County Recorder of Deeds, Karen Yarbrough will resign
from and vacate the office of Cook County Recorder; Karen Yarbrough will assume the Office of Cook
County Clerk; and
WHEREAS, effective December 3, 2018, the Office of the Cook County Recorder will be vacant; and
WHEREAS, in accordance with 10 ILCS 5/25-11, a vacancy in the Office of the Recorder of Deeds
shall be filled by the County Board of Commissioners for the remainder of the current term; and
WHEREAS, the current term for the Cook County Recorder of Deeds expires on December 1, 2020;
and
WHEREAS, in 2016, the voters in Cook County approved a binding referendum which will eliminate the
Office of the Cook County Recorder of Deeds and transfer its responsibilities to the Cook County Clerk’s
Office by December 7, 2020; and
WHEREAS, Edward M. Moody is nominated to fill the remainder of the term of the Cook County
Recorder of Deeds; and
WHEREAS, Edward M. Moody has most recently served as the 6th District Commissioner for the Cook
County Board of Commissioners and in that capacity is familiar with the operations of the Recorder of
Deeds; and
WHEREAS, Edward M. Moody has also served as Chairman of the Worth Township Fair Housing
Review Board, Worth Township Highway Commissioner, Board Member and President of School District

WHEREAS, Edward M. Moody has been an effective legislator and administrator and will be influential

in accordance with the 2016 referendum.
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that the Cook County Board of Commissioners does
hereby approve the appointment of Edward M. Moody as the Cook County Recorder of Deeds through
the remainder of the current term; and
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that Edward M. Moody shall undertake the oath of office for the
Recorder of Deeds on December 4, 2018 in accordance with 55 ILCS 5/3-5003.

MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL MEETING
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
OCTOBER 15, 1999
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS

NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
A Special Meeting of the Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”) was held on October 15, 1999, in room #S-304 of the State of
Illinois Building, Chicago, Illinois, pursuant to the Illinois Open Meetings Act, 5 ILCS 120.
The following Board members participated telephonically, Robert F. Vickrey, Chairman, and members Gregory Jones,
Joseph Lamendella, Stuart Levine and Staci Yandle.
Also in attendance were: Administrator Sergio E. Acosta, Special Counsel to the Board Robert F. Casey, Deputy
Administrator Allan McDonald, Chief Counsel Mareile’ Cusack, other members of the staff, and Roger Flahaven from the
Office of the Attorney General.
The meeting was convened at 4:15 p.m. Member Jones moved that the Board retire to Closed Session pursuant to
pursuant to Section 2(c), paragraphs (4) and (11) of the Open Meetings Act to discuss the following subject matters:
Issues concerning applicants and licensees; and
Pending Litigation
Member Lamendella seconded the motion. The Board adopted the motion by unanimous consent.
The Board adjourned at 5:16 p.m.
Respectfully submitted,

Susan O. Weber
Secretary of the Board

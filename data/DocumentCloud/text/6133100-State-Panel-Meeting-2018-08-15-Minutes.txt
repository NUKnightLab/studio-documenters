STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
STATE PANEL
Minutes of the State Panel Meeting
Held on August 15, 2018
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.— at 160 N. LaSalle Street, S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

John Hartnett, Chairman; Christopher Glynn, Member of the Board; John Samolis, Member of the Board; Keith Snyder,
Member of the Board; Kimberly F. Stevens, Executive Director; Helen J. Kim, General Counsel; Anna Hamburg-Gal,
Associate General Counsel; and staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD July 10, 2018

Chairman Hartnett called for approval of the minutes of the July 10, 2018 meeting. Member Samolis moved to approve
the minutes. Member Snyder seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Hartnett asked Executive Director Stevens to present the Executive Director’s reports. She presented the reports
and summarized the highlights.
V.

REVIEW OF PROPOSED WRITTEN DECISIONS AND ORDERS
1. Case No. S-CA-16-132
Service Employees Int’l Union Healthcare Illinois & Indiana/State of Illinois, Department of
Central Management Services

General Counsel Kim called the case. Member Glynn made a motion to approve the proposed written decision. Member
Samolis seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
2.

Case No. S-CB-17-030
Roger McComb/American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31

General Counsel Kim called the case. Member Samolis made a motion to approve the proposed written decision. Member
Glynn seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
3.

Case No. S-CB-18-017
Ramtin Sabet/Fraternal Order of Police

General Counsel Kim called the case. Member Snyder made a motion to approve the proposed written decision. Member
Samolis seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
4.

Case No. S-CB-18-021
Carlo J. Carlotta/Illinois Council of Police

General Counsel Kim called the case. Member Glynn made a motion to approve the proposed written decision. Member
Snyder seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VI.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.

Case No. S-UC-17-083
State of Illinois, Department of Central Management Services/American Federation of State, County
and Municipal Employees, Council 31

General Counsel Kim called the case and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member Samolis
moved to reject the Administrative Law Judge’s Recommended Decision and Order (RDO). Member Glynn seconded the
motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

 2. Case Nos. S-CA-15-095 and S-CA-15-111
James Young/Village of University Park (Police Department)
General Counsel Kim called the case and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member Glynn
moved to reject the RDO. Member Snyder seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
3. Case No. S-CB-18-023
George A. Pruitt, III/American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31
General Counsel Kim called and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member Samolis moved to
accept the Executive Director dismissal. Member Snyder seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a
voice vote.
4. Case No. S-RC-18-041
American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31/ State of Illinois,
Department of Central Management Services
General Counsel Kim called and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member Glynn moved to
accept the Executive Director Order. Member Samolis seconded the motion. The motion passed with three ayes and one
nay; Member Snyder dissented.
5. Case No. S-CA-18-084
Mattoon Fire Fighters Association, Local 691/City of Mattoon (Fire Department)
General Counsel Kim asked ALJ Michelle Owen to call and present the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion,
Member Glynn moved to affirm the Executive Director dismissal. Member Samolis seconded the motion, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
6. Case No. S-CA-17-091
Int’l Brotherhood of Teamsters, Local 371/City of Colona
General Counsel Kim asked Associate General Counsel Anna Hamburg-Gal to call and present the issues of the case to the
Board. After discussion, Member Samolis moved to accept the Executive Director dismissal. Member Glynn seconded the
motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VII.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded it was unnecessary.
VIII.

PUBLIC COMMENT

No members of the public sought to make comments.
IX.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Hartnett called for a motion to adjourn. Member Samolis moved to adjourn the meeting, and the Board passed it
unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the State Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board, in
Chicago, Illinois, this 11th day of September 2018.

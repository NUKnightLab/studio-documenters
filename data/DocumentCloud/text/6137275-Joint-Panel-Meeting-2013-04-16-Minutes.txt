Held on April 16, 2013

II.
PRESENT
John Hartnett, Joint Panel Chairman; Paul S. Besson, Member of the State Panel; James Q. Brennwald,
Member of the State Panel; Albert Washington, Member of the State Panel; Robert M. Gierut, Local Panel
Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Local Panel; Richard Lewis, Member of the Local Panel;
Melissa Mlynski, Executive Director; Jerald S. Post, General Counsel; and staff members.
III.
APPROVAL OF MINUTES
Chairman Hartnett called for approval of the minutes of the January 8, 2013 meeting. Local Chairman Gierut
moved to approve the minutes. Member Lewis seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a
voice vote.
IV.
BOARD ACTIONS
General Counsel Post, Executive Director Mlynski and Counsel Stevens presented and discussed in detail
adoption of Rules to implement Public Act 097-1172. After discussion General Counsel Post asked the Board
to consider four motions:
1) To promulgate new Part 1300 by use of the emergency rulemaking procedures in 5 ILCS 100/545.
2) To promulgate new Part 1300 by use of regular rulemaking procedures in 5 ILCS 100/5-40.
3) To promulgate the amendment to Section 1200.3 by use of emergency rulemaking procedures in 5
ILCS 100/5-45.
4) To promulgate the amendment to Section 1200.3 by use of regular rulemaking procedures in 5
ILCS 100/5-40.
After discussion by the Board: (1) Member Besson moved to promulgate new Part 1300 by use of the
emergency rulemaking procedures in 5 ILCS 100/5-45, Member Washington seconded, and the Board passed
it unanimously, by a voice vote; (2) Member Besson moved to promulgate new Part 1300 by use of regular
rulemaking procedures in 5 ILCS 100/5-40, Member Washington seconded, and the Board passed it
unanimously, by a voice vote; (3) Member Besson moved to promulgate the amendment to Section 1200.3 by
use of emergency rulemaking procedures in 5 ILCS 100/5-45, Member Washington seconded, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote; (4) Member Besson moved to promulgate the amendment to Section
1200.3 by use of regular rulemaking procedures in 5 ILCS 100/5-40, Member Anderson seconded, and the
Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
V.
LEGISLATIVE AND FISCAL REPORTS
Executive Director Mlynski reported various legislative issues. She then presented the fiscal report, reviewing
in detail the general ledger sheet and the disbursement record. The Board then discussed and questioned
various aspects of the fiscal report.
VI.
EXECUTIVE SESSION
After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded that it
was unnecessary at this time.
VII.
ADJOURNMENT
Chairman Hartnett called for a motion to adjourn. Member Brennwald made a motion to adjourn the meeting,
and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

 Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Joint Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations
Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 9th day of July, 2013.

 I.

Update from the Bureau of Economic Development
• Andre Ashmore, Deputy Bureau Chief, Bureau of Economic Development
o Bureau of Economic Development in partnership with Chairman Moore’s
office hosted Census Coordinator briefings with 20 people from 14
separately elected official and other County departments on September 25th
o External stakeholder meetings are on-going throughout the County
o Municipality outreach – Cook County sent a letter to every municipality in
the County informing them about the Census Outreach Application
o Cook County Census 2020 Digital Resource Toolkit will be available soon
and posted online
o The first round of Census Outreach application closed on October 1, 2019.
• 79 applications received
• Application deadlines – January 1, 2020, and April 1, 2020
o The William Everett Group was chosen to be Cook County’s Program
Administrator and the contract start date is October 1, 2019

II.

Presentation from the William Everett Group
• David Schwab, Program Manager, The William Everett Group
o The William Everett Group serves as the Program Administrator for the
Cook County 2020 Census
• Collaborating with the Bureau of Economic Development and
Cook County Complete Count Census Commission with the goal
of a complete count of all Cook County residents in the 2020
Census
• Focus on hard-to-count populations across Cook County
o Shared a project roadmap including the first 60-day process of planning and
discovery phase
o The William Everett Group engagement with the Cook County Complete
Count Census Commission
• Review and vote on census outreach applications
• Monthly presentations from Program Administrator
• Creation of communications system and data sharing between
Census Commission and Program Administrator
• Participation in Census Ambassadors program
• Collaboration on events, sponsorship, social media, and media
buys
• Assistance and collaboration on marketing materials
o Overview of census outreach applications
• 79 applications received
• Analysis included mentions of areas served, plans to collaborate
with other agencies, applications by community-based
organizations and local municipalities

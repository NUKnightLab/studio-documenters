51.
Submitting report relative to Court of Appeals upholds DWSD Drainage Charge. (As
Council knows, in recent years the Detroit Water and Sewage Department
(DWSD) has faced a series of class action lawsuits challenging its charges for
treating drainage of rainwater runoff and human waste in “combined sewer
overflow” (CSO) events during heavy rain storms. DWSD’s new drainage
charges went into effect in October 2016. On November 6, 2018, the Michigan
Court of Appeals upheld DWSD’s drainage charge in a detailed, unanimous 23page written opinion resolving two such consolidated claims. Binns, et al v City of
Detroit, No. 337609, and Detroit Alliance Against the Rain Tax, et al v City of
Detroit, No. 339176.)

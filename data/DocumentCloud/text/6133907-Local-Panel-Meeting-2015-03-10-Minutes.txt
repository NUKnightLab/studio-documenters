STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on March 10, 2015
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Board; Richard Lewis, Member of the Board; Melissa
Mlynski, Executive Director; Jerald S. Post, General Counsel; Anna Hamburg-Gal, Associate General Counsel; and staff.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD February 10, 2015

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the February 10, 2015 meeting. Member Anderson moved to
approve the minutes. Member Lewis seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gierut asked Executive Director Mlynski to present the Local Panel administrative actions report as well as the
fiscal, legislative and mediation reports. She presented the reports and summarized their highlights. The Board examined
the reports and discussed them.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS

1.
Case Nos. L-CA-13-009 and L-CA-13-063
Pamela Mercer/ County of Cook and Sheriff of Cook County
Chairman Gierut called the case and asked General Counsel Post to present the issues of the case to the Board. General
Counsel Post stated that on December 18, 2015, Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) Anna Hamburg-Gal issued a
Recommended Decision and Order (RDO) recommending that the Board dismiss the complaints. He then discussed the
exception and response that were filed. After his presentation, General Counsel Post recommended that the Board affirm
the ALJ’s RDO. After discussion, Member Anderson moved to affirm the RDO. Member Lewis seconded the motion, and
the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VI.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded it was
unnecessary.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board, in
Chicago, Illinois, this 16th day of April, 2015.

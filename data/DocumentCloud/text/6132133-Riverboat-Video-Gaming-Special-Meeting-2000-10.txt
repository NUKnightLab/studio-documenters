MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL MEETING
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
October 4, 2000
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
A Special Meeting of the Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”) was held on October 4, 2000
in Room #S-304 of the State of Illinois Building, Chicago, Illinois, pursuant to the Illinois
Open Meetings Act, 5 ILCS 120/1 et seq.
Chairman Greg Jones was present. The following Board members were present
telephonically: Joseph Lamendella, Sterling M. Ryder, Stuart Levine, and Staci Yandle.
Also in attendance were: Administrator Sergio E. Acosta, Deputy Administrators Joseph
Haughey, Allan S. McDonald, Deputy Chief Legal Counsel Jeannette P. Tamayo and
other members of the staff. Deputy Administrator Tom Swoik was present telephonically.
The meeting was convened at 9:35 a.m. Member Yandle moved that the Board retire
to Closed Session pursuant to Section 2 (c), paragraphs (1), (11) (14) and (21) of the
Open Meetings Act and Section 6(d) of the Riverboat Gambling Act to discuss the
following subject matters:
1. Pending litigation and matters involving probable litigation;
2. Investigations concerning applicants and licensees; and
3. Personnel matters.
Member Lamendella seconded the motion. The Board adopted the motion by unanimous
consent.
The Board adjourned at 11:30 a.m.
Respectfully submitted,

Monica L. Thomas
Secretary to the Board

 III.

NEW BUSINESS
A.

RIVER SOUTH TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 3)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Housing and Economic
Development=s intention to enter into a negotiated sale with 1625 South Clark
Street, LLC for the disposition of the property located at 1600 S. Clark Street in
the River South Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, to request
alternative proposals, and to approve the sale of the property to 1625 South Clark
Street, LLC if no responsive alternative proposals are received.
Project Manager: Essie Escareno-Banks
Motioned by Perez, seconded by Martin. Approved 8-0. Yeas: Buford, Carrott,
Kostiner, Logan, Martin, Mooney, Perez and Sahli. Ward recused.
12-CDC-1

B.

PERSHING/KING TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 3)
Request authority for the Department of Housing and Economic Development to
negotiate a redevelopment agreement with Bronzeville Associates Senior
Apartments for redevelopment of the property located at 400 E. 41st Street in the
Pershing/King Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and to
recommend to the City Council of the City of Chicago the designation of
Bronzeville Associates Senior Apartments as Developer.
Project Manager: Dinah Wayne
Motioned by Martin, seconded by Logan. Approved 8-0. Yeas: Carrott, Kostiner,
Logan, Martin, Mooney, Perez, Sahli and Ward. Buford abstained.
12-CDC-2

C.

PROPOSED MIDWEST TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
AMENDMENT NO. 1 (WARDS 2, 12, 24, 28)
Accept for review the amended redevelopment plan for the proposed Midwest
Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area Amendment No. 1, and set
dates for a public meeting of the Joint Review Board and a public hearing.
Project Manager: Beth McGuire
Motioned by Logan, seconded by Sahli. Approved 9-0. Yeas: Buford, Carrott,
Kostiner, Logan, Martin, Mooney, Perez, Sahli and Ward.

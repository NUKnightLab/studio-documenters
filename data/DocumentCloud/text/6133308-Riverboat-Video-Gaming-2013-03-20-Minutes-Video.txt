MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING - VIDEO
OPEN SESSION
March 20, 2013
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
On Wednesday, March 20, 2013 a Regular Meeting - Video of the Illinois Gaming Board
(“Board”) was held on the 9th floor at 100 W. Randolph at the JRTC Building, Chicago, Illinois.
The Open Session on Video Gaming started at approximately 10:38 A.M. Present were
Members Lee Gould, Michael Holewinski and Maribeth Vander Weele. Chairman Jaffe
participated via speaker phone.
APPROVAL OF MINUTES –
Member Vander Weele moved that the Board approve the open session minutes of its
Regular Meeting of February 21, 2013. Member Vander Gould seconded the motion. The
Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
ADMINISTRATOR’S COMMENTS –
Administrator Ostrowski summarized the progress on video gaming.
MANUFACTURER, DISTRIBUTORS AND SUPPLIERS LICENSEE ITEMS –
•

Chicago Gaming Company – Manufacturer and Distributor License Renewals
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Gould moved that the Board renew the
Manufacturer and Distributor Licenses of Chicago Gaming Company, effective
March 20, 2013, for a term of one year, expiring in March 2014. Member Vander
Weele seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call
vote.

• Golden Route Operations LLC – Initial Manufacturer, Distributor and Supplier
Licenses
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Vander Weele that the Board deny the
Manufacturer, Distributor and Supplier License applications of Golden Route
Operations LLC. Member Holewinski seconded the motion. Member Gould
abstained. Chairman Jaffe was in favor of the motion. The motion passed 3 to 1.

 TERMINAL OPERATOR LICENSEE ITEMS –
•

Initial Terminal Operator License – Windy City Gaming LLC
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Gould moved that the Board approve the
Terminal Operator License of Windy City Gaming LLC effective March 20,
2013 for a period of one year, expiring in March 2014. Member Holewinski
seconded the motion. Member Vander Weele abstained. Chairman Jaffe was in favor
of the motion. The motion passed 3 to 1.

•

Terminal Operator License Renewals
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Vander Weele moved that the Board renew
the Terminal Operator Licenses of the following entities, effective March 20,
2013 for a period of one year, expiring in March 2014: Accel Entertainment
Gaming, LLC; Arkadian Gaming, LLC; B&B Amusement of Illinois, LLC;
Compact Disc Amusements, Limited; Emperor Video Slots Corporation; Fair
Share Gaming, LLC; Illinois Video Gaming, Inc.; Leisure Time Gaming &
Amusements, Inc.; Tower Gaming, LLC; and WAMI Gaming, LLC. Member
Gould seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call
vote.

•

Metro Gaming, LLC
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Gould moved that the Board deny the
renewal of the Terminal Operator License of Metro Gaming, LLC. Member
Vander Weele seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by
roll call vote.

TECHNICIAN & TERMINAL HANDLER LICENSEE ITEMS –
•

Approvals & Denials
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Vander Weele moved that the Board
approve 2 applications for Technician Licenses and 56 applications for Terminal
Handler Licenses. Member Gould seconded the motion. The Board approved the
motion unanimously by roll call vote.

ADMINISTRATIVE REQUESTS FOR HEARING –


Requests for Hearing –
o Based on staff’s investigation, Member Vander Weele moved that the Board
deny the following requests for hearing, as none has offered facts to
establish a prima facie case: Antronio’s Bar and Grill Inc.; Down the
Street; Family Beer East, Inc.; B&B Harris Management, LLC; and
Diamond Gaming of Illinois, Inc. Member Gould seconded the motion. The
Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
o Based on staff’s investigation, Member Gould moved that the Board deny
the following requests for hearing, as none has offered facts to establish a
prima facie case:
Danville Columbus Club; Foreningen Lyran;
Blackhawk Athletic Club Inc.; and Rockford Harmony Singing Society.
Member Holewinski seconded the motion. Member Vander Weele was
opposed to the motion. Chairman Jaffe was in favor of the motion. The
motion passed 3 to 1.

•

Requests to Reapply –
o Based on staff’s investigation, Member Vander Weele moved that the Board
grant the requests to reapply within one year following licensure denial
from Chaoz Sports Lounge, LLC and Paul McVey Post #1592; and deny
the request from Long Shot To Win, Inc. Member Gould seconded the
motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.

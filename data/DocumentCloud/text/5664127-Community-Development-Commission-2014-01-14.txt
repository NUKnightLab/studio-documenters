ENGLEWOOD MALL TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 16)
The Department of Planning and Development described its intention to engage
Chicago Neighborhood Initiatives as Contractor for the environmental
remediation and site preparation of City-owned land located in the Northwest
Quadrant of 63rd and Halsted streets in the Englewood Mall Tax Increment
Financing Redevelopment Project Area in anticipation of commercial
development of the property.

MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING - RIVERBOAT
OPEN SESSION
November 21, 2013
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
On Thursday, November 21, 2013 a Regular Meeting - Riverboat of the Illinois Gaming Board
(“Board”) was held on the 5th floor at 160 N. LaSalle at the Michael J. Bilandic Building,
Chicago, Illinois at 10:35 A.M. Present were the following: Members Maribeth Vander Weele,
Lee Gould, Michael Holewinski and Chairman Aaron Jaffe.
APPROVAL OF MINUTES
Member Gould moved that the Board approve the Closed Session minutes of its Regular
Meeting of October 23, 2013. Member Holewinski seconded the motion. The Board approved
the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
ADMINISTATOR’S REPORT –
Administrator Ostrowski stated there will not be a riverboat meeting in December, 2013.

 ITEMS FOR FINAL ACTION –
•

Southern Illinois Riverboat Casino Cruises, Inc. d/b/a Harrah’s Metropolis Casino –
Matthew Pariso, Staff Internal Auditor – Level One
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Vander Weele moved that the Board approve
Matthew Pariso as an Occupational Licensee Level One in his position as Staff
Internal Auditor of Southern Illinois Riverboat Casino Cruises, Inc. d/b/a Harrah’s
Metropolis Casino. Member Gould seconded the motion. The Board approved the
motion unanimously by roll call vote.

•

Par-A-Dice Gaming Corporation d/b/a Par-A-Dice Hotel Casino – Paula EylarLauzon, Vice President of Internal Audit & Sustainability of Boyd Gaming
Corporation – Key Person
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Gould moved that the Board approve Paula
Eylar-Lauzon as a Key Person of Par-A-Dice Gaming Corporation d/b/a Par-A-Dice
Hotel Casino in her position as Vice President of Internal Audit and Vice President
of Sustainability for Boyd Gaming Corporation. Member Vander Weele seconded the
motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.

SUPPLIER LICENSEE ITEM –
•

Multimedia Games, Inc. – License Renewal
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Holewinski moved that the Board renew
Multimedia Games, Inc.’s Supplier’s License for a period of four years, expiring in
November 2017 and the Board designate the following as Key Persons of
Multimedia Games, Inc.: Multimedia Games Holding Company; Chief Executive
Officer and Patrick J. Ramsey. Member Gould seconded the motion. The Board
approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.

OCCUPATIONAL LICENSE APPROVAL AND DENIALS –
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Vander Weele moved that the Board find
suitable and approve 37 applications for Level 2 Occupational Licenses and 115
applications for Level 3 Occupational Licenses and that the Administrator issue a
Notice of Denial to Ivan Escobar and Teraza Simmons. Member Gould seconded the
motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.

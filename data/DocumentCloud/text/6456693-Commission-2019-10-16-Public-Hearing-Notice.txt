In the matter of the proposed landmark designation of the John Nuveen House as a Chicago
Landmark and located at 3916 North Tripp Avenue, Chicago, Illinois, (Docket 2019-06),

public hearing will be held by the Commission on Chicago Landmarks on Wednesday,
October 16, 2019, at 1:00 p.m. in Room 1103, City Hall, 121 North LaSalle Street, Chicago,
Illinois.

REPORT
Department: Planning & Development
Request: Receive and File
Report Title: 2016 Update to the 5-Year Capital Improvement Plan
Report Period: 2016-2020
Summary: This Plan is an update of capital improvement projects and funding needs for years
2016-2020. The location of funded projects that are still in progress as well as new 2016 projects are
identified on maps for northwest, north, central, southwest and south Cook County. The Plan also
includes projected capital funding needs for projects in 2017-2020 that were identified by Forest
Preserve staff, partner organizations and members of the public. The draft Capital Improvement Plan has
been posted for additional review and comments on the Forest Preserves’ website (www.fpdcc.com/cip/
<http://www.fpdcc.com/cip/>) during December and January and will be finalized by February 2016.

 Legislative History: 12/15/15 FPD Board of Commissioners referred to the Capital Development
Committee

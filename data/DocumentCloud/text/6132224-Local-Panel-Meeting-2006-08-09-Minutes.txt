APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD JUNE 6, 2006

Chairman Gallagher called for approval of the minutes of the June 6 2006 meeting. Member Sadlowski

Chairman Gallagher asked Executive Director Brosnan to present the Local Panel administrative actions
for the period of time from June 1 through July 31, 2006. He distributed the report to the Board for
review, and the Board examined and discussed it.

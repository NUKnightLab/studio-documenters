Signature Resolution
The Board was presented with an updated Signature Resolution adding Juan Lopez, Jr., Fund Investment
Officer, to the list of Fund representatives who may sign necessary documentation. Based on this
resolution and the advice of staff and Fund Counsel, Trustee McMahon made a motion to approve the
Signature Resolution. Seconded by Trustee Guest.
Carried: Ayes – Trustee Clancy, Trustee Guest, Trustee McMahon, Proxy Martinez and Trustee
Widawsky. Nays – None.
FOIA Update
Ms. Ruffolo reviewed requests made under the Illinois Freedom of Information Act. No actions taken.
Old Business
Mr. Mohler updated the Board on the current status of the “unraveling” of Public Act 98-0641. After this
update and with the advice of Fund Counsel, Trustee McMahon made a motion to authorize the
disbursement of all retroactive payments to affected members in accordance with the Cook County Circuit
Court’s finding that the Act is unconstitutional. Seconded by Proxy Martinez.
Carried: Ayes – Trustee Clancy, Trustee Guest, Trustee McMahon, Proxy Martinez and Trustee
Widawsky. Nays – None.
Mr. Mohler presented the Board with an updated draft of the letter to be directed to Stakeholders with
respect to the Fund’s solvency. After reviewing and discussing the content of the correspondence, Trustee
McMahon made a motion to send the letter as presented. Seconded by Proxy Martinez.
Carried: Ayes – Trustee Clancy, Trustee Guest, Trustee McMahon, Proxy Martinez. Present – Trustee
Widawsky. Nays – None.

Agenda item:

Approval of Minutes of March 7, 2017
Board action on agenda item: Chair Burton requested a motion
to approve the minutes of the meeting held on March 7, 2017.
Member Sabree, duly supported by Member Garrett, moved to
approve the minutes of the meeting held on March 7, 2017. The
motion was adopted unanimously by a voice vote.

Agenda item:

Determination of Clarity: James Godbout, City of Westland,
City Council
Chair Burton asked Assistant Corporation Counsel Janet AndersonDavis to remind the commission of its role in determining clarity.
Assistant Corporation Counsel Anderson-Davis reminded the
commission of its role and duties for a determination of clarity.
Anderson-Davis read the statute regarding the role of the Election

 Commission in regard to recall petition.
Board action on agenda item: Chair Burton requested a motion
regarding item 5A-1.
Reason for recall as printed on the Recall Petition 5A-1 for James
Godbout:
“In 2016 James Godbout voted in favor of a water rate increase
while being delinquent on his personal water bill.”
Chair Burton, duly supported by Member Garrett, moved to bring
item 5A-1 before the commission for the purpose of discussion and
to determine that the language is sufficiently clear. The motion was
adopted unanimously by a voice vote.
Agenda item:

Determination of Clarity: James Godbout, City of Westland,
City Council
Reason for recall as printed on the Recall Petition 5A-2 for James
Godbout:
“James Godbout supported a 10 million dollar bond for smart water
meters.”
Board action on agenda item: Chair Burton requested a motion
regarding the language on petition 5A-2. Member Garrett posed a
question to Corporation Counsel regarding the approval of more
than one petition for circulation. Janet Anderson-Davis offered to
the commission an opinion that addressed a similar situation. Chair
Burton indicates item 5A-2 dies for lack of a motion.

Agenda item:

Determination of Clarity: James Godbout, City of Westland,
City Council
Reason for recall as printed on the Recall Petition 5A-3 for James
Godbout:
“On 10/17/16 Westland City Council President James Godbout
voted in favor of a lighting assessment.”
Board action on agenda item: Chair Burton requested a motion
regarding item 5A-3. Member Garrett, duly supported by Member
Sabree, moved to reject item 5A-3 because item 5A-1 is already
approved.

 Board action on agenda item: Chair Burton requested a motion
regarding item 5A-2. Member Sabree, duly supported by Member
Garrett, moved to reject item 5A-2 because item 5A-1 is already
approved.
Agenda item:

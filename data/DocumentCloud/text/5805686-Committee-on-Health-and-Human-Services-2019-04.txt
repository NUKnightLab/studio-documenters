2.

Communication dated March 14, 2019 (received March 21st) requesting
Commission approval of a sub-recipient, intergovernmental agreement between the
Charter County of Wayne and the Detroit Wayne Mental Health Authority (DWMHA)
in the amount of $221,000 to provide mental health services, including access to its
network of providers for intensive wrap-around services, utilization of its Access
Management System for immediate client placement, and access to its detox
residential, methadone, dual diagnosis services and recovery homes; the County
Executive advises that DWMHA is the Prepaid Inpatient Health Plan (PIHP) for
Wayne County and manages federal and state prevention treatment and recovery
services and was specifically listed as a sub-recipient in the Community Corrections
grant from the Michigan Department of Corrections for FY 2019; the DWMHA will
subcontract with Black Family Development (of Detroit) in the amount of $72,500 for
relapse recovery services, case management and drug court groups and with the
Detroit Recovery Program (of Detroit) in the amount of $148,500 for relapse
recovery services, case management and urine drug testing; the term of the
agreement is from October 1, 2018 through September 30, 2019; the cost of the
agreement will be charged to Account No. 281 32000 817000 (Community
Correction). (2019-20-050)

PROPOSED ORDINANCE
Cook County Firearms Training Certification
This Ordinance may be cited as the Cook County Firearms Training Certification
Ordinance. Cook County and other counties throughout Illinois and the United
States have experienced incidents of violent crimes involving firearms,
specifically, handguns.
WHEREAS, the Cook County Board of Commissioners is committed to protect
the health, safety and welfare of the citizens of Cook County, and

NOW THEREFORE, PURSUANT TO THE HOME RULE AUTHORITY OF
THE COOK COUNTY BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS AS VESTED IN IT BY THE
ILLINOIS CONSTITUTION, BE IT ORDAINED:
That it is in the best interest of the public that purchases of firearms In cook
county shall prior to the purchase of a firearm have completed a Certified Firearm
Training Course which must have been taught by a Certified Firearms Instructor.
(a)

The firearms instructor shall have completed and have been
certified by the Illinois Law Enforcement Training Standards

 III.

OLD BUSINESS
A.

95TH/WESTERN TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 19)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Planning and Development’s
intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Barraco’s Pizza, LLC for the
disposition of the property located at 2105-2121 W. 95th Street and 9448 S.
Pleasant Avenue in the 95th/Western Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment
Project Area, to request alternative proposals, and to approve the sale of the
property to Barraco’s Pizza, LLC if no responsive alternative proposals are
received.
Project Manager: Lisa Washington
Motioned by Perez, seconded by Buford. Approved 13-0. Yeas: Alphonse,
Buford, Butler, Cepeda, Davis, Griggs, Moreno, Newsome, Perez, Reifman,
Rhodes, Ward and Carrott.
15-CDC-30

B.

MIDWEST TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 24)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Planning and Development’s
intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Sinai Health System for the
disposition of the property located at 3100-58 W. Ogden Avenue and 3142 W.
16th Street in the Midwest Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area,
to request alternative proposals, and to approve the sale of the property to Sinai
Health System if no responsive alternative proposals are received.
Project Manager: Sarah Wilson
Motioned by Butler, seconded by Moreno. Approved 12-0. Yeas: Alphonse,
Buford, Butler, Cepeda, Griggs, Moreno, Newsome, Perez, Reifman, Rhodes,
Ward and Carrott. Davis abstained.
15-CDC-31

C.

EDGEWATER/ASHLAND TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA

15-CDC-32

 D.

EDGEWATER/ASHLAND TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 40)

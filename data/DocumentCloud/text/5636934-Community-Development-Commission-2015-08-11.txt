IV.

NEW BUSINESS
A.

HALSTED/VINCENNES REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 21)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Planning and Development’s
intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Green Era Educational NFP for the
disposition of the property located at 650 West 83rd Street in the
Halsted/Vincennes Redevelopment Project Area, to request alternative proposals,
and to approve the sale of the property to Green Era Educational NFP if no
responsive alternative proposals are received.
Project Manager: Ed Lewis
Motioned by Perez, seconded by Buford. Approved 8-0. Yeas: Buford, Davis,
Duque, Garner, Newsome, Olander, Perez and Carrott. Cepeda not present.
15-CDC-24

 B.

JEFFERSON PARK TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 45)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Planning and Development’s
intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Jefferson Park Residences, LLC for
the disposition of the property located at 5161 and 5201 W. Lawrence Avenue in
the Jefferson Park Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, to
request alternative proposals, and to approve the sale of the property to Jefferson
Park Residences, LLC if no responsive alternative proposals are received.
Project Manager: Michelle Nolan
Motioned by Olander, seconded by Perez. Defeated 3-2. Yeas: Olander, Perez
and Carrott. Nays: Duque and Newsome. Abstentions: Buford, Davis and
Garner. (Five yes votes are needed for approval). Cepeda not present.

C.

CICERO/ARCHER TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 22)
The Department of Planning and Development requests authority to negotiate a
redevelopment agreement with Midway Pointe, LLC for the redevelopment of the
property located at 5001-5021 W. 47th Street in the Cicero/Archer Tax Increment
Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and to recommend to the City Council of
the City of Chicago the designation of Midway Pointe, LLC as Developer.
Barbara Taylor
Motioned by Perez, seconded by Duque. Approved 7-0. Yeas: Buford, Davis,
Duque, Newsome, Olander, Perez and Carrott. Cepeda and Garner not present.
15-CDC-25

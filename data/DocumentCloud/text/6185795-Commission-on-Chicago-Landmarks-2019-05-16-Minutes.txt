COMMISSION ON CHICAGO LANDMARKS

The Commission on Chicago Landmarks held a special meeting on May 16, 2019. The meeting
was held at the Cook County Board Room, 118 North Clark Street, Room 569, Chicago, Illinois.
The meeting began at 12:45 p.m.

Dijana Cuvalo presented background information. Matt Crawford presented the report. The

Public Hearing held Wednesday, April 24, 2019

9:30 a.m.
Hearing Officer: Commissioner Ernest Wong
Commissioner Wong presented the findings and recommendation. Vote on the resolution to
adopt the Hearing Officer’s proposed “Findings and Conclusions” in their entirety as the
“Findings and Conclusions” of the Commission to disapprove the permit applications to
demolish the buildings at 1730, 1732 and 1734 W. 18th Street.
Motion by Moreno, seconded by Dziekiewicz to disapprove the demolition permit for
1730 W. 18th Street. Approved (6-1).
Motion by Tolliver, seconded by Moreno to disapprove the demolition permit for 1732
W. 18th Street. Approved (6-1).
Motion by Smith, seconded by Dziekiewicz to disapprove the demolition permit for
1734 W. 18th Street. Approved (6-1).

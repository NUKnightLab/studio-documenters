REPORT OF THE CHICAGO DEPARTMENT OF AVIATION (“CDA”)
A. 1st Quarter 2017 Airport Noise Management System (ANMS) Report
Mr. Frame asked Mr. Jackson of Landrum & Brown to present the 1st Quarter 2017
Airport Noise Management System Report.
Mr. Jackson provided a brief overview of the information contained in the report
and proceeded with the details.
Runway Use Report
In the 1st Quarter the majority of arrivals and departures were on Runway 31C.
January – The most used departure runway was 31C going northwest with 49% of
all departures and 22 R/L with 29%, followed by 04 L/R with 21% and last 13C with
1%.
The most used arrival runway coming in from the southeast was 31C with 54%,
then 04 L/R with 24%, followed by 22 L/R with 22% and last 13C with 0%.
February – The most used departure runway was 22 R/L with 44% followed by
31C with 43%, next 04 L/R with 10% and last 13C with 3%.
The most used arrival runway was 31C with 49% of all arrivals, followed by 22 R/L
with 36%, then 04 L/R with 11% and13C with 4%.
March – The most used departure runway was 31C with 35% followed by 04 L/R
with 33%, then 22 R/L with 29% and last 13C with 3%.
The most used arrival runway was 04 L/R with 40%, followed by 31C with 30%,
then 22 R/L with 28% and last 13C with 2%.
Airline Fleet Mix Report
There was an average of 591 operations per day with 70 of those occurring at
night. Southwest Airlines had the highest operations with an average of 413
operations per day with 57 of those occurring at night. The B737, used almost
exclusively by Southwest, was the most used aircraft with an average of 414
operations per day. General Aviation followed with 133 operations per day and 5
of which were helicopters.
Permanent Noise Monitor Locations
The report indicates the addresses of the 13 permanent noise monitor locations
near the airport as reflected in relation to the Midway 2018 Noise Contour. The
report shows the results for January 2017 through March 2017 with quarterly

Noise Hotline Report
In the 1st Quarter there was a total of 30,924 complaints. The highest number of
complaints came from Burbank with 21,319 and Justice with 6,618. This
significant number of complaints were from 7 addresses and 76% of all
complaints came from 2 address. There was a substantial decrease in the
number of complaints in the 1st Quarter of 2017 compared to previous the 4th
Quarter of 2016. The number of communities that submitted complaints in the 1st
Quarter was greater than previous months.

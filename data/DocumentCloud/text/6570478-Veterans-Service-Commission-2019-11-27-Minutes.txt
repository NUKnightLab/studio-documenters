PURPOSE: GENERAL MEETING

The meeting was called to order by President Clay Uzell followed by the Pledge of Allegiance.

A motion was made by Mel Baher and seconded by Chris Grau to dispense with the reading of the
Consent Agenda and to accept the agenda with the necessary corrections and/or additions. (All
Commissioners have a copy of the minutes).

AYES: Baher, Grau, Pocci, Potts, Uzell. NAYS: None. Motion carried.

OLD BUSINESS:
The Executive Director provided the Board with pictures of the vehicle wrap for the new mobile vehicle.
The Board directed the Executive Director to make some changes.

NEW BUSINESS:

training and maintain membership and accreditation with the Ohio State Association of County Veterans
Service Of?cers (OSACVSO) and National Association of County Veterans Service Of?cers (NACVSO)
in 2020, not to exceed $64,209.00.

AYES: Baher, Grau, Pocci, Potts, Uzell. NAYS: None. Motion carried.

purchase of employee computer headsets to be used with the new customer service phone system

AYES: Baher, Grau, Pocci, Potts, Uzell. NAYS: None. Motion carried.

GOOD OF THE ORDER:
Terry Sansbury from the DAV addressed the Board. Mr. Sansbury mentioned the incredible work being
done at the Vetarans Service Commission.

Mr. Sansbury asked the Board to consider donating a jukebox to the Ohio Veterans Home (OVH). The
Board explained that the VSC is funded by taxpayers? dollars and was unable to donate. However, the
staff holds various fundraisers. After some discussion it was decided that a jukebox would be donated
and paid for by the staff fundraiser. Mr. Jones also inquired about ?shopping? for OVH veterans at the
upcoming Stand Down. Mr. Reiss, Executive Director, will reach out to Mr. Sansbury at a future date.

Mr. Jones, a VSC ?nancial assistance client, addressed the Board regarding his situation. Mr. Jones was
directed on how to proceed.

EXECUTIVE SESSION:

A motion was made by Mel Baher and seconded by Bob Potts to go into Executive Session to discuss
appeals. The Board would be hearing two (2) client appeals today.

AYES: Baher, Gran, Pocci, Potts, Uzell. NAYS: None. Motion carried. (9:57 am.)

The Board came out of Executive Session and returned to the regular order of business at 10:18 am.

The votes for further assistance, recorded by the Secretary, of the client appeals that were discussed in
Executive Session, are attached.

A motion was made by Mel Baher and seconded by Bob Potts to return to Executive Session to discuss
collective bargaining issues.
AYES: Baher, Grau, Pocci, Potts, Uzell. NAYS: None. Motion carried. (10:23 am.)

The Board came out of Executive Session and returned to the regular order of business at 10:39 am.
In Executive Session the Board discussed various issues related to collective bargaining. The next
meeting will be held on December 19, 2019.

With no further business, a motion was made by Mel Baher and seconded by Frank Pocci to adjourn the
meeting at 10:40 am.
AYES: Baher, Gran, Pocci, Potts, and Uzell. NAYS: None. Motion carried.

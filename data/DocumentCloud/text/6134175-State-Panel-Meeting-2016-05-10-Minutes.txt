STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
STATE PANEL
Minutes of the State Panel Meeting
Held on May 10, 2016
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 11:30 P.M.— at 160 N LaSalle Street, S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

John Hartnett, Chairman; Michael Coli, Member of the Board; John Samolis, Member of the Board; Keith Snyder,
Member of the Board; Albert Washington, Member of the Board; Melissa Mlynski, Executive Director; Kathryn
Zeledon Nelson, General Counsel; Sarah Kerley, Deputy General Counsel Anna Hamburg-Gal, Associate General
Counsel; and staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD April 12, 2016

Chairman Hartnett called for approval of the minutes of the April 12, 2016, meeting. Member Washington moved to
approve the minutes. Member Snyder seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Hartnett asked Executive Director Mlynski to present the Executive Director’s report. She presented the
report and summarized the highlights.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.

Case No. S-CA-15-116
American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31/City of East
Moline

Chairman Hartnett asked General Counsel Nelson to call and present the case. General Counsel Nelson stated that
Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) Sarah Kerley issued a Recommended Decision and Order (RDO) concluding that
the Employer failed to bargain in good faith. After discussion of the exceptions to the RDO, Member Samolis moved
to accept the RDO with modification. Member Washington seconded the motion, and the Board passed it
unanimously by a voice vote.
2.

Case No. S-CA-16-006
American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31/State of Illinois,
Department of Central Management Services

Chairman Hartnett asked General Counsel Nelson to call and present the case. General Counsel Nelson stated that
ALJ Sarah Kerley issued a RDO alleging that the Respondent engaged in unfair labor practices within the meaning of
Sections 10(a)(4) of the Act. She then summarized the issues presented by the exceptions, cross-exceptions and
responses that were filed. After her presentation, she recommended that the Board accept the RDO. After
discussion, Member Coli moved to accept the RDO. Member Washington seconded the motion, and the Board
passed it unanimously by a voice vote.
VI.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Hartnett called for a motion to adjourn. Member Coli moved to adjourn the meeting, and the Board passed
it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the State Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations
Board, in Springfield, Illinois, this 14th day of June, 2016.

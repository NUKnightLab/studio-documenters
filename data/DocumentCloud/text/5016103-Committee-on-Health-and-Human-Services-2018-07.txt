D.

New Business

1.

Communication dated June 15, 2018 (received June 21st) requesting Commission
approval of a renewal agreement between the Charter County of Wayne and the
Michigan Department of Education in the amount of $38,575 for the 2018 Summer
Food Service Program, through Gregg’s Pizza and Bar-B-Que, to provide free or
reduced-priced meals to eligible community youth; the County Executive advises
that Wayne County has operated a Summer Food Service Program since 1984 and
provides 10,000 meals at sites in the communities of Dearborn, Inkster, River
Rouge, Wayne and Westland; the term of the renewal agreement is from June 18,
2018 through August 17, 2018; the revenue from the agreement will be deposited
into Account No. 221 60127 516000 (Summer Food Serv Prog). (2018-08-016)

2.

Communication dated June 18, 2018 (received June 30th) requesting Commission
approval of an intergovernmental service provider agreement between the Charter
County of Wayne and the Detroit Wayne Mental Health Authority (DWMHA) in the
amount of $5,200,000 to receive and disburse funding to provide community-based
services to at-risk and juvenile justice involved youth who have been diagnosed or
are at significant risk for developing a mental illness; the County Executive advises
that the DWMHA provides funding for services rendered through the Assured
Family Services, formerly the Juvenile Assessment Center (JAC), that assure
access and delivery of mental health services and JIFF assessments to at-risk
youth, including those involved in the juvenile justice system who are diagnosed
with Serious Emotional Disturbance (SED); the term of the agreement is from
October 1, 2017 through September 30, 2018; the revenue from the agreement will
be deposited into Account Nos. 292 35612 627150/627153/627156 (Program
Development-Juvenile). (2018-20-072)

3.

Communication dated April 30, 2018 (received May 15th) requesting Commission
approval of a retroactive cooperative operational agreement between the Charter
County of Wayne and Wayne County Healthy Communities (WCHC) to create a coapplicant Community Health Center Board (WCHCB) to operate a Federally
Qualified Health Center (FQHC) and to outline the responsibilities of each party as
to the operation of the clinic; the term of the agreement is from June 1, 2018
through May 31, 2020. (2018-19-030)

4.

Discussion on the impact of Budget Adjustment Nos. 2018-35-195, 2018-35-201,
2018-35-202 and 2018-35-172.

E.

Such other matters as may properly be submitted before the Committee

F.

Public Comments

Committee on Budget and Finance - Second Reading

10538-18

A Resolution of the County of Allegheny amending the Grants and Special
Accounts Budget for 2018 (Submission #01-18).

Sponsors:

Chief Executive
This resolution authorizes the amendment of the 2018 Grants & Speical Accounts
Budgets to accomodate two newly awarded grants (one to support re-accreditation efforts
in the Office of the Medical Examiner and one to provide training relating to opioid
response to the local Medical Reserve Courps units), and an increase in the awarded
amount in two existing grants (one to aid in reducing mosquito-borne illness and one to
provide additional tools for court personnel to better address the opioid epidemic). None
of the amendments requires the County to provide matching funds.

Committee on Public Works - Second Reading

10536-18

An Ordinance authorizing the County of Allegheny to acquire by
condemnation certain right-of-way and temporary construction easement
areas from a parcel required to reconstruct Jacks Run Bridge No. 4
carrying Jacks Run Road over Jacks Run in Ross Township, and to take
such further action as may be necessary under applicable law, including
the Eminent Domain Code.

Sponsors:

Chief Executive
This ordinance authorizes - but does not require - the exercise of the County's powers
under the Commonwealth's Eminient Domain Code in order to secure right-of-way and
temporary construction easement areas from the owners of a single property in
connection with the reconstruction of Jacks Run Bridge #4 in Ross Township in a fashion
that allows for the timely completion of the project. The ordinance does not preclude the
County from reaching an amicable agreement with the property owner.

A. The Chairman called the January 24, 2019, Regular Hearing of the Chicago Plan Commission, to
order at 10:08 AM and then undertook a roll call to establish the presence of a quorum; the hearing
commenced with nine members present(*). A court reporter was present to record the proceedings. A
transcript is available for the purpose of pursuing all matters in greater detail and is a part of the
permanent public record.
B. A motion by Samita Shah, seconded by Raul Garza, to approve the Minutes of the December 20,
2018, Regular Hearing of the Chicago Plan Commission, was approved by an 11-0 vote.
C. A motion by Samita Shah, seconded by Les Bond, to approve the following matters, pursuant to the
Inter-Agency Planning Referral Act, was approved by an 11-0 vote.

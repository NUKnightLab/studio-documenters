Regular Meeting, Open Session Minutes – Riverboat and Video Gaming
Illinois Gaming Board
Springfield, Illinois
November 2, 2018
The Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”) convened for a Regular Meeting – Riverboat and Video Gaming
– on Friday, November 2, 2018 in the Capitol Room of the Wyndham Hotel in Springfield, Illinois
pursuant to the Illinois Open Meetings Act, 5 ILCS 120/1 et seq.
The following Board members were physically present: Chairman Don Tracy, Members Hector
Alejandre and Steve Dolins. Member Dee Robinson participated telephonically. Three members of the
Board physically being present, a quorum was satisfied.
The following Staff members were also in attendance: Acting Administrator Agostino Lorenzini,
Assistant Counsel Daniel Gerber, Assistant General Counsel Jared Smith, Assistant General Counsel
James Jozefowicz, Deputy Administrator-Investigations Scott Deubel, Deputy Administrator-Audit and
Financial Analysis Jim Lopinski, Audit and Financial Analysis Manager Tammy Compton, License
Coordinator Robert Burke, Administrative Assistant and Acting Secretary to the Board Alicia Passfield,
Senior Project Manager Mark Stevens, Deputy Administrator-Enforcement Frank Spizzirri and
Legislative Liaison, Caleb Melamed.
Chairman Tracy called the meeting to order at 8:05 AM. The Board Secretary verified that no one from
the public was present to attend the meeting at that time.
At 8:06 A.M. Member Dolins moved that the Board go into closed session pursuant to Section 2(c),
paragraphs (1), (11), (14), and (21) of the Open Meetings Act and Section 6(d) of the Riverboat
Gambling Act to discuss items on the closed session agenda relating to specific personnel, the closed
session minutes, investigations, and pending litigation. Member Alejandre seconded the motion. The
Board approved the motion unanimously by voice vote. Thereafter, the Board discussed matters
referenced in the closed session agenda in the conference room in the lower level of the hotel.
At 8:41AM, Member Dolins moved to adjourn the closed session meeting and return to open session.
Member Alejandre seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by voice vote.
The Board reconvened in open session on Friday November 2, 2018 at 9:00 AM in the Capitol Room of
the Wyndham Hotel in Springfield, Illinois. Present were the following: Chairman Don Tracy, Member
Steve Dolins and Member Alejandre. Member Robison participated telephonically.

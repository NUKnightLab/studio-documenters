INVOCATION
Chairman Woronchak called upon Commissioner Barone for the introduction of Darcel
Brown, Legislative Research Assistant, who gave the following invocation:
“Lord, we thank you for this season of giving. We thank you for the provisions that you
have made for the people of Wayne County. I pray that you would lead and guide the
Commissioners during today’s deliberations. Reward each Commissioner for the sacrifice of
their time, efforts and resources. During this season, we ask that you make provision for those
in need. Comfort those who have lost loved ones and allow the good and joyful memories to
sustain them during this season. Help us to remember that you are the reason for the season.
We ask these things in Jesus’ name. AMEN.”

 GENERAL BACKGROUND
Innovative Financial Housing Solutions, Inc. is a premier Atlanta, Georgia woman-owned small
business dedicated to providing management, financial program, technical and logistical support
services to public housing authorities and other affordable housing providers. IFH Solutions
professionals have extensive experience administering, assessing, evaluating, executing and
innovating United Stated Department of Housing and Urban Development (“HUD”) housing and
community development related programs and initiatives.
PROCUREMENT HISTORY
The RFP for Accounting Services (Mini RFP No. 09-00466) was released on December 22,
2009. Nine (9) vendors downloaded the solicitation from BuySpeed Online. The RFP was also
advertised on the CHA website. The Department of Procurement and Contracts received one (1)
proposal on January 11, 2010 from Innovative Financial Housing Solutions, Inc. Based upon the
review of the proposal; the evaluation committee determined that Innovative Financial Housing
Solutions, Inc. would be the best candidate to provide Accounting Services to the Authority.
Based on the foregoing, it is in the best interest of the CHA to enter into a contract with
Innovative Financial Housing Solutions, Inc. in an amount not-to-exceed $250,000.00 for a base
term of two (2) years, with one (1) year option to provide Accounting Services.

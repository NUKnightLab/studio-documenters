A.

111TH STREET/KEDZIE BUSINESS DISTRICT TIF REDEVELOPMENT
PROJECT AREA (WARD 19)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Community Development=s
intention enter into a negotiated sale with Gendell Partners–Mt. Greenwood, LLC
for the disposition of the property located at the northwest corner of 111th Street
and Kedzie Avenue in the 111th Street/Kedzie Business District Tax Increment
Financing Redevelopment Project Area; to request alternative proposals, to
approve the sale of the property to Gendell Partners–Mt. Greenwood, LLC if no
responsive alternative proposals are received, and to recommend to the City
Council of the City of Chicago the designation of Gendell Partners – Mt.
Greenwood, LLC as Developer if no responsive alternative proposals are
received. This action seeks to amend the terms of the negotiated sale and
developer designation approved by the Community Development Commission on
February 19, 2008.
Project Manager: Daniel Klaiber
Motioned by Leon, seconded by Sahli. Approved 5-0. Yeas: Leon, Martin, Stein,
Sahli and Lowry. Bland not present.

LASALLE CENTRAL TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 42)
Request authority for the Department of Community Development to negotiate a
redevelopment agreement with Lyric Opera of Chicago for restoration of certain
exterior features of the Civic Opera Building located at 20 N. Wacker Drive in
the LaSalle Central Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and to
recommend to the City Council of the City of Chicago the designation of Lyric
Opera of Chicago as Developer. The Civic Opera Building is a designated
Chicago Landmark.

Creating ?Accountability Charges? for Violations of the Shakman Consent Decree

WHEREAS, violations of the Shakman Consent Decree cost Cook County over $600,000 in Fiscal Year 2013
and millions of dollars in previous ?scal years; and

WHEREAS, payments for Violations of the Shakman Consent Decree are deducted from the Cook County Self-
Insurance Fund; and

WHEREAS, there is no ?nancial penalty to individual departments that violate the Shakman Consent Decree;
and

NOW THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED, that the Finance Committee?s Subcommittee on Litigation of the
Cook County Board of Commissioners approves a plan to assess an ?Accountability Charge? against any
Department that is found to be in violation of the Shakman Consent Decree in Fiscal Year 2014 and future years
for any action occurring after January 1, 2014; and

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the ?Accountability Charge? approved plan includes a deduction in the
operating funds of the Department in the amount of two percent of the total amount assessed for the
violation of the Shakman Consent Decree and the deducted funds shall be transferred to the Cook County Self
Insurance Fund.

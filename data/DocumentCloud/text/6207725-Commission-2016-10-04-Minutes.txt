SSA #2 Commissioners’ Meeting
Central Gyros Restaurant
October 4, 2016, 2pm
Commissioners Present: Kurt Koziol, Mike Valenti, John Toumplis
Others Present: Larry Lynch, Darlene Ortiz, Kevin Lamm, Aaron Roppolo, Bob Garza,
Michael Caldarulo, Reid Mackin, Javier Herrera, Jim Miller.
Quorum & Meeting Minutes
With three commissioners present, a quorum was determined to exist. Mike motioned
to accept the July minutes; Kurt seconded the motion, and the motion was approved
unanimously.
Administration
Gina Buda has notified the chamber of her intention to resign from the commission. A
letter of resignation addressed to the mayor is forthcoming. Bob Garza’s application for
SSA Commissioner is still being processed.
Finances
Three financial statements were presented: (1) Year-to-date Profit & Loss vs. 2015; (2)
Year-to-date vs. 2016 budget; (3) Current Balance Sheet vs. 2015.
Garage Maintenance & Repair
Aaron Roppolo of Superior Surfaces provided an update regarding the resurfacing
project. Since questions were raised concerning the continuing presence of stains on
the concrete as well as a dip in the concrete on the top ramp near the Parkside
stairwell, Aaron will investigate possible solutions.
The commission reviewed two snow-removal quotes: TSI and Imperial Landscaping.
Based on cost and experience, Mike motioned to accept Imperial Landscaping’s
proposal for the 2016-2017 season; Kurt seconded the motion, and the motion was
approved unanimously.
The commissioners declined to approve an expense for holiday light-pole decorations
Next Meeting
The next scheduled meeting is December 7 to select a CPA firm for the 2016 audit.

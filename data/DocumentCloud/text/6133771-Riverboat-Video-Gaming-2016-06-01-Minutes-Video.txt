MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING - VIDEO
OPEN SESSION
June 2, 2016
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
Chicago, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
On Thursday, June 2, 2016 a Regular Meeting - Video of the Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”)
was held on the 5th floor at 160 N. LaSalle at the Michael J. Bilandic Building, Chicago, Illinois.
The Open Session on Video Gaming started at approximately 9:22 A.M. Present were Chairman
Don Tracy, Members Thomas Dunn, Hector Alejandre, Member Dee Robinson and Member
Steve Dolins.
APPROVAL OF OPEN SESSION MINUTES:
Member Alejandre moved that the Board approve the open session minutes from its Regular
Meeting of April 21, 2016. Member Dolins seconded the motion. The Board approved the
motion unanimously by roll call vote.
CHAIRMAN'S COMMENTS –
Chairman Tracy commented on the progress of implementing an SEP program for video gaming.
The Chairman also commented on the Inducement Policy which will not go into effect on June 1,
2016.
ADMINISTRATOR'S COMMENTS –
Administrator Ostrowski stated that Agenda Items Illinois Gaming Investors, FJC and Tangent
are tabled for this month.
The Administrator also stated that 5,468 locations are up and running and there are 23,400 live
VGTs. The Administrator also gave an update on a search warrant that was executed and illegal
gaming devices were recovered.

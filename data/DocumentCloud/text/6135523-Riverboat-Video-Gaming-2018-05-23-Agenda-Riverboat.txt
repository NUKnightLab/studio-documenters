Notice of Illinois Gaming Board Meeting
Regular Board Meeting - Riverboat
The Illinois Gaming Board will convene a regular meeting of the Board in Open Session on
Wednesday, May 23, 2018 at 11:00 A.M. in the Board’s Conference Room on the 3rd floor of the
Michael A. Bilandic Building, and immediately retire to Closed Executive Session. The regular meeting
of the Board will reconvene in Open Session on Thursday, May 24, 2018 at 9:00 A.M. in the Assembly
Hall of the James R. Thompson Center at 100 W. Randolph, Chicago, Illinois. The subject matters to be
discussed are included on the attached proposed Riverboat agenda.
Please note that the regular Riverboat meeting agenda is typically posted 48 hours prior to the
scheduled meeting date.

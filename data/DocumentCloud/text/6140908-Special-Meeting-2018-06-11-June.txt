CONSIDERATION AND POSSIBLE SELECITON OF CIO CANDIDATE:

Three final candidates were present for review by the Board.
On motion by Ford, second by Cody, the Board went into executive session as allowed under 5
ILCS 120/2 (c) 1 of the Open Meetings Act. The vote was:
Yeas: Cody, Ford, Fortuna, O’Sullivan, Summers
Nays: None
Trustees, Keane, Valencia and Martin entered the meeting during the executive session.
Out of executive session the following motions were made.
On motion by Martin, second by Ford to hire Lorna Scott as the Fund’s CIO.
Motion was withdrawn by the maker and the second and additional discussions ensued after which
a second motion was made.
Motion by Martin, second by Ford to hire Lorna Scott as the Fund’s CIO subject to successful
negotiations and reference checks. The vote was:
Yeas: Martin, O’Sullivan, Cody, Ford, Fortuna
Nays: Keane, Summers, Valencia
Motion passes

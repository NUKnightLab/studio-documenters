CALL TO ORDER OF THE JANUARY 20, 2014 SPECIAL BOARD MEETING
Secretary to the Board, Matthew B. DeLeon, reads call of the meeting into the record.
Toni Preckwinkle, President of the Board of Commissioners of the Forest Preserve District of Cook
County, Illinois (the "District"), has directed me to call a Special Meeting of the District Board of
Commissioners on Tuesday, January 20, 2015, at the hour of 11:00 a.m. in the County Board Room, Room
569, County Building, 118 North Clark Street, Chicago, Illinois, to conduct a public hearing, pursuant to
Section 10 of the Bond Issue Notification Act, 30 ILCS 352110, concerning the District's intent to sell
approximately $3 ,000,000 principal amount of its general obligation limited tax bonds.
____________________

Secretary to the Board, Matthew B. DeLeon, read the President’s Introduction into the record.
I, Toni Preckwinkle, President of the Forest Preserve District of Cook County, Illinois, pursuant to the
authority invested in me by Title 1, Chapter 5-4 of the Official Forest Preserve District Code (the “District
Code of Ordinances”), hereby call this Special meeting to order. This is a Special Meeting of the Board of
Commissioners of the Forest Preserve District of Cook County, Illinois (the “District”) in connection with
the issuance by the District of not to exceed $3,000,000 General Obligation Limited Tax Bonds, Series
2015B, hereinafter called the Bonds.
The purpose of the meeting is to conduct a public hearing pursuant to published notice required by Illinois
law, the Bond Issue Notification Act, 30 ILCS 352/1 et seq.. Notice of the meeting was published in The
Chicago Tribune on January 13, 2015, at least 7 days prior to the date hereof. The Notice of the meeting was
also posted at 118 North Clark Street, in the City of Chicago, Illinois, the principal office of the Forest
Preserve District of Cook County, Illinois at least 48 hours before the date of the meeting. I have the Proof
of Publication from The Chicago Tribune which I will now present to the Secretary of the Board for filing
with the Minutes of the Meeting and which will become part of the permanent record of the District.
The proceeds of the Bonds will be used by the District (a) to construct, acquire, equip, repair and renovate
buildings and make other improvements to land of the District; (b) to acquire land and equipment for the
District; (c) to capitalize interest relating to the Bonds; and (d) to finance costs of issuance relating to the
Bonds.
The Bonds are direct and general obligations of the District for which the District’s pledges its full faith and
credit. The Bonds will be payable from ad valorem taxes levied on all taxable property within the
boundaries of the District, without limitation as to rate, but limited as to amount.
____________________

Secretary to the Board, Matthew B. DeLeon, read the notice of Public Hearing into the record.
FOREST PRESERVE DISTRICT OF COOK COUNTY, ILLINOIS BOND ISSUE

the hearing will be to receive public comments on the proposal to sell general obligation limited tax bonds

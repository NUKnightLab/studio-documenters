MINUTES OF THE
DETROIT BROWNFIELD REDEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY
PUBLIC HEARING FOR THE
FORMER FREE PRESS BUILDING
BROWNFIELD REDEVELOPMENT PLAN
Tuesday, April 18, 2017
Detroit Economic Growth Corporation Offices
500 Griswold, Suite 2200
Detroit, MI 48226
5:30 PM
In attendance were:
Jennifer Kanalos (DEGC/DBRA)
Brian Vosburg (DEGC/DBRA)
Steve Ogden (Bedrock Detroit)
Scott Collins (Bedrock Detroit)
Anna Kurth (Bedrock Detroit)
Anne Jamieson (Jamieson Development Consulting)
Brad Lutz (DBRA-CAC)
Louis Aguilar (Detroit News)

Mr. Vosburg called the meeting to order at 5:35 PM.
Mr. Vosburg informed the hearing of the tax increment financing request per the
Brownfield Plan. Mr. Ogden introduced the Bedrock Detroit development team. Mr. Collins
provided details regarding the redevelopment plan for the project location and answered
questions regarding the history of the building, attempts by previous developers to
redevelop the property, commercial rental plans for the building, residential rental plans
for the building, historic preservation plans, construction timeline and plan for employment
of Detroit residents. Mr. Ogden provided information regarding Bedrock Detroit’s plan to
voluntarily enter into a Community Benefits Agreement for the project. Renderings of the
project were provided via a PowerPoint presentation and copies of the Brownfield Plan
were also provided.
There were no comments received in opposition to the project.
Citing no further public comments, Mr. Vosburg closed the public hearing at 6:06 PM.

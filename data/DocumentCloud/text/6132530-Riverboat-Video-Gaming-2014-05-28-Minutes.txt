MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING - RIVERBOAT
OPEN SESSION
May 29, 2014
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
On Thursday, May 29, 2014 a Regular Meeting - Riverboat of the Illinois Gaming Board
(“Board”) was held on the 5th floor at 160 N. LaSalle at the Michael J. Bilandic Building,
Chicago, Illinois at 10:23 A.M. Present were the following: Chairman Aaron Jaffe, Members
Maribeth Vander Weele, Lee Gould, and Michael Holewinski.
APPROVAL OF MINUTES
Member Gould moved that the Board approve the open session minutes of its Regular
Meeting of April 29, 2014. Member Vander Weele seconded the motion. The Board approved
the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
ADMINISTRATOR’S REPORT –
The Administrator stated that he and Commander Vega; Deputy Administrator of Enforcement,
are making visits to various casinos for Commander Vega to introduce himself to staff and tour
the individual casinos.
ITEM FOR INITIAL CONSIDERATION –
•

Caesars Entertainment – Financing Transaction

ITEMS FOR FINAL ACTION –
•

Des Plaines Development Limited Partnership d/b/a Harrah’s Joliet Casino & Hotel
– Jacqueline Dowdy-Co-Trustee of John Q. Hammons Irrevocable Trust - Key
Person
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Holewinski moved that the Board approve
Jacqueline Dowdy as a Key Person of Des Plaines Development Limited Partnership
d/b/a Harrah’s Joliet Casino & Hotel in her role as Co-Trustee of the John Q.
Hammons Irrevocable Trust. Member Gould seconded the motion. The Board
approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.

•

Par-A-Dice Gaming Corporation d/b/a Par-A-Dice Hotel Casino – Robert Haines –
Director of Internal Audit for Boyd Gaming – Key Person
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Vander Weele moved that the Board approve
Robert Haines as a Key Person of Par-A-Dice Gaming Corporation d/b/a Par-ADice Hotel Casino in his role as Director of Internal Audit for Boyd Gaming.

 Member Gould seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by
roll call vote.
SUPPLIER LICENSEE ITEMS –
•

Novomatic Americas Sales LLC – Supplier – Initial License
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Gould moved that Novomatic Americas Sales,
LLC be granted a Supplier’s License for a term of one year and that the following
positions and individuals be designated as Key Persons of the licensee.
o Novomatic Americas Holding, Inc.
o Novomatic AG
o Novo Invest GMBH
o Chief Executive Officer
o Johann F. Graf and
o Jens Halle
Further the Board moved that Novomatic Americas Sales LLC be authorized to
distribute electronic gaming devices and related supplies to Illinois Owner
Licensees. Member Vander Weele seconded the motion. The Board approved the
motion unanimously by roll call vote.

•

House Advantage, LLC – License Renewal
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Holewinski moved that the Board renew the
Supplier’s License of House Advantage, LLC for a period of four years and that the
following positions and individuals be designated as Key Persons of the licensee:
President/CEO and Jon C. Wolfe.
Further Member Holewinski moved that House Advantage, LLC be authorized to
supply the HALo Kiosk and HALo Promotions software products to Illinois Owner
Licensees. Member Gould seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion
unanimously by roll call vote.

OCCUPATIONAL LICENSE APPROVALS AND DENIALS –
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Vander Weele moved that the Board find
suitable and approve 44 applications for Level 2 Occupational Licenses and 77
applications for Level 3 Occupational Licenses and issue a Notice of Denial to
Thomas E. Lee, Jr. Member Gould seconded the motion. The Board approved the
motion unanimously by roll call vote.

At 10:32 A.M. Member Gould motioned to adjourn and Member Vander Weele seconded the
motion. All Members voted in favor of adjournment.

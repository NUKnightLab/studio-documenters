BY TRUSTEE NEARY – SUPPORTED BY TRUSTEE SERDA
WHEREAS, at its regular meeting on December 1, 2016, the Board of Trustees of the Police and
Fire Retirement System of the City of Detroit (“PFRS”), upon the recommendation of UHY
Advisors, Inc., and staff, reviewed and approved execution of an engagement agreement with
Vitech Systems Group, Inc.(“Vitech”), to provide professional services for software implementation
(the “Contract”) related to the Enterprise Resource Planning (“ERP”) being conducted by UHY; and
WHEREAS, the Contract and the services received will benefit the joint administration of PFRS
and the General Retirement System of the City of Detroit (“GRS”), and the costs for which services
related to the Contract will be equally shared between PFRS and GRS; and
WHEREAS, the Board continues to view investment in improvements to network and security
administration and ERP as a use of funds consistent with the obligation to discharge fiduciary
duties to PFRS pursuant to the Public Employee Retirement System Investment Act, Act 314 of
1965; and
WHEREAS, the Board has been advised by the Executive Director and General Counsel regarding
this matter; and
WHEREAS; the Board has considered this matter in open session,
NOW THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED, that the Board authorizes the retention of Vitech as set
forth in the Contract; and
FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Board authorizes a budget as set forth in the Contract to be
equally supported and shared between PFRS and GRS; and
FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Executive Director is authorized to execute the Contract subject
to review and approval of the form of Contract by General Counsel, and
FURTHER RESOLVED, that a copy of this resolution will be forwarded to UHY Advisors and Clark
Hill:
YEAS – TRUSTEES MOSLIMANI, NAGLICK, NEARY, SERDA, SZYMANSKI, THOMAS,
TROZAK, TUCKER AND CHAIRPERSON ROBERSON – 9
NAYS – NONE

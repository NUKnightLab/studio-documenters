MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL MEETING
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
July 24, 2000
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
A Special Meeting of the Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”) was held on July 24, 2000 in
Room #S-304 of the State of Illinois Building, Chicago, Illinois, pursuant to the Illinois
Open Meetings Act, 5 ILCS 120.
The following Board member was present in person, Chairman Gregory C. Jones. Board
members Joseph Lamendella, Sterling M. Ryder, Staci Yandle and Stuart Levine were
present telephonically.
Also in attendance were: Administrator Sergio E. Acosta, Special Counsel to the Board
Robert F. Casey, Deputy Administrators Joseph Haughey, Allan S. McDonald, Deputy
Chief Legal Counsel Jeannette P. Tamayo, other members of the staff and members of the
Attorney General’s office.
The meeting was convened at 1:10p.m. Member Yandle moved that the Board retire to
Closed Session pursuant to Section 2 (c), paragraphs (4) and (11) of the Open
Meetings Act. Member Lamendella seconded the motion. The Board adopted the
motion by unanimous consent.
The Board adjourned at 2:10 p.m.
Respectfully submitted,

Monica L. Thomas
Secretary to the Board

STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on June 9, 2015
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Board; Richard Lewis, Member of the Board; Kathryn
Zeledon Nelson, General Counsel; Sarah Kerley, Deputy General Counsel; Anna Hamburg-Gal, Associate General
Counsel; and staff. Melissa Mlynski, Executive Director; and staff members participated by phone.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD MAY 19, 2015

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the May 19, 2015 meeting. Member Anderson moved to approve
the minutes. Member Lewis seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gierut asked Executive Director Mlynski to present the Local Panel administrative actions report as well as the
fiscal, legislative and mediation reports. She presented the reports and summarized their highlights. The Board examined
the reports and discussed them.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.
Case No. L-RC-14-018
Chicago Joint Board, Local 200, RWDSU, United Food and Commercial Workers International Union/
County of Cook (John H. Stroger Hospital)

Chairman Gierut asked General Counsel Nelson to call and present the case to the Board. General Counsel Nelson stated
that Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) Anna Hamburg-Gal issued a Recommended Decision and Order (RDO)
recommending that the Board dismiss the petition. She then discussed the exceptions and response that were filed. After
her presentation, General Counsel Nelson recommended that the Board affirm the ALJ’s RDO. After discussion, Member
Lewis moved to affirm the RDO. Member Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a
voice vote.
VI.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded it was
unnecessary.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board, in
Chicago, Illinois, this 7th day of July, 2015.

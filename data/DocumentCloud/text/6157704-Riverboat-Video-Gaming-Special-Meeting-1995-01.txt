MINUTES OF SPECIAL MEETING
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
JANUARY 4, 1995
VIA TELEPHONE CONFERENCE CALL

A Special Meeting of the Illinois Gaming Board was held on January 4, 1995
at 10:00 A.M. The meeting was held via telephone conference call and notice was
duly and timely given to each board member and the general public in conformity
with the Illinois Open Meetings Act, 5 ILCS 120.
The following Board Members participated in the conference call: J.
Thomas Johnson, Chairman; and Members William B. Browder, Gayl S. Pyatt, J.
William Roberts and Robert F. Vickrey.
Upon unanimous consent, the Board retired to Closed Session to discuss
personnel issues at 10:01 A.M.
The Illinois Gaming Board reconvened in Open Session at 10:50 A.M. with
all members present.
There being no further business to come before the Board and upon
unanimous consent, the Board adjourned at 10:50 A.M.
Submitted by,

______________________
J. Thomas Johnson
Chairman of the Board

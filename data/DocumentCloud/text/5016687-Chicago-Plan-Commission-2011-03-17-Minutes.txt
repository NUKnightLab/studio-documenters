I.

The Chairman called the meeting to order at 1:106 PM and then undertook a roll
call to establish the presence of a quorum. The hearing commenced with eleven
members present.

II.

A court reporter was present to record the proceedings. A transcript is available
for the purpose of pursuing all matters in greater detail and is a part of the
permanent public record of the Regular Hearing of the Chicago Plan Commission
held on March 17, 2011.

MATTERS SUBMITTED IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE LAKE MICHIGAN AND
CHICAGO LAKEFRONT PROTECTION ORDINANCE AND/OR THE CHICAGO
ZONING ORDINANCE:

1.

A Lake Michigan and Chicago Lakefront Protection Application No. 595 for
the property generally located at 501-509 South Wabash Avenue submitted by
Roosevelt University. The Applicant is proposing to construct a 2-story
athletic field house in the Private Use Zone of the Lake Michigan and Chicago
Lakefront Protection District. (2nd Ward) Approved 12-0, Yeahs –
Commissioners Holleb, Migala, Mooney, Nelson, Pacher, Scudiero, Shah,
Shiffrin, Slaughter, Stone, Weinstein and Searl

2.

A proposed amendment to Institutional Planned Development No. 921 for
the property generally located at 7101-49 South South Chicago, 7200 South
Ingleside, 7137-39 South Drexel and 7230-96 South South Chicago Avenue
submitted by Comer Science and Education Foundation. The Applicant is
proposing to amend the Planned Development to expand the boundary to include
Gary Comer College Prep High School and a proposed Youth Garden and Youth
Center offices. Prior to establishing PD No. 921 as amended, the Applicant is
proposing to rezone the site from RS-3 Residential Single-Unit (Detached House)
District and M1-2 Limited Manufacturing/Business Park District to C1-2
Neighborhood Commercial District. (5th Ward) Approved 11-0, Yeahs –
Commissioners Holleb, Migala, Mooney, Nelson, Pacher, Scudiero, Shah,
Shiffrin, Slaughter, Stone, and Searl

3.

A proposed Institutional Planned Development for the property generally located
at 6537-6557 North Central Avenue, 6501-6547 N. Hiawatha Avenue and 65006542 North Minnehaha Avenue submitted by the Public Building Commission of
Chicago. The Applicant is proposing to construct a new 2-story addition to the
existing Edgebrook Elementary School. Prior to establishing the Planned
Development, the Applicant proposes to rezone the site from POS-1 Parks and
Open Space District to RS-1 Residential Single (Detached-House) District. (41st
Ward) Approved 12-0, Yeahs – Commissioners Holleb, Migala, Mooney,
Nelson, Pacher, Scudiero, Shah, Shiffrin, Slaughter, Stone, Weinstein and
Searl

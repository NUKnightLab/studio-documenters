February 28, 2017 Preliminary Performance Report/Manager Review
These items were not reviewed at the meeting.
Resolution on Fossil Fuels Divestment Update
The Board discussed the letter from the City of Chicago’s Committee on Finance regarding the resolution calling for
divestment in fossil fuel companies. Mr. Walsh reviewed this matter and presented a draft of the Fund’s response to
the letter. The Board gave direction to proceed with the response letter to the City of Chicago’s Committee on Finance.

ADMINISTRATIVE REPORT
Cash Needs
Mr. Walsh reviewed the cash needs for the month of March 2017.
Legislative Matters
Mr. Walsh informed the Board that he would be joining a phone call on March 22, 2017 with MEABF and
representatives from Senator John Cullerton’s office regarding Senate Bill 16. Mr. Walsh reminded the Board of the
deadline for Governor to sign Senate Bill 2437.
Miscellaneous
Special Investment Meeting – The Board decided to cancel the special investment meeting scheduled for Thursday,
April 13, 2017 at 9:00 at the Fund office.
Statements of Economic Interest – Mr. Carroll reminded the Board that they should receive an email from the
County Clerk regarding the next steps in completing the Statement of Economic Interest for year 2016.

LEGAL REPORT
DV Urban Litigation
Mr. Donham updated the Board on the DV Urban matter and indicated that additional details will be provided at the
next Board meeting. The Trustees decided to enter executive session to discuss another aspect of the DV Urban
matter.

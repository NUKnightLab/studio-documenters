II.

PRESENT

Jacalyn J. Zimmerman, Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Board; Edward E. Sadlowski,
Member of the Board; John F. Brosnan, Executive Director; Jerald S. Post, General Counsel; and staff
members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD JULY 13, 2010

Chairman Zimmerman called for approval of the minutes of the July 13, 2010 meeting. Member
Anderson moved to approve the minutes. Member Sadlowski seconded. The Board passed the motion
unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Zimmerman asked Executive Director Brosnan to distribute the Local Panel administrative
actions report for the period of time from July 1 through July 31, 2010. He distributed the report to the
Board, and the Board examined it.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.
Case No. L-CA-09-009
Illinois Fraternal Order of Police, Lodge 7/City of Chicago

Chairman Zimmerman called the case. The Board heard oral argument, recorded by a court reporter. At
the conclusion of the parties' arguments, the Board had a short discussion and unanimously agreed to
hold the case over until the next meeting, for a decision.
2.
Case Nos. L-RC-09-018 and L-UC-09-008
American Fed. of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31/City of Chicago
Chairman Zimmerman called the case. General Counsel Post reviewed the issues presented by the
exceptions to Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) Harvey's Recommended Decision and Order (RDO), and
recommended as to her determination, that the Board affirm in part and reverse in part. After
discussion, Member Sadlowski moved to accept the general counsel's recommendation. Member
Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
3.
Case No. L-RC-08-022
Metropolitan Alliance of Police/County of Cook/Sheriff of Cook County
Chairman Zimmerman called the case, noting that no party filed exceptions to ALJ Wells' RDO, and
asked whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own motion. The Board declined to do so.

 4.
Case No. L-RC-10-031
Service Employees International Union, Local 73/County of Cook/Sheriff of Cook County
Chairman Zimmerman called the case, noting that no party filed exceptions to ALJ Clifford's RDO, and
asked whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own motion. The Board declined to do so.
VI.

OTHER BOARD MATTERS

The Board heard and considered the fiscal and legislative reports. After a short discussion regarding
whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded that it was unnecessary at this time.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Zimmerman called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the
meeting, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor
Relations Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 14th day of September, 2010.

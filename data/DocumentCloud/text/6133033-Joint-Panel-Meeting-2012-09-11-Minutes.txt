JOINT PANEL
Minutes of the Joint Panel Meeting
Held on September 11, 2012

II.

PRESENT

John Hartnett, Joint Panel Chairman; Paul S. Besson, Member of the State Panel; James Q. Brennwald,
Member of the State Panel; Michael G. Coli, Member of the State Panel; Albert Washington, Member of the
State Panel; Robert M. Gierut, Local Panel Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Local Panel;
Richard Lewis, Member of the Local Panel; Melissa Mlynski, Executive Director; Jerald S. Post, General
Counsel; and staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES

Chairman Hartnett called for approval of the minutes of the August 14, 2012 meeting. Member Coli moved to
approve the minutes. Member Washington seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice
vote.
IV.

LEGISLATIVE/FISCAL REPORTS

Executive Director Mlynski reported various legislative issues. She then presented the fiscal report, reviewing
in detail the general ledger sheet and the disbursement record. The Board then discussed and questioned
various aspects of the fiscal report.
V.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

Member Coli made a motion to leave open session. Member Besson seconded the motion, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote. Member Coli made a motion to go into executive session to discuss
personnel/hiring matters pursuant to 5 ILCS 120/2(c)(1). Member Besson seconded the motion, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote. Subsequently, at the end of the discussion, Member Coli made a
motion to leave executive session. Chairman Hartnett seconded the motion, and the Board passed it
unanimously, by a voice vote. Member Anderson then made a motion to return to open session. Member
Besson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VI.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Hartnett called for a motion to adjourn. Local Panel Chairman Gierut made a motion to adjourn the
meeting, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Joint Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations
Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 15th day of November, 2012.

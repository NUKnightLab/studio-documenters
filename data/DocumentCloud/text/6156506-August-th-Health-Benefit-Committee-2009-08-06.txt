Finch and Degnan

Review of final proposed 2019 Health Benefit Premiums.
The committee and staff met to finalize the 2010 health benefit premiums. This was a continuation of
the discussion held at the July 2009 Health Benefit Committee meeting. The final analysis presented
included a 4 year plan developed to align the unified and non-unified rates as per direction given by
the committee during the July 2009 committee meeting.
It was moved by Trustee Kilgallon and seconded by Trustee Benjamin that the healthcare plan
premiums for the 2010 calendar year be presented to the Board for approval at the August 6, 2009
Full Board meeting.

Vote Result: MOTION ADOPTED UNANIMOUSLY BY VOICE VOTE.

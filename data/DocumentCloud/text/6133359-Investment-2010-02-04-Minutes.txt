MUNICIPAL EMPLOYEES’ ANNUITY AND BENEFIT FUND OF CHICAGO
SPECIAL MEETING NO. 2010-02-01
FEBRUARY 4, 2010

A special meeting of the Retirement Board of the Municipal Employees' Annuity and Benefit Fund of
Chicago was held in the Offices of the Fund on February 4, 2010 at 10:00 a.m. regarding investments.
The meeting was called to order at 10:00 a.m.

ABSENT:

Private Equity Discussion
Mr. Wrubel of Marquette Associates presented an overview regarding the Fund’s current investments in
Private Equity. Marquette Associates suggested that the Fund advertise a Request for Information for
qualified Private Equity Managers that will create a pool of potential candidates for future allocations into
Private Equity.
The Board directed Marquette to begin the RFI process by placing the information on the Fund’s website.
William Blair Presentation – Emerging Markets
Stephanie G. Braming, and John V. McLaughlin of William Blair presented an overview of Emerging
Markets and their role in the Fund’s portfolio.
After their presentation, there was a brief question and answer period. The Board thanked them for their
presentation.
Cash Overlay Education
Mr. Wrubel gave an overview of cash overlay strategies to the Board and the potential impact these
strategies could have for the Fund’s cash flow.
After the presentation, there was a brief question and answer period. The Board directed Mr. Wrubel to
provide more information regarding the cash overlay process.

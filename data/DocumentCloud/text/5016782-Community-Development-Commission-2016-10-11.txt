 III.

NEW BUSINESS
A.

BRONZEVILLE TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 4)
Request authority to advertise and issue a Request For Proposals for the purchase
and redevelopment of the property known as the Michael Reese Site, with an
approximate address of 2960 S. Ellis Avenue, in the Bronzeville Tax Increment
Financing Redevelopment Project Area.
Project Manager: Aarti Kotak
Motioned by Ward, seconded by Moreno. Approved 13-0. Yeas: Alphonse,
Butler, Cepeda, Curtis, Delgado, Griggs, Moreno, Newsome, Reifman, Rhodes,
Ward, Whiteside and Perez.
16-CDC-25

B.

107TH/HALSTED TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 34)
Request authority for the Department of Planning and Development to enter into a
negotiated sale with the Chicago Park District for the disposition of the property
located at 201-203 and 207-209 W. 110th Street and 11000-11010 S. Wentworth
Avenue in the 107th/Halsted Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project
Area, to request alternative proposals, and to approve the sale of the property to
the Chicago Park District if no responsive alternative proposals are received.
Project Manager: Michele A. Rhymes
Motioned by Delgado, seconded by Newsome. Approved 13-0. Yeas: Alphonse,
Butler, Cepeda, Curtis, Delgado, Griggs, Moreno, Newsome, Reifman, Rhodes,
Ward, Whiteside and Perez.
16-CDC-26

C.

119TH/HALSTED TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 34)

Project Manager: Michele A. Rhymes

Communication dated July 17, 2019 (received August 14th) requesting
Commission approval of a one-year extension to a $900,000 grant award
between the Charter County of Wayne and the U.S. Department of Justice,
Office on Violence Against Women, with no change in the dollar amount, to allow
for the Wayne County Prosecutor’s Office (WCPO) to continue to investigate and
prosecute the cases stemming from the 11,341 rape kits found in a Detroit Police
Department warehouse in 2009, with grant money being used to fund the
salaries and fringe benefits of two assistant prosecuting attorneys and one
WCPO sexual assault victim advocate; the term of the agreement is extended
from October 1, 2016 through September 30, 2020; the revenue from the grant
will continue to be deposited into Account No. 101 22967 529000 (Encourage
Arrests). (2017-93-008M1)

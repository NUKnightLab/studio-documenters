Ladies and Gentlemen:
Your Committee on Legislation & Intergovernmental Relations of the Board of Commissioners of
Cook County met pursuant to notice on Tuesday, July 1, 2003 at the hour of 9:30 AM in the Board
Room, Room 569, County Building, 118 North Clark Street, Chicago, Illinois.
Your Committee has considered the following item and upon adoption of this report, the
recommendations are as follows:

259865

COOK COUNTY COMMISSION ON HUMAN RIGHTS (APPOINTMENT).
Transmitting a Communication, dated May 28, 2003 from John H. Stroger, Jr.,
President, Cook County Board of Commissioners:
Pursuant to Article X, A(1) of the Cook County Human Rights Ordinance, I hereby
recommend WALTER J. KENDALL III for appointment to the Cook County
Commission on Human Rights for a term ending July 1, 2005.
I submit this appointment for your approval.
*Referred to the Legislation & Intergovernmental Relation Committee 6/17/03.

Commissioner Peraica extolled Mr. Kendall as an excellent professor.
Commissioner Silvestri expressed his wholehearted support.
Commissioner Suffredin remarked that he has known Mr. Kendall for twenty-five years since they were
colleagues at John Marshall Law School and gave his enthusiastic support of the appointment.
Vice Chairman Silvestri, seconded by Commissioner Daley, moved the approval of
Communication No. 259865. The motion carried.
Commissioner Daley moved to adjourn the meeting, seconded by Commissioner Claypool. The
motion carried and the meeting was adjourned.

Respectfully submitted,

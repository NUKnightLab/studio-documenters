MONTHLY NEWS AND INFORMATION FROM YOUR RETIREMENT BOARD

Retiree Brunch
The annual retiree brunch, that is co-sponsored by the Local 2 and the Retirement Board, will be held on
May 6, 2018, at De La Salle Institute located at 3434 S Michigan Ave. The doors will open at 10:00am
and the event will begin at 10:30am. Please call Tugi at Local 2 (773) 536-0450 to register for the
brunch.

Pre-Retirement Seminar
The FABF’s next pre-retirement seminar will be held on Saturday, September 8th (1C EMS-2) and a
limited number of seats are still available.
An RSVP is required, so please call (312) 726-5823 or email seminar@fabf.org. The seminars are
provided for those members within three years of actual retirement.
The pre-retirement seminars are held at the Quinn Fire Academy on 558 W. De Koven from 7:30 A.M.1:30 P.M. Some of the topics that will be discussed include:



Retiree Healthcare
Social Security and Medicare
Deferred Compensation

The materials presented at the pre-retirement seminars are available on the Fund’s website at
http://fabf.org/PreRetirement.html and everyone is invited to review the materials.

Wills For Heroes
All members should consider the need for a will and a power of attorney over healthcare. Please find
details about the Wills for Heroes program on the Fund’s website at
http://fabf.org/PDF/IndexPDF/WillsForHeroes.pdf.

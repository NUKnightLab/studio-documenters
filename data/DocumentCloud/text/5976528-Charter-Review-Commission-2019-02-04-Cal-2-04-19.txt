NOTICE OF SPECIAL MEETING OF THE DETROIT
CHARTER REVISION COMMISSION
In accordance with Section 5(4) of the Michigan Open Meetings Act, MCL 15.265(4), the
Detroit Charter Revision Commission will hold a Sub-Committee Charter meeting on Monday,
February 4, 2019, at 5:00 p.m. (Bylaws Committee). The meeting will be held at the 14th District
Democratic Party Headquarters located at 18409 Livernois, Detroit, Michigan 48221. The
telephone number is (313) 451-3367. The Public is invited and encouraged to attend.
Additional information regarding this meeting may be obtained from the Office of the City
Clerk at (313) 224-3266.

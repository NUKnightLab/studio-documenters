4.

Communication dated January 25, 2018 (received February 18th) requesting
Commission approval of an intergovernmental agreement between the Charter
County of Wayne and Schoolcraft College in the amount of $39,218 to provide
police academy training to Wayne County Sheriff’s Office recruits as required by
Michigan Commission of Law Enforcement Standards (MCOLES); the County
Executive advises that Article 35 of the Collective Bargaining Agreement
mandates that the Sheriff conduct one traditional police academy class per
calendar year; the term of the agreement will commence upon Commission
approval through November 9, 2018; the cost of the agreement will charged to
Account No. 101 35120 961000 (Sheriff-Training Academy). (2018-71-104)

5.

Communication dated February 13, 2018 (received February 28th) requesting
Commission approval of a cooperative contract between the Charter County of
Wayne and Berger Chevrolet, Inc. (of Grand Rapids) via MIDeal (Contract No.
071B7700177) in the amount of $390,272 for a fleeting business purchase to
secure sixteen (16) 2018 Chevy Impala LS sedans equipped with 360-degree
red/blue lighting, siren and a public address controller; the County Executive
advises that the vehicles will be assigned to various enforcement units within the
Sheriff’s Office and in addition, this purchase will be replacing current vehicles
that are over 8 to 12 years old with an average of 120,000 miles and requiring
constant maintenance; the term of the contract will commence upon Commission
approval through March 22, 2018; the cost of the contract will be charged to
Account No. 401 40100 978010 (Capital Programs). (2018-71-106)

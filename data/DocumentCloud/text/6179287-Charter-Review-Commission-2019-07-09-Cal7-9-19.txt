NOTICE OF DETROIT CHARTER
REVISION COMMISSION MEETING
In accordance with Section 5(4) of the Michigan Open Meetings Act, MCL 15.265(4), the
Detroit Charter Revision Commission will hold a meeting on Tuesday, July 9, 2019, at 6:30 p.m.
The meeting will be held at the Coleman A. Young Municipal Center, 2 Woodward Ave., Detroit,
MI 48226. The telephone number is (313) 224-3270. The Public is invited and encouraged to
attend.
Additional information regarding this meeting may be obtained from the Office of the City
Clerk at (313) 224-3266.

MINUTES OF TELEPHONIC MEETING
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
March 25, 2004
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
A Telephonic Meeting of the Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”) was held on March 25, 2004.
The Board met in the conference room of the Gaming Board Offices on the 3rd floor at 160
N. LaSalle for closed session.
The following Board Members were present: Chairman Elzie Higginbottom, and Member
Gary Peterlin. Members Violet Clark, William Dugan, and William Fannin were present
telephonically.
The meeting convened at 8:32 A.M. Pursuant to Section 2(c), paragraphs (1), (11), and (14)
of the Open Meetings Act and Section 6(d) of the Riverboat Gambling Act, Member Fanning
moved that the Board retire to Closed Session to discuss the items listed under Closed
Session on today’s agenda and relating to the following subject matters:
1. Investigations concerning applicants and licensees;
2. Pending litigation and matters involving probable litigation; and
3. Personnel matters.
Member Peterlin seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by
voice vote.
At approximately 10:10 A.M. Member Fanning moved that the Board adjourn. Member
Clark seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by voice vote.

Respectfully submitted,

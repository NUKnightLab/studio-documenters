 
Item No. 2
RESOLUTION NO. 2014-CHA-78
WHEREAS,

The Board of Commissioners has reviewed the Board Letter dated August 13,
2014, entitled Settlement Agreement in the matter of Donnell Outlaw and
Levelle Outlaw vs. Chicago Housing Authority, 12 L 5366, requesting
authorization to enter into a settlement agreement in the amount of
$250,000.00, in the matter of Donnell Outlaw and Levelle Outlaw vs. Chicago
Housing Authority, Case No. 12 L 5366.

THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE CHICAGO
THAT,

HOUSING AUTHORITY

the Board of Commissioners authorizes the General Counsel, or his designee,
to enter into a settlement agreement in the amount of $250,000.00, with the
CHA in the matter of Donnell Outlaw and Levelle Outlaw vs. Chicago
Housing Authority, Case No. 12 L 5366.

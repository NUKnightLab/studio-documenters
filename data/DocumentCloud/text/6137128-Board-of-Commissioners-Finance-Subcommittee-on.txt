REPORT OF THE LITIGATION SUBCOMMITTEE OF THE FINANCE COMMITTEE
December 15, 2010
The Honorable,
The Board of Commissioners of Cook County
Ladies and Gentlemen,
Your Litigation Subcommittee of the Finance Committee of the Board of Commissioners
met pursuant to notice on Wednesday, December 15, 2010 at 1:00 p.m., in the County
Building, Room 569, Chicago, Illinois.

Commissioner Collins, seconded by Vice Chairman Fritchey, moved to receive
and file the Case Disposition Summary Report submitted by the State’s Attorney’s
Office. The motion carried.
Vice Chairman Fritchey, seconded by Commissioner Schneider, moved to
convene Executive Session. The motion carried.
Commissioner Collins, seconded by Commissioner Tobolski, moved to return to
Regular Session. The motion carried.
Vice Chairman Fritchey, seconded by Commissioner Suffredin, moved to
recommend authority to settle or, in the alternative, authority to try, in the matter
of Diane Bucki v. County of Cook, Case No. 08 L 10026. The motion carried.
Vice Chairman Fritchey, seconded by, moved to authorize invoice payment for
Tabs #1 through #18 in Volume 2 which are in compliance with the County’s
Attorney-Fee Guidelines. The motion carried.

6-1

 BACKGROUND
NOACA’s Section 5310 Program Provides:

Capital grants to assist public transit agencies,
municipalities, private non-profits, and for profit
companies to provide coordinated transportation
services that are planned, designed, and carried
out to meet the needs of seniors and individuals
with disabilities.

6-1

 BACKGROUND
NOACA’s Section 5310 Program
Provides:

Operational funding to public transit
agencies to provide coordinated
transportation services that are planned,
designed, and carried out to meet the needs
of seniors and individuals with disabilities.

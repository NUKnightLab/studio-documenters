ASSISTANT EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR’S REPORT
ASSISTANT EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR DAVID CETLINSKI INFORMED THE BOARD
THAT BECAUSE TRUSTEE JOHN SERDA WAS THE ONLY MEMBER OF THE RANK
OF CAPTAIN AND ABOVE TO SUBMIT HIS INTENT TO RUN FOR TRUSTEE, NO
POLICE DEPARTMENT ELECTION WAS NECESSARY, PER AN APRIL 26, 2017
COMMUNNICATION FROM POLICE CHIEF JAMES CRAIG, AND HE WILL RETAIN HIS
SEAT AS AN ACTIVE TRUSTEE OF THE SYSTEM FOR THE PERIOD COMMENCING
JULY 1, 2017 AND TERMINATING JUNE 30, 2020
MR. CETLINSKI ALSO INFORMED THE BOARD THAT JOSEPH ENGLISH (WHOM HE
INTRODUCED TO THE BOARD) IS DECLARED TO BE DULY ELECTED TO THE OFFICE
OF FIRE DEPARTMENT ACTIVE TRUSTEE OF THE SYSTEM FOR THE PERIOD JULY
1, 2017 THROUGH JUNE 30, 2020, PER THE AMERICAN ARBITRATION
ASSOCIATION’S APRIL 28, 2017 FIRE DEPARTMENT ELECTION RESULTS. MR.
ENGLISH WILL SUCCEED TRUSTEE TRACY THOMAS ON THE BOARD OF
TRUSTEES.
AT THE CHAIR’S DIRECTION, THE APRIL 26, 2017 COMMUNICATION FROM POLICE
CHIEF JAMES E. CRAIG AND THE APRIL 28, 2017 ELECTION RESULTS FROM THE
AMERICAN ARBITRATION ASSOCIATION WILL BE MADE A MATTER OF RECORD
AND MESSRS. SERDA AND ENGLISH WILL CONTACT THE CITY CLERK’S OFFICE
FOR THE PURPOSE OF BEING ADMINISTERED THE OATH OF OFFICE PRIOR TO
JULY 1, 2017.
IN CONCLUSION, MR. CETLINSKI INFORMED THE BOARD THAT PROCENTIA’S
CONTRACT (THE FIRM THAT WILL PROVIDE PROFESSIONAL SERVICES FOR
SOFTWARE IMPLEMENTATION RELATING TO ENTERPRISE RESOURCE PLANNING)
IS BEING FINALIZED AND WILL BE AVAILABLE FOR APPROVAL AND EXECUTION AT
THE BOARD’S JUNE 1, 2017 MEETING.
EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR’S REPORT
EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR CYNTHIA A. THOMAS STATED THAT SHE HAD INTENDED
TO PRESENT PROCENTIA’S CONTRACT TO THE BOARD FOR APPROVAL AND
EXECUTION THIS DATE, BUT NOTED THAT IT WAS REFERRED TO POLICE AND
FIRE’S GENERAL COUNSEL AND GENERAL’S GENERAL COUNSEL FOR FINAL
REVIEW.

Communication dated September 6, 2019 (received September 19th) requesting
Commission approval of Modification No. 1 to a contract with a one-year option to
renew between the Charter County of Wayne and the Conference of Western
Wayne (CWW) (of Detroit) not to exceed $630,258 to continue to provide Youth
Assistance Programs (YAP) and services for diversion and at-risk children and
adolescents, ages 7 to 17, that will enhance positive youth development, provide
opportunities that build their confidence/self-esteem, improve their classroom
behavior and academic achievement; the County Executive advised that 15 bids
were received in response to 237 direct solicitations; after review, the Purchasing
Division recommended award to be divided among the top vendors; the Conference
of Western Wayne was recommended as one of the vendors to provide youth
services; CWW will subcontract for delinquency prevention services for at-risk with
Growth Works (of Plymouth), Vista Maria (of Dearborn Heights), the City of Garden
City YAP (of Garden City), Wayne Youth Services (of Wayne), Westland YAP (of
Westland), and Starfish Family Services (of Inkster); the term of Modification No. 1
is October 1, 2019 is through September 30, 2020; Modification No. 1 increases the
total amount of the contract from $1,575,645 to $2,205,903; the cost of the
modification will be charged to Account Nos. 292 35612 844990 (Program
Development-Juvenile) and 297 35900 844980 (Youth Services). (2016-37048DM1)

Discussion Regarding Recent GLWA Recognitions
Office of the Executive
LIFT Prize Article
Sue F. McCormick, Chief Executive Officer, introduced the newest member of the
Executive Team, Navid Mehram, who joined as GLWA's first dedicated Chief
Operating Officer of Wastewater Operations.
CEO McCormick stated that a team from GLWA and the University of Michigan
received the first ever Intelligent Water Systems Challenge Award for using data
analytics to develop a tool to maximize the use of collecting systems and
minimize combined sewer overflows in Detroit, and recognized Wendy Barrott,
who was instrumental in this project. The recognition was also recently
presented at an awards ceremony at WEFTEC 2018 in New Orleans.
CEO McCormick also stated that GLWA was awarded the "Utility of the Future
Today" Award by the Water Environment Federation, the Water Research
Foundation, NACWA, Water Reuse, and the US EPA, which was recently
presented at an awards ceremony at WEFTEC 2018. CEO McCormick recognized
Wendy Barrott, Majid Khan and Philip Kora, who were instrumental in the
start-up of the Biosolids Dryer Facility, which was used to show how GLWA has
put the principles of a Utility of the Future into action. Majid Khan and Wendy
Barrott received the award on behalf of the WRRF team.
CEO McCormick presented a GLWA award to Michelle Zdrodowski, Chief Public
Affairs Officer, who was recognized by Crains as a notable woman in marketing
and communications.

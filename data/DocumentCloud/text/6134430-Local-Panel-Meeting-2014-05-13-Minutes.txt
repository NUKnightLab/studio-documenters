STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on May 13, 2014
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, Suite S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Board; Richard Lewis, Member of the Board; Melissa
Mlynski, Executive Director; Jerald S. Post, General Counsel; and staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD April 15, 2014

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the April 15, 2014 meeting. Member Anderson moved to approve
the minutes. Member Lewis seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gierut asked Executive Director Mlynski to present the Local Panel administrative actions report for the period
of time from April 1 through April 30, 2014. She presented the report and summarized its highlights. The Board examined
the report and discussed it.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.
Case No. L-CA-14-031
Dontay Brassel/ City of Chicago

Chairman Gierut asked General Counsel Post to call the case. General Counsel Post called the case and stated that an
Executive Director dismissal issued on a charge filed by the Charging Party alleging the Respondent violated Section
10(a) of the Act. He then presented and discussed Charging Party’s appeal. After his presentation, General Counsel Post
recommended that the Board remand the case for further investigation. After discussion, Member Anderson moved to
accept General Counsel Post’s recommendation to remand the case for further investigation. Member Lewis seconded the
motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
2.
Case No. L-CB-14-014
Dontay Brassel/ International Brotherhood of Teamsters, Local 700
Chairman Gierut asked General Counsel Post to call the case. General Counsel Post stated that an Executive Director
dismissal issued on a charge filed by the Charging Party alleging that the Respondent violated Section 10(b) of the Act.
He then presented and discussed Charging Party’s appeal. After his presentation, General Counsel Post recommended that
the Board affirm the dismissal. After discussion, Member Lewis moved to affirm the Executive Director’s dismissal.
Member Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VI.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded it was
unnecessary.
VII.
ADJOURNMENT
Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board, in
Chicago, Illinois, this 12th day of June, 2014.

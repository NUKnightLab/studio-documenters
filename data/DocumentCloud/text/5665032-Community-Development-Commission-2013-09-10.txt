IV.

NEW BUSINESS
A.

LITTLE VILLAGE EAST TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 12)
Request authority for the Department of Housing and Economic Development to
enter into a negotiated sale with NeighborSpace for the disposition of the property
located at 2727 S. Troy Street in the Little Village East Tax Increment Financing
Redevelopment Project Area, to request alternative proposals, and to approve the
sale of the property to NeighborSpace if no responsive alternative proposals are
received.
Project Manager: Michele A. Rhymes
Motioned by Logan, seconded by Ward. Approved 11-0. Yeas: Buford, Davis,
Kostiner, Logan, Mooney, Newsome, Olander, Sahli, Stein, Ward and Carrott.
13-CDC-31

 B.

95TH/WESTERN TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 19)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Housing and Economic
Development’s intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Optimo Hat
Company, Inc. for the disposition of the property located at 1706 W. 95th Street in
the 95th/Western Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, to
request alternative proposals, and to approve the sale of the property to Optimo
Hat Company, Inc. if no responsive alternative proposals are received.
Project Manager: Lisa Washington
Motioned by Logan, seconded by Stein. Approved 10-0. Yeas: Davis, Kostiner,
Logan, Mooney, Newsome, Olander, Sahli, Stein, Ward and Carrott. Buford not
present.
13-CDC-32

C.

HOMAN/ARTHINGTON TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 24)
Request authority for the Department of Housing and Economic Development to
negotiate a redevelopment agreement with Uhlich Children's Advantage Network
for redevelopment of the property located at 3600 W. Fillmore Street in the
Homan/Arthington Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and to
recommend to the City Council of the City of Chicago the designation of Uhlich
Children's Advantage Network as Developer.
Project Manager: Michelle Nolan
Motioned by Ward, seconded by Olander. Approved 9-0. Yeas: Kostiner, Logan,
Mooney, Newsome, Olander, Sahli, Stein, Ward and Carrott. Buford and Davis
not present.
13-CDC-33

D.

CHICAGO/CENTRAL PARK TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 27)
Request authority for the Department of Housing and Economic Development to
negotiate a redevelopment agreement with The Children’s Place Association for
redevelopment of the property located at 3533-45 W. Chicago Avenue in the
Chicago/Central Park Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and
to recommend to the City Council of the City of Chicago the designation of The
Children’s Place Association as Developer.
Project Manager: Dinah Wayne
Motioned by Newsome, seconded by Olander. Approved 9-0. Yeas: Kostiner,
Logan, Mooney, Newsome, Olander, Sahli, Stein, Ward and Carrott. Buford and
Davis not present.
13-CDC-34

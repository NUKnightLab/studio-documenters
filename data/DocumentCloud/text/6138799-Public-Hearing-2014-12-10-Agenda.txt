Pursuant to 70 ILCS 2605/5.6 of the “Metropolitan Water Reclamation District Act”,
public notice is hereby given that a Public Hearing will be held at 10:00 a.m. on
Wednesday, December 10, 2014 in the Board Room, 100 East Erie Street, Chicago,
Illinois, to consider the proposed Budget and proposed tax levy of the Metropolitan
Water Reclamation District of Greater Chicago for the fiscal year ending December 31,
2015.
The Tentative Budget is available for review on the District’s website, www.mwrd.org
<http://www.mwrd.org> and also on file and available for inspection in the Office of the
Clerk of the District, Room 243,100 East Erie Street, Chicago, Illinois.

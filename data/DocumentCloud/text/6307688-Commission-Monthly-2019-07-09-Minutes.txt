Vacancy Tax Relief
a. The Chicago Central Area Committee (CCAC) is building a team of professionals to
collaborate on a corridor revitalization plan. Their collective skill sets include law, design,
architecture, real estate development, property title analysis and planning.
b. Data to be collected:
o Best and highest use research for vacant parcels
o Recommendations for energy and modernization efforts
o Architectural renderings for storefronts (existing businesses)
o Renderings for cohesive corridor design i.e. signage/neighborhood markers,
o Infrastructure recommendations for CDOT i.e. sidewalks, traffic calming features,
etc.

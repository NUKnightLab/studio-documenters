WHEREAS, the Board of Trustees for the Police and Fire Retirement System for the
City of Detroit (“Board”) is in receipt of an Eligible Domestic Relations Order (“EDRO”)
entered on May 9, 2018, which supplements a Judgment of Divorce entered on May 13,
2013, wherein Alternate Payee MARIA PERKINS is awarded certain rights to the
retirement benefits of Participant SYLVESTER HEWSTON, JR., who is an active
employee, and
WHEREAS, the Board has been informed that Participant SYLVESTER HEWSTON,
JR. is an active employee and is not receiving retirement benefits, and
WHEREAS, Participant has attained over 19 years of service credit, and
WHEREAS, the EDRO awards the Alternate Payee MARIA PERKINS fifty percent
(50%) of the marital portion of the Participant’s Component I Retirement Allowance, and
WHEREAS, the EDRO awards the Alternate Payee MARIA PERKINS a distribution
equal to fifty percent (50%) of the Participant’s Component I Voluntary Employee
Contributions accumulated balance as of the division date, May 13, 2013, and
WHEREAS, because the division date of May 13, 2013, precedes the establishment of
the Police and Fire Retirement System’s Component I plan effective July 1, 2014, no
Component I benefits are available to either the Participant or the Alternate Payee, and
WHEREAS, the EDRO awards the Alternate Payee MARIA PERKINS a portion of the
Participant’s Component II Pension, and
WHEREAS, the Alternate Payee MARIA PERKINS IS entitled to claim a portion of the
Participant’s Component II Pension when the Participant SYLVESTER HEWSTON, JR.
is eligible to receive such benefits, as set forth in the EDRO and SUBJECT TO
ALTERNATE PAYEE FILING AN APPLICATION FOR SAME, and
WHEREAS, the EDRO awards the Alternate Payee MARIA PERKINS a distribution of a
portion of the Participant’s Component II Annuity Savings Fund, and
WHEREAS, the Alternate Payee MARIA PERKINS IS entitled to claim a distribution of
a portion of the Participant’s Component II Annuity Savings Fund, when the Participant
is eligible to receive such benefits, as set forth in the EDRO and SUBJECT TO
ALTERNATE PAYEE FILING AN APPLICATION FOR SAME, and

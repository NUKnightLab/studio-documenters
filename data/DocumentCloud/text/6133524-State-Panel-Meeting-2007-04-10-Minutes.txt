a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gallagher asked Executive Director Brosnan to present the State Panel administrative actions
for the period of time from March 1 through March 31, 2007. He distributed the report to the Board for
review, and the Board examined it. The Executive Director summarized the report's highlights, noting
that during the month, the agency issued three certifications pursuant to representation elections, seven
certifications pursuant to majority interest petitions, nine certifications pursuant to unit clarification
petitions, and two certifications directed pursuant to unit clarification petitions. The agency also received
withdrawals in connection with five representation petitions, and the agency's Administrative Law Judges
(ALJs) issued seven Recommended Decisions and Orders (RDOs), resolving eight representation cases.
The Executive Director also noted that with regard to unfair labor practice cases, the agency issued
twenty-two complaints for hearing and eleven administrative dismissals, five deferrals, had twenty-eight
charges withdrawn, and the agency's ALJs issued RDOs in four unfair labor practice cases.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.
Case No. S-CA-04-217
Kewanee Firefighters Association, Local 513, IAFF/City of Kewanee

Chairman Gallagher began by summarizing the case, noting that the ALJ dismissing in part and

OTHER BOARD MATTERS

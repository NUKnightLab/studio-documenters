MATTERS SUBMITTED IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE LAKE MICHIGAN
AND CHICAGO LAKEFRONT PROTECTION ORDINANCE AND/OR THE
CHICAGO ZONING ORDINANCE:
1.

A Lake Michigan and Chicago Lakefront Protection Ordinance
Application No. 582 submitted by AeroScape-NYC LLC, for the
property generally located at 600 East Grand Avenue. The
Applicant proposes to construct an amusement ride consisting of a
tethered helium balloon with a gondola. The ride will contain an
approximately 576 square foot platform with ramp and winch
apparatus. The site lies within the Public Use Zone of the Lake
Michigan and Chicago Lakefront Protection District and within
Institutional Planned No. 527. There will be no other changes to
Institutional Planned No. 527. (42nd Ward) Approved 9-0, Yeahs
– Commissioners Holleb, Migala, Nelson, Pacher, Raguso,
Scudiero, Shffrin, Weinstein, and Searl

2.

A proposed Institutional Planned Development and Lake Michigan
and Chicago Lakefront Protection Application No. 569 submitted by
Northwestern Memorial Hospital for the property generally located
at 440-458 East Ohio Street and 441-459 East Ontario Street. The
Applicant is proposing to construct a 13-story building containing a
daycare center and parking garage with a total of 1,118 parking
spaces. The site is located within the Private Use Zone of the Lake
Michigan and Chicago Lakefront Protection District. (42nd Ward)
Institutional Planned Development Approved 8-0, Yeahs –
Commissioners Holleb, Migala, Nelson, Pacher, Raguso,
Scudiero, Shffrin, and Searl.
Lakefront Application # 569 Approved 8-0, Yeahs –
Commissioners Holleb, Migala, Nelson, Pacher, Raguso,
Scudiero, Shffrin, and Searl

3.

A proposed Zoning Map Amendment within the Kinzie Industrial
Corridor submitted by Thomas R. Bell for the property generally
located at 3150-3154 W. Chicago Ave. The Applicant proposes to
rezone the property from M1-2 to C3-2 to allow for retail and
commercial uses within an existing building. (27th Ward) Approved

 7-0, Yeahs – Commissioners Holleb, Nelson, Pacher, Raguso,
Scudiero, Shffrin, and Searl
4.

E.

A Lake Michigan and Chicago Lakefront Protection Ordinance
Application No. 578 submitted by the Chicago Park District for the
property generally located at 4300-4500 South Lake Shore Drive.
The Applicant proposes to rehabilitate 1,300 square feet of
concrete revetment between E. 43rd Street and E. 45th Street. The
site lies within the Public Use Zone of the Lake Michigan and
Chicago Lakefront Protection District. (4th Ward) Approved 7-0,
Yeahs – Commissioners Holleb, Nelson, Pacher, Raguso,
Scudiero, Shffrin, and Searl

MATTERS DEFERRED IN ACCORDANCE WITH SECTION 17-13-0108
OF THE CHICAGO ZONING ORDINANCE:
1.

A proposed amendment to Residential Planned Development No.
99 generally located at 3965 - 3977 South Dr. Martin Luther King
Drive, 4001 - 4025 South Dr. Martin Luther King Drive, 401 - 519
East 40th Street, 414 - 520 East 40th Street, 400 - 480 East 41st
Street, and 3964 - 4024 South Vincennes Avenue. The applicant,
Bronzeville Associates Limited Partnership, proposes to amend the
planned development to incorporate an additional parcel of land
within the planned development and is proposing a new site plan to
reconstruct 90 of the 607 existing housing units. The applicant
proposes to change the zoning of the site from Residential Planned
Development No. 99 to RM5 Residential Multi-Unit District prior to
re-establishing Residential Planned Development No. 99, as
amended. (3rd Ward) Deferral Approved by an omnibus vote

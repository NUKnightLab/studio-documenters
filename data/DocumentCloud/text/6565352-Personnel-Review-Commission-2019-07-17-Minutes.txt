Meeting Minutes
Cuyahoga County Personnel Review Commission
Wednesday, July 17, 2019
9830 Lorain Road
Cleveland, Ohio 44102
4:00 p.m.

1) CALL TO ORDER
Commissioner Southerington called the meeting to order at 4:05 p.m..
2) ROLL CALL
Commissioner Southerington asked Administrative Assistant Brett McBride to call the roll. Chairman
Boseman, Commissioner Colaluca, and Commissioner Southerington were present. No one was
absent; a quorum was determined.
3) APPROVAL OF MINUTES from June 5, 2019
Commissioner Southerington made a motion to approve the minutes from June 5, 2019;
Commissioner Colaluca seconded the motion. All were in favor; no objections.
4) PUBLIC COMMENT – Nothing submitted.
5) UNFINISHED BUSINESS
a) Updated TWL Compliance Report
Staff Attorney Sara DeCaro presented the TWL Compliance report; discussion ensued.
6) NEW BUSINESS
a) Appeals
(1) Vincenzo Lazzara – Report & Recommendation
Commissioner Southerington made a motion to accept the Recommendation for
Dismissal; Commissioner Colaluca seconded the motion. All were in favor; no objections.

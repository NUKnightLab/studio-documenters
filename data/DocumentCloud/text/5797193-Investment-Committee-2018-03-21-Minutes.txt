management, and operation of the Retirement System, and

Internal Revenue Code, applicable state law, the applicable charter and ordinance

System, and

has previously reviewed and discussed with the Board of Trustees and the Investment
Committee in closed sessions its confidential memo regarding a proposed litigation
strategy and potential resolution, and

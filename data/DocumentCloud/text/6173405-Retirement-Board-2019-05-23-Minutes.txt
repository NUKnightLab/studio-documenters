RETIREMENT BOARD RESOLUTION
The Retirement Board of the Municipal Employees’ Annuity and Benefit Fund of Chicago take this
opportunity to recognize the service of Jim Mohler into the record of the minutes of the meeting of the
Retirement Board on May 23, 2019.
WHEREAS, Jim Mohler began as Investment Accountant on May 24, 1991; and
WHEREAS during his 28 years as a Fund employee, Jim was committed to educating himself to prepare
for the various responsibilities required in his various roles; and
WHEREAS Jim provided direction for the Fund in leadership roles multiple times as Chief Investment
Officer and Executive Director; and
WHEREAS Jim played an important role during times of great challenge to the Fund: striving to achieve
adequate funding for the Fund, providing leadership in the administration of the Fund, and investing during
volatile market conditions;
NOW THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED that the Retirement Board of the Municipal Employees’ Annuity and
Benefit Fund of Chicago recognizes Jim Mohler’s steadfast dedication to his role as Executive Director and
his commitment to the financial health of the Fund on behalf of its members. The Retirement Board thanks
Jim for his unwavering support of the Fund and wishes him much success in his future endeavors.

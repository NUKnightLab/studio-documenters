Agenda item:

Determination of Clarity – Dan Paletko, Mayor, City of
Dearborn Heights
Chair Burton asked Assistant Corporation Counsel Janet AndersonDavis to remind the commission of its role in making a determination
1

 of clarity. Assistant Corporation Counsel Anderson-Davis reminded
the commission of its role and duties for a determination of clarity.
Ms. Anderson-Davis read the statute regarding the role of the
Election Commission in regards to recall petitions and stated that
there was litigation before Judge Levy in Federal Court in which
she issued an opinion regarding recall petitions.
Ms. Anderson-Davis summarized the commission’s role when
reviewing petitions and what the commission should consider. Ms.
Anderson-Davis also advised the commission that a couple officers
are in the first year of a four year term and under statute they can’t
be recalled at this point. Ms. Redmond indicated Mayor Paletko,
Clerk Prusiewicz, and Councilmember Constan are in the first year
of their term.
Board action on agenda item: Chair Burton requested a motion to
dismiss 5A-1, 5A-2, and 5A-3.
Reason for recall as printed on the Recall Petition 5A-1 for Dan
Paletko:
“Unethical conduct, for knowingly withholding truthful and factual
information of extreme importance, to undermine and election,
misleading and lying to the residences of Dearborn Heights the
people he represents.”
Reason for recall as printed on the Recall Petition 5A-2 for Walter
Prusiewicz:
“Unethical conduct, for knowingly withholding truthful and factual
information of extreme importance, to undermine and election,
misleading and lying to the residences of Dearborn Heights the
people he represents.”
Reason for recall as printed on the Recall Petition 5A-3 for Bob
Constan:
“Unethical conduct, for knowingly withholding truthful and factual
information of extreme importance, to undermine and election,
misleading and lying to the residences of Dearborn Heights the
people he represents.”
Member Sabree, duly supported by Member Garrett, moved to
dismiss items 5A-1, 5A-2 and 5A-3. The motion was adopted
unanimously by a voice vote.

 Agenda item:

Determination of Clarity – Ray Muscat, City Council, City of
Dearborn Heights
Reason for recall as printed on the Recall Petition 5A-4 for Ray
Muscat:
“Unethical conduct, for knowingly withholding truthful and factual
information of extreme importance, to undermine and election,
misleading and lying to the residences of Dearborn Heights the
people he represents.”
Member Garrett, duly supported by Member Sabree, moved that
item 5A-4 be rejected for lack of clarity. Chair Burton asked the
petitioner if there was anything he would like to state in support of
the recall language. Mr. Camilleri addressed the commission and
made a statement in support of his petition.
Member Garrett, duly supported by Member Sabree, moved that
item 5A-4 be rejected for lack of clarity. The motion was adopted
unanimously by a voice vote.

Agenda item:

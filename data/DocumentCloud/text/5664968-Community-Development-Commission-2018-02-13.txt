 III.

NEW BUSINESS
A.

CHICAGO/CENTRAL PARK TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 27)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Planning and Development’s
intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Clark Street Real Estate, LLC for the
disposition of the property located at 408 N. Homan Avenue in the
Chicago/Central Park Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, to
request alternative proposals, and to approve the sale of the property to Clark
Street Real Estate, LLC if no responsive alternative proposals are received.
Project Manager: Michael Parella
Motioned by Griggs, seconded by Alphonse. Approved 9-0. Yeas: Alphonse,
Buford, Davis, Griggs, Newsome, Reifman, Rhodes, Ward and Perez.
18-CDC-2

B.

NORTH PULLMAN TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 9)
Request authority for the Department of Planning and Development to negotiate a
redevelopment agreement with Chicago Neighborhood Initiatives for
redevelopment of the property located at 10700 S. Doty Avenue in the North
Pullman Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and to
recommend to the City Council of the City of Chicago the designation of Chicago
Neighborhood Initiatives as Developer.
Project Manager: Chip Hastings
Motioned by Newsome, seconded by Ward. Approved 9-0. Yeas: Alphonse,
Buford, Davis, Griggs, Newsome, Reifman, Rhodes, Ward and Perez.
18-CDC-3

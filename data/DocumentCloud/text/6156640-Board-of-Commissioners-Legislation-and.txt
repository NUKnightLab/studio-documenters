NOTICE

There will be a meeting of the Legislation, Intergovernmental & Veterans Relations
Committee of the Board of Commissioners of Cook County on Monday, February 2, 2009 at
the hour of 10:00 A.M. in the Board Room, Room 569, County Building, 118 North Clark Street,
Chicago, Illinois to consider the following:
298138

ADMINISTRATIVE HEARING DIRECTOR (APPOINTMENT). Transmitting a
Communication, dated January 6, 2009 from Todd H. Stroger, President, Cook
County Board of Commissioners:
Pursuant to Chapter 2 Administration, Section 2-901(b) of the Cook County Code
of Ordinances, I hereby appoint JACK WEINRAUCH to the position of
Administrative Hearing Director effective immediately.
I submit this communication for your approval.
*Referred to the Committee on Legislation, Intergovernmental & Veterans
Relations on January 13, 2009.

**

PURSUANT TO RESOLUTION 09-R-63, approved by the County Board on
January 13, 2009, a meeting to discuss the Resolution, in relevant part:
NOW, THEREORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that, pursuant to Resolution 09-R-63,
the Cook County Board of Commissioners does hereby request that a meeting of
the Legislation, Intergovernmental & Veterans Relations Committee be convened
at a time to be determined on Monday, February 2, 2009 to discuss the status of
pending legislation, including the Federal Economic Stimulus Plan and the State
of Illinois Capital Program; and
NOW, THEREORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that, pursuant to Resolution 09-R-63,
Mr. Derek Blaida of the President’s Office governmental relations staff and the
County’s hired lobbyists appear before the Committee Meeting an be prepared to
update the Committee on legislation pending before the General Assembly and
the U.S. Congress that effects Cook County’s services and operations and report
on their 2008 activities.
**Approved by the County Board on January 13, 2009.

Reso. 2019-100 Continued Public Hearing on Charges
Highland Park Public Hearing Comments RE Proposed FY 2020
Charges
Chairman Baker opened the Continued Public Hearings regarding the Proposed
Fiscal Year 2020 and Fiscal Year 2021 Biennial Budget and the Proposed Fiscal
Year 2020 Water and Sewerage Service Charges at 2:20 p.m., and asked if there
was anyone in the audience that would like to make comments regarding the
Biennial Budget, Water and Sewerage Service Charges, and/or the Capital
Improvement Plan.
William M. Wolfson, Chief Administrative and Compliance Officer, stated that
written comments have been received from the City of Highland Park regarding
proposed FY 2020 Charges.
There being no comments by the public, Chairman Baker adjourned today's
Continued Public Hearings at 2:27 p.m., and advised the public that written
comments will continue to be accepted until the Budget, Charges and CIP have
been approved by the Board of Directors.

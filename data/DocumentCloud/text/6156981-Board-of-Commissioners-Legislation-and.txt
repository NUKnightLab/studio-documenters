NOTICE AND AGENDA
There will be a meeting of the Committee or Subcommittee of the Board of Commissioners of Cook County at the
date, time and location listed above to consider the following:
PUBLIC TESTIMONY
Authorization as a public speaker shall only be granted to those individuals who have submitted in writing, their
name, address, subject matter, and organization (if any) to the Secretary 24 hours in advance of the meeting. Duly
authorized public speakers shall be called upon to deliver testimony at a time specified in the meeting agenda.
Authorized public speakers who are not present during the specified time for public testimony will forfeit their
allotted time to speak at the meeting. Public testimony must be germane to a specific item(s) on the meeting agenda,
and the testimony must not exceed three minutes; the Secretary will keep track of the time and advise when the time
for public testimony has expired. Persons authorized to provide public testimony shall not use vulgar, abusive, or
otherwise inappropriate language when addressing the Board; failure to act appropriately; failure to speak to an item
that is germane to the meeting, or failure to adhere to the time requirements may result in expulsion from the
meeting and/or disqualify the person from providing future testimony.

PROPOSED APPOINTMENT
Appointee(s): Amy Patton Campanelli
Position: Cook County Public Defender
Department/Board/Commission: Office of the Cook County Public Defender
Effective date: 4/1/2015
Expiration date: 3/31/2021

 Summary: Amy Patton Campanelli is hereby appointed to serve as the Cook County Public Defender for a six
year term beginning 4/1/2015 and ending 3/31/2021. Ms. Campanelli will assume the position of Public Defender
upon approval by the Board of Commissioners and following the expiration of Mr. Abishi
Carrando
"A.C."
Cunningham, Jr.’s appointed term. Mr. Cunningham’s appointment expires March 31, 2015 and all of Cook
County thanks him for his service.
Ms. Campanelli brings 23 years of service in the Office of the Cook County Public Defender, and nearly 30 years
of legal experience, to the position of Cook County Public Defender. She currently serves as the Deputy
of
Suburban Operations for the Office of the Cook County Public Defender. In that position she manages more than
150 assistant public defenders, supervisors, chiefs, support staff and investigators and
oversees
all
specialty/therapeutic courts. Ms. Campanelli previously served as an Assistant Cook County Public Defender
before entering management in the Office in 2003. She has experience in all divisions of the Office, including
Juvenile, Felony Trial, Capital, and Homicide cases. Her extensive legal and administrative experience will be an
asset to the administration of the Office of Cook County Public Defender.
Ms. Campanelli satisfies all of the qualifications for the position of Public Defender as set forth in the Counties
Code (55 ILCS 5/3-4004.2). This appointment is recommended for your approval.

Ethnicity.
WatchList

November benefit payments. Seconded by Trustee Thompson.

Thompson. Nays-None.

LEGAL UPDATE
Ms. Burns discussed the result of the litigation between the Firemen’s Annuity and Benefit Fund of Chicago
and the City of Chicago regarding the utilization of the “intercept” to capture unpaid employers contributions.

accounts.

in the calculation of retirement benefits. Ms. Burns gave a history on this matter. After discussion amongst
the Board, it was decided that this would be further discussed at the Regular November Board Meeting.
Ms. Burns also touched on the proposed legislation pertaining to the consolidation of Article 3 and 4 Funds.
Fund Counsel notified the Board that they could find the status report on pending legal matters in the
Board’s materials.

OPEN SESSION

ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
Chicago, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
On Tuesday, August 25, 2015 a Regular Meeting - Video of the Illinois Gaming Board
(“Board”) was held on the 5th floor at 160 N. LaSalle at the Michael J. Bilandic Building,
Chicago, Illinois. The Open Session on Video Gaming started at approximately 9:24 A.M.
Present were Chairman Don Tracy, Members Lee Gould, Hector Alejandre and Member Dunn.
APPROVAL OF MINUTES – Open Session
Member Alejandre moved that the Board approve the open session minutes of its Regular
Meeting of July 23, 2015. Member Gould seconded the motion. The Board approved the
motion unanimously by roll call vote.

Licensed establishments that failed to disclose information to the Board
Effingham Knights of Columbus Building Corporation d/b/a Effingham Knights of
Columbus
Based on staff’s investigation and recommendation, Member Dunn moved to issue a
Disciplinary Complaint to revoke the License of the Effingham Knights of
Columbus for violating Board Rule 210, said action to take effect 21 days from the
date of service of the Complaint unless an Answer is properly filed or fine of $1,000
is paid within that time frame. Member Alejandre seconded the motion. The Board
approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
Sit-N-Bull 2 Incorporated d/b/a Sit-N-Bull
Based on staff’s investigation and recommendation, Member Gould moved to
issue a Disciplinary Complaint to revoke the establishment license of Sit-N-Bull 2
Incorporated d/b/a Sit-N-Bull due to owner Penny Berg’s criminal convictions and
her failure to disclose those convictions, said action to take effect 21 days from the
date of service of the Complaint unless an Answer is properly filed within that
timeframe. Member Dunn seconded the motion. The Board approved the
motion unanimously by roll call vote.

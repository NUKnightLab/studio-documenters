700-2019

AN EMERGENCY ORDINANCE Giving consent of the City of Cleveland to
the Director of Transportation of the State of Ohio for constructing the
complete streets project along the East 93rd Street/Woodhill Road/East
105th Street corridor; to apply for and accept any gifts or grants for this
purpose from any public or private entity; authorizing professional
services, agreements with public and private entities, and any relative
agreements; authorizing the Commissioner of Purchases and Supplies to
acquire, accept, and record for right-of-way purposes any real property
and easements necessary to make the improvement; and causing
payment to ODOT of Cleveland’s share of the improvement.

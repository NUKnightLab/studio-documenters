STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on March 15, 2013
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, Suite S-400, Chicago, IL

II.
PRESENT
Robert M. Gierut, Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Board; Richard Lewis, Member of the Board;
Melissa Mlynski, Executive Director (participated by telephone-conference); Jerald S. Post, General Counsel;
and staff members.
III.
APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD February 7, 2013
Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the February 7, 2013 meeting. Member Anderson moved to
approve the minutes. Member Lewis seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gierut asked Executive Director Mlynski to present the Local Panel administrative actions report for the
period of time from February 1 through February 28, 2013. She presented the report and summarized its highlights.
The Board examined the report and discussed it.
V.

LEGISLATIVE/FISCAL REPORTS

Executive Director Mlynski reported various legislative issues. She then presented the fiscal report, reviewing in
detail the general ledger sheet and the disbursement record. The Board then discussed and questioned various
aspects of the fiscal report.
VI.

BOARD ACTIONS

1.
Case No. L-CA-10-076
International Brotherhood of Teamsters, Local 700/ County of Cook and Sheriff of Cook County
Chairman Gierut called the case noting that no exceptions were filed for Administrative Law Judge Eileen Bell’s
Recommended Decision and Order (RDO) and asked whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own
motion. The Board declined to do so.
2.
Case No. L-CA-11-007
Amalgamated Transit Union, Local 241/ Chicago Transit Authority
Chairman Gierut recused himself. General Counsel Post called the case noting that no exceptions were filed for
ALJ Stevens’ RDO and asked whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own motion. The Board
declined to do so.
3.
Case No. L-CA-12-022
Service Employees International Union, Local 73 / County of Cook
Chairman Gierut called the case noting that no exceptions were filed for ALJ Hamburg-Gal’s RDO and asked
whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own motion. The Board declined to do so.

 4.
Case No. L-CB-13-009
Beverly Boyd/ American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31
Chairman Gierut called the case. General Counsel Post then presented the case by stating that on December 19,
2012 the Executive Director dismissed the charge and an appeal had been filed. General Counsel Post stated that
the Charging Party alleged that the Union engaged in unfair labor practices in violation of Section 10(b)(1) of the
Illinois Public Labor Relations Act. General Counsel Post reviewed the issues raised by the Charging Party’s appeal
and then recommended that the Board affirm the Executive Director’s dismissal. After discussion, Member Lewis
moved to accept the dismissal. Member Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a
voice vote.
VII.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded that it was
unnecessary at this time.
VIII. ADJOURNMENT
Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the
Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations
Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 16th day of April, 2013.

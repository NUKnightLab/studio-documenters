2.

Communication dated October 16, 2018 (received October 26th) requesting
Commission approval of a proposed amendment to the 2018-2019
Appropriations Ordinance (Enrolled Ordinance No. 2018-683) in accordance with
Budget Adjustment No. 2019-35-009 decertifying revenue in the amount of
$30,000 in the Jail Commissary Fund (575); the County Executive advises that
the budget adjustment serves to decertify funds that were anticipated to be
received from the sale of commissary items provided to jail inmates and will align
the budget with the negotiated contractual agreement. (2019-35-009)

3.

Communication dated October 17, 2018 (received October 26th) requesting
Commission approval of a proposed amendment to the 2017-2018
Appropriations Ordinance (Enrolled Ordinance No. 2017-635) in accordance with
Budget Adjustment No. 2018-35-275 decertifying revenue in the amount
$320,000 in the General Fund (101) and reallocating expenditures in the amount
of $275,000 in the Community Corrections Fund (281); the County Executive
advises that the budget adjustment for the Department of Health, Veterans and
Community Wellness serves to provide sufficient funding for professional
services contracts within the Community Corrections program which provides
diversion services for adult offenders within Wayne County. (2018-35-275)

4.

Communication dated September 11, 2018 (received October 26th) requesting
Commission approval of a proposed amendment to the 2017-2018
Appropriations Ordinance (Enrolled Ordinance No. 2017-635) in accordance with
Budget Adjustment No. 2018-35-263 certifying additional revenue in the amount
of $57,683 in the County Health Fund (221); the County Executive advises that
the Department of Health, Veterans and Community Wellness has received a
grant modification from the Health Resources Services Administration (HRSA) for
the purpose of funding the Wayne County Healthy Communities comprehensive
Federally Qualified Health Centers (FQHC); the purpose of this budget
adjustment is to certify these additional grant dollars. (2018-35-263)

5.

Communication dated October 29, 2018 (received November 5th) requesting
Commission approval of a proposed amendment to the 2017-2018
Appropriations Ordinance (Enrolled Ordinance No. 2017-635) in accordance with
Budget Adjustment No. 2018-35-281 certifying revenue in the amount of $3,727
in the HCS-Nutrition Fund (223); the County Executive advises that the budget
adjustment for Senior Services serves to add funds provided by The Senior
Alliance in the amended budget for Nutrition Services in order to provide
sufficient funding to address financial operational deficiencies in assorted
accounts. (2018-35-281)

6.

Communication dated July 25, 2018 (received October 26th) requesting
Commission approval of a proposed amendment to the 2017-2018
Appropriations Ordinance (Enrolled Ordinance No. 2017-635) in accordance with
Budget Adjustment No. 2018-35-233 certifying revenue in the amount of $8,571
in the Law Enforcement Fund (266); the County Executive advises that the Office
of the Wayne County Sheriff proposes to certify funds for the Justice Assistance
Grants (JAG) relating to grant program years 2014 and 2015; this certification
represents available earned interest in fiscal year 2018 for JAG programming.
(2018-35-233)

Communication dated July 25, 2018 (received October 26th) requesting

9.

Communication dated October 12, 2018 (received October 26th) requesting
Commission approval of a proposed amendment to the 2018-2019
Appropriations Ordinance (Enrolled Ordinance No. 2018-683) in accordance with
Budget Adjustment No. 2019-35-004 reallocating expenditures in the amount of
$57,600 in the General Fund (101); the County Executive advises that the budget
adjustment requested by the Register of Deeds Office serves to reallocate
appropriations from one account to another to provide sufficient appropriation for
the purchase of 3 wireless access points as well as a new server, software,
licenses and camera to support security/surveillance system; this budget
adjustment will not increase nor decrease 2018-2019 total appropriation sources
and uses. (2019-35-004)

MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL MEETING
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
JANUARY 14, 2000
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS

NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
A Special Meeting of the Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”) was held on January 14, 2000,
in room #S-304 of the State of Illinois Building, Chicago, Illinois, pursuant to the Illinois
Open Meetings Act, 5 ILCS 120.
The following Board member was present, Gregory C. Jones, Chairman. Members
Joseph Lamendella, Stuart Levine and Staci Yandle were present telephonically.
Also in attendance was Administrator Sergio E. Acosta.
The meeting was convened at 11:00 a.m. Member Lamendella moved that the Board
retire to Closed Session pursuant to Section 2(c), paragraph (1) of the Open
Meetings Act to discuss the following subject matter:
1. Personnel matters
Member Levine seconded the motion. The Board adopted the motion by unanimous
consent.
The Board adjourned at 12:40 p.m.
Respectfully submitted,

Susan O. Weber
Secretary to the Board

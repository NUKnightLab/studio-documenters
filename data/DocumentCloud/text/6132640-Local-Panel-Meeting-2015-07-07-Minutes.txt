BOARD ACTIONS
1.
Case No. L-CA-13-055
International Brotherhood of Teamsters, Local 700/County of Cook, Sheriff of Cook County

Chairman Gierut asked General Counsel Nelson to call and present the case to the Board. General Counsel Nelson stated
that Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) Kelly Coyle issued a Recommended Decision and Order (RDO) finding that the
Employer violated the Act when it issued two General Orders without notice and without opportunity to bargain. She
then discussed the exceptions and response that were filed. After her presentation, General Counsel Nelson recommended
that the Board reject the ALJ’s RDO. After discussion, Member Anderson moved to reject the RDO. Chairman Gierut
seconded the motion, and the Board passed it by a voice vote. Member Lewis dissented in part.
2.
Case No. L-CA-14-016
International Brotherhood of Teamsters, Local 700/County of Cook, Sheriff of Cook County
Chairman Gierut asked General Counsel Nelson to call and present the case to the Board. General Counsel Nelson stated
that ALJ Deena Sanceda issued a RDO finding that the Respondent violated Sections 10(a)(4) and (1) of the Act.
She then discussed the exceptions and response that were filed. After her presentation, General Counsel Nelson
recommended that the Board accept the ALJ’s RDO. After discussion, Member Lewis moved to affirm the RDO.
Member Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

Re: ICG Leaseback Fund I, LLC Mezzanine Loan
Resolution Moved By: Trustee Hatty – Supported By: Trustee Nickleberry
WHEREAS, the Board of Trustees is in receipt of a confidential memorandum from
Special Counsel Sean Gallagher of Clark Hill PLC concerning the status of the
Retirement System’s “mezzanine” loan, along with the Police and Fire Retirement
System of the City of Detroit, in the ICG Leaseback Fund I, LLC investment
(“Leaseback”); and
WHEREAS, AXA Equitable Life Insurance Company (“AXA”), the first priority lender in
Leaseback, has requested, through counsel, that both the Retirement Systems, in
addition to the borrower, ICG Leaseback Fund I, LLC (the “Borrower”), authorize
release of escrowed lease payments; direct use of $7.5 million of those escrowed lease
payments for use as the “Tenant Reserve Requirement” under the loan documents; and
provide wire instructions for transferring the remainder of the escrowed funds to the
Retirement Systems; and
WHEREAS, the Board desires to release and receive its portion of escrowed lease
payment funds from Leaseback; and
WHEREAS, the Board has been advised by the Executive Director and General
Counsel in this matter, and
WHEREAS, the Board has considered this matter in open session;
NOW THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED, that the Board, expressly contingent on similar
authorization by the Police and Fire Retirement System of the City of Detroit, authorizes
release of escrowed lease payments related to Leaseback (i) to first satisfy the Tenant
Reserve Requirement under the Leaseback loan documentation; (ii) to next release
remaining funds to the Retirement Systems; and (iii) authorizes the Executive Director,

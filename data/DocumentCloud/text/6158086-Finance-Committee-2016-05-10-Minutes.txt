16-0191
Presented by: ARNOLD RANDALL, GENERAL SUPERINTENDENT
PROPOSED BID RECOMMENDATION
Department: Planning and Development
Request: Authorization for the Forest Preserves District of Cook County (the “Preserves”) to enter into a
contract with Matthew Paving, Inc., Chicago, Illinois for various trail crossings improvements on the Salt Creek
Bicycle Trail, North Branch Bicycle Trail, and Tinley Creek Bicycle Trail.
Reason: Contract 13-80-02-14C1, Trail Crossings Improvements. The work includes, but is not limited to,
various trail crossings improvements at three highest priority crossings at Glenview Road and Harms Road on
the North Branch Bicycle Trail, and at 31st Street & Edgewood Ave and LaGrange Road & Cermak Road on the
Salt Creek Bicycle Trail, as well as signage and pavement markings improvements on the Tinley Creek Bicycle
Trail. Improvements include the realignment of the 10’ wide bicycle trail at various intersections, traffic signal
modifications, updating bicycle and pedestrian signing and advanced signing, updating pavement markings and
other related work as specified in the plans & specifications.

STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on July 12, 2016
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 N LaSalle Street, 4th Floor, Room S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Board; Richard Lewis, Member of the Board; Kathryn
Zeledon Nelson, General Counsel; Melissa Mlynski, Executive Director; Sarah Kerley, Deputy General Counsel; Anna
Hamburg-Gal, Associate General Counsel; and staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD ON June 9, 2016

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the June 9, 2016 meeting. Member Anderson moved to approve
the minutes. Member Lewis seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR’S REPORT

Executive Director Mlynski presented the Executive Director report. The Chair and Members discussed the report.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.

Case No. L-CA-13-035
Service Employees International Union, Local 73/County of Cook

General Counsel Nelson called the case, noted that no party filed exceptions to Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) Kerley’s
Recommended Decision and Order (RDO) and asked whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own motion.
The Board declined to do so.
2.

Case No. L-CB-16-001
Debra Larkin/Amalgamated Transit Union, Local 241

General Counsel Nelson called the case, noted that no party filed exceptions to ALJ Coyle’s RDO and asked whether the
Board wished to review the decision on its own motion. The Board declined to do so.
VI.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded it was
unnecessary.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board, in
Chicago, Illinois, this 9th day of August, 2016.

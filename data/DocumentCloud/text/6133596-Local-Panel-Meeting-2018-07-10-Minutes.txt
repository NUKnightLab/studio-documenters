STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on July 10, 2018
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman of the Local Panel; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Local Panel; Angela C. Thomas,
Member of the Local Panel; Kimberly Stevens, Executive Director; Helen J. Kim, General Counsel; Anna Hamburg-Gal,
Associate General Counsel; and staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD June 5, 2018

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the June 5, 2018 meeting. Member Anderson moved to approve the
minutes. Member Thomas seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

REVIEW OF PROPOSED WRITTEN DECISIONS AND ORDERS
1.
Case No. L-RC-16-031
American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31/City of Chicago

General Counsel Kim called the case. Member Thomas made a motion to approve the proposed written decision. Member
Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
2.
Case No. L-CA-18-039
April D. Glenn/Chicago Transit Authority
General Counsel Kim called the case. Member Anderson made a motion to approve the proposed written decision.
Member Thomas seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.
Case No. L-UC-18-002
Local 220, Chicago Joint Board, Retail Wholesale and Department Store Union/County of Cook, Health and
Hospital System

General Counsel Kim called the case, and noted that no party filed exceptions to Administrative Law Judge Sanceda’s
Recommended Decision and Order and asked whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own motion. The
Board declined to do so
2.
Case No. L-CA-16-056
Annie Burton/Chicago Transit Authority
General Counsel Kim called and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member Thomas moved to
remand the case for further investigation. Member Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously,
by a voice vote.
3.
Case No. L-CA-18-042
John J. Kugler/Chicago Park District
General Counsel Kim called and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member Anderson moved
to remand the case for further investigation. Member Thomas seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously,
by a voice vote.

 VI.

PUBLIC COMMENT

No members of the public were present.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board, in
Chicago, Illinois, this 7th day of August 2018.

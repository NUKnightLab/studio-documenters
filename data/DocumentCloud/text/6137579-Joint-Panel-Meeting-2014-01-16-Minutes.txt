STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
JOINT PANEL
Minutes of the Joint Panel Meeting
Held on January 16, 2014

I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:30 A.M.â€”160 North LaSalle Street, Suite S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

John Hartnett, Joint Panel Chairman; Paul S. Besson, Member of the State Panel; James Q. Brennwald,
Member of the State Panel; Michael Coli, Member of the State Panel; Albert Washington, Member of the State
Panel; Robert M. Gierut, Local Panel Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Local Panel; Richard
Lewis, Member of the Local Panel; Melissa Mlynski, Executive Director; Jerald S. Post, General Counsel; and
staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES

Chairman Hartnett called for approval of the minutes of the November 5, 2013 meeting. Member Brennwald
moved to approve the minutes. Member Washington seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by
a voice vote.
IV.

EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR REPORTS

Executive Director Mlynski presented the fiscal, legislative and mediation reports. The Chair and Members
discussed the reports at length.
V.

LITIGATION REPORT

Deputy General Counsel Kerley presented and discussed the litigation report. The Chair and Members
discussed the report at length.
VI.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

Member Coli made a motion to leave open session. Member Brennwald seconded the motion, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote. Member Besson made a motion to go into executive session to discuss
personnel matters pursuant to 5 ILCS 120/2(c)(1). Local Chairman Gierut seconded the motion, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote. Subsequently, at the end of the discussion, Member Coli made a
motion to leave executive session. Member Besson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it
unanimously, by a voice vote. Member Anderson then made a motion to return to open session. Member
Washington seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Hartnett called for a motion to adjourn. Chairman Gierut made a motion to adjourn the meeting,
and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Joint Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations
Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 11th day of March, 2014.

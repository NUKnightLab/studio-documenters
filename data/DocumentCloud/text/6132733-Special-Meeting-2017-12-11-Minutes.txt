Motion carried.
The Board moved into open session at 11:59 a.m. on a motion by Trustee McMahon.
Seconded by Trustee Johnson. Carried by a voice vote.
In open session the Board directed Fund Counsel to notify Counsel for the Plaintiffs in
the Underwood matter that the Fund declined his request for the Fund to join Plaintiffs in
their Motion for Reconsideration of the Illinois Supreme Court’s denial of Plaintiffs’
Petition for Leave to Appeal the Appellate Court’s decision.
There being no further business to come before the Board, Trustee Johnson made a
motion to adjourn at 12:00 p.m. Seconded by Trustee McMahon.
Carried: Ayes – Trustee Johnson, Trustee Keane and Trustee McMahon. Nays - None

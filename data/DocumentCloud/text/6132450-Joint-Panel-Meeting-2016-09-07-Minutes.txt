STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
JOINT PANEL
Minutes of the Joint Panel Meeting
Held on September 9, 2016
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 12:30 A.M.—via videoconference at 160 N LaSalle Street, N703,
Chicago, Illinois and One Natural Resources Way, Rock River Room, Springfield, IL.

II.

PRESENT

John Hartnett, Chairman of State Panel; Michael Coli, Member of the State Panel; John Samolis, Member of
the State Panel; Keith Snyder, Member of the State Panel; Albert Washington, Member of the State Panel;
Melissa Mlynski, Executive Director; Kathryn Zeledon Nelson, General Counsel; Sarah Kerley, Deputy
General Counsel; present in Springfield. Robert M. Gierut, Local Panel Chairman; Charles E. Anderson,
Member of the Local Panel; Richard Lewis, Member of the Local Panel; Anna Hamburg-Gal, Associate
General Counsel; and staff members in Chicago.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD AUGUST 9, 2016

Chairman Hartnett called for approval of the minutes of the August 9, 2016, meeting. Member Washington
moved to approve the minutes. Member Samolis seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a
voice vote.
IV.

EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR’S REPORTS

Executive Director Mlynski presented her reports. The Chair and Members discussed the reports.
V.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

Local Chairman Gierut made a motion to leave open session. Member Snyder seconded the motion, and the
Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote. Member Anderson made a motion to go into executive session
to discuss personnel matters pursuant to Section 2(c)(1), 5 ILCS 120/2(c)(1). Subsequently, at the end of the
discussion, Member Samolis made a motion to leave executive session. Member Anderson seconded the
motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote. Member Anderson then made a motion to
return to open session. Member Washington seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a
voice vote.
VI.

JOINT BOARD ACTIONS

The Board announced the resignation of General Counsel Nelson. Member Coli moved to accept the General
Counsel Nelson’s resignation. Member Washington seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by
a voice vote.
VII.

PUBLIC COMMENT

No members of the public were present.
VIII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Hartnett called for a motion to adjourn. Member Coli made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and
the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Joint Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations
Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 4th day of October, 2016.

MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL MEETING
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
NOVEMBER 14, 1997
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS

NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
A Special Meeting of the Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”) was held on November 14,
1997, in room #S-304 of the State of Illinois Building, Chicago, Illinois, pursuant to the
Illinois Open Meetings Act, 5 ILCS 120.
The following Board members participated via telephonic conferencing: Chairman J.
Thomas Johnson and Members William B. Browder and Robert F. Vickrey.
The meeting was convened at 9:15 a.m. Member Browder moved that the Board
retire to Closed Session pursuant to Section 2(c), paragraph (14) of the Open
Meetings Act to discuss Investigatory Matters.
Member Vickrey seconded the motion. The Board adopted the motion by unanimous
consent.
The Board adjourned at 10:28 a.m.
Respectfully Submitted,

Christine I. Takada
Executive Assistant to the Administrator

(WARD 16)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Planning and Development’s
intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Englewood Square, LP for the

Project Manager: Mary Bonome

Cepeda, Duque, Mooney, Newsome, Perez, Sercye, Ward and Carrott.
14-CDC-27

B.

WESTERN/OGDEN TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 25)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Planning and Development’s
intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Casa Queretaro, LP and The
Resurrection Project for the disposition of the property located at the northwest
corner of 17th Street and Damen Avenue in the Western/Ogden Tax Increment
Financing Redevelopment Project Area, to request alternative proposals and to
approve the sale of the property to Casa Queretaro, LP and The Resurrection
Project if no responsive alternative proposals are received; and request authority
for the Department of Planning and Development to negotiate a redevelopment
agreement with Casa Queretaro, LP and The Resurrection Project for
redevelopment of the property and to recommend to the City Council of the City
of Chicago the designation of Casa Queretaro, LP and The Resurrection Project as
Developer if no responsive alternative proposals are received.
Project Manager: Sylvia A. Ruffin
Motioned by Cepada, seconded by Duque. Approved 9-0. Yeas: Buford, Cepeda,
Duque, Mooney, Newsome, Perez, Sercye, Ward and Carrott.

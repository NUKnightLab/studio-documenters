3. October 23, 2012 Investment Committee Recommendations
It was moved by Trustee Janura and seconded by Trustee McFadden that the
Board approve the recommendation of the Investment Committee made at their
meeting on October 23, 2012, in fulfillment of the 6% allocation to private equity,

due diligence and successful contract negotiations. It is further moved that the
Fund’s Investment Staff be authorized to take all action reasonably necessary to
effectuate the foregoing including the execution and delivery of related written
agreements by the Executive Director on behalf of the Fund.

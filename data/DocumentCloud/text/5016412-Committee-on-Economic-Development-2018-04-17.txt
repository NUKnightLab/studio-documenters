2.

Communication dated February 23, 2018 (received April 3rd) requesting
Commission approval of a sub-recipient agreement between the Charter County
of Wayne and the Detroit Recovery Project in the amount of $120,000 for the
“Tenant Based Recovery Rental Supportive Housing” (TBRRSH) program to
provide rental assistance to low- to moderate-income people pursuant to the
Federal HOME Investments Partnership Act; the County Executive advises that
the goal of the TBRRSH program is to provide 15 adults, who have been
released within the previous 24 months from the Wayne County Dickerson Jail,
decent and affordable housing in Wayne County if they had an existing diagnosis
of substance abuse disorder, mental health disorder, homelessness or any
combination of the disorders herein listed; the term of the agreement is from July
1, 2016 through December 31, 2018; the revenue from the agreement will be
deposited into Account No. 250 69110 954215 (HOME). (2018-20-033)

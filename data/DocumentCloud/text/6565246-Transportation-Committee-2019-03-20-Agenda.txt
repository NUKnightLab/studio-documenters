143-2019

AN EMERGENCY ORDINANCE To amend the title and Sections 1, 2, and
4 of Ordinance No. 700-16, passed August 10, 2016, as amended; and to

the public improvement of rehabilitating the storm and sanitary sewer
systems in and around the IX Center and discharge centers and
constructing infrastructure improvements needed for the rehabilitation and

meeting. Duly authorized public speakers shall be called upon to deliver testimony at a time speci?ed in

testimony will forfeit their allotted time to speak at the meeting. Public testimony must be germane to a
speci?c item(s) on the meeting agenda, and the testimony must not exceed three minutes; the Secretary Will

BOARD ACTIONS
1.

Case No. L-CA-12-062
Service Employees International Union, Local 73/County of Cook

Chairman Gierut asked General Counsel Nelson to call the case to the Board. General Counsel Nelson called the case then
asked Associate General Counsel Anna Hamburg-Gal to present the case to the Board. Counsel Hamburg-Gal stated that
Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) Katherine Vanek issued a Recommended Decision and Order (RDO) dismissing the
charge filed by the Charging Party alleging that the Respondent violated Sections 10(a)(4) and (1) of the Illinois Public
Labor Relations Act. After her presentation, Associate General Counsel Hamburg-Gal recommended that the Board
affirm the RDO with modifications. After discussion, Member Anderson moved to accept the RDO with modifications.
Member Lewis seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

2.

Case No. L-CA-14-045
Service Employees International Union, Local 73/County of Cook

General Counsel Nelson called the case, noted that no party filed exceptions to ALJ Deena Sanceda’s RDO and asked
whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own motion. The Board declined to do so.

Summary: The US Green Building Council requires LEED certification applicants to work with its
licensee, Green Business Certification Inc. to review project specifics for compliance with and
determination of the level of LEED certification. Swallow Cliff Woods Visitor Center is anticipated to
receive Silver Level Certification and Rolling Knolls Visitor Center is anticipated to receive Gold Level

District of Columbia. The Forest Preserve is specifically obligated to indemnify GBCI for GBCI’s
negligence. Recourse under this contract for the Forest Preserves is limited. Costs associated with this
certification are reimbursable through an Illinois Clean Energy & Community Foundation grant that has
already been awarded to the Preserves.

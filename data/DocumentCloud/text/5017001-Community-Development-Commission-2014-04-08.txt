 III.

NEW BUSINESS
A.

NEAR NORTH TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 27)
Request authority for the Department of Planning and Development to negotiate a
redevelopment agreement with Parkside Phase IIB, LP for redevelopment of the
property located at 459 W. Division Street and 1151 N. Cleveland Avenue in the
Near North Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and to
recommend to the City Council of the City of Chicago the designation of Parkside
Phase IIB, LP as Developer.
Project Manager: Anna M. Booth
Motioned by Newsome, seconded by Stein. Approved 8-0. Yeas: Davis, Kostiner,
Martin, Mooney, Newsome, Perez, Stein and Carrott.
14-CDC-13

B.

WOODLAWN TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 20)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Planning and Development’s
intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Historic Strand, LP for the
disposition of the property located at 6315 S. Cottage Grove Avenue and 6314 S.
Maryland Avenue in the Woodlawn Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment
Project Area, to request alternative proposals and to approve the sale of the
property to Historic Strand, LP if no responsive alternative proposals are received;
and request authority for the Department of Planning and Development to
negotiate a redevelopment agreement with Historic Strand, LP for redevelopment
of the property and to recommend to the City Council of the City of Chicago the
designation of Historic Strand, LP as Developer if no responsive alternative
proposals are received.
Project Manager: Tamra Collins
Motioned by Stein, seconded by Martin. Approved 7-0. Yeas: Davis, Kostiner,
Martin, Mooney, Newsome, Stein and Carrott. Perez not present.
14-CDC-14

 C.

FULLERTON/MILWAUKEE TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 30)
Request authority for the Department of Planning and Development to negotiate a
redevelopment agreement with Full Circle Communities, Inc. for redevelopment
of the property located at 3060 N. Milwaukee Avenue in the Fullerton /Milwaukee
Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and to recommend to the
City Council of the City of Chicago the designation of Full Circle Communities,
Inc. as Developer.
Project Manager: Dinah Wayne
Motioned by Newsome, seconded by Stein. Approved 8-0. Yeas: Davis, Kostiner,
Martin, Mooney, Newsome, Perez, Stein and Carrott.
14-CDC-15

D.

NEAR SOUTH TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 4)

disposition of the property located at 300 E. 11th Street in the Near South Tax

14-CDC-16

E.

24TH/MICHIGAN TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 25)

24th/Michigan Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and the

Martin, Mooney, Newsome, Perez, Stein and Carrott.

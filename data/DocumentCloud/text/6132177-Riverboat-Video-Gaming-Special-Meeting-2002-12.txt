MINUTES OF SPECIAL MEETING
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
December 19, 2002
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
A Special Meeting of the Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”) was held on December 19, 2002.
The Board met in the conference room of the Gaming Board Offices on the 3rd floor at 160
N. LaSalle for closed session.
The following Board Members were present: Chairman Elzie Higginbottom and Members
Ira Rogal, Violet Clark, and William Dugan. Member Gary Peterlin was present
telephonically.
The meeting convened at 10:37 A.M. Pursuant to Section 2(c), paragraphs11 and 14 of the
Open Meetings Act and Section 6(d) of the Riverboat Gambling Act, Member Rogal moved
that the Board retire to Closed Session to discuss the items listed under Executive
Session on today’s (December 19, 2002) agenda and relating to the following subject
matters:
1. Pending litigation and matters involving probable litigation; and
2. Personnel matters

Member Clark seconded the motion. The Board adopted the motion by unanimous consent,
and retired to closed session.
The Board adjourned at 12:30 P.M.

Respectfully submitted,
Monica Thomas

Secretary to the Board

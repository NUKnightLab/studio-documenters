New Business
o Commissioners discussed objectives for the 2017 SSA Strategic Plan. Three key aspects
of the plan were decided upon:
 Unify branding of the SSA, to be used on future projects like bike racks
 Unite the neighborhood, bringing ‘satellite’ areas together with the 4700 Block
of Lincoln Avenue
 Identifying ‘Wow Factor’ projects that people can readily associate with the SSA
and make Lincoln Square a greater destination
o Review of proposed Landscaping Projects
 4700 Block, general enhancements – REJECTED
 Gunnison Holiday Light Removal – Waiting on Response from Dept. of Forestry
 Rose Garden Plant Beds – Will revisit at next meeting

 Lincoln Avenue Pebble Trash Can disposal – APPROVED
 A. Qaiyum (motion), Z. Maiorca (second)

ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on July 5, 2007
I.

II.

PRESENT

Jackie Gallagher, Chairman; Charles Anderson, Member of the Board; Edward E. Sadlowski, Member of the
Board; John F. Brosnan, Executive Director; and staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD JUNE 5, 2007

Chairman Gallagher called for approval of the minutes of the June 5, 2007 meeting. Member Sadlowski
made a motion for approval of the minutes. Member Anderson seconded the motion. The Board approved
the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gallagher asked Executive Director Brosnan to present the Local Panel administrative actions for
the period of time from July 1 through July 31, 2007. He distributed the report to the Board for review, and
the Board examined and discussed it.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.
Case No. L-CB-01-038
Chicago Transit Authority/Amalgamated Transit Union, Local 241
Case Nos. L-CA-02-003 and L-CA-02-004
Amalgamated Transit Union, Local 241/Chicago Transit Authority

Chairman Gallagher began by noting that at the June 5 meeting, the Board directed Acting General Counsel
Brosnan to prepare a draft decision for review at this meeting. The Board discussed the draft decision at
length. At the conclusion thereof, Member Sadlowski moved that the Board issue the draft decision as
written. Chairman Gallagher seconded the motion. Member Anderson abstained. Chairman Gallagher and
Member Sadlowski passed the motion by a voice vote.
2.
Case No. L-CA-06-044
Chicago Regional Council of Carpenters, Local 13/City of Chicago
Chairman Gallagher began by summarizing the case, noting the ALJ dismissed the complaint. Acting
General Counsel Brosnan reviewed the issues presented by the exceptions and recommended that the Board
accept the ALJ's recommendation. After the Board's discussion, Member Sadlowski moved to accept the
ALJ's recommendation. Member Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a
voice vote.
3.
Case No. L-CB-06-013
Kimberly Bottom/Licensed Practical Nurses Association of Illinois
Chairman Gallagher began by summarizing the case, noting that no party filed exceptions to the ALJ's RDO,
and asked whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own motion. The Board declined to do so.

 VI.

OTHER BOARD MATTERS

Chairman Gallagher updated the Board as to the status of the State's overall budget and other general fiscal
issues, and reported on certain personnel matters. The Chairman, Member Sadlowski, and Member
Anderson then discussed various aspects of the matters presented. Thereafter, the Board discussed whether
there was a need for executive session, concluding it was not necessary at this time.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gallagher called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the
meeting, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor
Relations Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 14th day of August, 2007.

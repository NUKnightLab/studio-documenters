BC2019-562
Sheriff’s Department, submitting an amendment to an agreement with Board of Park Commissioners of
the Cleveland Metropolitan Park District for inmate housing services for the period 9/4/2018 –
11/30/2018 to extend the time period to 12/31/2019, to change the terms, effective 7/1/2019 and for
additional revenue in the amount not to exceed $36,000.00; recommending to ratify and confirm the
terms and conditions of said agreement.
Funding Source: Revenue Generating
Lauryn Harwell, Sheriff’s Department, presented. Mr. Miller stated that he has a problem housing the
inmates for other municipalities when the County Jail is already over capacity that contribute to issues at
the Jail; stated that he will approve this agreement, so as not to create issues for the Board of Park
Commissioners of the Cleveland Metropolitan Park District but will not vote in favor of any future

 renewals until the Jail is at or below capacity and a comprehensive strategy is in place before the County
can take on more municipalities. Nan Baker concurred with Mr. Miller’s comments and asked for
clarification on the time period; asked if services have been impacted since 11/30/2018 and if any
payments have been received for services; and asked if additional agreements are expected to come
before the Board. Dennis Kennedy motioned to approve the item; Joseph Nanni seconded. Item BC2019562 was unanimously approved.

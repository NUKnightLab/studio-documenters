I. Call to Order at 3:10pm
II. Public Input
a. Rebeca Vazquez from Asia Insurance introduced herself and wanted to learn
more about the SSA.
b. Robert Taylor and Edin Seferovic were invited by the Commission to discuss
the relationship between the Chamber and SSA. The goal for the Chamber is to
have support of and work with the SSA to benefit the Devon SSA business
community. Irshad said he’d like to see more events and want more people to
know what the organizations do for the SSA community. The Commission likes
the idea of working together with the Chamber on special events and sense of
support and community. The Chamber is open to being a vendor for the SSA
and to different model. The Commission likes the idea of offering businesses a
go-to place for businesses to get help, which is part of the Chamber’s delegate
agency contract.
III. Review and approve draft Meeting Minutes. Pete motioned to approve the arch 2018
meeting minutes with a second by Maura. APPROVED
IV. 2017 Audit- Sandi reviewed the 2017 SSA #43 audit with the commissioners: page 2 is
boilerplate; page 3 financial statement shows there is sufficient cash in the bank;
pages 4-5 shows detail of money spent; pages 6-7 boilerplate; page 8 just shows that
property taxes are paid; page 9 summary of findings shows the standard finding that
the SSA was overspent in certain line items, which is common in most SSA audits and
is accounted for through administrative shifts within categories. Motion to approve
the 2017 Audit by Peter with a second by Maura. APPROVED

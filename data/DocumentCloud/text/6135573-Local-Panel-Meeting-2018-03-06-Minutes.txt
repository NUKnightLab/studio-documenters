STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on March 6, 2018
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman of the Local Panel; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Local Panel; Angela C. Thomas,
Member of the Local Panel; Kimberly F. Stevens, Executive Director; Helen J. Kim, General Counsel; Anna Hamburg-Gal,
Associate General Counsel; and staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD February 6, 2018

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the February 6, 2018 meeting. Member Anderson moved to approve
the minutes. Member Thomas seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gierut asked Executive Director Stevens to present the Executive Director’s reports. She presented the reports
and summarized the highlights.
V.

REVIEW OF PROPOSED WRITTEN DECISIONS AND ORDERS
1.

Case No. L-RC-14-004
Teamsters, Local 700/County of Cook and Cook County Sheriff

General Counsel Kim called the case. Member Thomas made a motion to approve the proposed written decision. Member
Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
2.

Case No. L-CB-18-002
Edward Donaldson/Amalgamated Transit Union, Local 241

General Counsel Kim called the case. Member Anderson made a motion to approve the proposed written decision.
Member Thomas seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VI.

BOARD ACTIONS
1. Case No. L-CA-16-017
Erek Slater/Chicago Transit Authority

General Counsel Kim called and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member Thomas moved
to accept the Administrative Law Judge’s Recommended Decision and Order with modification. Member Anderson
seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
2. Case No. L-CA-16-023
Randle E. Martin/City of Chicago (Department of Streets and Sanitation)
General Counsel Kim called the case, noted that no party filed exceptions to Administrative Law Judge Sharon Purcell’s
Recommended Decision and Order and asked whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own motion. The
Board declined to do so.
VII.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded it was unnecessary.

 VIII.

PUBLIC COMMENT

No members of the public were present.
IX.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board, in
Chicago, Illinois, this 17th day of April 2018.

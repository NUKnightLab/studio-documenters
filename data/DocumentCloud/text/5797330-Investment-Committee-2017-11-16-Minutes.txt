WHEREAS, the Board of Trustees for the Police and Fire Retirement System for the City of
Detroit (“Board”) is in receipt of a Consent Order Modifying Consent Judgment of Divorce
dated July 11, 2012 Nunc Pro Tunc Re: Pension and Retirement Accounts, entered by the
Macomb County Circuit Court on July 3, 2017, which amends/supplements a Consent Judgment
of Divorce entered on July 11, 2012 and Eligible Domestic Relations Order (“EDRO”) dated
April 4, 2013 (“Prior Orders”) wherein Alternate Payee KERRY DELIBERA was awarded certain
rights to the retirement benefits of Participant ANTHONY DELIBERA, who is an active employee,
and
WHEREAS, based on the Prior Orders, the Board adopted a Resolution on May 16, 2013
resolving that Alternate Payee KERRY DELIBERA was entitled to receive a portion of
Participant’s Defined Benefit Plan and Defined Contribution Plan benefits (“May 16, 2013
Resolution”), and
WHEREAS, the Board has been informed that Participant ANTHONY DELIBERA is an active
employee and is not receiving retirement benefits, and
WHEREAS, Participant’s date of birth is November 15, 1970 and to date Participant has attained
21 years of service credit, and
WHEREAS, the July 3, 2017 Order Vacating April 4, 2013 Eligible Domestic Relations Order
City of Detroit Police and Fire Retirement System provides:
Pursuant to the Consent Order Modifying Consent Judgment of Divorce Dated
July 11, 2012 Nunc Pro Tunc Re: Pension and Retirement Accounts entered
simultaneously with this Order:
IT IS ORDERED that the April 4, 2013 Eligible Domestic Relations Order
(EDRO) City of Detroit Police and Fire Retirement System granting Defendant an
interest in Plaintiff’s City of Detroit retirement is vacated and hereby set aside.

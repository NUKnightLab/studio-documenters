STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
JOINT PANEL
Minutes of the Joint Panel Meeting
Held on May 13, 2014

I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:30 A.M.â€”160 North LaSalle Street, Suite S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

John Hartnett, Joint Panel Chairman; Paul S. Besson, Member of the State Panel; James Q. Brennwald,
Member of the State Panel; Michael Coli, Member of the State Panel; Albert Washington, Member of the State
Panel; Robert M. Gierut, Local Panel Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Local Panel; Richard
Lewis, Member of the Local Panel; Melissa Mlynski, Executive Director; Jerald S. Post, General Counsel;
Sarah Kerley, Deputy General Counsel, and staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES

Chairman Hartnett called for approval of the minutes of the April 15, 2014 meeting. Member Besson moved
to approve the minutes. Member Washington seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a
voice vote.
IV.

EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR REPORTS

Executive Director Mlynski presented the fiscal, legislative and mediation reports. The Chair and Members
discussed the reports at length.
V.

LITIGATION REPORT

Deputy General Counsel Kerley presented and discussed the litigation report. The Chair and Members
discussed the report.
VI.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

Member Besson made a motion to leave open session. Member Brennwald seconded the motion, and the
Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote. Member Besson made a motion to go into executive session to
discuss minutes of meetings lawfully closed under this Act, whether for purposes of approval by the body of
the minutes or semi-annual review of the minutes as mandated pursuant to Section 2.06. 5 ILCS 120/2(c)(21)
and to discuss personnel matters pursuant to 5 ILCS 120/2(c)(1). Local Panel Chairman Gierut seconded the
motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote. Subsequently, at the end of the discussion,
Member Coli made a motion to leave executive session. Member Besson seconded the motion, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote. Member Coli then made a motion to return to open session. Member
Washington seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VII.

JOINT BOARD ACTIONS

Chairman Hartnett called for approval of the executive session minutes from the following meetings: July 9,
2013, Joint Panel Meeting; September 10, 2013, Joint Panel Meeting; November 18, 2013, State Panel
Meeting; January 16, 2014, Joint Panel Meeting; February 11, 2014, State Panel Meeting. Member Besson
moved to approve the executive session minutes. Member Washington seconded. The Board passed the
motion unanimously, by a voice vote. Member Coli then made a motion to keep the approved executive
session minutes closed. Member Washington seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice
vote.
VIII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Hartnett called for a motion to adjourn. Chairman Gierut made a motion to adjourn the meeting,
and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

 Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Joint Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations
Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 8th day of July, 2014.

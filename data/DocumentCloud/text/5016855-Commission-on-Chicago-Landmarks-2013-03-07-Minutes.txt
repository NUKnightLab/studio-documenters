The following projects were reviewed by the PRC:
1.

2202 N. Cleveland
(Mid-North District – 43rd Ward)
Proposal:

Proposed new rear 2½-story addition to a corner single-family residence

Action:

Approved unanimously with the following conditions:
1. As proposed, the new siding on the addition shall match the profile
and lap exposure of the existing siding, with details to be included in
the permit plans;
2. As proposed, the new windows shall match the existing windows in
size, glass size, muntin arrangement and muntin profile. Existing and
proposed window details shall be submitted with the permit
application; and,
3. As proposed, any new masonry shall match the size, color, texture and
joint profile of the historic masonry.

2.

2030 N. Orleans
(Mid-North District – 43rd Ward)
Proposal:

Proposed exterior and interior rehabilitation of the 3-unit masonry
building including extending the height of the side parapet walls, moving
the chimney, masonry repairs, and alterations to existing rooftop addition.

Action:

Approved unanimously with the following conditions:

 1. The new dormer additions are approved as proposed on plans dated
February 15, 2013. Exterior cladding of the additions shall be dark
painted wood or fiber-cement siding;
2. The height extension of the front portion of the masonry side parapet
walls and moving of masonry chimney are approved as proposed on
plans dated February 15, 2013;
3. Any brick replacement units shall match the original brick in size,
color, texture and finish. New mortar shall match the historic mortar
in color, joint profile, and strength/type. Samples shall be reviewed
and approved by Historic Preservation staff prior to order and
installation; and,
4. Masonry cleaning specifications and material safety data sheets shall
be reviewed and approved as part of the permit application.

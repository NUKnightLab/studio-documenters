The Board of Water Commissioners for the City of Detroit Water and Sewerage
Department authorizes the Director to enter into a Settlement Agreement
between DWSD and Gail Beasley, class representative, on behalf of a class
of individuals regarding basement backup claim filed by Ms. Beasley, as
class representative, against DWSD. The Agreement is intended to settle
all the claims alleged by the Plaintiff in Wayne County Circuit Court Case
Number 16-012602-NZ, entitled Gail Beasley v. City of Detroit and Great
Lakes Water Authority; and authorizes the Director and General Counsel to
take such other action as may be necessary to accomplish the intent of this
vote.

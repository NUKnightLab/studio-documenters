EXPLANATION
The CHA originally entered into an IGA with CPD in 2000 for above baseline services after
disbanding CHA’s police department. The CHA entered into subsequent IGAs with CPD with
the current one set to expire on December 31, 2010. In the past and in accordance with the prior
IGAs, CPD provided special policing programs for the Henry Horner and the surrounding
Westhaven Community, Ickes, Cabrini-Green Homes, and 1230 Burling.
The IGA for 2011 provides for specialized policing programs at Altgeld/Murray, Henry
Horner/Westhaven, Oakwood Shores, Parkside/SewardPark, Lake Park Place and other CHA
communities as assigned. These specialized services include:

Dedicated watches for Horner/Westhaven Community, Altgeld/Murray, Lake Park Place,
Oakwood Shores, Parkside/SewardPark and other CHA locations as needed.
Increased police services for the above mentioned developments, including increased foot
patrols
Customized policing strategy collaborated by CHA, CHA’s private property managers,
and CPD.

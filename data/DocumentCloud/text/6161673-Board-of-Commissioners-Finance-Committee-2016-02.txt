PROPOSED RESOLUTION
REQUEST FOR FINANCE COMMITTEE HEARING ON COOK COUNTY 2016 REVENUE
SHORTFALLS CAUSED BY FAILURE OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS TO FUND STATE GRANTS
AND OTHER PAYMENTS
WHEREAS, the most recent revenue report shows that the State of Illinois owes the County of Cook over $90
million dollars in grants and other payments for services outside of the health care budget for the 2016 Budget;
and
WHEREAS, the State of Illinois owes the Cook County Health and Hospital System various funds .

Page 1 of3

 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that the Finance Committee should hold a hearing and accomplish
the following:
(1) Have the Chief Financial Officer prepare a report on all state grants and other State of Illinois revenues that
impact the 2016 Budget of Cook County. Such report will include the details of grant programs, the
number of people served and the number of employees paid by such grants.
(2) Have the Budget Director prepare a report on what actions can be taken to balance the 2016 budget of Cook
County if state support is withheld.
(3) Have the Cook County Health and Hospital System prepare a report on all state related funds owed and
what impact nonpayment or late payment will have on the Cook County Health and Hospital System.
(4) Develop a plan for keeping the 2016 Budget balanced despite the failure of the State of Illinois making
essential payments.
(5) Have tbe Cook Co unty State s Attorney to review and research all existing court rder and consent decrees
related to the 2016 Proposed State of Illinois Budget to determine if either thev cover payment to Cook
Countv or could be amended to cover payments to Cook aunty. Fu1ther, the Cook County State s Attorney
should research any new cause of action that would cause a Federal or State Court to order payment owed
to Cook County. The States Attorney shall report to the County Board a soon a practical.
The meeting shall take place on or before February 9, 2016.

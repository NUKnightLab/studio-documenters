Pursuant to Chapter 2, Section 2-411 of the Cook County Code, I hereby appoint
JOHN R. MORALES to the position of County Comptroller, effective immediately.

Mr. Morales has twenty-eight years experience in financial and operations
administration in the public and private sector. Most recently, he has held the
position of Chief Financial Officer of Stroger Hospital of Cook County. Prior to that,
he served as Chief Financial Officer for the Clerk of the Circuit Court of Cook County.
In the private sector he has served in several key positions, including that of Senior
Auditor and Management Consultant for Ernst Young LLP.

Mr. Morales? experience in financial and operations administration in the public and
private sector will be an asset to the Cook County Bureau of Finance.

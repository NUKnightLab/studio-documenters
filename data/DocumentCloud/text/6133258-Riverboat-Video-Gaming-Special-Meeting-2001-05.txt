MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL MEETING
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
May 9, 2001
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
A Special Meeting of the Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”) was held on May 9, 2001 in
Room #S-304 of the State of Illinois Building, Chicago, Illinois, pursuant to the Illinois
Open Meetings Act, 5 ILCS 120/1 et seq.
Chairman Greg Jones was present. The following Board members were present
telephonically: Ira Rogal, Sterling M. Ryder, Stuart Levine, and Staci Yandle.
Also in attendance were: Administrator Sergio E. Acosta, Deputy Administrator Joseph
Haughey, Deputy Chief Legal Counsel Jeannette P. Tamayo and other members of the
staff. Deputy Administrators Tom Swoik and Allan S. McDonald were present
telephonically. Deputy Attorney General Roger Flahaven and Outside Counsel Robert
Shapiro were present.
The meeting was convened at 11:00 a.m. Member Yandle moved that the Board retire
to Closed Session pursuant to Section 2 (c), paragraphs (1) and (11) of the Open
Meetings Act and Section 6(d) of the Riverboat Gambling Act to discuss the
following subject matters:
1. Pending litigation and matters involving probable litigation;and
2. Personnel matters.
Member Ryder seconded the motion. The Board adopted the motion by unanimous
consent.
The Board adjourned at 12:30 P.M.
Respectfully submitted,

Monica L. Thomas
Secretary to the Board

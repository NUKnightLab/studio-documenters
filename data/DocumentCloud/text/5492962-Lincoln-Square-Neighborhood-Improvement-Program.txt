Meeting Minutes
Minutes from Thursday, May 24, 2018 – 8:30am at Bistro Campagne
 Perfunctory Business
o Commissioners Present: Anthony Qaiyum, Melaina Prest, Todd Feinberg, Zach Maiorca,
Ben Munro
o Others Present: Ryan Warsing (LSRCC), Rudy Flores (LSRCC), Sean Cooper (LSRCC), John
Ratzenberger (Gateway to Learning), Nick Yassan (Rany Management); consultants from
PLACE Consulting, Teska Associates, and The Lakota Group
o Commissioners Absent: Pat Bader
 Meeting called to order at 8:42am – B. Munro (motion), T. Feinberg (second)
 Minutes from 4/19/18 meeting approved – M. Prest (motion), A. Qaiyum (second)
 2018 Financials approved – T. Feinberg (motion), B. Munro (second)
o The SSA has received $164,247 out of $238,619 in expected taxes
o The SSA has spent $46,885 of its $277,070 budget
o Profit and Loss – R. Warsing presented detailed YTD profit/loss and balance sheet
 With the departure of current Chair Zachary Maiorca, Melaina Prest was nominated as the next
SSA Chair, with Todd Feinberg as Vice Chair – A. Qaiyum (nomination), B. Munro (second)
 John Ratzenberger and Nick Yassan were introduced as prospective Commissioners
 Master Plan consultant teams from PLACE Consulting, Teska Associates, and The Lakota Group
were invited to speak with the Commission. Each team had roughly a half-hour of presentation
time, and Commissioners were able to ask team questions about their bids.
o The three meetings took so much time that all subsequent agenda items were tabled for
a meeting that will be scheduled within the next few weeks.
o The Commission did not select a final consultant, opting to take more time reviewing
their notes.
 Announcements
 Shelley Young has resigned the commission, bringing total vacancies to four
 The application period for Ryan’s soon-to-be vacant position will close on May
31st. Ryan’s replacement will hopefully attend the next regular meeting.
o Next meetings
 Emergency Meeting – date/time TBD at Bistro Campagne
 Regular Meeting – 9:00am, June 28th, 2018 at Bistro Campagne
 Meeting adjourned at 10:56am

March 5, 2019

NOTICE OF SPECIAL MEETING OF THE DETROIT
CHARTER REVISION COMMISSION
In accordance with Section 5(4) of the Michigan Open Meetings Act, MCL 15.265(4), the
Detroit Charter Revision Commission will hold a special meeting on Tuesday, March 12, 2019,
at 6:00 p.m. The meeting will be held at Roberto Clemente Recreation Center located at 2631
Bagley Street, Detroit, MI 48216. The telephone number is (313) 224-0228. The Public is invited
and encouraged to attend.
Additional information regarding this meeting may be obtained from the Office of the City
Clerk at (313) 224-3266.

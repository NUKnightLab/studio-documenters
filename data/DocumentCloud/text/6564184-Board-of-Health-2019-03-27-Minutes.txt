Legislative Updates – Tobacco 21
Mr. Allan provided an update related to the local legislative efforts in Cuyahoga County regarding
Tobacco 21. Currently, five communities within the Board’s jurisdiction have passed Tobacco 21
legislation: Euclid, Cleveland Hts., University Hts., Brooklyn and Lakewood. The Agency is currently
working with these communities to develop programming to implement permitting and enforcement
strategies within these communities.

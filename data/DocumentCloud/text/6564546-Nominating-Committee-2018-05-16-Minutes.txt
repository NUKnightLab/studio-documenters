ALCOHOL, DRUG ADDICTION AND MENTAL HEALTH SERVICES BOARD OF CUYAHOGA COUNTY
NOMINATING COMMITTEE MINUTES
MAY 16, 2018
Committee Members Present: Reginald C. Blue, Ph.D., J. Robert Fowler, Ph.D., Rev. Benjamin F. Gohlstin, Sr.,
Pythias Jones, M.D., Eugenia Kirkland, LSW, MSSA, CDCA, Harvey A. Snider / Board member: Elsie Caraballo
Absent: Mary M. Step, Ph.D., Ericka L. Thoms
Board Staff Present: Scott Osiecki, C.E.O., Tami Fischer, Carol Krajewski, Maggie Tolbert
1. Call to Order
Dr. Reginald Blue, Nominating Committee Chair, called the meeting to order at 3:00 p.m. in the Ohio Room.
2. Approval of Minutes
The minutes of the April 18, 2018 Nominating Committee Meeting were approved as submitted.
3. Executive Session
Dr. Blue announced the need to enter into Executive Session for the purpose of discussing and considering the
appointment of public officials.
Motion to enter into Executive Session. MOTION: P. Jones / SECOND: B. Fowler / A roll call vote was taken.
AYES: R. Blue, E. Caraballo, R. Fowler, B. Gohlstin, P. Jones, E. Kirkland, H. Snider / NAYS: None / Motion
carried.
Motion to exit the Executive Session and resume the Nominating Committee Meeting. MOTION: H. Snider
SECOND: B. Gohlstin / A roll call vote was taken. / AYES: R. Blue, E. Caraballo, R. Fowler, B. Gohlstin, P. Jones,
E. Kirkland, H. Snider / NAYS: None / Motion carried.
4. Recommended Slate of Officers for FY2019-FY2020
Based upon the discussion held in Executive Session, a secret ballot was taken. Outcome of the Nominating
Committee’s vote is shown below:
Recommended State of Officers for FY2019-FY2020:
 Chairperson: Rev. Benjamin F. Gohlstin, Sr.
 Vice Chair: Eugenia Kirkland, LSW, MSSA, CDCA
 Second Vice Chair: J. Robert Fowler, Ph.D.
Motion to recommend the Slate of Officers for FY2019-FY2020 listed above to the full Board.
MOTION: H. Snider / SECOND: E. Kirkland / AYES: R. Blue, R. Fowler, B. Gohlstin, P. Jones, E. Kirkland, H. Snider
NAYS: None / Motion carried unanimously.
5. Recommended Candidates for Appointment Seat Vacancies
Dr. Blue thanked committee members for their feedback from interviews held with applicants and for their thoughtful
consideration of potential Board member candidates. Based upon input shared, committee members agreed to
recommend three of the six candidates interviewed for the three existing vacancies. As two of the candidates were
interviewed earlier in 2017, it was determined that they should be contacted soon to see if they are still available and
interested in serving as a Board member. Ms. Krajewski was asked to do this on behalf of the committee.
Motion to recommend approving the agreed upon OhioMHAS appointment candidate to the full Board at the
May General Meeting. MOTION: H. Snider / SECOND: E. Kirkland / AYES: R. Blue, R. Fowler, B. Gohlstin,
P. Jones, E. Kirkland, H. Snider / NAYS: None / Motion carried unanimously.

6. New Business


For future deliberation, Dr. Blue suggested the Nominating Committee consider the concept of amending the
ADAMHS Board Bylaws to include more specificity around the preferred qualifications and/or requirements
desired for Board Officer applicants.



Elsie Caraballo indicated her interest in serving on the Nominating Committee. Board Chair Kirkland was in
agreement, appointed her as a committee member going forward and asked Ms. Krajewski to revise the Board
of Directors Committee Roster.

There being no further business to discuss, the meeting adjourned at 3:45 p.m.
Submitted by: Carol Krajewski, Executive Specialist

INVOCATION
Chairman Woronchak called upon Commissioner Webb for the introduction of Darcel
Brown, Legislative Research Assistant, who gave the following invocation:
“Lord, thank you for allowing us to see another day. Thank you for this body of
Commissioners who you have called to fulfill your purpose for Wayne County. Lord, we ask
that you provide your wisdom to them as they deliberate. Allow the decisions decreed here
today to improve the quality of life of those who live in this region. We pray that you would give
each Commissioner a spirit of courage to stand during adversity and boldness to lead
righteously. We ask that you meet their hearts desires and provide the provision for them and
their families as they serve this county. In Jesus’ name we pray. AMEN.”

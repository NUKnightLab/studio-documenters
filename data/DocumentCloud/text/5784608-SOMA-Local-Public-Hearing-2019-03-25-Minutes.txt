In attendance were:
Jennifer Kanalos (DEGC/DBRA)
Brian Vosburg (DEGC/DBRA)

Mike Kirk (Neumann/Smith)
Sue Mosey (Midtown Detroit, Inc.)

Mr. Vosburg called the public hearing to order at 5:35 PM.
Two members of the general public were in attendance.
Ms. Mosey stated that the project follows neighborhood plans for the area and she is in
support of the project.
Mr. Mooney stated that he has traffic safety concerns with the project and on the streets
and intersections near the project. He stated that his sole concern with the project is
traffic safety issues and is opposed to the project until those issues are addressed.
Citing no additional comments, Mr. Vosburg closed the public hearing at 6:00 PM.

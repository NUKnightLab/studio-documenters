MINUTES: Finance Audit and Budget Committee. December 13th, 2017.
NOTICED for 1:30 PM; Commenced at 1:40 PM.
AGENDA: The posted agenda for the meeting can be found at www.transitchicago.com, “About CTA”,
“Transit Board Meetings”, “Meeting Notices, Agendas, and Minutes”, “12/13/2017”, “Committee on
Finance Audit, and Budget”, “Agenda”.
ROLL CALL: Present: Committee Chairman Silva, Irvine, Peterson, Youngblood, Miller, and Alva Rosales.
There was a quorum of six committee members present.
COMMITTEE ACTION: The committee reviewed the Finance report and approved the November 13th,
2017 committee minutes.
Then, after extensive review by the committee, Chairman Silva asked for a motion to place the
recommended approved items on the omnibus for Board approval. The recommended items included
all agenda items.
After the motion was moved and seconded, the motion was approved with six yes votes. The
recommended omnibus contained the following items:
1

An ordinance adopting a budget for Calendar Year 2018 and financial plan for Calendar Years
2019—2020.
2 An ordinance approving the fiscal year 2018-2022 Capital Improvement Program and
authorizing the filing and execution of grant and cooperative agreements and the amendments
and related materials.
3 An ordinance authorizing implementation of fare structure revisions.
4 An ordinance authorizing an intergovernmental agreement with the City of Chicago to provide
ground transportation taxes to the Authority for capital expenditures.
5 An ordinance authorizing the establishment of a short-term borrowing program for operating
purposes secured by sales tax receipts; authorizing the issuance, from time to time, of
obligations secured by sales tax receipts in a principal amount outstanding at any one time not
in excess of $25,000,000 pursuant to such short-term borrowing program; and authorizing the
execution and delivery of one or more supplemental indentures under which such obligations
are issued.
6 An ordinance authorizing an intergovernmental agreement with the County of Cook, Illinois for
the Sheriff of Cook County to provide grounds keeping and maintenance work on Authority
property.
7 An ordinance authorizing a lease of property located at 350 North Orleans Street, Chicago,
Illinois.
8 An ordinance authorizing the lease of property located at 1130 West Lawrence Avenue for Red
and Purple Modernization Project.
9 Contract Number B17OP00006: $375,000.00

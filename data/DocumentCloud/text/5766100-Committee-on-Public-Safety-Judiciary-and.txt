Communication dated February 14, 2019 (received February 19th) requesting
Commission approval of an extension to a four-year grant award between the
Charter County of Wayne and the U.S. Department of Justice, Office of Justice
Programs, Bureau of Justice Assistance, with no additional funds, to extend the
term of the grant to continue the provision of needed resources to focus on
projects or initiatives that will improve law enforcement efforts in the region
through the 2015 Justice Assistance Grant (JAG) Program; the Wayne County
Sheriff’s Office will serve as fiscal agent for Wayne County, and the 12 units of
local government receiving the JAG award (Dearborn, Dearborn Heights, Detroit,
Hamtramck, Harper Woods, Inkster, Lincoln Park, Livonia, Romulus, Taylor,
Westland and the township of Redford) as designated by the Wayne County
Department of Homeland Security; the total amount of the grant remains
$1,324,015; the term of the grant extension is from October 1, 2018 through
September 30, 2019; the revenue from the grant will continue to be deposited
into Account No. 266 31690 529000 (JAG-2015). (2015-71-046M1)

MUNICIPAL EMPLOYEES’ ANNUITY AND BENEFIT FUND OF CHICAGO
SPECIAL MEETING NO. 2011-05-01
MAY 5, 2011
A special meeting of the Retirement Board of the Municipal Employees’ Annuity and
Benefit Fund was held in the Fund offices on May 5, 2011 regarding legal issues
currently involving the Fund (Utz v MEABF). Mr. Malatesta was excused from the
meeting. The meeting was called to order at 11:20 a.m.
PRESENT:
Timothy Guest – President
Steve Lux – Vice President
Stephanie Neely – Treasurer
John Gibson – Recording Secretary
Michael Walsh – Deputy City Treasurer (Proxy for the Treasurer)
Frederick Heiss – Attorney (upon direction)
Terrance Stafanski – Executive Director (upon direction)
ABSENT:
Jeremy Fine – Assistant Comptroller (Proxy for the Vice President)
The Board moved into closed session at 11:30 a.m. in accordance with ILCS 120/2(c)(11)
order to discuss issues related to litigation involving the Fund (Utz v MEABF). Motion
by Ms. Neely. Second by Mr. Lux.
Carried: Ayes – Messrs. Guest, Gibson, Lux, Ms. Neely. Noes – None
The Board moved out of closed session at 1:30 p.m. Motion by Mr. Gibson. Second by
Ms. Neely.
Carried: Ayes – Messrs. Guest, Gibson, Lux, Ms. Neely. Noes – None
No actions taken.
In open session, the Trustees directed that the Mr. Gibson continue settlement
negotiations with Ms. Utz. Motion by Ms. Neely. Second by Mr. Lux.
Carried: Ayes – Messrs. Guest, Gibson, Lux, Ms. Neely. Noes – None
The meeting was adjourned at 1:35 p.m. Motion by Mr. Gibson. Second by Ms. Neely.
Carried: Ayes – Messrs. Guest, Gibson, Lux, Ms. Neely. Noes – None

There being a quorum present, the meeting duly convened and business was transacted as
follows:
Per Chairwoman Scott, during closed meeting, commissioners discussed certain matters
pertaining to a personnel matter and pending, probable, imminent litigation.
A motion was then presented by Commissioner Harris for the approval of the following
resolution for Item 1:
RESOLUTION 2013-CHA-71
WHEREAS, The Board of Commissioners of the Chicago Housing Authority (“Authority”)
deems it to be in the best interests of the Authority to retain outside legal counsel
for the purpose of reviewing certain personnel matters:
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS OF THE
CHICAGO HOUSING AUTHORITY AS FOLLOWS:
Section 1. Outside counsel shall be retained for the purpose of reviewing certain
personnel matters, pursuant to a contract for such services. The law firm shall
periodically report the results of its review directly and only to the Special
Personnel Committee and/or to the Board.
Section 2. This Resolution shall be in full force and effect after its passage as
provided by law.
The Motion to adopt resolution for Item 1 was seconded by Commissioner Cepeda and the
voting was as follows:
Ayes:
Deverra Beverly
Adela Cepeda
Dr. Mildred Harris
Harriet Johnson
Jack Markowski
Z. Scott
Nays:

None

There being no questions or discussion, Chairwoman Scott thereupon declared said Motion
carried and said resolution adopted.
Chairwoman Scott then introduced Item No. 2 also discussed in closed meeting.
A motion was then presented by Commissioner Harris to approve the following resolution for
Item 2:

3

 RESOLUTION 2013-CHA-72
WHEREAS, The Board of Commissioners of the Chicago Housing Authority (“Authority”)
deems it to be in the best interests of the Authority to establish and appoint a
special committee of the Board for the purpose of overseeing the work of outside
legal counsel concerning certain personnel matters:
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS OF THE
CHICAGO HOUSING AUTHORITY AS FOLLOWS:
Section 1. There is hereby established a special committee of the Board for the
purpose of overseeing the work to be conducted by outside legal counsel
concerning certain personnel matters. The special committee shall be comprised
of four (4) members of the Board, and shall be known as the Special Personnel
Committee. The responsibilities of the Committee include reviewing reports from
the outside counsel retained by the Board with respect to these matters, and
advising the Board in closed session of the results of such reports.
Section 2. The Special Personnel Committee shall be dissolved at such time as
the personnel matters referred to above are resolved.
Section 3. This Resolution shall be in full force and effect after its passage as
provided by law.
The Motion to adopt resolution for Item 2 was seconded by Commissioner Johnson and the
voting was as follows:
Ayes:
Deverra Beverly
Adela Cepeda
Dr. Mildred Harris
Harriet Johnson
Jack Markowski
Z. Scott
Nays:

None

There being no questions or discussion, Chairwoman Scott thereupon declared said Motion
carried and said resolution adopted.
Chairwoman Scott then presented a Motion, pursuant to the Special Personnel Committee having
been established by resolution, to appoint Commissioner Adela Cepeda as Chair of the Special
Committee, together with Harriet Johnson, Jack Markowski and Bridget Reidy as the remaining
members of the Committee. The motion was properly seconded by Commissioner Harris and
passed with a unanimous vote.
There being no further discussion, the Special Board Meeting was adjourned at approximately
5:20pm.

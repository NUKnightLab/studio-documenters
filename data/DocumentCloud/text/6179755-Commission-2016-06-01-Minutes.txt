5. 2017 Budget Workplan
Carolina and Sandi presented different Budget template provided by the City of Chicago.
Beginning with the 2017 budget, administrative expenses will consist of 100% of
Category 6.0 SSA Management expenses plus 100% of Category 7.0A Non-Service
(Office) Personnel expenses. Service Personnel, such as in-house sidewalk sweeping
employees, now have a separate Personnel Category 7.0B, which does not count toward
administrative expenses. To compensate for this change, the administrative limit has
been increased from 20% to 30% of the levy portion of the 2017 budget.
6. Manager’s Updates
Carolina updated the commission on all the following:
• On the Table Conversation
Carolina hosted an on the table conversation with Latino businesses owners on
Clark Street. 5 business owners were present, they decided they want to
continue meeting.
• Clark Street Revitalization
RPBA staff presented a proposal to their board for support with placing banners
and planters along Clark Street. Proposal was approved, implementation will
begin immediately. Chris asked that new landscaping expenses be added to
2017 Budget.
• Mile of Murals
7 artists are working on both sides of Estes Viaduct this year. The project is an
extension of a similar project in New York involving extinct birds from the
region.
• Maintenance (landscaping/window washing/mulching)
Spring planters are up, window washing is happening in the next two weeks and
mulching was provided by the City of Chicago to be placed in tree grates and inground planters.
• Newly opened businesses –Safari Lounge, Adriana’s Bridal, Ayo Fashion

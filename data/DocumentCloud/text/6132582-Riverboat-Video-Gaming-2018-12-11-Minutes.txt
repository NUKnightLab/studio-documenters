Regular Meeting, Open Session Minutes – Video and Riverboat
Illinois Gaming Board
Chicago, Illinois
December 12, 2018
The Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”) convened for a Regular Meeting – Video and Riverboat– on
Wednesday, December 12, 2018 at 10:00 A.M. in the Conference Room of the Gaming Board office on
the 5th Floor of the Michael Bilandic Building Center, Chicago, IL pursuant to the Illinois Open
Meetings Act, 5 ILCS 120/1 et seq.
APPROVAL OF OPEN SESSION MINUTES
Member Alejandre moved that the Board approve the open session minutes from its Regular Meeting
of November 2, 2018. Member Robinson seconded the motion. The motion passed unanimously via
voice vote.
CHAIRMAN’S REPORT/COMMENTS
The Chairman announced that the full 2019 Board meeting schedule is now known. The Board meeting
dates were announced as follows: January 30th, March 1st, April 18th, June 13th, August 1st, September
5th, October 24th, November 21st and December 19th. The Chairman also announced that the proposed
inducement rule for Video Gaming was pulled from the December 12, 2018 agenda.
ADMINISTRATOR’S REPORT
Acting Administrator Agostino Lorenzini noted that there are currently 6,753 licensed Video Gaming
Location establishments operating approximately 30,384 video gaming terminals.

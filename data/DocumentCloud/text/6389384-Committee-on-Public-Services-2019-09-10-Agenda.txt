Communication dated July 1, 2019 (received July 9th) requesting Commission approval of
Modification No. 2 to a grant agreement between the Charter County of Wayne and the U.S.
Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), with no change in the dollar amount, to extend the
term of the contract to allow for the completion of the Nankin Lake Restoration project for the
project titled “Rouge River Areas of Concern Wayne County Habitat Restoration Project” to
allow Wayne County and project partners to restore habitats at the Henry Ford Estate Dam,
Rouge River Oxbow and at Nankin Mills Lake within the Rouge River Areas of Concern (AOC);
the County Executive advises that combined, the three projects will reconnect 50 miles of the
Rouge River and 108 additional miles of its tributaries to the Great Lakes system, remove over
40,000 cubic yards of fill and sediment, restore 4 acres of upland habitat and control 3 acres of
invasive species; the total contract amount remains $7,900,000; Modification No. 2 extends the
term of the agreement from November 1, 2019 through December 31, 2020; the cost of the
agreement will continue to be charged to Account No. 228 60149 529000 (Watershed
Management). (2017-70-004M2)

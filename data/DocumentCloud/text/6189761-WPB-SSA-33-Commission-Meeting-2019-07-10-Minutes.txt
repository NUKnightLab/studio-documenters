Motion 3: The Executive Committee recommends to approve that SSA #33’s ordinance and bylaws be
altered to specify 9 appointed slots on its SSA Commission (down from 12) beginning January 1st 2020.
Motion to approve motion made by DG; Seconded by JH.
Discussion: GK explained that since SSA #33 has rarely had more than 7 active commissioners
this will allow for more responsible succession planning and rotating of commissioners when
terms end (instead of all commissioners serving indefinitely)

 All in favor, no opposed, no abstentions. Motion passes.

b. Arts
Motion 7: The Arts Committee moves to approve funding not to exceed $1,600.00 from line item 2.06
(Public Art) for Out of Sight’s additional funding for Wicker Park Fest’s Arts Area to take place July 2628th. (19-0612)
Motion to approve motion made by DG
Discussion: DG explained the current RFP process. This additional funding was requested after
the proposal was sent in. Multiple Commissioners agreed that artists needed to fit within the
constraints of the $5,000 initially outlined in the RFP and adjust their budgets accordingly. JH

 mentioned that additional funding to this organization specifically could be seen as showing
favoritism to a current committee member, as no other applicants for this initiative sit on Arts
Committee.
DG in favor, MH and JH opposed, no abstentions. Motion not approved.

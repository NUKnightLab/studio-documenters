Executive Director Report
Dr. McKinney thanked everyone for attending the meeting. She then provided an update on the
following projects:
CTA Shuttle – Developing a comprehensive shuttle system for the District. The vision is to
develop a new integrated shuttle system that will replace the four disparate shuttle systems that
currently operate.
IMD Website – Work continues and the website will feature a fly-over video that highlights
Chicago’s rich history and will incorporate the IMD.

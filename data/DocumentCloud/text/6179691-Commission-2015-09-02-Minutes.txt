3. Reimbursement of Public Way Maintenance contract
Commission discussed the situation with State Budget impasse and how to plan for
public way maintenance in connection with its contract with the Earnfare program in
the event the program is dissolved by the State. They discussed if the possibility of
reimbursing RPBA in case the state does not. Motion to pay Rogers Park Business
Alliance its proportion of Earnfare Program, at cost, for 6 months from the first day of
contract, July 1, 2015- in the event that they are not reimbursed by the State of Illinois
(Goldberg / Owings). Unanimously approved.

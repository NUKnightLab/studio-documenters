STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on November 15, 2016
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman of the Local Panel; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Local Panel; Richard Lewis, Member
of the Local Panel; Melissa Mlynski, Executive Director; Helen J. Kim, General Counsel; Anna Hamburg-Gal, Associate
General Counsel; and staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD OCTOBER 4, 2016

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the October 4, 2016 meeting. Member Anderson moved to approve
the minutes. Member Lewis seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gierut asked Executive Director Mlynski to present the Executive Director’s reports. She presented the reports
and summarized the highlights.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.

Case No. L-CB-16-010
Jason Monsour/Amalgamated Transit Union, Local 308

Chairman Gierut called the case and asked General Counsel Kim to present the issues of the case to the Board.
General Counsel Kim stated that the Executive Director issued a dismissal on a charge alleging that the Respondent violated
Section 10(b) of the Act and that Charging Party filed an appeal and Respondent filed a response. After her presentation,
General Counsel Kim recommended that the Board uphold the Executive Director’s dismissal. After discussion, Member
Lewis moved to affirm the Executive Director’s written dismissal with modification to note two events that occurred six
months prior to the filing of the charge. Member Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by
a voice vote.

VI.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded it was
unnecessary.
VII.

PUBLIC COMMENT

No members of the public were present.
VIII. ADJOURNMENT
Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and
the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board, in
Chicago, Illinois, this 16th day of May, 2017.

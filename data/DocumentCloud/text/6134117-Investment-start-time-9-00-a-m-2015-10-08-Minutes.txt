MUNICIPAL EMPLOYEES’ ANNUITY AND BENEFIT FUND OF CHICAGO
INVESTMENT MEETING 2015-10-01
OCTOBER 8, 2015

An Investment Meeting of the Retirement Board of the Municipal Employees’ Annuity and
Benefit Fund of Chicago was held in the Offices of the Fund on October 8, 2015 at 9:05 a.m. to
hear economic updates and market perspectives by three investment firms.

A quorum was present.
After the roll call, Trustee McMahon made a motion to allow Trustee Guest to attend the
Investment Meeting via teleconference in accordance with 5 ILCS 120/7. Seconded by Proxy
Martinez.
Carried: Ayes – Trustee Clancy, Trustee McMahon, Trustee Widawsky and Proxy Martinez.
Nays – None.
The following staff and consultants were present: James Mohler, Executive Director, Mary
Patricia Burns, Burke, Burns and Pinelli, Fund Counsel, Juan Lopez, Jr, Investment Officer,
Shawn Evans, Investment Analyst, Brian Wrubel, Marquette Associates, Neil Capps, Marquette
Associates and Stacey Ruffolo, Project Coordinator.
Prudential
Mark Hoffmeister of Prudential gave an overview of World credit markets and discussed the
future of direct lending and mezzanine debt financing.
American Realty Advisors
Todd Fowler, Director of Marketing and Client Services and Chris Macke, Managing Director of
Research and Strategy gave an update on the American Core Realty Fund’s portfolio positioning
and an overview of the current condition of the commercial real estate market.
William Blair
John McLaughlin, CFA, Partner, Head of Institutional Client Relationships and Casey Preyss,
CFA, Partner, Portfolio Manager, gave an overview and outlook of the emerging markets,
specifically China, as well as a walkthrough of William Blair’s investment strategy.
Trustee Widawsky departed the meeting and Jeremy Fine, proxy for the Vice President joined
the meeting.

MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL MEETING
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
August 25, 2000
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
A Special Meeting of the Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”) was held on August 25, 2000
in Room #S-304 of the State of Illinois Building, Chicago, Illinois, pursuant to the Illinois
Open Meetings Act, 5 ILCS 120/1 et seq.
Chairman Gregory C. Jones was present in person; Board members Joseph Lamendella,
Sterling M. Ryder and Stuart Levine were present telephonically. Member Yandle joined
in at 10:20 a.m.
Also in attendance were: Administrator Sergio E. Acosta, Deputy Administrators Joseph
Haughey, Allan S. McDonald and other members of the staff. Deputy Chief Legal
Counsel Jeannette P. Tamayo and Deputy Administrator Tom Swoik were present
telephonically.
The meeting was convened at 10:10 a.m. At 10:20 a.m., Member Yandle moved that the
Board retire to Closed Session pursuant to Section 2 (c), paragraphs (1), (4) and (11)
of the Open Meetings Act and Section 6(d) of the Riverboat Gambling Act. Member
Levine seconded the motion. The Board adopted the motion by unanimous consent.
The Board adjourned at 11:30 a.m.
Respectfully submitted,

Monica L. Thomas
Secretary to the Board

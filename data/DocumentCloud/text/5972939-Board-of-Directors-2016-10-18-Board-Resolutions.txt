RESOLUTIONS ADOPTED BY THE
CHICAGO INFRASTRUCTURE TRUST
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
ON OCTOBER 18, 2016

MEETING MINUTES
RESOLVED, that the minutes of the meeting of the Board of Directors
held on July 19, 2016, in the form previously provided to the members of the
Board of Directors, be and hereby are approved.

2015 ANNUAL AUDIT
RESOLVED, that the audited financial statements of the Chicago
Infrastructure Trust’s (the “CIT”) covering its 2015 fiscal year, including the report
thereon by independent auditors Prado & Renteria and attached hereto as Exhibit
A, be and hereby are approved.

EMPLOYEE BENEFITS
WHEREAS, the Board of Directors previously authorized the staff of the
CIT to structure, negotiate and obtain a competitive set of employee benefits for
employees of the CIT; and
WHEREAS, the staff has determined that the provision of appropriate
health, dental, and vision insurance coverage is necessary to attract and retain
qualified personnel;
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the CIT’s officers be, and
hereby are, authorized to obtain, on behalf of the CIT’s employees, a health,
dental, and vision insurance plan that achieves, in the judgment of such officers,
the best balance of coverage and cost.

 GENERAL
RESOLVED, that in order to fully carry out the intent and effectuate the
purposes of the foregoing resolutions, any of the CIT’s officers be, and each hereby
is, authorized to take all such further actions, and to execute and deliver all such
further agreements, instruments, documents or certificates in the name and on
behalf of the CIT, and under its corporate seal or otherwise, and to pay all such fees
and expenses, which shall in their judgment be necessary, proper or advisable and
to perform all of the obligations of the CIT in connection with the foregoing
resolutions.

Re: Reconsideration of Request to Return to Work of member Orlando Potts
Resolution Moved By: Trustee Diaz Supported By: Trustee Gnatek
WHEREAS, Member Orlando Potts made a request to return to work on January 14, 2016;
WHEREAS, during the processing of this request, Mr. Potts obtained 25 years of service;
WHEREAS, the Police and Fire Retirement System Board of Trustees (“Board”) denied the
request to return to work on June 1, 2017 for the reason Mr. Potts had obtained 25 years of
services;
WHEREAS, on or about March 26, 2018, Mr. Potts appealed the June 1, 2017 decision of the
Board;
WHEREAS, in correspondence dated April 5, 2018, Mr. Potts was notified that the Board had
affirmed its prior decision denying his request to return to work;
WHEREAS, at its June 21, 2018 meeting, the Board took up this matter again for further
discussion and re-consideration;
WHEREAS, consistent with its broad authority under section 14.2 of Component I of the
Combined Plan, the Board determined that under the unique circumstances of this particular
case, it would give additional consideration to Mr. Pott’s request;
WHEREAS, this matter was discussed extensively in open session;
NOW THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED, that consistent with its authority in section 14.2 of
Component 1 of the Combined Plan, the Board re-considers its previous action with respect to
Mr. Pott’s request to return to work and hereby approves the request to return to work subject to
applicable re-examination requirements, if any, and conditions of employment required by the
City of Detroit, the Fire Department or applicable collective bargaining agreements;
RESOLVED, that a copy of this resolution shall be provided to Mr. Potts and the appropriate
persons at the Human Resource Department of the City of Detroit and Fire Department.

Yeas – Battle, Diaz, Gnatek, James, Jones, Moslimani, Roberson, Trozak, Watson and
Chairperson Serda - 10
Nays – Pegg and Tucker- 2

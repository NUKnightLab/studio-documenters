BC2019-845
Department of Information Technology, submitting an amendment to Contract No. CE1800194 with RSM
US LLP for third-party assurance services for components of the County’s Enterprise Resource Planning
System for the period 4/17/2018 – 12/31/2019 to extend the time period to 6/30/2020, for Organizational
Readiness Assessment, effective 10/1/2019 11/25/2019 and for additional funds in the amount not-toexceed $50,000.00.
Funding Source: General Fund

 Jeanelle Greene, Department of Information Technology and Jack Rhyne, Project Manager, Infor,
presented. Dale Miller asked why the effective date of the amendment is 10/1/2019. Lenora Lockett
stated that the effective date should reflect the date that the services were authorized to begin and asked
what the date for the change in scope of services was. Mr. Rhyne indicated that this amendment is not
changing the scope of services but is only requesting additional funds and an extension of time. Michael
Chambers stated that his understanding from the discussion at the Executive Review Meeting was
different than what was being presented and asked for confirmation that this amendment is only for
continuation of services. Mr. Rhyne stated that was a previous draft that has since been amended and to
remove the change in scope. Ms. Greene stated that the corrected amendment was uploaded to OnBase.
Mr. Rhyne requested that the effective date be changed. Mr. Chambers said that the contract document
will need to be changed to reflect the new effective date. Joseph Nanni commented that his recollection
of the documents that he reviewed did include a change in scope and is in line with what was presented
to the Council Finance Committee. Nan Baker asked if the additional $50,000.00 will be the final
amendment and if the department is confident that no future requests for funding will be made for this
purpose. Michael Chambers motioned to approve the item; David Marquard seconded. Item BC2019-845
was unanimously approved, as amended.

Communication dated March 13, 2018 (received March 24th) requesting Commission approval
of a retroactive, two-year agreement between the Charter County of Wayne and the Alliance of
Rouge Communities (ARC) in the amount of $83,472 to allow for the continuation of illicit
discharge elimination investigations in Wayne County Rouge Watershed priority areas to
further isolate problem areas, identify illicit connections, and take corrective action(s) to
remove them; the County Executive advises that the primary purpose of the Alliance of Rouge
Communities is to provide an institutional mechanism to encourage watershed-wide
cooperation and mutual support to meet water quality stormwater permit requirements under
the Michigan Department of Environmental Quality’s municipal separate storm sewer system
discharge permit and to restore beneficial uses of the Rouge River to the area residents; the
term of the agreement is from January 1, 2018 through December 31, 2019; the revenue from
the agreement will be deposited into Account Nos. 228 60149 626000/569000/529000
(Watershed Management). (2018-70-003)

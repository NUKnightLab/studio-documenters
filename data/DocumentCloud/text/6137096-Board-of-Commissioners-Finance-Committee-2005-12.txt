HARRIS & HARRIS (PROPOSED CONTRACT) FOR COLLECTION
SERVICES FOR DELINQUENT TRAFFIC FINES AND FEES . Transmitting a
Communication, dated November 17, 2005 from Dorothy Brown, Clerk of the
Circuit Court:
I am writing to inform you of an initiative for which I would appreciate your
consideration and support at the December 6, 2005 Board Meeting.
Specifically, I would like to request authorization from the Cook County Board
of Commissioners for the Office of the Clerk of the Circuit Court to engage the
firm of Harris & Harris for a two year period to perform accounts receivable
collection services for delinquent fines and fees due and owing to the Clerk's
Office. The goals of this initiative are to collect as much revenue as possible
and to bring past due accounts that are deemed uncollectible by an
independent qualified firm to closure.
The collection services provided by Harris & Harris will be performed on a
competitive basis with no cost to Cook County. All fees associated with the
collection of a past due fine or fee will be added in addition to the fine or fee
amount as permitted by Illinois Public Act 093-0836, which became law in
2004.
The scope of the collection services that Harris & Harris would be engaged to
perform would concentrate solely on past due Traffic fines and fees.
The horizon of time that will be addressed for the collection of past due fines
and fees will span in excess of ten (10) years.
Established in 1968, Harris & Harris has the resources necessary to perform
collection services for a court system the size of the Circuit Court of Cook
County efficiently and effectively, using state-of-the art technology as well as
industry standard collection procedures. The decision to select Harris & Harris
for this engagement was predicated on a due diligence review performed by
the Clerk of the Circuit Court of several firms who presented their services for
consideration.
* Referred to the Finance Committee on December 6, 2005.

 III.

NEW BUSINESS
A.

35TH/HALSTED TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 11)
Request authority for the Department of Housing and Economic Development to
negotiate a redevelopment agreement with Vienna Beef, Ltd. for redevelopment
of the property located at 1000 W. Pershing Road in the 35th/Halsted Tax
Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and to recommend to the City
Council of the City of Chicago the designation of Vienna Beef, Ltd. as Developer.
Project Manager: Edward Lewis
Motioned by Perez, seconded by Ward. Approved 12-0. Yeas: Buford, Davis,
Duque, Kostiner, Mooney, Newsome, Olander, Perez, Sahli, Stein, Ward and
Carrott.
13-CDC-17

B.

HOMAN/ARTHINGTON TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 24)
Request authority to enter into a negotiated sale with Uhlich Children’s
Advantage Network for the disposition of the property located at 1106-12 S.
Central Park Avenue in the Homan/Arthington Tax Increment Financing
Redevelopment Project Area, to request alternative proposals and to approve the
sale of the property to Uhlich Children’s Advantage Network if no responsive
alternative proposals are received.
Project Manager: Michelle Nolan
Motioned by Ward, seconded by Kostiner. Approved 12-0. Yeas: Buford, Davis,
Duque, Kostiner, Mooney, Newsome, Olander, Perez, Sahli, Stein, Ward and
Carrott.
13-CDC-18

 D.

24TH/MICHIGAN AND MICHIGAN/CERMAK TIF REDEVELOPMENT
PROJECT AREAS (WARD 3)
Request authority to acquire the property located at 2206-58 S. Indiana Avenue in
the 24th/Michigan Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area and to
acquire the property located at 2207 S. Michigan Avenue in the Michigan/Cermak
Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area.
Project Manager: Robert McKenna
Motioned by Newsome, seconded by Duque. Approved 10-0. Yeas: Davis,
Duque, Kostiner, Mooney, Newsome, Olander, Perez, Sahli, Stein and Carrott.
Buford and Ward not present.

E.

24TH/MICHIGAN AND MICHIGAN/CERMAK TIF REDEVELOPMENT
PROJECT AREAS (WARD 3)

V.

She

BOARD ACTIONS
1.

Case No. L-CA-13-023
Kevin Sroga/Forest Preserve of Cook County

Chairman Gierut asked General Counsel Nelson to call the case to the Board. General Counsel Nelson called the case and
stated that Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) Thomas Allen issued a Recommended Decision and Order (RDO) dismissing
the charge filed by the Charging Party alleging that the Respondent violated Section 10(a)(1) of the Illinois Public Labor
Relations Act. After her presentation, General Counsel Nelson recommended that the Board affirm the RDO with
modifications. After discussion, Member Lewis moved to accept the RDO with modifications. Member Anderson
seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

2.

Case Nos. L-RD-15-001, L-RD-15-002, L-RD-15-003, L-RD-15-004
Beronica Wooden, Carla Salvo, Gwendolyn Williams, Ellen Greene/County of Cook

General Counsel Nelson called the case, noted that no party filed exceptions to ALJ Anna Hamburg-Gal’s RDO and asked
whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own motion. The Board declined to do so.

 III.

NEW BUSINESS

PROPOSED NEAR SOUTH TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
AMENDMENT NO. 5 (WARDS 2 & 3)
Accept for review the amended redevelopment plan for the proposed Near South

Project Manager: Beth McGuire

10-CDC-75

B.

NEAR SOUTH TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 2)
Request authority to acquire the property known as Mark Twain Park located at
the southeast corner of 15th Place and Indiana Avenue in the Near South Tax
Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area.
Project Manager: Michele Rhymes
Motioned by Sahli, seconded by Carrott. Approved 8-0. Yeas: Carrott, Bland,
Gomez, Kostiner, Martin, Sahli, White and Randall.
10-CDC-76

 C.

NEAR SOUTH TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 2)
Request authority to advertise the intention of the Department of Zoning and Land
Use Planning and the Department of Community Development to enter into a
negotiated sale with the Chicago Park District for the disposition of the property
known as Mark Twain Park located at the southeast corner of 15th Place and
Indiana Avenue in the Near South Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment
Project Area, to request alternative proposals, and to approve the sale of the
property to the Chicago Park District if no responsive alternative proposals are
received.
Project Manager: Michele Rhymes
Motioned by Sahli, seconded by White. Approved 8-0. Yeas: Bland, Carrott,
Gomez, Kostiner, Martin, Sahli, White and Randall.
10-CDC-77

D.

24TH/MICHIGAN TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 25)
Request authority to acquire the property located at 2101-2115 S. Archer Avenue
and 2100-2124 S. Wentworth Avenue in the 24th/Michigan Tax Increment
Financing Redevelopment Project Area.
Project Manager: Eva-Marie Tropper
Motioned by Carrott, seconded by Gomez. Approved 8-0. Yeas: Bland, Carrott,
Gomez, Kostiner, Martin, Sahli, White and Randall.
10-CDC-78

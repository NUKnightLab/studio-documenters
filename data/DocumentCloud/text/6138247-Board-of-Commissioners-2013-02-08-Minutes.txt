321830

COOK COUNTY LAND BANK AUTHORITY BOARD OF DIRECTORS
(APPOINTMENT). Transmitting a Communication, dated January 30, 2013 from Toni
Preckwinkle, President, Cook County Board of Commissioners.
I hereby appoint the following individuals to the Cook County Land Bank Authority Board of
Directors for terms to begin immediately and expiring as noted below:
Commissioner Bridget Gainer, serving for the length of her Cook County Commissioner
term, pursuant to the requirement that the Land Bank Authority Board contain one (1) Cook
County Commissioner to serve as an ex-officio member;
The Honorable Jeffrey Sherwin, expiring January 1, 2014, pursuant to the requirement that
the Land Bank Authority Board contain two (2) Suburban Cook County Mayors, Presidents
or Village Managers;
Lawrence Grisham, expiring January 1, 2014, pursuant to the requirement that the Land Bank
Authority Board contain one (1) representative from the City of Chicago as recommended by
the Mayor of the City of Chicago;
Herman Brewer, expiring January 1, 2015, pursuant to the requirement that the Land Bank
Authority Board contain one (1) representative from the Cook County Bureau of Economic
Development;
Calvin Holmes, expiring January 1, 2015, pursuant to the requirement that the Land Bank
Authority Board contain one (1) representative from a community development finance
institution;
Carl A. Jenkins, expiring January 1, 2015, pursuant to the requirement that the Land Bank
Authority Board contain one (1) representative from the banking community;
Sarah L. Ware, expiring January 1, 2016, pursuant to the requirement that the Land Bank
Authority Board contain one (1) representative from a local or state realtor association;
Stephen Porras, expiring January 1, 2016, pursuant to the requirement that the Land Bank
Authority Board contain one (1) representative from the non-profit housing development
community;
Emy Brawley, expiring January 1, 2017, pursuant to the requirement that the Land Bank
Authority Board contain one (1) representative from the open space community; and
Julie Dworkin, expiring January 1, 2017, pursuant to the requirement that the Land Bank
Authority Board contain one (1) representative from a City of Chicago community
organization.
I submit this communication for your approval.
*Referred to the Legislation and Intergovernmental Relations Committee on 02/05/13.

Chairman Suffredin requested that the backup material for this meeting, which contains information about
all of the nominees and which had been distributed earlier, be entered into the record.

Each of the seven (7) nominees present – Bridget Gainer, Lawrence Grisham, Herman Brewer, Sarah L.
Ware, Stephen Porras, Emy Brawley and Julie Dworkin – delivered remarks about his /her qualifications
for and interest in an appointment to the Land Bank Authority Board of Directors.
Commissioner Daley, seconded by Commissioner Silvestri, moved to approve Communication No.
321830. Commissioner Garcia called for a Roll Call Vote on the Motion, the vote of yeas and nays
being as follows:
Roll Call on Motion to Approve Communication No. 321830
Yeas:

Chairman Suffredin, Vice Chairman Fritchey, Commissioners Collins, Daley, Gainer,
Garcia, Schneider, Silvestri, Sims and Steele (10).

Nays:

None (0).

Present:

None (0).

Absent:

Beavers, Butler, Gorman, Goslin, Murphy, Reyes and Tobolski (7).

The motion carried and Communication No. 321830 was recommended for approval.
Chairman Suffredin noted for the record that had there been a divided vote, Commissioner Collins would
have voted present on the Honorable Jeffrey Sherwin’s nomination.
Commissioner Daley moved to adjourn the meeting, seconded by Vice Chairman Fritchey. The
motion carried and the meeting was adjourned.

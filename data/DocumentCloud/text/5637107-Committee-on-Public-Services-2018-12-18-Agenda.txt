1.

Communication dated October 10, 2018 (received December 5th) requesting Commission
approval of an unsolicited, five-year master infrastructure management agreement between
the Charter County of Wayne and Neo Network Development, Inc. (of Naperville, IL) to form a
public-private partnership with Neo Network’s Universal Access program to provide
commercial wireless carriers with accelerated access to and use of the Wayne County street
light poles, traffic signal poles and rights-of-way to host emerging technologies; the County
Executive advises that Wayne County benefits by receiving recurring rent revenues from the
wireless carriers, upgrading asset management to a current GIS mapping database and
maintaining compliance with federal law and FCC orders; the term of the agreement will
commence upon Commission approval for five years; the anticipated revenue from the
agreement will be deposited into Account No. 201 44905 670010 (Roads-Equipment). (201933-001)

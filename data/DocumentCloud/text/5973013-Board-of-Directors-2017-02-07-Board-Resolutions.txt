RESOLUTIONS ADOPTED BY THE
CHICAGO INFRASTRUCTURE TRUST
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
ON FEBRUARY 7, 2017

MEETING MINUTES
RESOLVED, that the minutes of the meeting of the Board of Directors
held on October 18, 2016, in the form previously provided to the members of the
Board of Directors, be and hereby are approved.

2017 ANNUAL BUDGET
RESOLVED, that the annual budget of the Chicago Infrastructure Trust
(the “Trust”) covering its 2017 fiscal year, in the form attached hereto as Exhibit
A, be and hereby is approved.

SMART LIGHTING VENDOR RECOMMENDATION
WHEREAS, the Board of Directors previously authorized the staff of the
Trust to develop and execute, in conjunction with the appropriate personnel at the
City of Chicago, a competitive procurement process to provide lead contractor
services on the Smart Lighting project; and
WHEREAS, the staff received responses from six proposers; and
WHEREAS, an independent evaluation committee reviewed and,
informed by input from appropriate technical advisors, scored all of the proposals
received and made recommendations to a selection committee (the “Smart
Lighting Selection Committee”) from among the six proposals; and
WHEREAS, the Smart Lighting Selection Committee has rank ordered its
two finalists;
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Smart Lighting
Selection Committee’s ranked finalists be recommended to the City of Chicago
for the commencement of negotiations regarding a contract for the Smart Lighting
project.

 GENERAL
RESOLVED, that in order to fully carry out the intent and effectuate the
purposes of the foregoing resolutions, any of the Trust’s officers be, and each
hereby is, authorized to take all such further actions, and to execute and deliver all
such further agreements, instruments, documents or certificates in the name and
on behalf of the Trust, and under its corporate seal or otherwise, and to pay all
such fees and expenses, which shall in their judgment be necessary, proper or
advisable and to perform all of the obligations of the Trust in connection with the
foregoing resolutions.

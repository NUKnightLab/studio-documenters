A.

CENTRAL WEST TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 27)
Request authority to approve the sale of the property located at 1517-21 W.
Warren Boulevard and 28 N. Ogden Avenue in the Central West Tax Increment
Financing Redevelopment Project Area to Viceroy Hotel, LP, an entity composed
of Heartland Housing, Inc. and First Baptist Congregational Church, the
designated successful respondent to the Request for Proposals issued by the
Department of Housing and Economic Development on April 14, 2009, for the
purchase and redevelopment of the property, and to recommend to the City
Council of the City of Chicago the designation of Viceroy Hotel, LP as
Developer. The Viceroy Hotel, located at 1519 W. Warren Boulevard, is listed on
the National Register of Historic Places and is a designated Chicago Landmark.
Project Manager: Paul Zalmezak
Motioned by Sahli, seconded by Logan. Approved 8-0. Yeas: Gomez, Kostiner,
Logan, Martin, Mooney, Sahli, Stein and White. Carrott and Todman recused.

NEAR WEST SIDE TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA

The Executive Director discussed notices received regarding a class action suit involving the
Northern Trust. Such suit, in which securities lending reinvested collateral held in the Core USA
Fund, reached a final settlement of $4,250,000, of which the Fund may be entitled to a share of the
proceeds based on the relevant dates and investment values.

•

As previously noted, GRS appeared before the Board at the November investment committee
meeting. At that meeting, the Committee requested that GRS prepare an evaluation of the impact of a
7.25 % discount rate versus a 7.5 % discount rate. GRS has since supplied quotes for the study. In
essence, GRS is proposing that they compare both the accounting and funding values of Public Act
99-0506 at both 7.5% and 7.25%. The Board approved continuation of the study and proposed fees.

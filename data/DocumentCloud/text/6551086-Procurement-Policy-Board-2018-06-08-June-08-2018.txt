Chairman Vala called the special June meeting of the Procurement Policy Board to order.
Member Bedore made a motion to allow Member Morales to participate by via telephone.
Member Black seconded the motion all members voting “aye” and the motion carries.
First on the agenda, CMS is presenting new rules for the Business Enterprise Program. Mr.
Johnson stated that these rules are for Business owned controlled by Minorities, Females, and
persons with Disabilities and these rules provide some clarification procedures on contracting
with Sheltered Markets, and also rules regarding how the agency will handle any evidence of
discrimination against the Sheltered Markets. It was explained how these rules are under the BEP
Act and not the Procurement Code. Veterans are covered under the Procurement Code and they
have their own separate Administrative Rules. Member Bedore makes a motion issue a letter of
no objection to the BEP Rules. Member Ivory seconded the motion. With all Members voting
“aye” motion carries.
Member Bedore made a motion to approve the hiring that was discussed during the previous
meeting Executive Session. Member Black seconded the motion. With all Members voting “aye”
motion carries.
Member Bedore made a motion to adjourn. Member Black seconded the motion. With all
Members voting “aye” motion carries.

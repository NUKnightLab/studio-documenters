In the matter of Carolina Jacobo, the Board finds "Pursuant to 40 ILCS 5/5-214.2, and for all the reasons
stated herein, Jacobo has not established entitlement to the police pension service credit for the period at
which time she was employed with Northwestern University Police Department as a Police Officer.
As the statutory provision of the Act does not grant police pension credit for such service, Jacobo’s request
for police pension service credit for all or part of the time period requested is therefore denied."
It was moved by Trustee Hamburger, seconded by Trustee Wright that the Board approve the order on
Carolina Jacobo, as submitted.

In the matter of Hornowski; During an audit of the documents it was discovered that Chester Hornowski
terminated his employment August 15, 2004, and applied for his pension on August 16, 2004, which
qualifies for payment of the Death Benefit under 40 ILCS 5/5-153. However, as detailed on page 15 of this
Agenda, his annuity was calculated effective May 1, 2004, and he was paid annuity for the period May 1,
2004 through August 15, 2004. That calculation resulted in a $15,177.16 overpayment of annuity.
t was moved by Trustee Hamburger, seconded by Trustee Wodnicki that the Board approve payment of the
$6,000 death benefit and that staff make arrangements with Mr. Hornowski’s widow to recapture the
$15,177.16 overpayment.

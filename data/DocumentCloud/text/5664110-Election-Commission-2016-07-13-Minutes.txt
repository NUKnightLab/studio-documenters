Acceptance of the August 30, 2016 Special Primary Candidate

Board action on agenda item: Chair Burton requested a motion to

supported by Chair Burton, moved to accept the August 30, 2016
Special Primary Candidate list as presented by the Wayne County
Clerk. The motion was adopted unanimously by a voice vote.

 Agenda item:

Approval of Ballot Printing for the August 30, 2016 Special
Primary Election
Jennifer Redmond indicated that the withdrawal deadline for

therefore, this will be a conditional ballot approval.
Board action on agenda item: Chair Burton requested a motion
for the conditional approval of ballot printing for the August 30,
2016 Special Primary Election. Member Sabree, duly supported by
Chair Burton, moved for the conditional approval of ballot printing

adopted unanimously by a voice vote.

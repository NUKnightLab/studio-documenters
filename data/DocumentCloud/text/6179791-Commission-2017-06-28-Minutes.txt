Sandi explained that RPBA will no longer have a line of credit with another institution,
instead they will be the entity providing credit when needed, with interest charged
according to federal regulations.
6. 2018 Budget/Workplan Approval
Commission reviewed the budget/workplan draft. Carolina explained that she will
increase tax rate. Commission requested that the additional funds from raising tax rate
should go to Holiday Decorations, Façade Improvement Program and Landscaping.
Motion to approve 2018 Budget / Workplan with increase to tax rate cap and additional
funds allotted to Holiday Decorations, Façade Improvement Program and Landscaping
(Andersen/ Goldberg). Unanimously approved.
7. 2018 Sole Service Provider
Motion to approve that Rogers Park Business Alliance act as sole service provider for
SSA #24 during the year 2018 (Andersen/Milne). Unanimously approved.

STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
JOINT PANEL
Minutes of the Joint Panel Meeting
Held on November 18, 2014

I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:30 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, Suite N-703, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Paul S. Besson, Member of the State Panel; James Q. Brennwald, Member of the State Panel; Michael Coli,
Member of the State Panel; Albert Washington, Member of the State Panel; Robert M. Gierut, Local Panel
Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Local Panel; Richard Lewis, Member of the Local Panel;
Jerald S. Post, General Counsel; John Hartnett, Joint Panel Chairman; Melissa Mlynski, Executive Director;
Sarah Kerley, Deputy General Counsel, and staff members participated via video conference from the Board’s
Springfield office.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES

Chairman Hartnett called for approval of the minutes of the October 7, 2014 meeting. Member Washington
moved to approve the minutes. Chairman Gierut seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a
voice vote.
IV.

EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR REPORTS

Executive Director Mlynski presented the fiscal, legislative and mediation reports. The Chair and Members
discussed the reports at length.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS

Chairman Hartnett introduced and welcomed to the Board the three new employees. The board then reviewed
the proposed 2015 board meeting dates. Member Besson moved to approve the 2015 board meeting dates.
Member Washington seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
VI.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded that it
was unnecessary.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Hartnett called for a motion to adjourn. Chairman Coli made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and
the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Joint Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations
Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 16th day of December, 2014.

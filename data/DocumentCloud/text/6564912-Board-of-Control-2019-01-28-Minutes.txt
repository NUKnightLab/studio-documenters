 BC2019-75
Department of Workforce Development, recommending an award on RQ43493 and enter into a contract
with Growth Transitions, Inc. dba Maher & Maher (36 – 7) in the amount not-to-exceed $175,000.00 for
Workforce Innovation and Opportunities Act Consultant Services for the period 2/1/2019 – 12/31/2019.

Director, Office of Procurement and Diversity to provide the amounts for the other bids submitted. Trevor
McAleer asked what the approximate amount of the lowest bid submitted was. Dennis Kennedy motioned
to approve the item; Joseph Nanni seconded. Item BC2019-75 was unanimously approved.

 BC2019-78
Department of Health and Human Services/Division of Senior and Adult Services,
a) Submitting an RFP exemption on RQ44597, which will result in an award recommendation to Cleveland
Clergy Alliance in the amount not-to-exceed $300,000.00 for Community Outreach Navigator services to
seniors and adults with disabilities within faith-based communities throughout Cuyahoga County for the
period 2/1/2019 – 12/31/2019.
b) Recommending an award on RQ44597 and enter into a contract with to Cleveland Clergy Alliance in
the amount not-to-exceed $300,000.00 for Community Outreach Navigator services to seniors and adults
with disabilities within faith-based communities throughout Cuyahoga County for the period 2/1/2019 –
12/31/2019.
Funding Source: Health and Human Services Levy Fund
Paul Porter, Division of Senior and Adult Services, presented. Trevor McAleer asked for confirmation that
this is a new project that was presented in 2017 and is being paid from the 2019 budget and asked if there
are goals and objectives included in the contract to measure program success. Dennis Kennedy asked if
this is a new agency that has filed a W-9 with the County; asked if billing will occur on a monthly basis and
if this contract is in a not-to-exceed amount rather than a fixed amount. Dennis Kennedy motioned to
approve the item; Joseph Nanni seconded. Item BC2019-78 was unanimously approved.

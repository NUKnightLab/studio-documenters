MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL MEETING
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
NOVEMBER 10, 1999
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS

NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
A Special Meeting of the Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”) was held on November 10, 1999, in room #S-304 of the State of
Illinois Building, Chicago, Illinois, pursuant to the Illinois Open Meetings Act, 5 ILCS 120.
The following Board members were present telephonically, Robert F. Vickrey, Chairman, and members Gregory Jones,
Stuart Levine and Staci Yandle.
Also in attendance were: Administrator Sergio E. Acosta, Special Counsel to the Board Robert F. Casey, Deputy
Administrator Allan McDonald, Chief Counsel Mareile’ Cusack and other members of the staff.
The meeting was convened at 2:32 p.m. Member Jones moved that the Board retire to Closed Session pursuant to
pursuant to Section 2(c), paragraph (11) of the Open Meetings Act to discuss pending litigation.
Member Levine seconded the motion. The Board adopted the motion by unanimous consent.
The Board adjourned at 2:45 p.m.
Respectfully submitted,

Susan O. Weber
Secretary of the Board

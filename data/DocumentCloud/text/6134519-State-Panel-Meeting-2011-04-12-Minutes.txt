III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD MARCH 15, 2011

Chairman Zimmerman requested modifications of the draft minutes of the March 15, 2011 meeting, to
more completely reflect the Board's deliberations. After the Board discussed the proposal, Member Hade
moved to approve the minutes of the March 15, 2011 meeting, with the requested modifications thereto.
Member Washington seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Zimmerman asked Executive Director Brosnan to present the State Panel administrative actions
report for the period of time from March 1 to March 31, 2011. He presented the report and summarized its
highlights. The Board examined the report and discussed it.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.
Case No. S-RC-11-004
American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31/
State of Illinois, Department of Central Management Services

Chairman Zimmerman called the case. Counsel Kehoe reviewed the issues presented by the exceptions to
Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) Tansino's Recommended Decision and Order (RDO), and recommended
that the Board accept the ALJ's recommendation. After discussion, Member Hade moved to reject the
ALJ's recommendation and remand the case for hearing. Member Kimbrough seconded the motion, and
the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
2.
Case No. S-RC-10-023
Metropolitan Alliance of Police, Chapter No. 259/Village of Roselle
Chairman Zimmerman called the case. Counsel Kazanjian reviewed the issues presented by the exceptions
to ALJ Kehoe's RDO, and recommended that the Board accept in part, and reverse in part, the ALJ's
recommendation. After discussion, Member Kimbrough moved to accept the ALJ's recommendation as
written. Member Washington seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

 3.
Case No. S-CB-09-005
Billy McCaskill/American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31
Chairman Zimmerman called the case, noting that Executive Director Brosnan issued a dismissal on
November 16, 2010, with an appeal filed. Chairman Zimmerman ascertained that there were no questions
or objections regarding Counsel Hamburg-Gal's recommendation to accept the Executive Director's
dismissal. Member Hade moved to accept the Executive Director's dismissal. Member Washington
seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
4.
Case No. S-CB-10-043 and Case No. S-CB-10-045
David W. Jarvis/Chicago Regional Council of Carpenters, Local No. 792
Chairman Zimmerman called the cases, noting that Executive Director Brosnan issued dismissals on
October 25, 2010, with an appeal filed. Chairman Zimmerman ascertained that there were no questions or
objections regarding Counsel Owen's recommendation to accept the Executive Director's dismissal.
Member Hade moved to accept the Executive Director's dismissal. Member Washington seconded the
motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
5.

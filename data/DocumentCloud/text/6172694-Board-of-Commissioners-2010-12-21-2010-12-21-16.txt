ITEM NO. C2

December 15, 2010

ADOPTION OF REVISED INVESTMENT POLICY
To the Honorable Board of Commissioners:
RECOMMENDATION
It is recommended that the Board of Commissioners approve and adopt the Revised Investment
Policy.
FUNDING
N/A
EXPLANATION
This revised Investment Policy expands authorization of eligible Authorized Investments to
include the following security types:
1. State and Local Municipal Bonds
2. Illinois Housing Development Authority Mortgage Participation Certificates
Additionally, appropriate risk control procedures have been incorporated for both types of
securities.
This policy revision has been reviewed and approved by the CHA Investment Committee. This
policy takes effect immediately upon approval.

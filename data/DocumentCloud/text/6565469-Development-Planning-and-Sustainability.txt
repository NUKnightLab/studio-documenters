AN EMERGENCY ORDINANCE Authorizing the Director of Capital
Projects to issue a permit to The Convention and Visitors Bureau of
Greater Cleveland, Inc. to encroach into the public right-of-way of West
29th Street by installing, using, and maintaining public art to enhance
the Lakefront West Bikeway Trail; and authorizing the Director of Public
Works to enter into one or more license agreements with The
Convention and Visitors Bureau of Greater Cleveland, Inc. to install and
maintain public art on City-owned property in an area south of the
Cleveland Memorial Shoreway between West 49th Street and West 54th
Street on City-owned property to enhance the Lakefront West Bikeway
Trail.

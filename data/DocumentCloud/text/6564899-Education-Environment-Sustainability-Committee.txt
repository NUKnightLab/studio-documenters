1. CALL TO ORDER
Chairwoman Simon called the meeting to order at 3:08 p.m.
2. ROLL CALL
Ms. Simon asked Deputy Clerk Carter to call the roll. Committee members Simon, Brown,
Jones, Stephens and Brady were in attendance and a quorum was determined.
Councilmember Miller was also in attendance.

There were no public comments given.

A motion was made by Mr. Brady, seconded by Ms. Brown and approved by unanimous
vote to approve the minutes from the July 17, 2019 meeting.

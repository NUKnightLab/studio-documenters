WESTERN AVENUE SOUTH TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 47)
Request approval of corrections to the description of the properties authorized for
acquisition at approximately 2620 W. Grace Street and an approximately 33 feet

NEW BUSINESS
A.

73RD/UNIVERSITY TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 5)
Request authority for the Department of Housing and Economic Development to
negotiate a redevelopment agreement with Comer Science and Education
Foundation for redevelopment of the property located on the 7200 block of S.
South Chicago Avenue in the 73rd/University Tax Increment Financing
Redevelopment Project Area, and to recommend to the City Council of the City
of Chicago the designation of Comer Science and Education Foundation as
Developer.
Project Manager: Lisa Washington
Motioned by Newsome, seconded by Buford. Approved 11-0. Yeas: Buford,
Duque, Garner, Logan, Newsome, Olander, Perez, Sahli, Stein, Ward and Carrott.
12-CDC-11

B.

MADISON/AUSTIN TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 28)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Housing and Economic
Development=s intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Salmakil, LLC for the
disposition of the city property located at 4711 W. Madison Street in the
Madison/Austin Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, to
request alternative proposals and to approve the sale of the property to Salmakil,
LLC if no responsive alternative proposals are received; and request authority for
the Department of Housing and Economic Development to negotiate a
redevelopment agreement with Salmakil, LLC for redevelopment of the city
property plus the developer property located at 4703-09 and 4725-27 W. Madison
Street and to recommend to the City Council of the City of Chicago the
designation of Salmakil, LLC as Developer if no responsive alternative proposals
are received.
Project Manager: Mary Bonome
Motioned by Ward, seconded by Garner. Approved 11-0. Yeas: Buford, Duque,
Garner, Logan, Newsome, Olander, Perez, Sahli, Stein, Ward and Carrott.
12-CDC-12

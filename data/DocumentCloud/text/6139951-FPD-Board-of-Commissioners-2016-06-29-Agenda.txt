Pursuant to Forest Preserve District Code of Ordinances, public testimony will be permitted at regular and

testimony germane to a specific item(s) on the meeting agenda, and the testimony must not exceed three

and Journal of Proceedings as prepared by the Clerk of the Board.

REPORT
Department: Office of the General Superintendent
Request: Receive & File
Report Title: Cook County Forest Preserve District Disparity Study 2015
Report Period: July 2009 - July 2014
Summary: Colette Holt & Associates was retained by Cook County, Illinois (“County”), the Cook
County Hospital Systems (“CCHS”), and the Forest Preserve District of Cook County (the “Forest
Preserve”) to perform a study of possible disparities on the basis of race and gender in access to their
prime contracting and associated subcontracting opportunities. In addition to the report prepared for the
county, which is coterminous with the Forest Preserve, additional data has been collected and analyzed
for the Forest Preserve.

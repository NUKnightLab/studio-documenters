MUNICIPAL EMPLOYEES’ ANNUITY AND BENEFIT FUND OF CHICAGO
INVESTMENT MEETING NO. 2014-09-02
SEPTEMBER 11, 2014
An investment meeting of the Retirement Board of the Municipal Employees’ Annuity
and Benefit Fund was held in the Fund offices on September 11, 2014. The meeting was
called to order at 9:10 a.m.

Global Economic Update – UBS Global Asset Management
Presenters:
Michael Dow, CFA, CPA, Head of Sovereign Research
and Emerging Market Corporate Debt
Christina De’Giuseppe, CFA, Account Manager
Kitty Martin, Client Advisor
Mr. Dow updated the Board regarding the broad economic environment, monetary policy
initiatives, credit cycles, labor markets, etc. Following his prepared remarks, the Board
engaged Mr. Dow in a lengthy conversation regarding such topics.
Real Estate Market Update
Presenters:
Alasdair Cripps, Senior Marketing Director, Mesirow Real Estate
Thomas F. Hynes, Senior Marketing Director, Mesirow Real Estate
Mr. Cripps updated the Board regarding its investment in Mesirow Financial Real Estate
Value Fund, L.P. Following that update, Mr. Cripps presented the Board with a brief
update regarding the current investment environment within various segments of the real
estate market—apartment, retail, office and industrial.
Derrick E. McGavic, Managing Principal, Newport Capital Partners
Lynnette Mays, Director, Newport Capital Partners

 Mr. McGavic and Ms. Mays updated the Board regarding its investment in DV Urban
Realty Partners Fund I. Mr. McGavic highlighted progress that has been made in exiting
many of the underlying properties.

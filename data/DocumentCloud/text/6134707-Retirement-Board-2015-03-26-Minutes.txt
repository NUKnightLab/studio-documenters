• Discussion was held regarding a request from an annuitant to have healthcare premiums withheld
from his pension and directed to a healthcare administrator, other than the City of Chicago. Such
treatment would allow the annuitant to receive the $3,000 public safety officer deduction on his
federal taxes, despite non-utilization of the City of Chicago's postretirement healthcare plan. The
current administration of this pension deduction and related tax benefit was discussed. It was
recommended that this annuitant's request be granted and that other research be conducted on
administration of similar programs at other public pension funds.

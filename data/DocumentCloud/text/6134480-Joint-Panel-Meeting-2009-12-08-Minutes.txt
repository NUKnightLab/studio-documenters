JOINT PANEL
Minutes of the Joint Panel Meeting
Held on December 8, 2009

II.

PRESENT

Jacalyn J. Zimmerman, Joint Panel Chairman; Michael Coli, Member of the State Panel; Michael Hade,
Member of the State Panel; Albert Washington, Member of the State Panel; Charles Anderson, Member
of the Local Panel; Edward Sadlowski, Member of the Local Panel; John F. Brosnan, Executive
Director; and staff members. John F. Brosnan acted as counsel to the Board at this meeting.
III.

BOARD ACTIONS

Member Hade made a motion to go into executive session to discuss personnel matters. Member
Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote. Subsequently, at
the end of the discussion, Member Hade made a motion to leave executive session. Member Anderson
seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote. Member Hade then made a
motion to return to open session. Member Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it
unanimously, by a voice vote. Member Hade made a motion to hire Jerald S. Post as the Board's general
counsel. Member Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it by a vote of five in favor, one
against.
IV.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Zimmerman called for a motion to adjourn. Member Coli made a motion to adjourn the
meeting, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Joint Panel of the Illinois Labor
Relations Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 13th day of April, 2010.

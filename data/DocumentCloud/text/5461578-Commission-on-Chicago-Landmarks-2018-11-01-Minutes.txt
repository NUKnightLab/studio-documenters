The following projects were reviewed by the PRC:
1. 1060 W. Addison
44th Ward
Wrigley Field
Proposed revised design of the northwest and southeast elevations and roof deck
areas.
Action:

Approved unanimously with the following condition:
1. The proposed work to the northwest corner of the ballpark
eliminating the Player’s Family Lounge are approved as shown on
Populous drawings dated 9/20/18. Enlarged details of the brick piers
shall be included in the permit plans;
2. The new elevator enclosure at the southeast corner upper deck,
consistent with the approved Master Plan, is approved. Enlarged
dimensioned plans, elevations, cut sheets etc… of the two new
elevators proposed in the bleachers, the new elevator on the
southeast corner upper deck, as well as for the proposed changes in
the elevator enclosure at the upper deck near the main gate shall be
provided as part of the permit application. The size and height of all
elevator enclosures must be as minimal as possible;
3. The proposed beige color, consistent with the previously installed
elevator enclosures at the upper deck, is approved for the upper deck
enclosures. The proposed green metal paneling is approved for the
bleacher elevator enclosures, consistent with the previously installed
elevator enclosures at the bleachers;
4. Enlarged details of the proposed replacement of the existing chainlink fencing at the upper deck shall be included in the permit plans.
The cross fencing panel design of this fence shall match the pattern

 of the cross fencing panels for the railings at the roof deck and
mezzanine levels;
5. The 9” tall signs for the “Cubs Store” and “Club & Suite Entry”
along the southwest corner are approved as proposed. Dimensioned
details of the “Wintrust” sign above the southeast gate shall be
included as part of a sign application permit;
6. The 6’-deep balconies at the Stadium Club along the east elevation,
south of the bleachers, are similar in appearance to the balconies
shown in the approved Master Plan and are approved as proposed;
7. Prior to the permit application, enlarged details of the metal cladding
and window system for the Stadium Club enclosure that will match
as closely as possible for the green metal framing and opening
proportions and profiles of the ballpark shall be submitted for review
and approval by Historic Preservation staff;
8. A roof plan of the Stadium Club enclosure shall be included in the
permit plans to ensure that any rooftop equipment is set back to be
minimally visible from the public rights-of-way;
9. All materials, colors, and finishes to be a continuation of those
approved in previous phases of work; and,
10. All conditions of approval from the previous review of the overall
master project will continue to be applicable.
2. 4649 N. Broadway
46th Ward
Uptown Square District
Proposed alterations of front façade of existing 2-story masonry non-contributing
building including new windows, storefronts, and replacement of deteriorated exterior
wythe of brick with matching brick.
Action:

Approved unanimously with the following conditions:
1. As proposed, the exterior wythe of deteriorated brick will be
replaced with new red brick matching the color, texture, and
appearance of the historic brick, per the brick sample viewed on-site
(McNeer Red); and,
2. As proposed, the existing terra cotta will be repaired and retained
whenever possible. A reconstructed second floor sill and any
required terra cotta replication is proposed to be glass fiber
reinforced concrete (GFRC) to match the historic color, texture and
reflectivity. Staff shall be notified to view and approve GFRC
samples on-site prior to order and installation.

3. 1848 N. Lincoln
43rd Ward
Old Town Triangle District
Proposed rehabilitation of a raised, three-story building including a rooftop deck and
access enclosures, rear addition, and construction of a new garage utilizing an
existing curb-cut.
Action:

Item was deferred at the reqiest of the applicant; no action was taken by
the Committee.

MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING - VIDEO
OPEN SESSION
January 28, 2016
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
Chicago, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
On Thursday, January 28, 2016 a Regular Meeting - Video of the Illinois Gaming Board
(“Board”) was held on the 5th floor at 160 N. LaSalle at the Michael J. Bilandic Building,
Chicago, Illinois. The Open Session on Video Gaming started at approximately 9:54 A.M.
Present were Members Lee Gould, Hector Alejandre and Member Dee Robinson. Chairman
Don Tracy and Member Dunn participated via telephone.
APPROVAL OF MINUTES: Open Session
Member Robinson moved that the Board approve the open session minutes of its Regular
Meeting of December 16, 2015 with an amendment to the vote take during the Video Gaming
Location Licensee Items. The vote to approve Tucker Station, LLC should be clarified from a 40 vote to a 3-1 vote to approve this location. Member Alejandre seconded the motion. The
Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
CHAIRMAN'S COMMENTS –
Chairman Tracy stated that he could not attend the open session meeting in person due to
employment purposes related to the business of the public body.
BOARD MEMBER'S COMMENTS –
Member Dunn stated that he could not attend the open session meeting in person due to personal
illness.
ADMINISTRATOR'S COMMENTS –
Administrator Ostrowski gave a review of the 2015 calendar year achievements.

ADMINISTRATIVE REQUESTS FOR HEARING, REQUESTS TO REAPPLY

Springfield Gas and Wash I, LLC
Phat & Shai, Inc. d/b/a PK Package Liquor & Gaming

the Requests for Hearing as moot. Member Gould seconded the motion. The motion passed

unanimously by roll call vote.

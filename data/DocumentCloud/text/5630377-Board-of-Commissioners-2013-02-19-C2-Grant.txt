BACKGROUND AND EXPLANATION

In January 2010, the CHA was awarded a two year $250,000 award from ICJIA through the
Edward Byme Recovery Act Grant Program. The purpose of the funding was to implement
community based youth violence prevention through Gang Resistance Education and Training
and the Youth Courts. The Altgeld-Riverside Community (ARC) Partnership
created a community partnership and increased resident awareness of violence and burglary
prevention at community schools. In 2012, CHA was invited to apply for le?over ICJ IA Byme
Recovery Act Grant Program funds to continue and enhance the ARC Partnership Program. On
December 11, 2012, the CHA received of?cial written notice of an award of $282,200.00 from
the ICJ IA through the Edward Byme Recovery Act Grant Program. CHA is required to provide
matching funds of $94,067 (mostly in-kind).

3. Draft Employee Handbook
The Committee then reviewed the draft of the Employee Handbook that included provisions
proposed by trustees in a draft presented to the Board in October, 2017, and other policies that had
been adopted by the Fund and currently in place. The trustees discussed that the Fund generally
followed the County’s employment practices and made they made recommendations relating to time
entry processes, holidays, vacation and other matters. The trustees noted that the County had very
recently revised its employee handbook and suggested that Fund staff refer to that resource when
revising the draft reviewed by the Committee.
4. Purchasing and Procurement Policies
The trustees reviewed and discussed the materials related to purchasing and procurement and noted
that the information needed to be revised to reflect the practices in place. Interim Executive Director
White stated that he and Fund staff would meet with the Finance Department to formalize
procedures for purchasing and procurement.
5. Old Business/New Business
There was no old business or new business discussed
6. Adjournment
There being no further business before the Committee, it was moved by Trustee Fitzgerald and
seconded by Trustee DeGraff that the meeting be adjourned.

MOTION ADOPTED UNANIMOUSLY BY VOICE VOTE

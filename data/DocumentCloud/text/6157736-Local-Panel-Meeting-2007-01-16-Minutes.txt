II.

PRESENT

Jackie Gallagher, Chairman; Edward E. Sadlowski, Member of the Board; John F. Brosnan, Executive
Director; and staff members.

III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD DECEMBER 12, 2006

Chairman Gallagher called for approval of the minutes of the December 12, 2006 meeting. Member
Sadlowski made a motion for approval of the minutes. Chairman Gallagher seconded the motion. The
Board approved the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gallagher asked Executive Director Brosnan to present the Local Panel administrative actions
for the period of time from December 1 through December 31, 2006. He distributed the report to the
Board for review, and the Board examined it. The Executive Director summarized the report's highlights,
noting that during the month, the agency issued one certification pursuant to a majority interest petition.
The Executive Director also noted that with regard to unfair labor practice cases, the agency issued
fourteen administrative dismissals.
V.

OTHER BOARD MATTERS

The Chairman noted that there was very little to report regarding legislative matters, and there was no
fiscal report this month. Thereafter, the Board discussed whether there was a need for executive session,
concluding it was not necessary at this time.
VI.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gallagher called for a motion to adjourn. Member Sadlowski made a motion to adjourn the
meeting, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Member of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor
Relations Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 6th day of February, 2007.

3.

Curb Appeal Rebate Program
a. Weidl presented a $3,545 rebate request from Preston Bradley Center, located at 941 W.
Lawrence Avenue.
i.
Weidl explained that the preferred fence contractor’s quote is $1,148 more
expensive than the competitive quote, but they are open to using either contractor.
ii.
Tuohy recommened approving a 50% rebate of the total project cost using the less
expensive fence quote.
Motion to approve rebate for Preston Bradley Center in the amount of $2,971.
Motion: Showers
Second: Eick-Hutzel
VOTE: Approve – All
Motion Passes

b.

Weidl presented a $3,368.69 rebate request from Human Citizen Workplace, located at
4101 North Broadway.
i.
Tuohy commented that $3,752 of the quote is for a side yard fence which may not
be very visible from the public way.
ii.
Eick-Hutzel commented that one quote calls for a crushed granite patio while the
other calls for pavers.
iii.
Showers asked Weidl to discuss the project in more detail with the applicant to
provide a better explaination of what will be visible from the public way for this
project.

Application to be tabled until September 25th, 2018 meeting.

 

  
4.

Security Rebate Program
a. Weidl presented a $582.95 rebate request from Human Citizen Workplace, located at 4101
North Broadway.
i.
The project includes a new, hard-wired, four camera security system.
ii.
Hoang commented that the costs seems in-line with this type of system.
iii.
Tuohy commented that it is beneficial to have a security camera at a corner
business that can monitor a street intersection.
Motion to approve grant request from Human Citizen Workplace for $582.95.
Motion: Showers
Second: Hoang
VOTE: Approve – All
Motion Passes

b.

Weidl presented a revised $4,175.50 rebate request from Buena Terrace Condominium
Association, located at 835 W Montrose Avenue.
i.
The project was origianlly approved for a $2,744.50 rebate, however project costs
have increased due to the contractor no longer carrying TVI cameras and
equipment. It was also determined that a high lift would be needed for
installation.
ii.
Showers commented that this rebate amount is much larger than most rebate
requests.
iii.
Tuohy commented that it is the contractor’s and applicant’s responsibility to
provide an accurate quote upfront, and not revise quotes after approval.
Motion to approve revised rebate request from Buena Terrace Condomonium Association.
Motion: Showers
Second: Hoang
VOTE: Approve – None
Oppose – All
Motion Fails

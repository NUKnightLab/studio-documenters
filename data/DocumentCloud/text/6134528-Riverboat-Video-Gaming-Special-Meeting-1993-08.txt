MINUTES
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
AUGUST 17, 1993

On August 17, 1993 and pursuant to Public Notice issued by the Board in
conformity with Illinois Revised Statutes Chapter 102, Paragraphs 41.02 and
42.02, the Illinois Gaming Board toured proposed riverboat casino docksites in
four Illinois communities. The Public Notice was issued by the instruction of
Chairman William J. Kunkle, Jr. and was duly and timely given to each Board
Member and the general public.
The following Board Members were present: William J. Kunkle, Jr.,
Chairman; and Board Members J. Thomas Johnson, Gayl M. Pyatt, J. William Roberts
and Robert F. Vickrey.
Also in attendance were Administrator Morton E. Friedman and Deputy
Administrator Joseph McQuaid; the media and the general public.
The Board viewed proposed riverboat casino docksites in the following
communities:
Elgin, Illinois
West Dundee, Illinois
Antioch, Illinois
Moline, Illinois.
It is noted that no testimony was received by the Board nor was any other
business of the Board transacted or discussed.

Executive Session Item No. 2
RESOLUTION NO. 2010-CHA-92
WHEREAS, The Board of Commissioners has reviewed the Board Letter dated August 17,
2010 requesting authorization to: 1) execute a Land Transfer Agreement between
the Chicago Housing Authority and Sinai Health Systems; 2) submit acquisition
and disposition applications to the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban
Development (HUD); and 3) execute any documents necessary or appropriate to
implement the foregoing.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE CHICAGO HOUSING AUTHORITY
THAT,

The Board of Commissioners authorizes the Chief Executive Officer or his
Designee to: 1) Execute a Land Transfer Agreement between the Chicago
Housing Authority (CHA) and Sinai Health Systems; 2) Submit acquisition and
disposition applications to the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban
Development (HUD); and 3) Execute any documents necessary or appropriate for
the implementation of a multi-phased, mixed-use redevelopment project in the
North Lawndale Neighborhood, subject to any necessary approvals of HUD, the
City of Chicago and the State of Illinois.

 III.

OLD BUSINESS
A.

BELMONT/SHEFFIELD REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 44)
Request authority to designate BT-Diplomat, LLC as the successful respondent
and to reject all other responses to the Request for Proposals issued by the
Department of Housing and Economic Development on February 14, 2011 for the
purchase and redevelopment of the former Diplomat Hotel located at 3208 N.
Sheffield Avenue in the Belmont/Sheffield Redevelopment Project Area and to
recommend to the City Council of the City of Chicago the sale of the property to
the successful respondent.
Project Manager: Tamra Collins
Motioned by Sahli, seconded by Logan. Approved 12-0. Yeas: Carrott, Buford,
Duque, Kostiner, Logan, Mooney, Newsome, Olander, Perez, Sahli, Todman and
Ward.
12-CDC-29

IV.

NEW BUSINESS
A.

24TH/MICHIGAN TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 2)
Request authority for the Department of Housing and Economic Development to
negotiate a redevelopment agreement with BSB Holdings, LLC for
redevelopment of the property located at 2337 S. Michigan Avenue in the
24th/Michigan Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and to
recommend to the City Council of the City of Chicago the designation of BSB
Holdings, LLC as Developer.
Project Manager: Essie Escareno-Banks
Motioned by Duque, seconded by Olander. Approved 12-0. Yeas: Carrott,
Buford, Duque, Kostiner, Logan, Mooney, Newsome, Olander, Perez, Sahli,
Todman and Ward.

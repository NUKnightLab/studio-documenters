NOTICE OF SPECIAL MEETING OF THE DETROIT CHARTER
REVISION COMMISSION
In accordance with Section 5(4) of the Michigan Open Meetings Act, MCL 15.265(4), the

#2210, Detroit, Michigan 48213. The telephone number is (313) 579-4100. The Public is
invited and encouraged to attend.
Additional information regarding this meeting may be obtained from the Office of the

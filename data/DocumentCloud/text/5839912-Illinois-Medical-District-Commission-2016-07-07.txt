June 30, 2016

NOTICE OF COMMUNITY MEETING: IMD MASTER PLAN
The Illinois Medical District Commission (IMDC) will host a community meeting
on Thursday, July 7, 2016 at 5:30 PM. During the meeting, IMDC staff will
give a brief presentation highlighting features of the 2016 IMD Master Plan.
Following the presentation, attendees will have the opportunity to provide
feedback on the plan, and may also ask questions of the IMDC. The meeting will
take place at the IMDC administrative offices, located at 2100 W. Harrison
Street.
All interested parties are invited to attend the meeting.

New Business

1.

Communication dated November 26, 2018 (received December 19th) requesting
Commission approval of a one-year, sub-award grant agreement between the
Charter County of Wayne and the Detroit Police Department (DPD) in the
amount of $303,570 under the Police-Prosecutor Partnership Initiative; the
County Executive advises that the Police-Prosecutor Partnership Initiative grant
project will utilize a grand jury to target non-fatal shootings and homicides in the
DPD’s 9th Precinct; the term of the agreement is from December 1, 2018 through
September 30, 2019, and the agreement may be extended for up to 18 months
contingent upon U.S. Department of Justice Bureau of Justice Assistance
approval; the cost of the agreement will be charged to Account No. 101 22926
817000 (Police-Prosecution Initiative). (2019-93-007)

2.

Communication dated October 12, 2018 (received December 27th) requesting
Commission approval of a memorandum of understanding (MOU) between the
Charter County of Wayne and the United States Marshals Service in the amount
of $30,000 to direct and coordinate a permanent Regional Fugitive Task Force
consisting of federal, state and local law enforcement authorities for the purpose
of locating and apprehending fugitives; the County Executive advises that the
mission of the task force is to investigate and arrest, as a part of joint law
enforcement operations, those who have active state and federal warrants for
their arrest; the term of the MOU is from October 1, 2018 through September 30,
2019; the revenue from the MOU will be deposited into Account No. 101 30794
626000 (PA511 Apprehension Unit). (2019-71-016)

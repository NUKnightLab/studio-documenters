The Executive Director reviewed with the Board the following matters.
•

Discussion concerning the upcoming Trustee Election. The terms of Trustees representing (1) police
officers / investigators or ranks equivalent thereto and (2) annuitants are expiring November 30, 2015.
In accordance with the Election Rules, and the time table set forth therein; the Board, with a quorum
present, is required to conduct a Petition Sufficiency meeting on Thursday October 1, 2015. Copies
of election rules, the proposed timeframe for the election, and a draft of the newsletter to members
informing them of the upcoming election were distributed.

•

Further discussion was held concerning the date election petitions shall be available for pick-up.
Through general agreement, petitions for the 2015 Trustee Election shall be available on Thursday
August 27, 2015. It was also agreed that Thursday, October 1, 2015, would be the sufficiency
meeting.

•

The Board’s physician has recommended testing to be completed on certain members in receipt of
disability benefits.

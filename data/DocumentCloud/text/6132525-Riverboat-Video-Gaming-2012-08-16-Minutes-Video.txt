MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING - VIDEO
OPEN SESSION
August 16, 2012
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
On Thursday, August 16, 2012 a Regular Meeting - Video of the Illinois Gaming Board
(“Board”) was held on the 3rd floor at 160 N. LaSalle, Chicago, Illinois. The Open Session on
Video Gaming started at approximately 10:41 A.M. Present were Chairman Aaron Jaffe,
Members Lee Gould, Michael Holewinski and Maribeth Vander Weele.
APPROVAL OF MINUTES – Open Session
Member Gould moved that the Board approve the open session minutes of its Regular
Meeting of July 19, 2012. Member Vander Weele seconded the motion. The Board approved
the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
BOARD POLICY ITEMS
• Proposed Rules – Section 110, 270 and 930
Member Holewinski moved that the Board authorize staff to submit proposals to
amend the Board’s rules on video gaming: Section 110 regarding Definitions;
Section 270 regarding Duties of Licensed Video Gaming Locations; and Section
930 regarding Prior Registration for First Notice Filing with the Secretary of
State upon final review and approval by the Administrator. Member Gould
seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
PUBLIC COMMENTARY –
Joe Sellens, Scientific Games International, Inc.
Mr. Sellens gave a brief update on the progress for the central communication system for video
gaming.
MANUFACTURER, DISTRIBUTOR AND SUPPLIER LICENSEE ITEMS
•

American Gaming & Electronics, Inc., American Vending Sales, Inc., and H.
Betti Industries, Inc. – Distributor Licenses
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Vander Weele moved that the Board renew
the Distributor Licenses of American Gaming & Electronics, Inc., American
Vending Sales, Inc., and H. Betti Industries, Inc. effective August 16, 2012, for a

 period of one year, expiring in August, 2013. Member Holewinski seconded the
motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
•

Moss Gaming, Inc. – Distributor License
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Gould moved that the Board renew the
Distributor License of Moss Gaming, Inc. effective August 16, 2012 for a period
of one year, expiring in August 2013, contingent upon Moss Gaming, Inc.
submitting to the Board an executed copy of the waiver of debt covenants not met
at June 30, 2012 by its lender within sixty (60) days from August 16, 2012.
Member Vander Weele seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion
unanimously by roll call vote.

•

Aristocrat Technologies, Inc., Elite Casino Products, Inc., IGT, Incredible
Technologies, Inc., MTD Gaming, Inc., and WMS Gaming Inc. – Manufacturer
and Distributor Licenses
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Holewinski moved that the Board renew the
Manufacturer and Distributor Licenses of Aristocrat Technologies, Inc., Elite
Casino Products, Inc., IGT, Incredible Technologies, Inc., MTD Gaming, Inc.,
and WMS Gaming Inc. effective August 16, 2012, for a period of one year,
expiring in August, 2013. Member Vander Weele seconded the motion. The Board
approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.

•

Bally Gaming, Inc. – Manufacturer, Distributor and Supplier Licenses
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Vander Weele moved that the Board renew
the Manufacturer, Distributor and Supplier Licenses of Bally Gaming, Inc.,
effective August 16, 2012, for a period of one year, expiring in August, 2013.
Member Gould seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by
roll call vote.

•

JCM American Corporation – Supplier License
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Gould moved that the Board renew the
Supplier License of JCM American Corporation, effective August 16, 2012, for a
period of one year, expiring in August, 2013. Member Vander Weele seconded the
motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.

TERMINAL OPERATOR LICENSEE ITEMS
•

Donico Gaming, LLC
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Holewinski moved that the Board approve
Donico Gaming, LLC for a Terminal Operator License effective August 16, 2012
for a period of one year, expiring in August 2013. Member Gould seconded the
motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.

 •

Bee-J Amusements, Inc.
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Vander Weele moved that the Board deny
the Terminal Operator License application of Bee-J Amusements, Inc. Member

TECHNICIAN & TERMINAL HANDLER LICENSEE ITEMS
•

Technician and Terminal Handler Licenses – Approvals & Denials
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Gould moved that the Board approve 23
applications for Technician Licenses and 105 applications for Terminal Handler
Licenses. Member Vander Weele seconded the motion. The Board approved the
motion unanimously by roll call vote.

VIDEO GAMING LOCATION LICENSEE ITEMS
•

Video Gaming Location Licenses – Approvals & Denials
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Holewinksi moved that the Board approve
90 applications for Video Gaming Location Licenses. Further, Member
Holewinski moved that the Administrator issue a Notice of Denial to the
following Video Gaming Location License Applicants:
 Boss’, Inc. d/b/a Boss’ Lounge
 Cabin Fever, Inc. d/b/a Cabin Fever
 James C. Clanton, Sr. d/b/a Leisure Lounge
 Jim and Pat’s Place of Jamestown, Inc. d/b/a Jim & Pat’s Place
 CLAM 1, Inc. d/b/a Cam’s Place
Member Gould seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously
by roll call vote.

ADMINISTRATIVE HEARINGS
•

Requests for Hearing – JAD Gaming, LLC and Alton Sports Tap, Inc.
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Vander Weele moved that the requests for
hearing of JAD Gaming, LLC and Alton Sports Tap, Inc. be denied, as neither
has offered facts that establish a prima facie case. Member Holewinski seconded
the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.

At 11:08 A.M. Member Gould motioned to adjourn and Member Vander Weele seconded the
motion. All Members voted in favor of adjournment.
Respectfully submitted,

Mary C. Boruta

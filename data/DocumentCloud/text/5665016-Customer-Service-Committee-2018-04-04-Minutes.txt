18-0082 Integrated Marketing Campaign Pres.4.4.18 revised

Presenter: Jerry O'Shea of Mort Crim Communications; Jermaine Dickens of DMC
Strategies; Tiffany Jones, Public Affairs Director; and Bryan Peckinpaugh, Public
Affairs Manager
Commissioner Kinloch asked if the drainage information will be available at City Hall,
libraries, and other various community groups and agencies.
Tiffany Jones, PA Director stated that the Mayor's team will provide a news letter on a
consistent basis. Customers will also be directed to contact the department via email
or by telephone. Bryan Pechinpaugh added there is also partnership with non-profit
organizations for community outreach, service credits, and job/assistance fair
programs.
Commissioner Blackmon asked if the communications team would handled media.
Tiffany Jones replied that Mort Crim Communications Inc. (MCCI) have made contact
with the media outlets to arrange education 101 meetings.
Commissioner Forte inquired about media outreach to 105.9 Kiss-FM.

(WARD 46)

redevelopment agreement with Farpoint Acquisitions, LLC and Uptown HM
Investments, LLC, and/or a related entity, for redevelopment of the Uptown
Theater, a Chicago Landmark located at 4816 N. Broadway Avenue in the
Lawrence/Broadway Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and
to recommend to the City Council of the City of Chicago the designation of
Farpoint Acquisitions, LLC and Uptown HM Investments, LLC, and/or a related
entity, as Developer if no responsive alternative proposals are received; and
request authority to advertise the Department of Planning and Development’s
intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Farpoint Acquisitions, LLC and
Uptown HM Investments, LLC, and/or a related entity, for the disposition of the
property located at 1130 W. Lawrence Avenue also in the Lawrence/Broadway
TIF, to request alternative proposals and to approve the sale of the property to
Farpoint Acquisitions, LLC and Uptown HM Investments, LLC, and/or a related
entity, if no responsive alternative proposals are received.
Project Manager: William Grams
Motioned by Newsome, seconded by Alphonse. Approved 8-0. Yeas: Alphonse,
Cepeda, Curtis, Davis, Newsome, Reifman, Rhodes and Perez.

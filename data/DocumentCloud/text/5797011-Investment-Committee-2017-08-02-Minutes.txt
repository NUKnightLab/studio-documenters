the Board to remain the same. The motion passed by a unanimous vote.

Motion by: Trustee Sheehan– Seconded by Trustee Cetlinski, to acknowledge
receipt of the correspondence by the Board’s advisor: Banyan Realty Advisors with
regards to the Fort Shelby loan modifications pursuant to the correspondence dated
July 24th, 2017 as revised August 2nd, 2017. Furthermore, with respect to the Fort
Shelby Apartments and the condo conversion that the terms as previously approved by
the Board be implemented subject to the following amendments:
1. That the loan maturity be extended to July 31, 2019
2. The principal repayment will be from release of funds at eighty percent (80%)
of gross sales price with those divided between “Invest Detroit” and the Board at
an 80/20 or 20/80 schedule and that the first unit be released for no
consideration to fund interest and renovations and reserve on seven percent
(7%) from gross sales on each subsequent closing
3. Borrower must maintain reserve of minimum two months interest plus
$100,000
4. Five percent (5%) of the gross sales price of each closing to repay the
incremental investment to “Invest Detroit” and a new equity investor as noted in
the correspondence and approve execution of the lenders consent and record
the condominium documents as approved by the Board’s Special Legal Counsel
Jeffrey Kraft. The motion passed by a unanimous vote.

STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
JOINT PANEL
Minutes of the Joint Panel Meeting
Held on October 16, 2018
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 9:30 A.M.— 160 N. LaSalle Street, Room S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

John Hartnett, Chairman; John Samolis, Member of the State Panel; Keith Snyder, Member of the State Panel; Robert
Gierut, Chairman of the Local Panel; Charles Anderson, Member of the Local Panel; Angela Thomas, Member of the
Local Panel; Kimberly Stevens, Executive Director; Helen J. Kim, General Counsel; Anna Hamburg-Gal, Associate
General Counsel; and staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD July 10, 2018

Chairman Hartnett called for approval of the minutes of the July 10, 2018, meeting. Chairman Gierut moved to approve
the minutes. Member Samolis seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Hartnett asked Executive Director Stevens to present the Executive Director’s reports. She presented the
report and summarized the highlights. Chairman Hartnett then asked General Counsel Kim to present the litigation
report. She presented the report and summarized the highlights.
V.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

Member Anderson made a motion to leave open session. Member Thomas seconded the motion, and the Board passed
it unanimously, by a voice vote. Member Samolis made a motion to go into executive session to discuss personnel
matters pursuant to 5 ILCS 120/2(c)(1). Subsequently, at the end of the discussion, Member Thomas made a motion
to leave executive session. Member Snyder seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice
vote. Member Anderson then made a motion to return to open session. Member Thomas seconded the motion, and
the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VI.

PUBLIC COMMENT

No members of the public sought to make comments.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Hartnett called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson moved to adjourn the meeting, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Joint Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board,
in Chicago, Illinois, this 11th day of December 2018.

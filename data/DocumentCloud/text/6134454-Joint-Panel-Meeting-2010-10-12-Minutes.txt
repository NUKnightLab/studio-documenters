III.

BOARD ACTIONS

Member Washington made a motion to leave open session. Chairman Zimmerman seconded the
motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote. Member Washington made a motion to
go into executive session to discuss personnel/hiring matters pursuant to 5 ILCS 120/2(c)(1). Chairman
Zimmerman seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote. Subsequently,
at the end of the discussion, Member Coli made a motion to leave executive session. Member
Washington seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote. Member Coli
then made a motion to return to open session. Member Washington seconded the motion, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Zimmerman called for a motion to adjourn. Member Washington made a motion to adjourn
the meeting, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Joint Panel of the Illinois Labor
Relations Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 9th day of November, 2010.

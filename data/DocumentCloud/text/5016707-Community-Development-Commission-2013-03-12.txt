 III.

NEW BUSINESS
A.

87TH/COTTAGE GROVE TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 8)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Housing and Economic
Development’s intention to enter into a negotiated sale with New Life Covenant
S. E. Church for the disposition of the property located on the 1000 and 1100
blocks of West 76th Street and the 7600 and 7700 blocks of South Greenwood
Avenue in the 87th/Cottage Grove Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment
Project Area, to request alternative proposals, and to approve the sale of the
property to New Life Covenant S. E. Church if no responsive alternative
proposals are received.
Project Manager: Michelle Nolan
Motioned by Ward, seconded by Newsome. Approved 12-0. Yeas: Buford,
Carrott, Duque, Kostiner, Logan, Martin, Mooney, Newsome, Olander, Sahli,
Stein and Ward. Perez not present.
13-CDC-3

71ST/STONY ISLAND TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA

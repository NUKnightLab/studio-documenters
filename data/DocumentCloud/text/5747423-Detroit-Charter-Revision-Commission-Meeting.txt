February 5, 2019

NOTICE OF SPECIAL MEETING OF THE DETROIT
CHARTER REVISION COMMISSION
In accordance with Section 5(4) of the Michigan Open Meetings Act, MCL 15.265(4), the
Detroit Charter Revision Commission will hold a special meeting on Tuesday, February 12, 2019,
at 6:00 p.m. The meeting will be held at the Northwest Activities Center located at 18100 Meyers
Rd, Detroit, MI 48235. The telephone number is (313) 578-7500. The Public is invited and
encouraged to attend.
Additional information regarding this meeting may be obtained from the Office of the City
Clerk at (313) 224-3266.

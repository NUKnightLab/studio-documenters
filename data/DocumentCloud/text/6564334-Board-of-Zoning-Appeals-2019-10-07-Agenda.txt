Eric Lutzo, prospective purchaser, proposes to construct a new three family residence and 3 paved
parking spaces on a City of Cleveland Land Bank Lot in a C1 Multi-Family Residential District. The
owner appeals for relief from the strict application of the following sections of the Cleveland Codified
Ordinances:
1. Section 337.23(a) which states that accessory uses shall be on the rear half of lot at 47 feet
back from the front property line and the appellant is proposing 28'-6".
2. Section 355.04(a) which states that the Required Minimum Lot area is 7,200 square feet and
the appellant is proposing 3,053 square feet. The maximum Gross Floor Area shall not be
greater than 50 percent of the lot size or in this case 1,526 square feet and the appellant is
proposing 2,053 square feet.
3. Section 357.09(b)(2)(D) which states that the minimum distance from property line is 7 feet
and the appellant is proposing 3 feet. This section also states that the required total of both
Interior Side Yards is 14 feet and the appellant is proposing 11 feet for dwelling and 7 feet for
porches. The minimum distance between buildings on adjacent lot is 10.
4. Section 357.13(c) which states that a wooden garden trellis is not a permitted Interior Side
Yard Encroachment.
5. Section 341.02 which states that City Planning approval is required prior to issuance of
building permit. (Filed August 30, 2019)

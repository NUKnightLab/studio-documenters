STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on February 6, 2018
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman of the Local Panel; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Local Panel; Angela C. Thomas,
Member of the Local Panel; Helen J. Kim, General Counsel; Anna Hamburg-Gal, Associate General Counsel; and staff
members. Kimberly Stevens, Executive Director participated by phone.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD January 9, 2018

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the January 9, 2018 meeting. Member Anderson moved to approve
the minutes. Member Thomas seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gierut asked Executive Director Stevens to present the Executive Director’s reports. She presented the reports
and summarized the highlights.
V.

REVIEW OF PROPOSED WRITTEN DECISIONS AND ORDERS
1.

Case No. L- CA-14-066
Amalgamated Transit Union, Local 308/Chicago Transit Authority

General Counsel Kim called the case. Member Thomas made a motion to approve the proposed written decision. Member
Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VI.

BOARD ACTIONS
1. Case No. L-RC-14-004
Teamsters, Local 700/County of Cook and Cook County Sheriff

General Counsel Kim called and presented the issues of the case to the Board. After discussion, Member Thomas moved
to reject the Administrative Law Judge’s Recommended Decision and Order. Member Anderson seconded the motion, and
the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
2. Case No. L-RC-17-016
Metropolitan Alliance of Police, Cook County Sheriff’s Police Lieutenants, Chapter 750/County of Cook
and Cook County Sheriff
General Counsel Kim called the case, noted that no party filed exceptions to Administrative Law Judge Anna HamburgGal’s Recommended Decision and Order and asked whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own motion.
The Board declined to do so.
3. Case No. L-CB-18-002
Edward Donaldson/Amalgamated Transit Authority, Local 241
General Counsel Kim asked Associate General Counsel Hamburg-Gal to call and present the issues of the case to the Board.
After discussion, Member Anderson moved to accept the Executive Director dismissal with a modification. Member
Thomas seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

 4. Case No. L-CB-16-038
Theopolis Hoffman/Service Employees International Union, Local 73
General Counsel Kim asked Associate General Counsel Hamburg-Gal to call and present the issues of the case to the Board.
After discussion, Member Anderson moved to reject the Executive Director’s Order and approve the variance. Member
Thomas seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VII.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded it was unnecessary.
VIII.

PUBLIC COMMENT

No members of the public were present.
IX.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board, in
Chicago, Illinois, this 6th day of March 2018.

OLD BUSINESS
A.

PROPOSED FOSTER/EDENS TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 39)
Recommend approval of the redevelopment plan for the proposed Foster/Edens
Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, recommend designation of
the area as a Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and
recommend adoption of Tax Increment Financing for the area.
Project Manager: Ryan Slattery
Motioned by Delgado, seconded by Buford. Approved 9-0. Yeas: Brooks,
Buford, Butler, Curtis, Delgado, Reifman, Rhodes, Ward and Newsome.

ANNOUNCEMENTS
Chairman Papadimitriu welcomed members of the public and staff.

The Clerk of the Board called the roll. All five Board Members answered present.

PUBLIC REMARKS
None.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES
The minutes of the October 18, 2018 Regular Board Meeting were approved by a vote of 5-0.

 RULEMAKINGS
R18-19

In the Matter of: Noise Rule Update: Amendments to 35 Ill. Adm.
Code Parts 900, 901, 902, and 910 (Noise) – The Board adopted
a final opinion and order in this rulemaking amending the
Board’s noise regulations.

5-0

R18-20

Amendments to 35 Ill. Adm. Code 225.233, Multi-Pollutant
Standards (MPS) (Air) – The Board denied Vistra’s motion for
expedited review.

5-0

R19-3

RCRA Subtitle C (Hazardous Waste) Update, USEPA
Amendments (January 1, 2018 through June 30, 2018) (Land –
The Board adopted a final opinion and order in this “identical-insubstance” rulemaking to amend the Board’s hazardous waste
rules.

5-0

R19-6

National Ambient Air Quality Standards, USEPA Regulations
(January 1, 2018 through June 30, 2018) – The Board adopted a
proposal for public comment in this “identical-in-substance”
rulemaking to amend the Board’s air pollution regulations.

5-0

ADJUSTED STANDARDS
AS 18-1

In the Matter of: Petition for Modification of Adjusted Standard
Order for Electric Arc Furnace Dust Stabilized Residue (Land) –
The Board denied Peoria Disposal Company’s petition to modify
the Board’s 2009 delisting order.

5-0

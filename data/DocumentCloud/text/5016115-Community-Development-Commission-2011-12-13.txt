STOCKYARDS ANNEX TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 11)
Request authority for the Department of Housing and Economic Development to
negotiate a redevelopment agreement with Experimur Properties, LLC for
redevelopment of the property located at 4045 S. Morgan Street in the Stockyards
Annex Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and to recommend
to the City Council of the City of Chicago the designation of Experimur
Properties, LLC as Developer.
Project Manager: John Molloy
Motioned by Sahli, seconded by Bland. Approved 9-0. Yeas: Bland, Carrott,
Garner, Hassan, Kostiner, Mooney, Perez, Sahli and Ward. Logan absent,
Buford abstains.
11-CDC-45

B.

STOCKYARD INDUSTRIAL-COMMERCIAL TIF REDEVELOPMENT
PROJECT AREA (WARD 11)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Housing and Economic
Development=s intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Chemical
Compositions, LLC for the disposition of the city property located at 1451 W. 41st
Street in the Stockyards Industrial-Commercial Tax Increment Financing
Redevelopment Project Area, to request alternative proposals and to approve the
sale of the property to Chemical Compositions, LLC if no responsive alternative
proposals are received; and request authority for the Department of Housing and
Economic Development to negotiate a redevelopment agreement with Chemical
Compositions, LLC for redevelopment of the city property plus the developer
property located at 4100 S. Packers Avenue and to recommend to the City
Council of the City of Chicago the designation of Chemical Compositions, LLC
as Developer if no responsive alternative proposals are received.
Project Manager: John Molloy
Motioned by Perez, seconded by Ward. Approved 10-0. Yeas: Bland, Buford,
Carrott, Garner, Hassan, Kostiner, Mooney, Perez, Sahli and Ward. Logan
absent.
11-CDC-46

 C.

CANAL/CONGRESS TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA

C.

NORTH PULLMAN TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 9)
Request authority to acquire 49 parcels located on the 107th block of S. Cottage
Grove Avenue, the 106th and 107th blocks of S. Champlain and S. Langley
avenues, the 104th and 105th blocks of S. Corliss and S. Maryland avenues, and
607 E. 107th Street in the North Pullman Tax Increment Financing
Redevelopment Project Area. All of the properties are located in the North
Pullman Historic District.
Project Manager: Patrick Brutus
Motioned by Perez, seconded by Garner. Approved 5-2. Yeas: Buford, Carrott,
Garner, Perez and Ward. Nays: Hassan and Kostiner. Logan, Sahli and Bland
absent.

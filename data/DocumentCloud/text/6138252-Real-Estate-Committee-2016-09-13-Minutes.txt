 16-0245
Presented by: ARNOLD L. RANDALL, General Superintendent
PROPOSED AGREEMENT
Vendor: PayNet, Inc., Skokie, Illinois
Request: Requesting authorization for the Forest Preserve District of Cook County (“the Preserves”) to
enter into an agreement
Reason: The Forest Preserves owns real property with improvements known as Harms Woods parking
lot (“Parking Lot”) in Skokie, Illinois. The Parking Lot currently has approximately seventy-five (75)
marked spaces available for parking and open to the public. PayNet (“PayNet”) is located at 5750 Old
Orchard Road, Suite 300, within an office building across from the Parking Lot, and has requested use of
said Parking Lot to provide overflow parking for its employees.
The Forest Preserves respectfully requests authorization to negotiate, draft and execute an agreement
with PayNet, LLC to allow access to the Parking Lot. The agreement will be for one (1) year with a one
(1) year extension option. The fee for this agreement shall be a Parking Fee of $57.00 per vehicle per
month for a minimum of five (5) and maximum of twenty (20) vehicles, if the extension option is
executed a 5% increase shall be applied to the Parking Fee. PayNet agrees to indemnify the Forest
Preserves and maintain an insurance policy naming the Forest Preserves as an additional insured.
PayNet’s access to the Parking Lot is limited to Monday through Friday from 7:00am to 6:00pm. PayNet
will not be granted exclusive use of the Parking Lot and it shall continue to be available for use and
benefit of the public.
Contract period: Maximum of 24 months from the effective date of the agreement.
Estimated Fiscal Impact: Minimum revenue generated $3,420.00 and maximum $28,080.00.
Concurrence(s):
The Chief Financial Officer and Chief Attorney have approved this item.
District(s): 13
A motion was made by Commissioner Steele, seconded by Commissioner García, to approve
16-0245. The motion carried by the following vote:
Ayes:

Acting Chairman Suffredin, Commissioners Arroyo, Boykin, Daley, García, Goslin,
Morrison, Schneider, Silvestri, Sims, Steele and Tobolski (12)

Absent:

Chairman Murphy, Vice Chairman Moore, Commissioners Butler, Fritchey and Gainer (5)

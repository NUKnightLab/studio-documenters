A.

MIDWEST TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 28)
Request authority for the Department of Planning and Development to negotiate a
redevelopment agreement with Harvest Homes Apartments, LP for redevelopment
of the property located at 3512-46 West Fifth Avenue in the Midwest Tax
Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and to recommend to the City
Council of the City of Chicago the designation of Harvest Homes Apartments, LP
as Developer.
Project Manager: Anna Booth
Motioned by Ward, seconded by Newsome. Approved 7-0. Yeas: Cepeda,
Martin, Mooney, Newsome, Olander, Ward and Perez. Davis not present.

MIDWEST TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA (WARD 27)

Martin, Mooney, Newsome, Olander, Ward and Perez.

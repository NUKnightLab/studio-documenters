Commissioner Peraica, seconded by Commissioner Schneider, moved to concur
with the recommendation to try the matter of Primla Sharma v. Cook County, Case
Number 01 C 432. The motion carried.
Commissioner Suffredin, seconded by Commissioner Gainer, moved to
recommend settlement authority in the case of Derrick Phipps, et al. v. Sheriff of
Cook County, Case Number 07 C 3889 or, in the alternative, authority to try. The
motion carried. Commissioners Peraica and Schneider voted no.
Commissioner Suffredin, seconded by Commissioner Gainer, moved that the
authority for settlement in the case of Derrick Phipps, et al. v. Sheriff of Cook
County, Case Number 07 C 3889 be approved. Commissioner Peraica called for a
Roll Call, the vote of yeas and nays being as follows:
ROLL CALL ON MOTION TO APPROVE
Yeas:

Chairman Silvestri, Commissioners Gainer and Suffredin (3)

Nays:

Commissioner Peraica and Schneider (2)

Absent:

Vice Chairman Beavers and Collins (2)

The motion carried and the settlement authority was APPROVED.

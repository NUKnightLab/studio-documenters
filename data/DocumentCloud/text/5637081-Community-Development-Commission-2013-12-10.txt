NEW BUSINESS
A.

71ST/STONY ISLAND TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 5)
Request authority for the Department of Housing and Economic Development to
enter into a negotiated sale with NeighborSpace for the disposition of the property
located at 2301–2307 E. 71st Street in the 71st/Stony Island Tax Increment
Financing Redevelopment Project Area, to request alternative proposals and to
approve the sale of the property to NeighborSpace if no responsive alternative
proposals are received.
Project Manager: Michele A. Rhymes
Motioned by Stein, seconded by Ward. Approved 12-0. Yeas: Buford, Carrott,
Duque, Kostiner, Logan, Martin, Mooney, Newsome, Olander, Perez, Stein and
Ward.
13-CDC-40

 B.

DIVISION/HOMAN TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 26)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Housing and Economic
Development’s intention to enter into a negotiated sale with L&MC Investments,
LLC for the disposition of the property located at 1122 and 1144 N. Christiana
Avenue in the Division/Homan Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project
Area plus 1222 N. Rockwell Street, 1226 N. Campbell Avenue, 1754 and 1756 N.
Drake Avenue, and 3221 W. Crystal Street, which are not located in a TIF district,
to request alternative proposals, and to approve the sale of the property to L&MC
Investments, LLC if no responsive alternative proposals are received.
Project Manager: Irma L. Morales
Motioned by Perez, seconded by Buford. Approved 11-0. Yeas: Buford, Carrott,
Duque, Kostiner, Logan, Martin, Mooney, Newsome, Olander, Perez and Ward.
Stein not present.

C.

KINZIE INDUSTRIAL CORRIDOR TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT

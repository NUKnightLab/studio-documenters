NOTICE OF SPECIAL MEETING OF THE DETROIT
CHARTER REVISION COMMISSION
In accordance with Section 5(4) of the Michigan Open Meetings Act, MCL 15.265(4), the
Detroit Charter Revision Commission will hold a special meeting on Saturday, June 22, 2019, at
12:00 p.m. The meeting will be held at the Ebenezer AME Church, 5151 W. Chicago, Detroit,
MI 48204. The telephone number is (313) 933-6943. The Public is invited and encouraged to
attend.
Additional information regarding this meeting may be obtained from the Office of the City
Clerk at (313) 224-3266.

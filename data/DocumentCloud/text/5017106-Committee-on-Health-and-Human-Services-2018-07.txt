2.

Communication dated June 15, 2018 (received June 29th) requesting Commission
approval of a retroactive intergovernmental agreement between the Charter County
of Wayne and the Detroit Wayne Mental Health Authority (DWMHA) in the amount
of $5,000,000 for the provision of mental health services for males and females,
either with or at-risk for developing serious mental illness, co-occurring disorders,
substance use disorders and developmental disabilities; the County Executive
advises that funding of the program ensured persons who received mental health
services at the Wayne County Jail would result in individuals progressing toward
psychiatric stability, developing coping skills that would help to avert crisis and learn
the importance of being compliant with treatment while incarcerated; the term of the
agreement is from October 1, 2017 through September 30, 2018; the cost of the
agreement will be charged to Account No. 221 32630 580010 (Jail Mental Health).
(2018-19-031)

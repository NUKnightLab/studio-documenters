Resolutions.

07-06-2017 RESOLUTION AUTHORIZING THE BOARD CHAIR TO OFFER EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR POSITION.
Chairperson Ward Gerson provided an overview of the search process, including interviews and robust
board discussion, both in open session on Monday, July 10, 2017. Since that meeting, Korn Ferry called
references, which came back very positive. There was substantial board discussion with each board
member speaking about their assessment of the respective candidates, their and what they
could offer the organization. Motion by Member Hosey to approve resolution and offer executive
director position to Saskia Thompson. Supported by Member PernelI-Shelton. MOTION UNANIMOUSLY
APPROVED.

The Board thanked Irene Tucker for her service as Interim Executive Director during this time of transition
on top of her duties as Chief Financial Officer.

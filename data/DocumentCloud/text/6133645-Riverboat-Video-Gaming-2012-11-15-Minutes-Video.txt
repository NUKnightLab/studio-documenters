MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING - VIDEO
OPEN SESSION
November 15, 2012
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
On Thursday, November 15, 2012 a Regular Meeting - Video of the Illinois Gaming Board
(“Board”) was held on the 2nd floor at 110 W. Randolph at the JRTC Building, Chicago, Illinois.
The Open Session on Video Gaming started at approximately 10:45 A.M. Present were
Chairman Aaron Jaffe, Members Lee Gould, Michael Holewinski and Maribeth Vander Weele.
APPROVAL OF MINUTES – Open Session
Member Gould moved that the Board approve the open session minutes of its Regular
Meeting of October 18, 2012. Member Vander Weele seconded the motion. The Board
approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
CHAIRMAN’S COMMENTS –
BOARD MEMBERS’ COMMENTS - Member Vander Weele congratulated Lynn Carter on
her new position at CMS and thanked her for all of her hard work at the Gaming Board.
ADMINISTRATOR’S REPORT –
Administrator Ostrowski gave a brief summary of the locations that are up and running and the
procedures that must be followed once the licenses are issued.
BOARD POLICY ITEMS –
•

Proposed Amendment to the Video Gaming Rules – Section 1800.420
Member Holewinski moved that the Board authorize staff to amend the Board’s rules
on video gaming, Section 1800.420, regarding the Board having discretion to deny a
license application on the basis that the applicant, a person with significant
influence or control over the applicant, a person with ownership interest in the
applicant of 5% or more; or a person involved in the operation or management of
the applicant’s business related to video gaming has placed himself or herself on the
Self-Exclusion List, by submitting a proposal for First Notice Filing with the
Secretary of State upon final review and approval by the Administrator. Member
Gould seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call
vote.

•

Proposed Amendment to the Video Gaming Rules – Section 1800.110
Member Vander Weele moved that the Board authorize staff to amend the Board’s
rules on video gaming, Section 1800.110, regarding the definition of a “place of

 worship under the Religious Corporation Act” to mean a structure belonging to or
operated by a church, congregation or society formed for the purpose of religious
worship and eligible for incorporation under the Religious Corporation Act,
provided that the structure is used primarily for purposes of religious worship and
related activities, by submitting a proposal for First Notice Filing with the Secretary
of State upon final review and approval by the Administrator. Member Holewinski
seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
MANUFACTURER, DISTRIBUTORS AND SUPPLIERS LICENSEE ITEMS –
• Happ IL Distribution, Inc. – Renewal of Distributor License
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Gould moved that the Board renew the Happ
IL Distribution, Inc. for a Distributor License, effective November 15, 2012 for a
period of one year, expiring in November 2013. Member Vander Weele seconded the
motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
TERMINAL OPERATOR LICENSEE ITEMS –
• Barcade Gaming, LLC – Terminal Operator’s License
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Holewinski moved that the Board approve
Barcade Gaming, LLC for a Terminal Operator license effective November 15, 2012
for a period of one year, expiring in November 2013. Member Gould seconded the
motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
•

Maness Gaming, LLC – Terminal Operator License
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Vander Weele moved that the Board

•

Angelle & Green, LLC – Terminal Operator License
Based on staff’s investigation, Member Holewinski moved that the Board approve

seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.

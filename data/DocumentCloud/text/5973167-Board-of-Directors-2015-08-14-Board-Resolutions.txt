RESOLUTIONS ADOPTED BY THE
CHICAGO INFRASTRUCTURE TRUST (THE “TRUST”)
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
ON AUGUST 14, 2015

MEETING MINUTES
RESOLVED, that the minutes of the meeting of the Board of Directors
held on October 16, 2014, in the form previously provided to the members of the
Board of Directors, be and hereby are approved.
EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR
WHEREAS, Stephen S. Beitler has indicated his intention to step down as
Executive Director of the Trust effective September 8, 2015; and
WHEREAS, the Board of Directors has determined to elect Leslie M.
Darling to serve as Executive Director of the Trust effective September 9, 2015;
and
WHEREAS, the Board of Directors has reviewed the terms of
compensation and benefits proposed to be provided to the Executive Director;
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that, pursuant to Section 8.1 of
the Trust’s Bylaws, Leslie M. Darling be, and hereby is, (i) elected to serve as
Executive Director of the Trust beginning on September 9, 2015 and continuing
until her earlier resignation, retirement or removal and (ii) entitled during such
period of service to the terms of compensation and benefits previously reviewed
by the Board of Directors, subject to such modification as shall be subsequently
agreed, by the Chairman, subject to subsequent ratification by the Board of
Directors.
AMENDED AND RESTATED BYLAWS
WHEREAS, the Board of Directors desires to amend further the Bylaws
of the Trust, as amended and restated as of December 6, 2012; and
WHEREAS, the Board of Directors has reviewed a draft of revised
Bylaws of the Trust, amended and restated through August 14, 2015;

 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Bylaws of the Trust,
amended and restated as of August 14, 2015, in the form provided to and
reviewed by the Board of Directors, be, and hereby are, adopted and approved.

SMART STREET LIGHTING PROJECT
WHEREAS, the staff of the Trust has reviewed with the Board of
Directors a proposal with respect to certain energy savings initiatives relating to
street lights in Chicago (the “Smart Street Lighting”); and
WHEREAS, the Board of Directors has determined that the Smart Street
Lighting Project is a worthwhile undertaking and is within the proper scope of the
Trust;
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that any of the Trust’s officers
be, and hereby are, authorized to prepare and issue a request for information in
furtherance of the Smart Street Lighting Project.
AUDIT REPORT
WHEREAS, the Trust has obtained from Cohn Reznick a draft report on
its audit of the Trust’s fiscal year 2014 financial statements (the “Audit Report”);
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Audit Report be and
hereby is accepted and approved.
2014 ANNUAL REPORT
WHEREAS, Section 11.2 of the Trust’s Bylaws requires that the Trust
prepare, and the Board of Directors approve, an annual report that details the
activities and accomplishments of the Trust for the prior year, including, without
limitation, information as to infrastructure projects financed or supported by the
Trust and the annual audited financial statements of the Trust.
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the 2014 Annual Report of
the Chicago Infrastructure Trust, in the form previously provided to the members
of the Board of Directors, be, and hereby is in all regards, approved, and that
management of the Trust be authorized to release such Annual Report for public
inspection and to disseminate such Annual Report to the persons and in the
manner prescribed by the Trust’s Bylaws.

 GENERAL
RESOLVED, that in order to fully carry out the intent and effectuate the
purposes of the foregoing resolutions, any of the Trust’s officers be, and each

Legal Services Contract-Counsel for the Board
Vice Chairman Munfakh made a Motion, Supported by Director Walker-Griffea,
for authorization to retain legal counsel to the Board to review whether GLWA's
implementation of the makeup benefit complied with IRS code; whether GLWA
communicated the details of the makeup benefit to eligible members; and
whether the makeup benefit communication was reasonable for eligible
members to make a reasoned decision on whether to participate in the makeup
benefit.
Motion By: Abe Munfakh
Support By: Beverly Walker-Griffea
Action: Approved
The motion carried by a unanimous vote.

The Board will also discuss such other matters.

PROPOSED APPOINTMENT
Appointee(s): Amy Patton Campanelli
Position: Cook County Public Defender
Department/Board/Commission: Office of the Cook County Public Defender
Effective date: 4/1/2015
Expiration date: 3/31/2021

 Summary: Amy Patton Campanelli is hereby appointed to serve as the Cook County Public Defender for a six
year term beginning 4/1/2015 and ending 3/31/2021. Ms. Campanelli will assume the position of Public Defender
upon approval by the Board of Commissioners and following the expiration of Mr. Abishi Carrando "A.C."
Cunningham, Jr.’s appointed term. Mr. Cunningham’s appointment expires March 31, 2015 and all of Cook
County thanks him for his service.
Ms. Campanelli brings 23 years of service in the Office of the Cook County Public Defender, and nearly 30 years
of legal experience, to the position of Cook County Public Defender. She currently serves as the Deputy of
Suburban Operations for the Office of the Cook County Public Defender. In that position she manages more than
150 assistant public defenders, supervisors, chiefs, support staff and investigators and oversees all
specialty/therapeutic courts. Ms. Campanelli previously served as an Assistant Cook County Public Defender
before entering management in the Office in 2003. She has experience in all divisions of the Office, including
Juvenile, Felony Trial, Capital, and Homicide cases. Her extensive legal and administrative experience will be an
asset to the administration of the Office of Cook County Public Defender.
Ms. Campanelli satisfies all of the qualifications for the position of Public Defender as set forth in the Counties
Code (55 ILCS 5/3-4004.2). This appointment is recommended for your approval.
Legislative History: 3/11/15 Board of Commissioners referred to the Legislation and Intergovernmental
Relations Committee
The nominee’s resume, statement of interest and affidavit were entered into the record. The nominee also gave an
oral statement as to his interest in and qualifications for the position.

STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on January 16, 2014
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, Suite S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Board; Richard Lewis, Member of the Board; Melissa
Mlynski, Executive Director; Jerald S. Post, General Counsel; and staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD December 17, 2013

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the December 17, 2013 meeting. Member Anderson moved to
approve the minutes. Member Lewis seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gierut asked Executive Director Mlynski to present the Local Panel administrative actions report for the period
of time from December 1 through December 31, 2013. She presented the report and summarized its highlights. The
Board examined the report and discussed it. Fiscal and legislative reports were deferred to the joint meeting that the local
and state panels scheduled later that morning.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.
Case No. L-CB-13-002
Shawn Hallinan/ Fraternal Order of Police, Lodge #7

General Counsel Post called the case. He then asked Counsel Kazanjian to present and discuss the case to the Board.
Counsel Kazanjian stated that on July 1, 2013, ALJ Hamburg-Gal issued a RDO finding that the Respondent violated
Section 10(b)(1) of the Act. Counsel Kazanjian then reviewed and discussed the issues of the exceptions and
recommended that the Board uphold the ALJ's recommended order with modifications. After discussion, Member
Anderson moved to accept the ALJ’s RDO with modifications. Chairman Gierut seconded the motion. The motion passed
with two ayes and one nay; Member Lewis concurred in part and dissented in part.
2.
Case No. L-CA-12-060
Keith Collins/ Chicago Transit Authority
General Counsel Post called the case, noted that no party filed exceptions to ALJ Owen’s RDO and asked whether the
Board wished to review the decision on its own motion. The Board declined to do so.
VI.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded that it was
unnecessary.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Lewis made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board, in
Chicago, Illinois, this 13th day of February, 2014.

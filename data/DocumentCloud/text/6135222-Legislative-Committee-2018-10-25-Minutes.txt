Mr. Zahn then continued his presentation by explaining his experience as a legislative liaison and his
relationship with the leadership in the General Assembly, including their respective staff members.
Mr. Zahn described some of the legislation for which ZGS has represented public pension funds. He
also described the legislative process for new legislation and for technical amendments to existing
legislation. The Board asked Mr. Zahn about who he would approach for sponsorship of legislation
that might be proposed by the Fund and about his ability to work with the Fund staff to develop and
introduce proposed legislation. They inquired about his availability to report to the Board as might
be needed. They also asked about the status of various legislation that he had help introduced and
which was passed or was pending before the General Assembly.
At the conclusion of Mr. Zahn’s presentation, the trustees discussed his qualifications and response
to the RFP. The trustees discussed that they would also like the incumbent legislative liaison to
appear before the Legislative Committee to present their qualifications.
It was then moved by Trustee Fitzgerald and seconded by Trustee DeGraff that the Fund staff invite
the incumbent legislative liaison to make a presentation to the Legislative Committee at their
meeting on November 1, 2018 at 8:30 am.
Vote Result:

MOTION ADOPTED UNANIMOUSLY BY VOICE VOTE

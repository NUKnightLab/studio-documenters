Quorum & Meeting Minutes
With four commissioners present, a quorum was determined to exist. Mike motioned to
accept the November minutes; Darlene seconded the motion, and the motion was
approved unanimously.
Mr. Planthaber attended the meeting in order to convey his concerns regarding the
Belmont-Central Shopping District, and he hopes that the Special Service Area will be a
positive force in strengthening the area.
Administration
Reid reminded the commissioners that the City and the County require separate
Statements of Financial Interest that need to be submitted by May 1.
Since Mike is closing Metro Sales and will not be present in the community on a regular
basis, he has submitted his resignation as chairman of the commission; however, Mike
will remain as a commissioner. Mike motioned to elect Darlene as the new chairperson;
John seconded the motion, and the motion was approved unanimously.
No action was taken on commission bylaws.
Finances
The 2018 year-end balance sheet, actual versus budget, and actual versus 2017 were
presented. No comments were made.
Proposed 2019 Meeting Schedule
The next scheduled meeting is April 23 to review and approve the 2018 audit.
Adjournment
The meeting was adjourned at approximately 3:30 p.m.

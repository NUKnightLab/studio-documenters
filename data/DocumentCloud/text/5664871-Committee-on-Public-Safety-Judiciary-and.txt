Communication dated April 16, 2018 (no email notification received) requesting
Commission approval of a grant agreement between the Charter County of
Wayne and the State of Michigan, Department of State Police-Emergency
Management in the amount of $6,000 for the 2017-18 Hazardous Material
Emergency Preparedness Planning Program (HMEP-PP) to assist Wayne
County in the review of new Superfund Amendment and Reauthorization Act
(SARA) Title III hazardous materials emergency response plans, as well as for
the updating of previously submitted plans for facilities subject to SARA Title III in
Wayne County; the County Executive advises that this grant requires a 25%
match of any federal funds received and matching funds will be supplied by the
County in the form of an “in kind” contribution credited from the salary and fringe
benefits of personnel working on this grant; the term of the grant is from October
1, 2017 through September 30, 2018; the revenue from the grant will be
deposited into Account No. 101 42617 529000 (HMEP-PP). (2018-02-002)

4.

Revision of Wayne County Commission Resolution No. 2018-553, a four-year
grant award between the Charter County of Wayne and the U.S. Department of
Justice, Office of Justice Programs in the amount of $1,297,731 to provide
delegated authority for the County Executive to execute each subrecipient
agreement for the 2017 Justice Assistance Grant (JAG) to the cities of
Dearborn, Dearborn Heights, Detroit, Hamtramck, Harper Woods, Highland
Park, Inkster, Lincoln Park, Livonia, Romulus, Taylor and Westland, the
township of Redford and the Wayne County Sheriff’s Office; the County
Executive advises that the Wayne County Sheriff’s Office has been designated
by the Wayne County Department of Homeland Security & Emergency
Management to serve as the grant administrator for the program administration
responsibilities including, distributing the funds, monitoring the award, submitting
reports including performance measure and program assessment data, and
providing ongoing assistance to the sub recipients of the funds; the term of the
grant is from October 1, 2016 through September 30, 2020; the revenue from the
grant will be deposited into Account No. 266 31692 529000 (JAG-2017). (201871-130)

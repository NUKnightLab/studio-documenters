STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
STATE PANEL
Minutes of the State Panel Meeting
Held on September 9, 2015
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 11:00 A.M.— at 160 North LaSalle Street, S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

John Hartnett, Chairman of the State Panel; Michael Coli, Member of the State Panel; John Samolis, Member of the
State Panel; Keith Snyder, Member of the State Panel; Albert Washington, Member of the State Panel; Melissa
Mlynski, Executive Director; Kathryn Zeledon Nelson, General Counsel; Sarah Kerley, Deputy General Counsel;
Anna Hamburg-Gal, Associate General Counsel; and staff.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD AUGUST 11, 2015

Chairman Hartnett called for approval of the minutes of the August 11, 2015, meeting. Member Washington moved
to approve the minutes. Member Samolis seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Hartnett asked Executive Director Mlynski to present the State Panel administrative actions report. She
presented the report and summarized the highlights.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.

Case No. S-CA-09-007-C
Oak Lawn Fire Fighters Association, Local 3405, International Association of Fire
Fighters/Village of Oak Lawn

Chairman Hartnett called the case and asked General Counsel Nelson to present the case to the Board. General
Counsel Nelson stated that Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) Anna Hamburg-Gal issued a Recommended Decision
and Order (RDO) vacating the Compliance Order. She then presented and discussed the case. After her presentation,
General Counsel Nelson recommended that the Board affirm the RDO and offered several alternative options for the
Board to consider. After discussion, Member Coli moved to accept the RDO and modify as offered by General
Counsel Nelson. Member Washington seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
2.

Case No. S-CA-15-071
Illinois Council of Police/Village of Maywood

General Counsel Nelson called the case, noted that no party filed exceptions to Administrative Law Judge Kelly
Coyle’s Recommended Decision and Order and asked whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own
motion. The Board declined to do so.
3.

Case No. S-CA-15-096
Metropolitan Alliance of Police, Grundy County Civilians, Chapter 693/County of Grundy

General Counsel Nelson called the case, noted that no party filed exceptions to Administrative Law Judge Deena
Sanceda’s Recommended Decision and Order and asked whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own
motion. The Board declined to do so.

 4.

Case No. S-UC-15-063
County of Mercer and Mercer County Clerk, Mercer County Treasurer and Sheriff of Mercer
County/International Union of Operating Engineers, Local 150

General Counsel Nelson called the case, noted that no party filed exceptions to Administrative Law Judge Sarah R.
Kerley’s Recommended Decision and Order and asked whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own
motion. The Board declined to do so.
VI.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded it was
unnecessary.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Hartnett called for a motion to adjourn. Member Washington moved to adjourn the meeting, and the
Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the State Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations
Board, in Springfield, Illinois, this 6th day of October, 2015.

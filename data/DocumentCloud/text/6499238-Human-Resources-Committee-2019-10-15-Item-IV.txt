Implicit Bias Defined

Biases are the intentional or unintentional beliefs
based on stereotypes that cause someone to
perceive traits or characteristics that may or may
not be true, which enables the favorable or
unfavorable treatment of said person or group.

Implicit biases are:

. Attitudes or stereotypes that unconsciously
affect our understanding, actions, and decisions.

- Activated involuntarily and without our
awareness or intentional control.

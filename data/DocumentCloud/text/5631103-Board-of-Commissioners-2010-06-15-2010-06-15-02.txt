EXECUTIVE SESSION ITEM NO. 2
RESOLUTION NO. 2010-CHA-72
WHEREAS, the Board of Commissioners has reviewed the Board letter dated June 15, 2010,
requesting authorization to (1) enter into an Intergovernmental Agreements
between and among the Chicago Housing Authority, the Chicago Park District
and the City of Chicago for land exchanges within or nearby the former Cabrini
Green Extension South, Rockwell Gardens, and Ida B. Wells/Madden Park
developments; (2) submit acquisition and disposition applications to the United
States Department of Housing and Urban Development; and (3) execute and
documents necessary or appropriate to implement the foregoing;
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE CHICAGO HOUSING AUTHORITY
THAT,

the Board of Commissioners authorizes the Chief Executive Officer or his
designee to: (1) enter into Intergovernmental Agreements (“IGAs”) with the
Chicago Park District (the “Park District”) and City of Chicago (the “City”) under
which the CHA, Park District and City will exchange land within or nearby the
former Cabrini Green Extension South, Rockwell Gardens, and Ida B.
Wells/Madden Park developments (or other sites acceptable to CHA); (2) submit
any necessary acquisition and disposition applications to the United States
Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD); and (3) execute, deliver
and perform any documents necessary or appropriate to implement the foregoing.

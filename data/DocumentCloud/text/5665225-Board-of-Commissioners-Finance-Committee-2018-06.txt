PUBLIC TESTIMONY

Duly authorized public speakers shall be called upon to deliver testimony at a time specified in the meeting agenda.
Authorized public speakers who are not present during the specified time for public testimony will forfeit their allotted
time to speak at the meeting. Public testimony must be germane to a specific item(s) on the meeting agenda, and the
testimony must not exceed three minutes; the Secretary will keep track of the time and advise when the time for public
testimony has expired. Persons authorized to provide public testimony shall not use vulgar, abusive, or otherwise
inappropriate language when addressing the Board; failure to act appropriately; failure to speak to an item that is
germane to the meeting, or failure to adhere to the time requirements may result in expulsion from the meeting and/or
disqualify the person from providing future testimony.

WHEREAS, the Cook County Health and Hospitals System (“CCHHS”), recognized as part of its
IMPACT 2020 strategic plan the need to adjust its billing and collection systems to address the changes in
national healthcare financing; and
WHEREAS, following the implementation of the Affordable Care Act, the payer mix at CCHHS
drastically changed from majority uninsured to majority insured; and

 Finance Committee

WHEREAS, this change in payer mix required the creation of a new billing and collection system; and
WHEREAS, the billing and collection practices are vital to maintain the fiscal health and stability of
CCHHS and crucial for the realization of the System’s strategic plan;
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that the Cook County Board of Commissioners does
hereby request that a meeting of the Finance Committee be convened to discuss the billing and collection
practices of the Cook County Health and Hospitals System; and
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that Chief Financial Officer and any other pertinent representatives
of the Cook County Health and Hospitals System appear before the Committee and be prepared to update
the Committee on the billing, collections and accounts payable cycle of the Cook County Health and
Hospitals System.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, the Chairman of the Finance Committee of the Cook County Board
of Commissioners shall develop an agenda for the hearing in consultation with the Board of
Commissioners.

IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gallagher asked Executive Director Brosnan to present the Local Panel administrative actions for
the period of time from June 1 through July 31, 2008. He distributed the report to the Board for review, and
the Board examined and discussed it.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
Freedom of Information Act-Appeal
Service Employees International Union, Local 73

Chairman Gallagher called the case, noting that Executive Director Brosnan denied on July 24, 2008, a
Freedom of Information Act request filed July 23, 2008, by Service Employees International Union, Local 73
(Local 73), with the Board, seeking a copy of the position statement, or a portion thereof, filed by the
National Nurses Organizing Committee in Case No. L-CB-05-009. Acting General Counsel Strizak
reviewed the issues presented by the appeal and recommended that the Board uphold the Executive
Director's denial. The Chairman and Board Members then engaged in a discussion of the issues raised by
Local 73. At the conclusion thereof, Member Anderson moved to accept the Executive Director's denial.
Chairman Gallagher seconded the motion. Member Anderson and Chairman Gallagher passed the motion by
a voice vote. Member Sadlowski opposed.
VI.

OTHER BOARD MATTERS

Chairman Gallagher noted that there was little to report regarding either fiscal or legislative matters.
Thereafter, the Board discussed whether there was a need for executive session, concluding it was not
necessary at this time.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gallagher called for a motion to adjourn. Member Sadlowski made a motion to adjourn the
meeting, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

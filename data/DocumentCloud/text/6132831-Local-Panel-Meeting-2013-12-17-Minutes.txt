STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on December 17, 2013
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, Suite S-401, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Robert M. Gierut, Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Board; Richard Lewis, Member of the Board; Melissa
Mlynski, Executive Director; Jerald S. Post, General Counsel; and staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD November 5, 2013

Chairman Gierut called for approval of the minutes of the November 5, 2013 meeting. Member Anderson moved to
approve the minutes. Member Lewis seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gierut asked Executive Director Mlynski to present the Local Panel administrative actions report for the period
of time from November 1 through November 30, 2013. She presented the report and summarized its highlights. The
Board examined the report and discussed it.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
1.
Case No. L-RC-12-004
America Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31/ County of Cook

General Counsel Post called the case, noted that no party filed exceptions to ALJ Tarver’s RDO and asked whether the
Board wished to review the decision on its own motion. The Board declined to do so.
Case No. L-CA-14-004
2.
Ronald D. Butler/ Chicago Transit Authority
Chairman Gierut asked General Counsel Post to call and present the case. General Counsel Post stated that an
Executive Director dismissal issued on October 18, 2013. The Charging Party alleged that Respondent violated
Section 10(a)(1) of the Illinois Public Labor Relations Act. General Counsel Post then reviewed the issues raised by the
Charging Party’s appeal. After his presentation, General Counsel Post recommended that the Executive Director's
dismissal be affirmed. After discussion, Member Lewis moved to accept the Executive Director’s dismissal. Member
Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
3.
Case No. L-CB-13-031
Tony Damarjian/ Service Employees International Union, Local 73
Chairman Gierut asked General Counsel Post to call and present the case. General Counsel Post stated that on August 27,
2013 the Executive Director issued a dismissal of the charge alleging that Respondent violated Section 10(b)(1) of the
Illinois Public Labor Relations Act. General Counsel Post then reviewed the issues raised by the Charging Party’s appeal
and Respondent’s response. After his presentation, General Counsel Post recommended that the Executive Director's
dismissal be affirmed. After discussion, Member Lewis moved to accept the Executive Director’s dismissal. Member
Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VI.

EXECUTIVE SESSION

After a short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded that it was not
necessary at this time.

 VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gierut called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the meeting, and the Board
passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor Relations Board, in
Chicago, Illinois, this 16th day of January, 2014.

Regular Meeting, Open Session Minutes - Video
Illinois Gaming Board
Chicago, Illinois
February 22, 2017
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
On Wednesday, February 22, 2017, a Regular Meeting - Video of the Illinois Gaming Board
(“Board”) was held on the 5th floor at 160 N. LaSalle at the Michael J. Bilandic Building,
Chicago, Illinois immediately following its Regular Meeting - Riverboat. Present were the
following: Chairman Don Tracy, Members Hector Alejandre and Steve Dolins. Members
Thomas Dunn and Dee Robinson participated via telephone.
APPROVAL OF OPEN SESSION MINUTES:
Member Alejandre moved that the Board approve the open session minutes from its
Regular Meeting of January 26, 2017. Member Dolins seconded the motion. The motion
passed unanimously by roll call vote.
BOARD POLICY ITEMS


Dissemination of Closed Session Minutes

Member Alejandre moved that, based upon a review of the minutes of closed
sessions held by the Board between April 2014 and December 2016, the Board
approve the dissemination of those minutes, as they no longer require confidential
treatment. Member Dolins seconded the motion. The motion passed unanimously by
roll call vote.

ADMINISTRATIVE REQUESTS FOR HEARING, REQUESTS TO REAPPLY
Requests for Hearing
Member Alejandre moved that the Board deny the requests for hearing submitted
by THE DEAD TREE TAVERN, INC. d/b/a THE DEAD TREE TAVERN; Luxe
Group Corporation d/b/a PLAYBOOK; and Thomas Stelmaszek, since each fails to
establish a prima facie case for a hearing. Member Dolins seconded the motion. The
motion passed unanimously by roll call vote.
Member Dolins moved that the Board grant the request for hearing submitted by
William S. Stanford III d/b/a Stanford Gaming, since it has established a prima facie
case for hearing. Member Alejandre seconded the motion. The motion passed
unanimously by roll call vote.
DISCIPLINARY ACTIONS
Active Tax Liability and Failure to Meet the Minimum Qualifications for Licensure
Member Alejandre moved to issue a Disciplinary Complaint revoking the
establishment license of Moore's Pub, Inc. d/b/a Moore's Pub for having an active
tax liability and for failing to maintain the minimum qualifications for licensure,
said action to take effect 21 days from the date of service of the Complaint, unless an
Answer if properly filed within that timeframe. Member Dolins seconded the motion.
The motion passed unanimously by roll call vote.

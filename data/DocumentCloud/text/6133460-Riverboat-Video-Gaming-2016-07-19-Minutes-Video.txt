MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING - VIDEO
OPEN SESSION
July 20, 2016
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
Chicago, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
On Wednesday July 20, 2016 a Regular Meeting - Video of the Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”)
was held on the 5th floor at 160 N. LaSalle at the Michael J. Bilandic Building, Chicago, Illinois.
The Open Session on Video Gaming started at approximately 10:17 A.M. Present were
Chairman Don Tracy, Members Thomas Dunn, Hector Alejandre, Member Dee Robinson and
Member Steve Dolins.
APPROVAL OF OPEN SESSION MINUTES:
Member Dunn moved that the Board approve the open session minutes from its Regular Meeting
of June 2, 2016. Member Dolins seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion
unanimously by roll call vote.
CHAIRMAN'S COMMENTS –
Chairman Tracy commented on the revised Inducement Policy which may be put into effect at
the August meeting. The Chairman also commented on the Board and Staff reviewing the
feasibility of a Self – Exclusion program for video gaming.
ADMINISTRATOR'S COMMENTS –
Administrator Ostrowski stated that 5,441 locations are up and running and there are 23,747 live
VGTs.

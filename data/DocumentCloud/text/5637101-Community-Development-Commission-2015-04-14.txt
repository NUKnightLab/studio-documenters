A.

STOCKYARDS SOUTHEAST QUADRANT TIF REDEVELOPMENT
PROJECT AREA (WARD 11)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Planning and Development’s
intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Opus Development Company, LLC
for the disposition of the property located at 4500 S. Halsted Street in the
Stockyards Southeast Quadrant Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project
Area, to request alternative proposals, and to approve the sale of the property to
Opus Development Company, LLC if no responsive alternative proposals are
received.
Project Manager: Ed Lewis
Motioned by Perez, seconded by Ward. Approved 8-0. Yeas: Buford, Duque,
Logan, Newsome, Olander, Perez, Ward and Carrott. Cepeda and Garner not
present.

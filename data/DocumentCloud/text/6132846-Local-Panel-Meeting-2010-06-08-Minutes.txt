STATE OF ILLINOIS
ILLINOIS LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on June 8, 2010
I.

OPENING OF MEETING 10:00 A.M.—160 North LaSalle Street, Suite S-400, Chicago, IL

II.

PRESENT

Jacalyn J. Zimmerman, Chairman (participated by telephone-conference); Charles E. Anderson, Member
of the Board; Edward E. Sadlowski, Member of the Board; John F. Brosnan, Executive Director; Jerald
S. Post, General Counsel; and staff members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD MAY 11, 2010

Chairman Zimmerman called for approval of the minutes of the May 11, 2010 meeting. Member
Sadlowski moved to approve the minutes. Member Anderson seconded. The Board passed the
motion—two in favor, none against—Chairman Zimmerman abstained.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Zimmerman asked Executive Director Brosnan to distribute the Local Panel administrative
actions report for the period of time from May 1 through May 31, 2010. He distributed the report to the
Board and summarized its highlights. The Board examined the report and discussed it.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
Case No. L-CA-09-009
Illinois Fraternal Order of Police, Lodge 7/City of Chicago

Chairman Zimmerman called the case and moved to grant Respondent's request for oral argument. After
discussion, Member Sadlowski seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice
vote.
VI.

OTHER BOARD MATTERS

The Board heard and considered the fiscal and legislative reports. In addition, the Board reviewed and
discussed the agency's FY11 budget and contemplated various scenarios given its constraints. After a
short discussion regarding whether there was a need for executive session, the Board concluded that it
was unnecessary at this time.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Zimmerman called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the
meeting, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor
Relations Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 13th day of July, 2010.

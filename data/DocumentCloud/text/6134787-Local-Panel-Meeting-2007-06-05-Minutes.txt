APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD MAY 8, 2007

Chairman Gallagher called for approval of the minutes of the May 8, 2007 meeting. Member Sadlowski

IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Gallagher asked Executive Director Brosnan to present the Local Panel administrative actions for
the period of time from June 1 through June 30, 2007. He distributed the report to the Board for review, and
the Board examined and discussed it.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS
Case No. L-CB-01-038
Chicago Transit Authority/Amalgamated Transit Union, Local 241
Case Nos. L-CA-02-003 and L-CA-02-004
Amalgamated Transit Union, Local 241/Chicago Transit Authority

Chairman Gallagher began by summarizing the case, noting that on April 30, 2004, the Board considered this
matter and accepted the ALJ's recommendation in almost its entirety, reversing only his dismissal of the
Union's 10(a)(1) allegation. Chairman Gallagher further noted that the CTA thereafter appealed the Board's
decision to the Illinois Appellate Court, and the court vacated the Board's order and remanded the matter for
reconsideration. The Board then proceeded to discuss the matter at length. At the conclusion thereof,
Chairman Gallagher suggested that a draft decision might assist the Board. Member Sadlowski moved that
Acting General Counsel Brosnan prepare a draft decision for review at the next meeting. Chairman
Gallagher seconded the motion, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
VI.

OTHER BOARD MATTERS

Member Sadlowski made motion that the Local Panel discuss the fiscal and legislative reports at the Joint
Panel meeting scheduled for 11:30 a.m. Member Anderson seconded the motion, and the Board passed it
unanimously, by a voice vote.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Gallagher called for a motion to adjourn. Member Sadlowski made a motion to adjourn the
meeting, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.

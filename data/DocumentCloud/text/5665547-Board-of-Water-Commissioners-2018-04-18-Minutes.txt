customer service received when inquiring about the department's policy on new service
deposit.

department should seek an affordable water plan.
12. Theodore Washington came before the Board regarding the lot he purchased and
asked how he can reduce or eliminate the costs on the driveway. Mr. Washington was
referred to the drainage team.
13. Classie Washington of 6115 15th Street came before the Board asking how she
can get her name removed from the bill. Ms. Washington was referred to the customer
care group.
14. Lorietta Yancy of 3250 Drexel came before the Board about cloudy water and
sewer cleaning. She asked why are downspouts removal required and believes it has
destroyed the concrete at her home. Director Brown replied that the downspout
removal is a state law and suggested that she file a claim. The department will
investigate and make a recommendation.
15. Gloria Anderson of 5464 Baldwin came before the Board on behalf of her mom
regarding a drainage charge for the lot she purchased next door and why she receives
two separate bills. Director Brown replied the department would investigate property for
leakage, and if deemed necessary will refer to the toilet replacement program. He also
suggest that she sign up for the WRAP program.

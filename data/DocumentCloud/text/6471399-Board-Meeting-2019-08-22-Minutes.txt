ADJUSTED STANDARDS
ADMINISTRATIVE CITATIONS
AC 19-19

County of LaSalle v. Jake Cook and Sarah Cook – After
respondents failed to petition for review, the Board found that
respondents violated Sections 21(p)(1), 21(p)(3), and 21(p)(7) of
the Environmental Protection Act (415 ILCS 5/21(p)(1), 21(p)(3),
and 21(p)(7) (2018)). Because there were three violations of
Section 21(p), the Board ordered respondents to pay a civil
penalty of $4,500.

5-0

AC 19-21

IEPA v. PB Acquisition Co. Illinois LLC – After respondent
failed to petition for review, the Board found that respondent
violated Section 55(k)(1) of the Environmental Protection Act
(415 ILCS 5/55(k)(1) (2018)). Because there was one violation
of Section 55(k), the Board ordered respondent to pay a civil
penalty of $1,500.

5-0

I.

Approval of Minutes
a. A motion was made by Commissioner Gibbons to approve the release of the
minutes of the June 26, 2019 Board Meeting. The motion was seconded by
Commissioner Pulido Sanchez. The motion was unanimously approved.

II.

Director’s Report
a. Director Enyia informed the Commission the ILCC Springfield location is up an
running.
b. Director Enyia introduced to the Commission newly hired Chief Financial
Officer, Michael Gentry.
c. Director Enyia announced the passing of HB2577.

 d. Director Enyia announced the Licensing foot traffic is down 53%, with an
increase in online renewals.
e. Acting Chairman O’Connell announced a Special Meeting to be held on July 31st
to cover Quantity Discounting.
f. Distilled Spirits presented to the IL Liquor Control Commission.

III.

Enforcement Division
a. The ILCC completed 216 SAM compliance checks, 40 failed. Twenty-five
compliance checks were repeat violators, 4 violated again. This gives a repeat
violator compliance rate of 84%.
b. The ILCC Enforcement Division has successfully closed out the LC-13 Project.

IV.

Licensing Division
a. The percentage of online renewals is 80%, a 9.5% increase from June 2018.
b. Mrs. Marijan also provided a report of all new licenses issued for June 2019.
c. In June 2019 the ILCC issued 270 new licenses across all categories.

V.

Industry Education
a. The total number of BASSET applicants trained in June 2019 was 10,673.
b. The total number of BASSET applicants in the look up is 276,622.

VI.

Legal Division

a. A motion was made by Commissioner Pulido Sanchez to approve the
Administrative Law Judge default recommendations. The motion was seconded
by Commissioner Gibbons. The motion was unanimously approved.

VII.

VIII.

Public Comment

Closing Remarks and Adjournment to Hearing

 a. Commissioner O’Connell made a motion to adjourn the meeting. Motion was

1. CALL TO ORDER
Chairman Tuma called the meeting to order at 10:06 a.m.

Mr. Tuma asked Deputy Clerk Carter to call the roll. Committee members Tuma,
Miller, Schron and Baker were in attendance and a quorum was determined.
Committee member Conwell entered the meeting shortly after the roll-call was
taken.

There were no public comments given.

A motion was made by Mr. Miller, seconded by Mr. Schron and approved by
unanimous vote to approve the minutes from the September 18, 2019 meeting.

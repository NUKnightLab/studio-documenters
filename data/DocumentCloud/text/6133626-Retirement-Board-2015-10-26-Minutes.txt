Richard Mack
The Board is reconvening the Richard Mack matter. Mr. Mack appeared before the Board on an earlier
date on a disability status review. At that time he was represented by an attorney who has since
withdrawn his appearance in this matter and now has a new attorney representing his interest before the
Board. At the previous hearing on Mr. Mack’s status review, entered into the record were medical records
and a videotape concerning activities of Mr. Mack. The Board suspended Mr. Mack’s disability benefits
effective September 30, 2015. In the interim, the Board received correspondence, to be entered in to the
record, which indicates Richard Mack is no longer disabled and is seeking a return to duty with the
Chicago Police Department.
It was moved by Trustee Hamburger, seconded by Trustee Summers that the duty disability benefit which
were suspended September 30, 2015, shall be terminated as of that date and Mr. Mack, who is not longer
disabled, should be directed to seek return to active duty with the Chicago Police Department.

19-4133
Presented by: EARL MANNING, Director, Office of Capital Planning and Policy
REPORT
Department: Bureau of Asset Management
Report Title: Court Utilization Study Phase I
Report Period: As of 6/12/2019
Summary:

This report reviews the results of a 12-month study by the National Center for State Courts

(NCSC), a nonprofit court and justice consultancy, to identify strategies regarding the best use and
deployment of County-provided space and facilities for adult criminal court operations and activities.

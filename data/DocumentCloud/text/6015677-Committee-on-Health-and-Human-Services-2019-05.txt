Communication dated April 22, 2019 (received May 8th) requesting Commission
approval of Amendment No. 2 to a grant award between the Charter County of
Wayne and the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services, Health Resources

expansion of Access to Quality Substance Use Disorder and Mental Health
Services; the term of the grant amendment is from June 1, 2018 through May 31,
2019; the revenue from the grant will be deposited into Account Nos. 221 60521
529000 (FQHC-Hamtramck $1,557,164) and 221 60520 529000 (FQHC-Wayne
$488,972). (2018-19-034M2)

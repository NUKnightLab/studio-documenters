NEW BUSINESS
A.

HUMBOLDT PARK TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 1)
Request authority to advertise the Department of Housing and Economic
Development=s intention to enter into a negotiated sale with Hispanic Housing
Development Corporation for the disposition of the city property located at 2656
W. North Avenue in the Humboldt Park Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment
Project Area, to request alternative proposals and to approve the sale of the
property to Hispanic Housing Development Corporation if no responsive
alternative proposals are received; and request authority for the Department of
Housing and Economic Development to negotiate a redevelopment agreement
with Hispanic Housing Development Corporation for redevelopment of the city
property plus the developer property located at 1617-19 N. Washtenaw Avenue
and 1618-20 N. Talman Avenue and to recommend to the City Council of the
City of Chicago the designation of Hispanic Housing Development Corporation
as Developer if no responsive alternative proposals are received.
Project Manager: James Cox
Motioned by White, seconded by Logan. Approved 9-0. Yeas: Bland, Carrott,
Garner, Kostiner, Logan, Martin, Mooney, Todman, and White.

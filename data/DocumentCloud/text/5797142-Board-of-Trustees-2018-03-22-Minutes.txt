RE: ACTION WITH RESPECT TO BOOK CADILLAC LITIGATION
Motion By: Chairperson Smith Supported By: Committee member Boodahn

WHEREAS, the Investment Committee of the Police and Fire Retirement System of the
City of Detroit convened a Special Meeting in compliance with all relevant
provisions of the Open Meetings Act to discuss pending litigation related to the Book
Cadillac in closed session;

WHEREAS, the PFRS IC was provided materials from American Realty Advisors

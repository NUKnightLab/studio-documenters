PUBLIC COMMENT
There were no requests to address the Board.
HEARINGS
The Matter of Member 85392 (Felony Conviction)
A hearing was held on the Matter of Member 85392 regarding such Member’s felony conviction. Counsel
for the Fund noted that the hearing was being held due to the Fund’s receipt of a notification that Member
85392 had been convicted of a felony relating to bribery allegations during his employment with the
Chicago Public Schools. Counsel for the Fund further noted that Section 8-251 of the Illinois Pension
Code required the Board to determine whether such felony conviction was related to or in connection with
the municipal employment of Member 85392. In the event the felony conviction was related to such
municipal employment, Counsel stated Section 8-251 required that the annuity benefits of Member 85392
be terminated. In the event the felony conviction was related to such municipal employment, Counsel

Legislative Updates – SB 2
Tom O’Donnell reported that Senate Bill 2 passed and is on its way to the Governor’s desk for
signature. The law will not go into effect for 90 days post signature by the Governor. The State still
has to develop rules and regulations which may take the better part of a year. Meanwhile, the CCBH
regulations will remain in effect until the State rules and regulations are adopted and implemented.

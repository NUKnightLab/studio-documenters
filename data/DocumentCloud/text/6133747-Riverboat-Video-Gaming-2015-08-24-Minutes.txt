MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING - RIVERBOAT
OPEN SESSION
August 25, 2015
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
Chicago, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
On Tuesday, August 25, 2015 a Regular Meeting - Riverboat of the Illinois Gaming Board
(“Board”) was held on the 5th floor at 160 N. LaSalle at the Michael J. Bilandic Building,
Chicago, Illinois at 9:04 A.M. Present were the following: Chairman Don Tracy, Members Lee
Gould, Hector Alejandre and Member Thomas Dunn.
Member Alejandre moved that the Board approve the open session minutes from the Illinois
Gaming Board Regular Meeting held on July 23, 2015. Member Gould seconded the motion.
The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
CHAIRMAN’S COMMENTS –
Chairman Tracy commented on the J&J lawsuit. The Chairman commended staff on their hard
work and also stated that the budget situation has not affected operations at the IGB.
ADMINISTRATOR’S COMMENTS –
Administrator Ostrowski stated that there will be a telephone conference with all the General
Managers on September 2, 2015.
OWNER LICENSEE ITEMS:
The Alton Gaming Company d/b/a Argosy Casino Alton: Brian Anthony Garvey
IT Operations Manager – Occupational License Level 1
Member Dunn moved to approve Brian Anthony Garvey as a Level 1
Occupational Licensee in his position as Manager of IT Operation at the Argosy
Casino Alton. Member Gould seconded the motion. The Board approved the
motion unanimously by roll call vote.
Empress Casino Joliet Corporation d/b/a Hollywood Casino Joliet: Peter
Nicholas Longi– Occupational License Level 1
Member Gould moved to approve Peter Nicholas Longi as a Level 1 Occupational
Licensee in his position as Vice President of Casino Operations at Hollywood
Casino Joliet. Member Alejandre seconded the motion. The Board approved the
motion unanimously by roll call vote.

 Des Plaines Development Limited Partnership d/b/a Harrah’s Joliet Casino &
Hotel: Rachael A. Bartolini– Occupational License Level 1
Member Alejandre moved to approve Rachael A. Bartolini as a Level 1
Occupational Licensee in her position as Vice President of Finance &
Administration at Harrah’s Joliet Casino & Hotel. Member Dunn seconded the
motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
The Rock Island Boatworks, Inc. d/b/a Jumer’s Casino & Hotel: Brian A.
Hansberry– Key Person
Member Dunn moved to Table consideration of Brian Hansberry as a Key Person
of the Rock Island Boatworks, Inc. until the September 2015 Board meeting.
Member Alejandre seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion
unanimously by roll call vote.
SUPPLIER LICENSEE ITEMS:
Global Cash Access Holdings, Inc.: Key Person of Multimedia Games Inc.
Member Alejandre moved to Approve Global Cash Access Holdings, Inc. as a Key
Person/Entity of Multimedia Games, Inc. Member Gould seconded the motion. The
Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
OCCUPATIONAL LICENSEE ITEMS (Approval and Denials)
Approvals & Denials:
Member Gould moved to find suitable and approve 61 level 2 occupational licenses,
and 121 level 3 occupational licenses. Member Dunn seconded the motion. The Board
approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
OCCUPATIONAL LICENSEE ITEMS (Requests for Hearing)
Kevin Nicholas Johnson
Member Alejandre moved to deny Kevin Johnson’s request for hearing as it failed to
comply with the requirements of Board Rule 405. Member Dunn seconded the
motion. The Board approved the motion unanimously by roll call vote.
COMPLAINTS & DISCIPLINARY ACTIONS
Gaming Partners International USA, Inc.
Member Gould moved to issue a disciplinary complaint fining Gaming Partners
International USA, Inc. $5,000 for failing to provide a complete and timely annual
update to the Board in violation of the Board Rules, said action to take effect 21
days from the date of service of the Complaint unless an Answer is filed within that
2

 time frame. Member Dunn seconded the motion. The Board approved the motion by a
vote of 3-0; Member Alejandre recused himself from voting.
MIDWEST GAMING & ENTERTAINMENT, LLC
Member Gould moved to fine Midwest Gaming & Entertainment, LLC Two Million
Dollars and suspend Administrator approval of promotions for a minimum period
of 60 days beginning October 1, 2015, until satisfactory remedial measures are
submitted to the Board; suspend Level 1 Occupational Licensee Michael St. Pierre
for a period of 60 days without pay; and suspend Level 3 occupational Licensee
Daniel Brockdorf for a period of 30 days without pay for violations of the Riverboat
Gambling Act, the Board’s Rules and its Internal Control, said actions to take effect
21 days from the date of service of the Complaint, unless each licensee files an
Answer to the Complaint within that time frame. Member Dunn seconded the
motion. The Board approved the motion by a vote of 3-0; Member Alejandre recused
himself from voting.
At 9:23 A.M. Member Gould motioned to adjourn and Member Dunn seconded the motion. All
Members voted in favor of adjournment.
Respectfully submitted,

Suite 1626, Chicago, Illinois for the consideration of applications and bills, and for other such matters that
came before the Board. The contents of this meeting were transcribed by Esquire Deposition Solution
LLC Court Reporters and are made a part of the record.

Trustee Beyna requested motions on Board orders submitted by the Board’s legal counsel.
In the matter of Michele M. Blasz, the Board finds that pursuant 40 ILCS 5/5-214.2, Blasz' application
for the benefits sought had to be made no later than February 17th, 2010, and her submission completed
on November 8th, 2018, was not timely brought. Accordingly, the Board finds that Blasz' application for
police pension service credit prior to her appointment as a CPD officer for all or part of the time period
requested is hereby denied.

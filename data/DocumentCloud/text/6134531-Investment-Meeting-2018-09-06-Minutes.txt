Infrastructure Education
Mr. Jeremy Zirin provided an overview of infrastructure asset class.
2018 Trustee Election
Ms. Ruffolo presented the 2018 Rules of Election for approval. Included in the
presentation was pricing and a description of the changes to the election process. Based
on this presentation, Trustee Summers made a motion to approve the 2018 Rules of
Election for the Trustee Election. Seconded by Trustee McMahon.
Carried: Ayes–Trustee Johnson, Trustee Keane, Trustee McMahon, Trustee Summers and
Trustee Thompson. Nays-None

 III. NEW BUSINESS
A.

PROPOSED 116TH/AVENUE O TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT
AREA (WARD 10)
Accept for review the eligibility study and redevelopment plan for the proposed
116th/Avenue O Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and set
the dates for a public meeting of the Joint Review Board and a public hearing.
Project Manager: Ryan Slattery
Motioned by Butler, seconded by Brooks. Approved 7-0. Yeas: Brooks, Butler,
Cepeda, Curtis, Newsome, Reifman and Perez. Buford not present.
18-CDC-15

B.

FULLERTON/MILWAUKEE TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 1)
Request authority for the Department of Planning and Development to negotiate a
redevelopment agreement with New Congress, LLC for redevelopment of the
Congress Theater, a Chicago Landmark, located at 2100 N. Milwaukee Avenue in
the Fullerton/Milwaukee Tax Increment Financing Redevelopment Project Area,
and to recommend to the City Council of the City of Chicago the designation of
New Congress, LLC as Developer.
Project Manager: Andrew Ranck
Motioned by Butler, seconded by Buford. Approved 8-0. Yeas: Brooks, Buford,
Butler, Cepeda, Curtis, Newsome, Reifman and Perez.
18-CDC-16

C.

71ST/STONY ISLAND TIF REDEVELOPMENT PROJECT AREA
(WARD 5)
Request authority for the Department of Planning and Development to negotiate a
redevelopment agreement with 7131 Jeffrey Development, LLC; Jeffery Advisors,
LLC dba Shop & Save Markets; and or affiliate entities for redevelopment of the
property located at 2101 E. 71st Street in the 71st/Stony Island Tax Increment
Financing Redevelopment Project Area, and to recommend to the City Council of
the City of Chicago the designation 7131 Jeffrey Development, LLC; Jeffery
Advisors, LLC dba Shop & Save Markets; and or affiliate entities as Developer.
Project Manager: Chip Hastings
Motioned by Newsome, seconded by Cepeda. Approved 8-0. Yeas: Brooks,
Buford, Butler, Cepeda, Curtis, Newsome, Reifman and Perez.
18-CDC-17

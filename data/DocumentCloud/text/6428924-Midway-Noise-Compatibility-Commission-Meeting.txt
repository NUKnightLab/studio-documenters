REPORT OF THE CHICAGO DEPARTMENT OF AVIATION (“CDA”)
A. 4th Quarter 2016 Airport Noise Management System (ANMS) Report
Mr. Frame asked Mr. Jackson of Landrum & Brown to present the 4th Quarter 2016
Airport Noise Management System Report.
Mr. Jackson provided a brief overview of the information contained in the report
and proceeded with the details.
Runway Use Report
In the 4th quarter the most used departure and arrival runway for all three months
was 31C.
October – The most used departure runway was 22R/22L going southwest with
40% of all departures and 31C with 30%, followed by 04L/04R with 17% and last
13C with 13%.
The most used arrival runway coming in from the southwest was 22R/22L with
37%, then 31C with 29%, followed by 04L/04R with 20% and last 13C with 14%.
November – The most used departure runway was 31C with 42% followed by
22R/22L with 39%, next 04L/04R with 15% and last 13C with 4%.
The most used arrival runway was 31C with 44% of all arrivals, followed by
22R/22L with 35%, then 04L/04R with 17% and 13C with 4%.
December – The most used departure runway was 31C with 52% followed by
22R/22L with 41%, then 13C with 5% and last 04L/04R with 2%.
The most used arrival runway was 31C with 61%, followed by 22R/22L with 33%,
then 13C with 5% and last 04L/04R with 1%.
Airline Fleet Mix Report
There was an average of 670 operations per day with 78 of those occurring at
night. Southwest Airlines had the highest operations with an average of 448
operations per day with 61 of those occurring at night. The B737, used almost
exclusively by Southwest, was the most used aircraft with an average of 448
operations per day. General Aviation followed with 170 operations per day and 6
of which were helicopters.

Permanent Noise Monitor Locations
The report indicates the addresses of the 13 permanent noise monitor locations
near the airport as reflected in relation to the Midway 2018 Noise Contour. The
report shows the results for October 2016 through December 2016 with quarterly
breakdowns and a twelve-month rolling average. The 4th quarter 2016 results are
consistent when compared to those of the 4th quarter 2015.
Noise Hotline Report
In the 4th quarter there was a total of 23,592 complaints. The highest number of
complaints came from Burbank with 19,071 and Justice with 1,853. This
significant number of complaints were from 11 addresses and 77% of all
complaints came from 1 address. There was a substantial decrease in the
number of complaints in the 4th quarter compared to previous the 3rd quarter of
2016. The number of communities that submitted complaints in the 4th quarter
was greater than previous months.

REPORT OF THE STANDING COMMITTEES
A. Residential Sound Insulation Sub-Committee Report
Ms. Conwell presented the Residential Sound Insulation Sub-committee Report
(see attached).
Currently there is a total of 10,173 homes completed over 21 years. Phase 15 had

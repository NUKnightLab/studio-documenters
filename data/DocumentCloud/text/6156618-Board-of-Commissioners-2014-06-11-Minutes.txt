By order of President Bryan Traubert
In compliance with Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973 (29 U.S.C. Section 794) and the
Americans with Disabilities Act (42 U.S.C. Section 12101 et seq.), the Chicago Park District does
not discriminate on the basis of disability in employment or admission/access to programs and
activities.

order to attend a meeting of the Chicago Park District Board of Commissioners, should contact

2. PRESENTATION FROM THE DEPARTMENTS:
CHICAGO HISTORY MUSEUM PRESENTATION FROM THE DIRECTOR GARY T. JOHNSON
Gary T. Johnson, Director of the Chicago History Museum addressed the
Commissioners. Mr. Johnson introduced the Executive Director and CEO of
the Institute of Puerto Rican Arts and Culture (IPRAC), Billy Ocasio.

RECOGNITION OF THREE MEMBERS OF THE CHICAGO PARK DISTRICT GYMMNASTICS
TEAM FOR QUALIFYING FOR THE NATIONAL GYMNASTICS CHAMPIONSHIPS AND
SPECIAL RECOGNITION OF THE WINNER OF THE FLOOR EXERCISE EVENT FROM
COMMUNITY RECREATION/GYMNASTICS
Juan Mendoza, Gymnastics Program Manager, introduced the qualifying
gymnasts. President Traubert and Superintendent Kelly presented the three
gymnasts with plaques.

TEENS REIMAGINING ART COMMUNITY AND ENVIRONMENT PROGRAM (TRACE) FROM
CULTURAL AND NATURAL RESOURCES
Peggy Stewart of Cultur, Arts, and Nature introduced Kimeco Roberson,
Program and Event Facilitator of the TRACE Program. Ms. Roberson
announced three former TRACE members who spoke of their experiences as
participants of the TRACE program.

1.

Communication dated March 1, 2019 (received March 8th) requesting
Commission approval of a grant agreement between the Charter County of
Wayne and the State of Michigan, Department of State to provide Electronic Poll
Book (EPB) software and equipment to automate and document certain voter
verification and ballot tracking functions and to automate the updating of the
Qualified Voter File (QVF) voter history following an election; the County
Executive advises that the purpose of this grant is to permit county clerks to train
precinct workers on the use of the EPB, as provided under Michigan Election
Law; the term of the agreement will commence upon Commission approval.
(2019-80-002)

2.

Communication dated March 13, 2019 (received March 25th) requesting
Commission approval of a grant agreement between the Charter County of
Wayne and the State of Michigan Department of Licensing and Regulatory
Affairs (LARA) through the Michigan Indigent Defense Commission (MIDC) in the
amount of $17,275,171 to support the County’s Fiscal Year 2019 Compliance
Plan and Cost Analysis for the provision of indigent criminal defense services
through the standards approved by LARA; the County Executive advises that the
County will contribute its statutory local share amount of $7,447,334 and it will
accept a separate $25,000 grant from the Wayne County Criminal Defense Bar
Association, for a total authorized budget of $24,747,505; the term of the grant is
from October 1, 2018 through September 30, 2019; the revenue from the grant
will be deposited into Account No. 260 13270 569000 (MIDC). (2019-40-030)

3.

Communication dated January 29, 2019 (received February 16th) requesting
Commission approval of Modification No. 1 to a personal services contract
between the Charter County of Wayne and Dawn Van Hoek (of Gregory), with no
change in the dollar amount, to continue to spearhead a team of national experts
to examine the County’s indigent defense system and make recommendations
that comply with Michigan Indigent Defense Commission (MIDC) standards; the
County Executive advises that this contract is contingent upon funding from the
State of Michigan via the MIDC standards and that Ms. Van Hoek is a national
expert and has knowledge of, and access to, experts throughout the country with
specialized expertise; the total amount of the contract remains $312,616;
Modification No. 1 extends the term of the contract from May 1, 2019 through
April 30, 2020; the cost of the contract will continue to be charged to Account No.
101 13290 817000 (Third Circuit Court). (2018-40-106M1)

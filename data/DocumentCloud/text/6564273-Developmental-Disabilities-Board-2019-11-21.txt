SUNSHINE LAW MEETING NOTIFICATION
November 13, 2019

The combined work session and regular monthly meeting of the Summit
County Developmental Disabilities Board will be held on Thursday, November
21, 2019 at 5:30 p.m. in the Board Room of the Summit DD Offices located at
89 East Howe Road, Tallmadge, Ohio 44278.
Meetings are subject to change and may be confirmed by calling 330-634-8082 or
by checking the Summit DD web site at: www.summitdd.org.

All Board Meetings are open to the public; however, the Board reserves the right
to enter into Executive Session in compliance with Ohio Revised Code 121.22.

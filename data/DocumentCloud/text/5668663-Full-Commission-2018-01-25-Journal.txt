 PLEDGE OF ALLEGIANCE TO THE FLAG OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA
Chairman Woronchak advised all to stand to recite the Pledge of Allegiance.

INVOCATION
Chairman Woronchak called upon Commissioner Varga, who gave the following
invocation:
“Dear God, today we give thanks for the simple pleasures a lot of us take for granted.
All we have to do is open our eyes and beauty of nature is all around us. We thank you for all
the people in our lives; our loved ones, our friends and those we work with and serve. Thank
you dear God for the public servants, especially those serving on the Wayne County
Commission, the Executive and of course, all of our employees. Help us make the correct
choices as we deliberate today. Put your arms around those who are ill and going through
tough times. Unite us to have tolerance instead of hate. Bring peace and understanding so
we can better serve you. We lift our voices to praise you for all of our blessings, individually
and collectively. AMEN.”

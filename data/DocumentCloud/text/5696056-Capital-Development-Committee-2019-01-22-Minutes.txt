 Capital Development Committee

Committee Report

January 22, 2019

18-0405
Presented by: ARNOLD RANDALL, General Superintendent
REPORT
Department: Planning & Development
Request: Receive and File
Report Title: 2019 Update to the 5-Year Capital Improvement Plan
Report Period: Five (5) Years, 2019-2023
Summary:

This report is an update of capital improvement projects and funding needs for the next five

(5) years (2019-2023).

Capital improvement needs were identified by Forest Preserves of Cook County

(the “Forest Preserves”) staff, partner organizations and members of the public. The draft can be downloaded from
the Forest Preserves’ website at www.fpdcc.com/cip <http://www.fpdcc.com/cip>. Additional requests and comments
can also be submitted on that web page or on the Forest Preserves’ Facebook page. A final report will be issued at the
January 2019 board meeting.

LOCAL PANEL
Minutes of the Local Panel Meeting
Held on July 13, 2010

II.

PRESENT

Jacalyn J. Zimmerman, Chairman; Charles E. Anderson, Member of the Board; Edward E. Sadlowski,
Member of the Board; John F. Brosnan, Executive Director; Jerald S. Post, General Counsel; and staff
members.
III.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING HELD JUNE 8, 2010

Chairman Zimmerman called for approval of the minutes of the June 8, 2010 meeting. Member
Anderson moved to approve the minutes. Member Sadlowski seconded. The Board passed the motion
unanimously, by a voice vote.
IV.

ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

Chairman Zimmerman asked Executive Director Brosnan to distribute the Local Panel administrative
actions report for the period of time from June 1 through June 30, 2010. He distributed the report to the
Board and summarized its highlights. The Board examined the report and discussed it.
V.

BOARD ACTIONS

Member Anderson moved that the Board decide the first case on the agenda after deciding the other two.
Member Sadlowski seconded. The Board passed the motion unanimously, by a voice vote.
1.
Case No. L-CA-08-004
Service Employees International Union, Local 73/County of Cook
Chairman Zimmerman called the case, noting that no party filed exceptions to Administrative Law
Judge (ALJ) Wells' Recommended Decision and Order (RDO), and asked whether the Board wished to
review the decision on its own motion. The Board declined to do so.
2.
Case No. L-CA-08-007
American Guild of Musical Artists/Chicago Park District
Chairman Zimmerman called the case, noting that no party filed exceptions to ALJ Strizak's RDO, and
asked whether the Board wished to review the decision on its own motion. The Board declined to do so.
3.
Case Nos. L-RC-09-018 and L-UC-09-008
American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Council 31/
City of Chicago
Chairman Zimmerman called the case. General Counsel Post reviewed the issues presented by the
exceptions to ALJ Harvey's RDO, and recommended as to her determination, that the Board affirm in
part and reverse in part. After discussion by the Board, Chairman Zimmerman moved to postpone

 deciding the case until the next meeting. Member Anderson seconded. The Board passed the motion
unanimously, by a voice vote.
VI.

OTHER BOARD MATTERS

After a short discussion, the Board unanimously agreed to defer consideration of the fiscal and
legislative reports until the joint panel meeting scheduled for 10:30 a.m.
VII.

ADJOURNMENT

Chairman Zimmerman called for a motion to adjourn. Member Anderson made a motion to adjourn the
meeting, and the Board passed it unanimously, by a voice vote.
Adopted and approved by the Chairman and Members of the Local Panel of the Illinois Labor
Relations Board, in Chicago, Illinois, this 10th day of August, 2010.

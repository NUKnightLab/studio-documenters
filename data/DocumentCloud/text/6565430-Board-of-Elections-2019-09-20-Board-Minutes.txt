Special Meeting of the Board
The Board Meeting began at 10:05 am. All Board Members were present.
Administrative
Discussion of the County’s proposal to use the Cuyahoga County Board of Elections facility to
film a movie scene of the “Minuteman”.
The transcript of proceedings from the protest hearings are incorporated by reference into the
minutes and are available for review upon request.
Board Member Wondolowski made the motion to approve the proposal to use the Cuyahoga
County Board of Elections facility to film a movie scene of the “Minuteman”.
After a vote of the Board Members the outcome resulted in a tie vote as follows:
Robert Frost – No vote
David J. Wondolowski – Yes vote
Inajo Davis Chappell – Yes vote
Jeff Hastings – No vote
Director Perlatti stated that he would review the tie vote outcome with the Secretary of State’s
Office to determine if any further action will need to be taken and report back to the Board
Members.

Public Comment:
There were no public comments at this meeting.
The Board unanimously voted to recess at 10:45 am.

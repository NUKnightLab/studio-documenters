MEETING MINUTES
Cuyahoga County Personnel Review Commission
Special Meeting
Friday, March 8, 2019
2429 Superior Viaduct, 2nd Floor
Cleveland, Ohio 44113
8:00 a.m.
1) CALL TO ORDER
Classification and Compensation Manager Albert Bouchahine called the meeting to order at 8:00
a.m.
2) ROLL CALL
Classification and Compensation Manager Albert Bouchahine called the roll. Chairman Boseman and
Commissioner Southerington were present. Chairman Boseman was absent; a quorum was
determined.
3) PUBLIC COMMENT—Nothing submitted
4) NEW BUSINESS
a) Class Plan Revision Requests
Classification and Compensation Manager Albert Bouchahine presented the Class Plan Revision
Requests. Albert Bouchahine asked that the Commissioners not consider deleting Senior
Administrative Secretary at the request of Human Resources. Discussion ensued.
Chairman Boseman made a motion to approve the Class Plan Revision Requests with the
exception of Senior Administrative Secretary; Commissioner Southerington seconded the
motion. All were in favor; no objections.
5) EXECUTIVE SESSION – Discussion of personnel matter
Director Rebecca Kopcienski presented a hiring recommendation for David Mizuta for the part-time
position of Intern for a period of six (6) months.
Chairman Boseman made a motion to approve the hiring recommendation of David Mizuta for the
part-time position of Intern for a period of six (6) months. Commissioner Southerington seconded
the motion. All were in favor; no objections.
6) ADJOURNMENT
Commissioner Southerington made a motion to adjourn the meeting at 8:07 a.m.; Chairman
Boseman seconded. All were in favor; no objections.
The next Personnel Review Commission Meeting is scheduled for Wednesday, April 3, 2019 at 4:00
p.m. at the PRC office located at 2429 Superior Viaduct, 3rd Floor. Free parking is available in the
front lot of the building. Persons needing accommodations to attend and participate in the meeting
should contact the PRC’s Administrative Assistant at 216-698-2975.

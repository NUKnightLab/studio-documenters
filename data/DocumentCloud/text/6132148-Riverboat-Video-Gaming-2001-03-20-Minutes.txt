MINUTES OF REGULAR MEETING
ILLINOIS GAMING BOARD
March 20, 2001
CHICAGO, ILLINOIS
NOTE: ITEMS IN BOLDFACE PRINT REFLECT OFFICIAL BOARD ACTIONS
The Illinois Gaming Board (“Board”) held its Regular Meeting on March 20, 2001. The
Board met in the conference room of the Gaming Board Offices on the 3rd floor at 160 N.
LaSalle for closed session.
Chairman Gregory C. Jones was present. Members Sterling M. Ryder and Staci Yandle were
present telephonically. Member Stuart Levine was not present.
Also in attendance were: Administrator Sergio E. Acosta, Deputy Administrators Joseph
Haughey, Allan S. McDonald, and Deputy Chief Legal Counsel Jeannette P. Tamayo, and
other members of the staff. Deputy Administrator Thomas Swoik was present telephonically.
Chairman Jones convened the March 20, 2001 Regular Meeting at 9:40 A.M. in the 3rd floor
Board Conference Room. Member Ryder moved that the Board retire to Closed
Session pursuant to Section 2(c), paragraphs (3), (4), (11), (14) and (21) of the Open
Meetings Act, and Section 6(d) of the Riverboat Gambling Act to discuss the
following subject matters:

Member Yandle seconded the motion. The Board adopted the motion by unanimous

Meeting of March 26, 2001, as previously scheduled.
